TITLE XXVI
CEMETERIES; BURIALS; DEAD BODIES

CHAPTER 290
BURIALS AND DISINTERMENTS

Section 290:1-a


[RSA 290:1-a effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA 290:1-a set out below.]
    290:1-a Fetal Death Records. –
    Whenever a fetal death shall occur, the attending physician or advanced practice registered nurse shall fill out a fetal death record. This will be solely a statistical report.

[RSA 290:1-a effective January 1, 2016; see also RSA 290:1-a set out above.]


    290:1-a Fetal Death Records. --
    Whenever a fetal death shall occur, the attending physician, advanced practice registered nurse, or physician assistant shall fill out a fetal death record. This will be solely a statistical report.

Source. 1977, 530:4. 1997, 325:10. 2007, 215:16. 2009, 54:5, eff. July 21, 2009. 2015, 140:16, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
