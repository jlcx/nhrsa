TITLE XXVI
CEMETERIES; BURIALS; DEAD BODIES

CHAPTER 291-A
UNIFORM ANATOMICAL GIFT ACT

Section 291-A:23

    291-A:23 Honoring an Individual's Decision to Donate. – A person's decision to make a donation of that person's own body, organ, or tissue after death shall be honored. In the absence of a revocation or amendment, health care providers and procurement organizations shall act in accordance with the donor's decision and may take appropriate actions to effect the anatomical gift.

Source. 2010, 111:1, eff. July 31, 2010.
