TITLE XXXVI
PAWNBROKERS AND MONEYLENDERS

CHAPTER 399-C
LENDERS OF MONEY

Section 399-C:2

    399-C:2 Penalty. – Whoever violates any provision of this chapter shall be guilty of a misdemeanor if a natural person, or guilty of a felony if any other person.

Source. 1969, 79:1. 1973, 529:96, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
