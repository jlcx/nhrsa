TITLE XXXVI
PAWNBROKERS AND MONEYLENDERS

CHAPTER 399-G
LICENSING OF MONEY TRANSMITTERS

Section 399-G:3

    399-G:3 License Required. – Any person not exempt under RSA 399-G:4 that, in its own name or on behalf of other persons, engages in the business of money transmission shall obtain a license from the banking department.

Source. 2006, 303:35, eff. Aug. 18, 2006.
