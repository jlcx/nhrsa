TITLE XXXVI
PAWNBROKERS AND MONEYLENDERS

CHAPTER 399-G
LICENSING OF MONEY TRANSMITTERS

Section 399-G:16

    399-G:16 Net Worth Requirements. – An applicant or licensee shall at all times maintain a net worth of the lesser of its average daily outstanding money transmissions for the prior calendar year or $1,000,000.

Source. 2015, 258:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
