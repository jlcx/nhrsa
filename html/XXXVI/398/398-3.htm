TITLE XXXVI
PAWNBROKERS AND MONEYLENDERS

CHAPTER 398
PAWNBROKERS

General Regulations

Section 398:3

    398:3 Penalty. – Whoever violates any provision of this subdivision shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1907, 26:3. PL 172:3. RL 209:3. RSA 398:3. 1973, 528:270, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
