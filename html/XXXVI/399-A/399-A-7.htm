TITLE XXXVI
PAWNBROKERS AND MONEYLENDERS

CHAPTER 399-A
REGULATION OF SMALL LOANS, TITLE LOANS, AND PAYDAY LOANS

Section 399-A:7

    399-A:7 License Posting. – No licensee shall engage in the business of a small loan lender without a conspicuously posted license in the licensee's principal place of business within this state and in each of the licensee's licensed branch offices.

Source. 2015, 73:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
