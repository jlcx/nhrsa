TITLE XXXVI
PAWNBROKERS AND MONEYLENDERS

CHAPTER 399-D
DEBT ADJUSTMENT SERVICES

Section 399-D:24-a


[RSA 399-D:24-a effective until January 1, 2017.]
    399-D:24-a Violations. –
    Any person who willfully violates any provisions of RSA 399-D:13-a, I or II or a cease and desist order or injunction issued pursuant to RSA 399-D:23, II shall be guilty of a class B felony. Each of the acts specified shall constitute a separate offense and a prosecution or conviction for any one of such offenses shall not bar prosecution or conviction of any other offense.

Source. 2008, 205:49, eff. Aug. 15, 2008.
