TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384-D
TRUST DEPOSITS IN BANKS

Section 384-D:5

    384-D:5 Effect on Other Legislation. – This chapter shall in no way affect or modify the provisions of RSA 386:19, RSA 393:12, and RSA 384:28-31.

Source. 1975, 199:2. 2002, 232:6, eff. Jan. 1, 2003.
