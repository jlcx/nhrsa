TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384-C
SUSPENSION OF BANKING ACTIVITIES

Section 384-C:2


[RSA 384-C:2 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XI, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384-C:2 Power of the Commissioner. –
    The commissioner may, by proclamation, close all of the offices of all banks on any day or days designated, by proclamation of the president of the United States or the governor of this state, as a day or days of mourning, rejoicing, or other special observance.

Source. 1971, 448:1, eff. Aug. 29, 1971.
