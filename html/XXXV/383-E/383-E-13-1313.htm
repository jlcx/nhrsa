TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 13
Corporate Credit Union

Section 383-E:13-1313


[RSA 383-E:13-1313 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:13-1313 Exemptions. –
    The corporate credit union shall be exempt from the securities laws of New Hampshire.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
