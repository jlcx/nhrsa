TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 13
Corporate Credit Union

Section 383-E:13-1304


[RSA 383-E:13-1304 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:13-1304 Charter and Name Exclusive. –
    Only one corporate credit union shall be incorporated under this chapter and no other credit union may use the term "corporate credit union'' as a part of its name.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
