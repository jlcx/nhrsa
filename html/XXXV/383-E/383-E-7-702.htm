TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 7
Meetings

Section 383-E:7-702


[RSA 383-E:7-702 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:7-702 Special Meetings. –
    Special meetings of members may be called by a majority of the directors, or of the supervisory committee, and shall be called by the clerk upon written application of 10 percent of the members entitled to vote.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
