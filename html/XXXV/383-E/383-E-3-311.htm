TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 3
Organization

Section 383-E:3-311

    383-E:3-311 Amendment of Bylaws. – The board of directors of a credit union may, by majority vote, amend the bylaws if the credit union files a notice of the proposed amendment under RSA 383-A:6-601 and it is permitted to do so by the commissioner based on the considerations set forth in RSA 383-E:3-301, as applicable.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
