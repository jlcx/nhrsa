TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 6
Supervisory Committee

Section 383-E:6-602


[RSA 383-E:6-602 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:6-602 Annual Audits. –
    The supervisory committee of every credit union under the supervision of the commissioner shall conduct or cause to be conducted annual audits as is required by RSA 383-A:5-509 and submit reports of condition as is required by RSA 383-A:5-510.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
