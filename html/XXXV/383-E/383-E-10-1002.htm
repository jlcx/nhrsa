TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 10
Combinations

Section 383-E:10-1002


[RSA 383-E:10-1002 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:10-1002 Required Vote. –
    The combination of credit unions is required to be approved by a state credit union first by a majority of its full board of directors and then by a majority of its members present and voting at a meeting called for that purpose.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
