TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 6
Supervisory Committee

Section 383-E:6-604


[RSA 383-E:6-604 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:6-604 Report. –
    The supervisory committee chairman or his designee shall make a full report at the annual meeting, and it shall be filed and preserved with the records of the credit union.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
