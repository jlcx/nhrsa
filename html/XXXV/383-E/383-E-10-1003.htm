TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 10
Combinations

Section 383-E:10-1003


[RSA 383-E:10-1003 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:10-1003 Name Change. –
    The credit union resulting from a combination may change the name of the resulting credit union with the permission of the commissioner.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
