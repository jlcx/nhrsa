TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 6
Supervisory Committee

Section 383-E:6-601


[RSA 383-E:6-601 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:6-601 Supervisory Committee. –
    The supervisory committee shall inspect from time to time the accounts of the credit union and shall keep fully informed of its financial condition, and shall supervise the acts of its board of directors, credit committee, and officers, as may be provided in the bylaws.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
