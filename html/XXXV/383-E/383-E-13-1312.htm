TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 13
Corporate Credit Union

Section 383-E:13-1312


[RSA 383-E:13-1312 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:13-1312 Reserves. –
    A corporate credit union shall be exempt from the regular reserve requirements of RSA 383-E:9-901 but shall be required to establish and maintain an equity reserve to meet losses, in accordance with rules adopted by the commissioner, under RSA 541-A.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
