TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 4
Powers of Credit Unions

Section 383-E:4-415


[RSA 383-E:4-415 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:4-415 Interest on Escrow Accounts. –
    Escrow accounts of credit unions, federal credit unions and foreign credit unions having escrow accounts in this state shall be subject to RSA 383-B:3-303(a)(7)(E).

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
