TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 4
Powers of Credit Unions

Section 383-E:4-413


[RSA 383-E:4-413 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:4-413 Deposit Accounts. –
    Deposits of a credit union shall be subject to Article 4 of RSA 383-B.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
