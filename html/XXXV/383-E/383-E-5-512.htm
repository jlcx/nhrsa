TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 5
Directors and Officers

Section 383-E:5-512


[RSA 383-E:5-512 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:5-512 Order of Payments. –
    The amounts paid on shares or deposited by members who have withdrawn or have been expelled shall be paid to them in the order of withdrawal or expulsion, but only as funds for the payments become available and after deducting any amounts due from the member.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
