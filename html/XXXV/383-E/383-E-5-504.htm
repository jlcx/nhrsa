TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-E
CREDIT UNION ACT

Article 5
Directors and Officers

Section 383-E:5-504


[RSA 383-E:5-504 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-E:5-504 Vacancies. –
    If a director or member of the credit or supervisory committee of any credit union ceases to be a member of the credit union, his office shall thereupon become vacant.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
