TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386
SAVINGS BANKS

Deposits

Section 386:58

    386:58 Demand Deposits. – Notwithstanding any other provision of the law to the contrary, there shall be no limitation upon the acceptance of demand deposits by savings banks or associations.

Source. 1983, 369:14, eff. Jan. 1, 1984.
