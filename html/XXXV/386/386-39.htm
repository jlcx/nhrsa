TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386
SAVINGS BANKS

Custody of Depositors' Books

Section 386:39

    386:39 Retention by Treasurer. – It shall be unlawful for the treasurer of any institution receiving savings deposits or transacting the business of a savings bank to retain in his custody for more than 10 days at a time any savings bank book belonging to a depositor therein, except when held by the bank as collateral security.

Source. 1905, 56:1. PL 261:60. RL 309:39.
