TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386
SAVINGS BANKS

Trustees and Officers

Section 386:6

    386:6 Compensation. – Savings banks may pay their presidents, trustees, and treasurers reasonable compensation for services rendered.

Source. 1869, 4:4. 1873, 35:1. GL 170:5. 1881, 103:6. PS 165:4. PL 261:6. RL 309:4.
