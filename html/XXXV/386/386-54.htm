TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386
SAVINGS BANKS

Dividends on Such Special Accounts

Section 386:54

    386:54 Savings Accounts Without Passbooks. – It shall be lawful for all savings banks and savings departments subject to this chapter to accept deposit accounts which are to be appropriately evidenced by a document other than a passbook or bank book. The provisions of RSA 386:21 and RSA 386:39 shall apply to such other evidence of deposit account.

Source. 1961, 248:10, eff. Aug. 30, 1961.
