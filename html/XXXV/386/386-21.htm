TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386
SAVINGS BANKS

Verification of Deposit Books

Section 386:21

    386:21 Verification. – Every trust company, savings bank, cooperative bank and building and loan association shall, at its own expense, to such extent and in such manner as the commissioner shall prescribe, cause to be conducted a verification of individual savings deposit accounts or due books of shareholders with the books of the company, bank or association.

Source. PS 165:23. 1895, 105:7. 1899, 72:1. PL 261:30. 1931, 96:4. RL 309:22. RSA 386:21. 1957, 258:2. 1959, 245:1. 1965, 113:1. 1967, 8:1, eff. April 21, 1967.
