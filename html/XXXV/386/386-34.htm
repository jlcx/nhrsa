TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386
SAVINGS BANKS

Sale of Checks or Drafts

Section 386:34

    386:34 Sale of Checks. – Savings banks, building and loan associations or cooperative banks may, under regulations made by the commissioner, sell negotiable checks and drafts issued by it or drawn by or on it and payable by or through a trust company or a national banking association.

Source. 1953, 146:1, eff. May 8, 1953.
