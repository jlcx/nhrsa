TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386
SAVINGS BANKS

Guaranty Savings Banks

Section 386:12

    386:12 Capital Stock. – Until the general deposits of a guaranty savings bank or a bank conducted as a guaranty savings bank shall exceed $1,000,000, its capital stock shall at all times be equal to 10 percent of its general deposits. After the general deposits exceed $1,000,000, said banks shall carry each year to a guaranty fund so much of their net earnings as shall maintain an unimpaired guaranty fund of 5 percent for all deposits in excess of $1,000,000.

Source. 1921, 71:1. PL 261:21. RL 309:13. RSA 386:12. 1975, 86:1, eff. June 14, 1975.
