TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386
SAVINGS BANKS

Deposits and Withdrawals

Section 386:20

    386:20 Inapplicability of Deposit Limits. – No limitation of the amount of deposits contained in the charter of any savings bank or trust company shall apply to trust funds, deposits for the creation of sinking funds, or deposits of the state, counties, towns, or village districts.

Source. 1907, 123:1. PL 261:29. RL 309:21.
