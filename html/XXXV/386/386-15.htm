TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386
SAVINGS BANKS

Guaranty Savings Banks

Section 386:15

    386:15 Distribution. – The amount of the increase or addition to the capital stock may be subscribed for and taken by the stockholders in proportion to their shares, or by other parties in case of the failure of said stockholders to take and pay for such increase or addition within 90 days.

Source. 1895, 92:1. PL 261:24. RL 309:16. RSA 386:15. 1975, 86:1, eff. June 14, 1975.
