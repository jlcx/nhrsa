TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-A
POWERS OF CORRESPONDING FEDERALLY-CHARTERED FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS

Section 394-A:6


[RSA 394-A:6 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXVIII, effective October 1, 2015.]
    394-A:6 Trust Companies and Commercial Banks. –
    Subject to the provisions of RSA 394-A:7, any trust company or commercial bank shall have and may exercise any power, right, benefit or privilege, now or hereafter authorized for national banks by federal legislation, regulation or ruling.

Source. 1981, 204:1, eff. Aug. 4, 1981.
