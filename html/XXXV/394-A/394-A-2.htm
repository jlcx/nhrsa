TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-A
POWERS OF CORRESPONDING FEDERALLY-CHARTERED FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS

Section 394-A:2

    394-A:2 Definition. – As used in this chapter, the term "commissioner'' shall mean the bank commissioner.

Source. 1981, 204:1, eff. Aug. 4, 1981.
