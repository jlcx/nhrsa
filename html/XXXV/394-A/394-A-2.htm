TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-A
POWERS OF CORRESPONDING FEDERALLY-CHARTERED FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS

Section 394-A:2


[RSA 394-A:2 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXVIII, effective October 1, 2015.]
    394-A:2 Definition. –
    As used in this chapter, the term "commissioner'' shall mean the bank commissioner.

Source. 1981, 204:1, eff. Aug. 4, 1981.
