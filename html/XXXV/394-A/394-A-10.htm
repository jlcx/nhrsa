TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-A
POWERS OF CORRESPONDING FEDERALLY-CHARTERED FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS

Section 394-A:10

    394-A:10 Charter Conversions. – As part of the authority granted under this chapter, the commissioner shall adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, permitting the conversion of the charter of any bank or association into any other type of charter available under state law for a bank, association or credit union, including a conversion of a charter from mutual to stock form.

Source. 1983, 369:16. 1985, 55:17. 1994, 315:3. 1995, 167:1, eff. June 1, 1995.
