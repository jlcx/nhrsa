TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 388
CONSOLIDATION OF BANKS

Consolidation by Contract

Section 388:15

    388:15 Subrogation on Dissolution. – Any decree or legislative act of dissolution of any contracting corporation shall be construed to subrogate the commissioner to its rights against any other contracting institution to the extent that the contract for union shall be enforceable after such dissolution as well as before.

Source. RL 311:20. 1947, 123:13, eff. April 30, 1947.
