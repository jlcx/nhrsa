TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 388
CONSOLIDATION OF BANKS

Consolidation by Decree of Court

Section 388:4

    388:4 Report. – The commissioner shall forthwith make a report to the court of his findings and determinations, and of the expense of said hearings, and findings, which expenses shall be paid by the petitioners. Upon due notice to all parties of record the court shall thereupon enter a final decree.

Source. 1917, 54:5. PL 263:6. RL 311:6. 1947, 123:2, eff. April 30, 1947.
