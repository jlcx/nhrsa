TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-A
BANK AND CREDIT UNION REGULATORY AND ENFORCEMENT

Article 6
Regulatory Approval Procedures

Section 383-A:6-607


[RSA 383-A:6-607 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-A:6-607 Time Limits. –
    If the time limits prescribed by this article conflict with specific time limits provided for by other provisions of RSA 383-A, RSA 383-B, RSA 383-C, RSA 383-D, or RSA 383-E, then the specific time limits provided for by the other provisions shall control.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
