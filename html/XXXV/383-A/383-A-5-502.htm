TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-A
BANK AND CREDIT UNION REGULATORY AND ENFORCEMENT

Article 5
Management and Operations

Section 383-A:5-502


[RSA 383-A:5-502 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-A:5-502 Board of Directors. –
    Each state bank and credit union shall have a board of directors elected or appointed in accordance with its organizational documents. No director shall serve on a board until he or she accepts his or her election or appointment in writing, which acceptance shall be maintained in the records of the state bank or credit union.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
