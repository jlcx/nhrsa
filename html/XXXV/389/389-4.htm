TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 389
CONVERSION BETWEEN STATE AND NATIONAL BANKS

Section 389:4


[RSA 389:4 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    389:4 Application for State Charter. –
    The converting national bank may apply for such certificate of incorporation by filing with the commissioner an application signed under oath by its president and cashier and by a majority of its entire board of directors setting forth:
    I. The corporate action taken in compliance with the provisions of the laws of the United States governing the conversion of the national to a state bank.
    II. The articles of association or agreement, approved by the stockholders, for the government of the bank as a state bank.

Source. 1951, 182:1, par. 24, eff. July 18, 1951. 2013, 56:23, eff. July 4, 2013.
