TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 389
CONVERSION BETWEEN STATE AND NATIONAL BANKS

Section 389:5


[RSA 389:5 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    389:5 Approval of Application. –
    The commissioner upon being satisfied that such application conforms to law shall indorse his or her approval thereon and thereupon said application shall be filed in the office of the secretary of state, who, upon payment of a fee equal to the fee charged by the secretary of state to business corporations under RSA 293-A, shall cause the same, with the approval indorsed thereon, to be recorded and shall issue a certificate of incorporation as prescribed in RSA 392:18.

Source. 1951, 182:1, par. 25. 2005, 124:1, eff. Aug. 14, 2005. 2013, 56:24, eff. July 4, 2013.
