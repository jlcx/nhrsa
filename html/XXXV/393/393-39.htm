TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Paid-Up and Investment Shares

Section 393:39


[RSA 393:39 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXVI, effective October 1, 2015.]
    393:39 Investment Shares. –
    Associations may issue investment shares which may be withdrawn or retired under the same conditions as provided for paid-up shares by RSA 393:37 and 38.

Source. 1925, 12:1, 2. PL 266:25. 1935, 82:2. 1937, 106:5. 1939, 72:7. 1941, 25:3. RL 314:35. 1943, 7:3. 1949, 106:9. 1953, 166:2. RSA 393:39. 1955, 139:3. 1965, 317:7, eff. Sept. 6, 1965.
