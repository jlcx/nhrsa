TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Dues, Fines, Loans, Investments, Etc.

Section 393:18-b

    393:18-b Loans on Buildings Situated on Leased or Rented Land. – Notwithstanding any other provisions of law to the contrary, cooperative banks, building and loan associations, savings and loan associations and any other lending institutions may loan on manufactured housing as defined in RSA 384:16-d, II, or buildings or units of the buildings, as defined in RSA 479-A, which are situated on leased or rented land.

Source. 1973, 250:1. 1981, 270:2, eff. Aug. 15, 1981.
