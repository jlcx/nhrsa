TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Dues, Fines, Loans, Investments, Etc.

Section 393:14


[RSA 393:14 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXVI, effective October 1, 2015.]
    393:14 Dues. –
    Any such corporation may collect of its installment shareholders monthly dues of $1 upon each share held by them, until the ultimate value of shares reaches an amount determined by the board of directors under RSA 393:7, or until they are withdrawn, cancelled, or forfeited.

Source. 1887, 93:2, 3, 7. PS 166:8. PL 266:8. 1939, 72:2. RL 314:9. RSA 393:14. 1967, 127:2, eff. July 18, 1967.
