TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Dues, Fines, Loans, Investments, Etc.

Section 393:22

    393:22 Borrowing. – It may, without consent of the commissioner, borrow not in excess of 15 percent of its share capital and may pledge as collateral for such borrowings real estate mortgages, notes and other securities. It may with the consent of the commissioner, borrow in excess of 15 percent of its share capital, and with his approval may pledge as collateral, real estate mortgages, notes, and other securities.

Source. 1917, 87:1. 1919, 11:1. PL 266:13. 1933, 26:1. 1939, 72:3. RL 314:19. 1947, 24:6. 1953, 178:1. RSA 393:22. 1961, 136:5. 1963, 313:3. 1967, 85:1, eff. June 26, 1967.
