TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 393:43


[RSA 393:43 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXVI, effective October 1, 2015.]
    393:43 Books. –
    The commissioner shall have power to prescribe the form of books to be used by all building and loan associations.

Source. 1891, 50:2. PS 166:18. PL 266:31. RL 314:39.
