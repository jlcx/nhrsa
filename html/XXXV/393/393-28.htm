TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Surplus and Division of Profits

Section 393:28

    393:28 Surplus. – A building and loan association by vote of its directors may set aside from time to time a part of its net earnings for the creation of a surplus for the protection of its shareholders.

Source. 1921, 35:2. PL 266:15. RL 314:24. 1943, 7:2, eff. Feb. 9, 1943.
