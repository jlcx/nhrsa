TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Conversion

Section 393:52

    393:52 Applicability of Statute to Converted Association. – The provisions of this chapter shall, so far as applicable, apply to such conversion.

Source. 1949, 168:1, par. 48, eff. April 28, 1949.
