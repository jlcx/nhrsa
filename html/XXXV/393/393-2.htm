TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Organization and Stock

Section 393:2

    393:2 Articles of Agreement. – The articles of agreement shall set forth the name of the corporation, the object for which it is established, and the place in which its business is to be carried on, and shall be signed by the parties who unite to form the corporation. They shall affix to their signatures their post office addresses.

Source. PS 166:2. PL 266:2. RL 314:2.
