TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Paid-Up and Investment Shares

Section 393:38

    393:38 Retirement of Paid-up Shares. – Paid-up shares may be paid off at any time at the option of the board of directors.

Source. 1917, 87:3. PL 266:24. 1939, 72:6. RL 314:34. 1949, 106:8, eff. March 24, 1949.
