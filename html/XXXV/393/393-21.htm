TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Dues, Fines, Loans, Investments, Etc.

Section 393:21

    393:21 Lien on Shares. – It shall have a lien upon the shares of delinquent shareholders for all sums due to it from them, and may dispose of the shares to enforce the lien, as provided in the bylaws.

Source. 1887, 93:3. PS 166:9. PL 266:12. RL 314:18.
