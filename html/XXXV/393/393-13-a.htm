TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Organization and Stock

Section 393:13-a

    393:13-a Rulemaking. – The bank commissioner shall adopt rules, under RSA 541-A, relative to:
    I. The requirements of RSA 393:15-a regarding unsecured loans.
    II. The books which the commissioner is authorized to prescribe under RSA 393:43.
    III. The procedure for conversions under RSA 393:53.
    IV. Mergers and consolidations under RSA 393:55.

Source. 1985, 55:15, eff. April 23, 1985.
