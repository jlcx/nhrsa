TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Organization and Stock

Section 393:11

    393:11 Voting. – Each shareholder shall be entitled to give one vote upon any proposition brought before a meeting of shareholders, and no more; and no shareholder shall vote by proxy. A borrower who is not a shareholder shall be a member and shall be entitled to vote in the same manner as a shareholder.

Source. 1887, 93:6. PS 166:7. PL 266:7. 1941, 25:2. RL 314:7.
