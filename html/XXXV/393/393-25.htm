TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Dues, Fines, Loans, Investments, Etc.

Section 393:25

    393:25 Membership in, and Investment in Securities of, Home Loan Bank. – It may become a member of the Federal Home Loan Bank of the New England district, and may purchase capital stock of that bank, and may invest in the bonds, debentures, or other similar obligations issued by any federal home loan bank.

Source. 1933, 26:2. RL 314:21.
