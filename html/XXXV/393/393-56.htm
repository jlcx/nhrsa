TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Insurance of Accounts, Act as Agent

Section 393:56

    393:56 Insurance of Accounts With the Savings Association Insurance Fund. – Any building and loan association or cooperative bank is empowered to insure its accounts with the Savings Association Insurance Fund.

Source. 1961, 136:9. 1990, 115:1, eff. April 13, 1990.
