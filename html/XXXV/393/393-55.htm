TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Merger

Section 393:55


[RSA 393:55 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXVI, effective October 1, 2015.]
    393:55 Power to Merge or Consolidate. –
    Cooperative banks, savings and loan associations, and building and loan associations organized under the laws of this state may merge or consolidate in accordance with the procedures and requirements set forth in RSA 388.

Source. 1949, 168:1, par. 51. RSA 393:55. 1969, 343:1. 1997, 194:7, eff. June 18, 1997.
