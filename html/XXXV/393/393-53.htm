TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Conversion

Section 393:53

    393:53 Rulemaking. – The bank commissioner shall adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, relative to the procedure to be followed by any such federal savings and loan association converting into a state building and loan association or cooperative bank under this subdivision.

Source. 1949, 169:1, par. 49. 1985, 55:16, eff. April 23, 1985.
