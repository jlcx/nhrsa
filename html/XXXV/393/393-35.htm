TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Paid-Up and Investment Shares

Section 393:35


[RSA 393:35 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXVI, effective October 1, 2015.]
    393:35 Issue. –
    Associations may issue paid-up shares in denominations of $100 or multiples thereof.

Source. 1917, 87:2. 1925, 14:1. PL 266:21. 1939, 72:4. RL 314:31. 1949, 106:5. RSA 393:35. 1965, 317:6. 1967, 127:4, eff. July 18, 1967.
