TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393
BUILDING AND LOAN ASSOCIATIONS

Organization and Stock

Section 393:8-a


[RSA 393:8-a repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXVI, effective October 1, 2015.]
    393:8-a Savings Accounts Without Passbooks. –
    A cooperative bank, savings and loan association or building and loan association may accept savings accounts which are appropriately evidenced by a document other than a passbook or a bank book. The provisions of RSA 384:33-35 and RSA 386:21 and 39 shall apply to such document evidencing a savings account.

Source. 1975, 203:1, eff. Aug. 1, 1975.
