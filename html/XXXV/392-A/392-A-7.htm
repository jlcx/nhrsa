TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392-A
MERCHANT BANKING

Section 392-A:7


[RSA 392-A:7 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIV, effective October 1, 2015.]
    392-A:7 Rules. –
    The bank commissioner may adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, governing the activities of merchant banks and implementing this chapter.

Source. 1998, 284:2, eff. Aug. 25, 1998.
