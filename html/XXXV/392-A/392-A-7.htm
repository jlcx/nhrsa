TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392-A
MERCHANT BANKING

Section 392-A:7

    392-A:7 Rules. – The bank commissioner may adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, governing the activities of merchant banks and implementing this chapter.

Source. 1998, 284:2, eff. Aug. 25, 1998.
