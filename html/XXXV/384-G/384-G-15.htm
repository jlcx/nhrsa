TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384-G
REGULATION OF REVOLVING CREDIT PLANS

Section 384-G:15

    384-G:15 Rulemaking. – Pursuant to RSA 541-A, the bank commissioner may adopt such rules as the bank commissioner deems necessary to the administration and enforcement of this chapter. Such rules shall be consistent with the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 2002, 225:1, eff. July 16, 2002.
