TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Organization

Section 392:10

    392:10 First Meeting. – The first meeting of the organizers shall be called by a notice signed either by that organizer who is designated in the organizational instrument for the purpose, or by a majority of organizers, and such notice shall state the time, place and purpose of the meeting.

Source. 1915, 109:5. PL 265:10. RL 313:10. 2006, 320:20, eff. Aug. 19, 2006.
