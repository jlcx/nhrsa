TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Organization

Section 392:17

    392:17 Record of Organizational Instrument. – Within 90 days after a favorable decision pursuant to RSA 392:8, petitioners shall file with the secretary of state the organizational instrument. The secretary of state, upon payment of a fee equal to the fee charged by the secretary of state to business corporations under RSA 293-A if the trust company is a corporation or the fee charged to limited liability companies under RSA 304-C if the trust company is a limited liability company, shall cause the same, with the indorsement thereon, to be recorded.

Source. 1915, 109:6. PL 265:17. RL 313:17. 2004, 231:13, eff. June 11, 2004. 2006, 320:26, eff. Aug. 19, 2006.
