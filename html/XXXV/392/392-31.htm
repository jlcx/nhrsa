TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Officers

Section 392:31

    392:31 Treasurer. – The treasurer shall be elected by the board of directors or managers and shall hold office during their pleasure.

Source. 1915, 109:12. PL 265:29. 1937, 103:3. RL 313:29. 2006, 320:38, eff. Aug. 19, 2006.
