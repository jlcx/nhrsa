TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Organization

Section 392:19

    392:19 Certificate or Record as Evidence. – The secretary of state shall also cause a record of the certificate of organization to be made, and such certificate, or such record or a certified copy thereof, shall be conclusive evidence of the existence of such trust company.

Source. 1915, 109:6. PL 265:19. RL 313:19. 2006, 320:28, eff. Aug. 19, 2006.
