TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Organization

Section 392:20

    392:20 When Organized; Beginning Business. – The existence of such trust company shall begin upon the filing of the organizational instrument with the secretary of state. Any trust company organized under this chapter shall begin business within 2 years from the date of its organization; otherwise its charter shall be void, unless the board of trust company incorporation, for good cause shown, shall grant one extension for not more than one year.

Source. 1915, 109:6, 7. PL 265:20. RL 313:20. RSA 392:20. 1975, 54:4. 1995, 283:1, eff. Aug. 20, 1995. 2006, 320:29, eff. Aug. 19, 2006.
