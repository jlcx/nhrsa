TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Issue of Stock

Section 392:24

    392:24 Unauthorized Acts. – The transaction of a banking or trust business by such trust company prior to the issuance of a certificate of authority to engage in business issued by the commissioner as required by RSA 392:23 shall make the organization void; and in such case the organizers shall be liable as partners for the contracts, debts, and engagements of the company.

Source. 1915, 109:7. PL 265:24. RL 313:24. 2006, 320:32, eff. Aug. 19, 2006.
