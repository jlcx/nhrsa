TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Organization

Section 392:8


[RSA 392:8 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIII, effective October 1, 2015.]
    392:8 Decision. –
    I. In deciding whether or not to grant the petition, the commissioner shall consider the factors set forth in RSA 392:5, II. Upon reaching its decision, the commissioner shall make a record thereof. If the petition is denied, it shall be dismissed and no new petition concerning the same company may be filed within one year thereafter.
    II. If in any case the commissioner shall be of the opinion that the petition does not satisfy the factors in RSA 392:5, II by the exercise proposed by the trust company of all the powers and privileges which are included in the petition, but that the same would be satisfied by the exercise of a part thereof, he or she shall so notify the petitioners; and in such case the petitioners may have leave to withdraw, and may at once file another petition setting forth new organizational documents, upon which the same procedure shall be had as upon the original petition.

Source. 1915, 109:4. PL 265:8. RL 313:8. RSA 392:8. 1969, 408:1. 1998, 139:22, V, eff. Aug. 8, 1998. 2006, 320:17, eff. Aug. 19, 2006. 2013, 56:30, eff. July 4, 2013.
