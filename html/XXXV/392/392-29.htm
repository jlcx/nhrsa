TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Amended Powers

Section 392:29


[RSA 392:29 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIII, effective October 1, 2015.]
    392:29 Fees for Recording. –
    The fee for recording with the secretary of state any amended certificate, which does not embody an increase of the authorized capital stock, shall be $35.

Source. 1947, 157:3. 2004, 231:18, eff. July 1, 2004 at 12:01 a.m.
