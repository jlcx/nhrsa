TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Voluntary Dissolution of Nondepository Trust Companies

Section 392:43

    392:43 Voluntary Dissolution of Nondepository Trust Company. – A trust company organized as a nondepository institution under the provisions of RSA 392 or under the provisions of RSA 392-A may voluntarily dissolve in the manner provided in this chapter. Such dissolution may be accomplished by the liquidation of the trust company or by reorganizing the trust company into a domestic or foreign corporation, limited liability company, limited partnership, or limited liability partnership that does not have banking or trust powers, and in both instances surrendering its trust company charter to the commissioner. A trust company that reorganizes into any other entity pursuant to this section shall not engage in any activity that is authorized only for a bank or a trust company.

Source. 2003, 65:1, eff. July 26, 2003. 2013, 56:39, eff. July 4, 2013.
