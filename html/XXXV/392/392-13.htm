TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Organization

Section 392:13


[RSA 392:13 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIII, effective October 1, 2015.]
    392:13 Organizers' Powers. –
    The organizers of a trust company in organization shall hold the franchise until the organization has been completed.

Source. 1915, 109:5. PL 265:13. RL 313:13. 2006, 320:22, eff. Aug. 19, 2006.
