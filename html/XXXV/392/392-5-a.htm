TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Organization

Section 392:5-a

    392:5-a Investigations. – For the purpose of any investigation under this chapter, the board or the commissioner shall have the power to subpoena witnesses and administer oaths in any adjudicative proceedings, and to compel, by subpoena duces tecum, the production of all books, records, files, and other documents and materials relevant to its investigation.

Source. 2003, 166:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2004. 2006, 320:13, eff. Aug. 19, 2006.
