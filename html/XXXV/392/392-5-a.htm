TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Organization

Section 392:5-a


[RSA 392:5-a repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIII, effective October 1, 2015.]
    392:5-a Investigations. –
    For the purpose of any investigation under this chapter, the commissioner shall have the power to subpoena witnesses and administer oaths in any adjudicative proceedings, and to compel, by subpoena duces tecum, the production of all books, records, files, and other documents and materials relevant to his or her investigation.

Source. 2003, 166:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2004. 2006, 320:13, eff. Aug. 19, 2006. 2013, 56:28, eff. July 4, 2013.
