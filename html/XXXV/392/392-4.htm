TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Organization

Section 392:4

    392:4 Signing Organizational Instrument. – Each organizer shall subscribe to the organizational instrument the organizer's name, business post office address, and the number of shares of stock or interests which the organizer agrees to take.

Source. 1915, 109:3. PL 265:4. RL 313:4. 2006, 320:11, eff. Aug. 19, 2006.
