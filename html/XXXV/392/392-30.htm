TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF TRUST COMPANIES

Officers

Section 392:30

    392:30 Election. – The management of a trust company shall include a president, a clerk or secretary, a board of not less than 5 directors or managers, a treasurer, and such other officers as may be prescribed in its organizational instrument or other organizational documents and the laws of the state. Such officers, except the treasurer, shall be chosen annually.

Source. 1915, 109:11. PL 265:28. RL 313:28. 2006, 320:37, eff. Aug. 19, 2006.
