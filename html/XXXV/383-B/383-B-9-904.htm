TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-B
DEPOSITORY BANK ACT

Article 9
Combinations and Acquisitions

Section 383-B:9-904


[RSA 383-B:9-904 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-B:9-904 Name Change. –
    The bank resulting from a combination may change the name of the resulting bank. In such event, it shall comply with the requirements of RSA 383-A:3-316, if applicable.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
