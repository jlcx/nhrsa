TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-B
DEPOSITORY BANK ACT

Article 1
General Provisions

Section 383-B:1-103


[RSA 383-B:1-103 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-B:1-103 Reservation of Power to Amend or Repeal. –
    The general court has power to amend or repeal all or part of this chapter at any time and all persons subject to this chapter are governed by the amendment or repeal.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
