TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Trustees, Directors, and Officers

Section 384:5

    384:5 Election; Oath. –
    I. Officers, directors, trustees, and managers in the case of a limited liability company shall be elected or appointed as frequently as the charter or bylaws of the bank or savings association may require, but not less than once every 3 years.
    II. All officers, directors, trustees, and managers in the case of a limited liability company upon election or appointment, shall be sworn to the faithful discharge of their duties.

Source. 1895, 105:1. PL 260:5. RL 308:5. RSA 384:5. 1961, 248:5. 1998, 139:4, eff. Aug. 7, 1998. 2013, 238:5, eff. Sept. 13, 2013.
