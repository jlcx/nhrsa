TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Assignments of Savings Deposits

Section 384:40

    384:40 Delivery of Passbook or Certificate of Deposit. – No assignment of a savings deposit which is evidenced by a passbook or certificate of deposit shall be a valid transfer of the savings deposit unless the passbook or certificate of deposit is delivered to the assignee accompanied by a written assignment or order for transfer.

Source. 1977, 360:2, eff. Aug. 30, 1977.
