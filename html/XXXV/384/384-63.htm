TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Interstate Bank Acquisitions and Branching

Section 384:63

    384:63 Monopolies Prohibited. – Nothing in this subdivision or in RSA 384-B:3 shall create or enlarge any exemption for the activities of or arrangements between any persons, banks, associations, bank holding companies, or association holding companies, except as provided by RSA 356:8-a.

Source. 1995, 304:1, eff. Sept. 29, 1995.
