TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Prohibitions and Penalties

Section 384:19

    384:19 Penalty. – If any officer or employee of such institution shall violate the provisions of the preceding section he shall be guilty of a class A felony.

Source. 1885, 83:2. PS 165:31. PL 260:19. RL 308:20. RSA 384:19. 1973, 528:266, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
