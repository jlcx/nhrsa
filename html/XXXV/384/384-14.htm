TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Loans, Investments, and Borrowing

Section 384:14

    384:14 Prohibited Collateral. – A New Hampshire-chartered bank, trust company, guaranty savings bank, or stock cooperative bank shall not accept its own capital stock or the capital stock of a bank holding company which controls such bank, trust company, guaranty savings bank, or stock cooperative bank as collateral.

Source. 1895, 105:9. PL 260:13. RL 308:14. RSA 384:14. 1994, 250:3, eff. June 2, 1994.
