TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Prejudgment Attachments Prohibited

Section 384:56

    384:56 Prejudgment Attachments Prohibited. – No attachment shall be issued against a bank, as defined in RSA 384-B:1, I, or its property before final judgment in any suit, action or proceeding is rendered.

Source. 1991, 251:8, eff. June 10, 1991.
