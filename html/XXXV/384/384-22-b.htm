TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Prohibitions and Penalties

Section 384:22-b

    384:22-b Penalty. – Any person convicted of a violation of RSA 384:22-a shall be, if a natural person, guilty of a misdemeanor, and any other person shall be guilty of a felony.

Source. 1931, 99:2. RL 442:39. RSA 572:48. 1973, 532:13, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
