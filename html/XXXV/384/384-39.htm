TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Powers of Attorney on Bank Accounts

Section 384:39

    384:39 Recognition and Termination. – Any bank specified in RSA 384:29 doing business in this state may continue to recognize the power of an attorney-in-fact authorized in writing to make withdrawals either in whole or in part from the account of a depositor, whether minor or adult, until it receives written notice or is on actual notice of the revocation of his authority. No such bank shall be liable for damages, penalty or tax by reason of any payment made pursuant to this section.

Source. 1969, 382:1. 1977, 453:2, eff. Sept. 10, 1977.
