TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Cease and Desist Orders

Section 384:12-b


[RSA 384:12-b repealed by 2015, 272:52, IX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384:12-b Temporary Cease and Desist Order. –
    Whenever, in the opinion of the commissioner, the violation or practice set forth in RSA 384:12-a, I requires immediate action for the protection of depositors, or where such violation or practice, or the continuation thereof, is likely to cause insolvency or substantial dissipation of the assets, capital or earnings of the institution, the commissioner may issue orders pursuant to RSA 384:12-a, I which shall become effective upon service thereof, without prior notice or hearing.

Source. 1985, 221:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1986.
