TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Fiscal Agent of United States

Section 384:38

    384:38 Authority to Act. – State banks, savings banks or institutions for savings, trust companies, banking companies, and all similar corporations, cooperative banks, building and loan associations, and credit unions are authorized to act as fiscal agent of the United States. When designated as fiscal agent by the secretary of the treasury, they shall perform, under such regulations as he may prescribe, all reasonable duties as fiscal agent of the United States as he may require, and they are authorized to act as agent for any instrumentality of the United States, as an agent of this state, or any instrumentality of this state.

Source. 1965, 37:1, eff. May 31, 1965.
