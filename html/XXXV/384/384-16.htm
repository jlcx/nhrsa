TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Loans, Investments, and Borrowing

Section 384:16


[RSA 384:16 repealed by 2015, 272:52, IX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384:16 Borrowing. –
    No savings bank, state bank, or trust company shall hire money or give the note of such institution except by vote of the trustees or directors thereof, duly recorded; and all such notes shall be signed by at least 2 officers designated in said vote. For the purpose of securing such loan or loans, said bank or company may pledge as security therefor real estate mortgages, notes, stocks, or other securities.

Source. 1895, 105:11. PL 260:15. 1933, 27:1. RL 308:16. RSA 384:16. 1967, 109:1. 1983, 369:1. 1985, 349:1, eff. Aug. 13, 1985.
