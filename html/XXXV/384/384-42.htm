TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Assignments of Savings Deposits

Section 384:42

    384:42 Application. – The provisions of this subdivision shall apply to savings deposits maintained in any institution described in RSA 384:1, or in any federally-chartered institution of like nature having a place of business in this state.

Source. 1977, 360:2, eff. Aug. 30, 1977.
