TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Interstate Bank Acquisitions and Branching

Section 384:62


[RSA 384:62 repealed by 2015, 272:52, IX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384:62 Insurance Not Affected. –
    This subdivision shall not be interpreted to limit or to enlarge the power of any state-chartered or federally-chartered financial institution to engage in the business of insurance or in any activity requiring licensing under RSA 401 or RSA 405.

Source. 1995, 304:1, eff. Sept. 29, 1995.
