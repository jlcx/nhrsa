TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Trustees, Directors, and Officers

Section 384:12

    384:12 Irregularities. – A clerk neglecting to give such notification to the bank commissioner or giving a false notification or knowingly giving or causing to be given a notice containing the name of a person who has not taken the oath of office shall be fined $100.

Source. 1895, 105:4. PL 260:11. RL 308:12. RSA 384:12. 1997, 194:9, eff. June 18, 1997.
