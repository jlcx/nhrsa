TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Distribution of Joint Accounts

Section 384:32

    384:32 Effect on Other Legislation. – This subdivision shall in no way affect the provisions of RSA 386:19 relative to savings banks and RSA 393:12 relative to building and loan associations.

Source. 1953, 162:5. 2002, 232:4, eff. Jan. 1, 2003.
