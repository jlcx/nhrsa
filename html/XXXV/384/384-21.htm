TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Prohibitions and Penalties

Section 384:21


[RSA 384:21 repealed by 2015, 272:52, IX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384:21 Report of Irregularities. –
    If in the opinion of the bank commissioner any such institution or its directors, trustees, or officers have persistently violated any provision of law, the commissioner shall forthwith report the same, with such remarks as the commissioner deems expedient, to the attorney general, who shall immediately institute a prosecution therefor in behalf of the state.

Source. PS 165:33. PL 260:21. RL 308:22. RSA 384:21. 1994, 250:5, eff. June 2, 1994.
