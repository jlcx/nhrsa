TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Prohibitions and Penalties

Section 384:22-a


[RSA 384:22-a repealed by 2015, 272:52, IX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384:22-a False Rumors Concerning Banks. –
    It shall be unlawful for any person to wilfully and maliciously make or transmit to another any false statement concerning the financial condition of any bank or other institution under the supervision of the bank commissioner or to aid, procure, or induce another so to do.

Source. 1931, 99:1. RL 442:38. RSA 572:47. 1973, 532:13, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
