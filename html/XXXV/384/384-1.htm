TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Section 384:1


[RSA 384:1 repealed by 2015, 272:52, IX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384:1 Application of Title. –
    The provisions of this title, so far as they properly may, shall apply to state banks, savings banks, or institutions for savings, trust companies, banking companies and all similar corporations, building and loan associations, and credit unions, unless otherwise limited in their operation.

Source. GS 152:1. GL 166:1. PS 162:1. PL 260:1. RL 308:1. RSA 384:1. 1961, 245:6; 248:1. 1967, 416:16, eff. Sept. 5, 1967.
