TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Lost Passbooks and Certificates

Section 384:37

    384:37 Penalty. – Any person who wilfully violates the provisions of RSA 384:36 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor if a natural person, or guilty of a felony if any other person.

Source. 1963, 145:1. 1973, 529:92, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
