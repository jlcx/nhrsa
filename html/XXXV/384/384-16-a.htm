TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Loans, Investments, and Borrowing

Section 384:16-a


[RSA 384:16-a repealed by 2015, 272:52, IX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384:16-a Real Estate. –
    Any institution described in RSA 384:1 may hold and lease real estate acquired in payment of a preexisting debt owed to the institution, by foreclosure of mortgage or otherwise.

Source. 1901, 114:2. PL 262:18. 1939, 170:5. RL 310:18. 1953, 195:1, par. 20. RSA 387:20. 1961, 248:3. 1965, 114:1, eff. July 27, 1965.
