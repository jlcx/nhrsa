TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Distribution of Joint Accounts

Section 384:30


[RSA 384:30 repealed by 2015, 272:52, IX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384:30 Application of Subdivision. –
    This subdivision shall apply to all types of accounts in the above-named institutions, whether such account is represented by a certificate, an account book, or by any other form of document, as well as to checking accounts.

Source. 1953, 162:3, eff. May 14, 1953.
