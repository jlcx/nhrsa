TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Prohibitions and Penalties

Section 384:17

    384:17 False Statements. – Any trustee or director of any institution under the supervision of the bank commissioner, who shall make a statement which purports to be the condition of an institution examined by him, without having fully and carefully examined into such condition, or who shall make a false statement thereof with an intent to deceive, shall be guilty of a class B felony.

Source. 1895, 105:5. PL 260:17. RL 308:18. RSA 384:17. 1973, 528:265, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
