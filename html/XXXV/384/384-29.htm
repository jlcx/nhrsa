TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Distribution of Joint Accounts

Section 384:29

    384:29 Scope of Provision. – The term bank in RSA 384:28 shall apply to state banks, mutual savings banks, guaranty savings banks, national banking associations, building and loan or co-operative banks, Morris Plan banks, trust companies, federal savings and loan associations and credit unions.

Source. 1953, 162:2, eff. May 14, 1953.
