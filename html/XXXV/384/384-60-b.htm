TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Interstate Bank Acquisitions and Branching

Section 384:60-b


[RSA 384:60-b repealed by 2015, 272:52, IX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384:60-b Examination of Out-of-State Branches of New Hampshire Banks. –
    The bank commissioner shall be authorized to conduct or to enter into agreements with federal bank regulatory agencies or bank regulatory agencies of other states to examine out-of-state offices of New Hampshire banks or New Hampshire bank holding companies. The cost of such examinations shall be chargeable and paid in accordance with the provisions of RSA 384:60-a and RSA 383:11, I. The provisions of RSA 384:60-a shall apply to department examination personnel employed in making such examinations.

Source. 1997, 187:1, eff. June 18, 1997.
