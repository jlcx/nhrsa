TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO SAVINGS BANKS, TRUST COMPANIES, AND DEPOSITORIES

Payment of Capital

Section 384:23


[RSA 384:23 repealed by 2015, 272:52, IX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384:23 Requirement. –
    No guaranty savings bank, trust company, or other similar corporation shall commence the transaction of business until it has satisfied the commissioner that its capital stock has been paid in in accordance with the provisions of its charter.

Source. 1889, 55:2. PS 162:11. PL 260:23. RL 308:24.
