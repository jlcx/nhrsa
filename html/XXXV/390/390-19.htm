TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 390
REGULATION OF TRUST AND BANKING COMPANIES

Fiduciary Powers of Trust Companies and National Banks

Section 390:19

    390:19 Penalty. – Whoever violates any provision of this subdivision shall be guilty of a misdemeanor if a natural person, or guilty of a felony if any other person.

Source. 1919, 121:4. PL 264:18. RL 312:19. RSA 390:19. 1973, 528:268, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
