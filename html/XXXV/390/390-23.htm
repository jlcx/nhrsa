TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 390
REGULATION OF TRUST AND BANKING COMPANIES

Final Adjustment of Statements of Account Between Bank and Depositor

Section 390:23


[RSA 390:23 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXII, effective October 1, 2015.]
    390:23 Statement of Account Final, When. –
    When a statement of account has been rendered by a bank to a depositor accompanied by vouchers, if any, which are the basis for debit entries in such account, or the depositor's passbook has been written up by the bank showing the condition of the depositor's account and delivered to such depositor with like accompaniment of vouchers, if any, such account shall, after the period of 5 years from the date of its rendition, in the event no objection thereto has been theretofore made by the depositor, be deemed finally adjusted and settled and its correctness presumed.

Source. 1959, 213:1, eff. Oct. 6, 1959.
