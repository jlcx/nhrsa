TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 390
REGULATION OF TRUST AND BANKING COMPANIES

Section 390:11-a

    390:11-a Rulemaking. – The bank commissioner shall adopt rules, under RSA 541-A, relative to:
    I. The reserves required by RSA 390:6.
    II. [Repealed.]
    III. The deposit of securities by trust companies and banks under RSA 390:18-a.

Source. 1985, 55:14. 1994, 250:11, III, eff. June 2, 1994.
