TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 390
REGULATION OF TRUST AND BANKING COMPANIES

Section 390:5


[RSA 390:5 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXII, effective October 1, 2015.]
    390:5 Director's Liability. –
    The directors voting for any dividend declared in violation of any of the provisions of RSA 390:3 shall be fined $500, for which sum they shall be jointly and severally liable.

Source. 1911, 120:3. PL 264:5. RL 312:5.
