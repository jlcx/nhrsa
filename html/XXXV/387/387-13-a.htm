TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 387
INVESTMENTS OF SAVINGS BANKS

Section 387:13-a


[RSA 387:13-a repealed by 2015, 272:52, XVIII, effective October 1, 2015.]
    387:13-a Capital Debentures. –
    Capital debentures are legal investments and may be purchased with the approval of the commissioner as provided in RSA 384:14-a.

Source. 1959, 109:5, eff. May 20, 1959.
