TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393-A
CAPITAL STOCK CONVERSION

Section 393-A:5

    393-A:5 Existence and Operation. – No association chartered by this state shall exist or operate as a capital stock association in this state except in accordance with this chapter.

Source. 1979, 428:1, eff. Aug. 22, 1979.
