TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 393-A
CAPITAL STOCK CONVERSION

Section 393-A:3


[RSA 393-A:3 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXVII, effective October 1, 2015.]
    393-A:3 Powers. –
    To the extent such powers are not inconsistent with the operation of the stock form of organization, all capital stock associations shall have the same powers granted to cooperative banks, building and loan associations or savings and loan associations pursuant to RSA 393.

Source. 1979, 428:1, eff. Aug. 22, 1979.
