TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386-B
REORGANIZATION OF MUTUAL SAVINGS BANKS INTO MUTUAL HOLDING COMPANIES

Section 386-B:12

    386-B:12 Rulemaking. – The bank commissioner may adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 1985, 267:1. 2003, 165:9, eff. Aug. 16, 2003.
