TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-C
TRUST COMPANY ACT

Article 7
Offices

Section 383-C:7-701


[RSA 383-C:7-701 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-C:7-701 Offices Generally. –
    A trust company may operate out of one or more trust offices and other offices located within the state or in any other state or jurisdiction consistent with the trust company's business plan.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
