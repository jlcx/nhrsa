TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-C
TRUST COMPANY ACT

Article 11
Foreign Trust Companies

Section 383-C:11-1102

    383-C:11-1102 Trust Office. – (a) Before opening or relocating a trust office within this state, a foreign trust company shall submit notice to the commissioner describing the opening or relocation under RSA 383-A:6-602. A foreign trust company may only proceed with the opening or relocation of a trust office if permitted to do so by the commissioner under RSA 383-A:6-604.
    (b) At least 10 days before closing a trust office within this state, a foreign trust company shall provide notice of the closure to the commissioner.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
