TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-C
TRUST COMPANY ACT

Article 7
Offices

Section 383-C:7-702


[RSA 383-C:7-702 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-C:7-702 Opening or Relocation of Trust Office. –
    Before opening or relocating a trust office, other than an opening or relocation that is expressly provided in the trust company's business plan approved by the commissioner, a trust company that, under the MOECA components under the Uniform Interagency Trust Rating System, has a composite rating of at least 2 as a result of its most recent examination by the commissioner, shall provide the commissioner with written notice of its proposal to open or relocate a trust office as required under RSA 383-A:6-602. All other trust companies shall submit an application to establish a trust office under RSA 383-A:6-602. A trust company may proceed with the opening or relocation of the trust office if permitted by the commissioner under RSA 383-A:6-604.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
