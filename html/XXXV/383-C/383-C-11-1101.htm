TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-C
TRUST COMPANY ACT

Article 11
Foreign Trust Companies

Section 383-C:11-1101

    383-C:11-1101 Engagement in Trust Business. – (a) A foreign trust company may engage in trust business in this state.
    (b) A foreign trust company that engages in trust business in this state is subject to RSA 383-A and this chapter.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
