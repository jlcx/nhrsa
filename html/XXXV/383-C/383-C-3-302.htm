TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-C
TRUST COMPANY ACT

Article 3
Trust Business

Section 383-C:3-302


[RSA 383-C:3-302 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-C:3-302 No Acceptance of Deposits. –
    A trust company shall not have the power to accept deposits, and a trust company's organizational instrument shall include a statement that the trust company does not have the power to accept deposits.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
