TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-C
TRUST COMPANY ACT

Article 3
Trust Business

Section 383-C:3-303


[RSA 383-C:3-303 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-C:3-303 No Bond. –
    Unless required by the order of a court with proper jurisdiction, no trust company authorized to act as trustee or executor in this state shall be required to give bond to secure performance of the trust company's duties as trustee or executor.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
