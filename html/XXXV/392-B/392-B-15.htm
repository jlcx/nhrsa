TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392-B
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF FAMILY TRUST COMPANIES

Section 392-B:15


[RSA 392-B:15 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXV, effective October 1, 2015.]
    392-B:15 Certificate or Record as Evidence. –
    The secretary of state shall also cause a record of the certificate of organization to be made, and such certificate, or such record or a certified copy thereof, shall be conclusive evidence of the existence of such family trust company.

Source. 2010, 349:2, eff. Sept. 18, 2010.
