TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392-B
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF FAMILY TRUST COMPANIES

Section 392-B:6


[RSA 392-B:6 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXV, effective October 1, 2015.]
    392-B:6 Investigation. –
    For the purpose of any investigation under this chapter, the bank commissioner shall have the power to subpoena witnesses and administer oaths in any adjudicative proceedings, and to compel, by subpoena duces tecum, the production of all books, records, files, and other documents and materials relevant to its investigation.

Source. 2010, 349:2, eff. Sept. 18, 2010.
