TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392-B
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF FAMILY TRUST COMPANIES

Section 392-B:9


[RSA 392-B:9 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXV, effective October 1, 2015.]
    392-B:9 Organizers' Powers. –
    The organizer or organizers of a family trust company in organization shall hold the franchise until the organization has been completed.

Source. 2010, 349:2, eff. Sept. 18, 2010.
