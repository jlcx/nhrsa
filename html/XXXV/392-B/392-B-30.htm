TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 392-B
INCORPORATION AND MANAGEMENT OF FAMILY TRUST COMPANIES

Section 392-B:30


[RSA 392-B:30 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXV, effective October 1, 2015.]
    392-B:30 Voluntary Dissolution by Reorganization. –
    A family trust company which voluntarily dissolves by means of a reorganization into a domestic or foreign corporation, limited liability company, limited partnership, or limited liability partnership which is not authorized to engage in banking or trust activities shall do so by compliance with the procedures for a reorganization into such type of business entity as set forth in the provisions of domestic and foreign law applicable to such business entities, provided, however, that any filing required to be made with the secretary of state shall be made instead with the bank commissioner.

Source. 2010, 349:2, eff. Sept. 18, 2010.
