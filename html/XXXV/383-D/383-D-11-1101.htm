TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-D
FAMILY TRUST COMPANY ACT

Article 11
Examinations

Section 383-D:11-1101

    383-D:11-1101 Examination. – Except as provided in RSA 383-D:11-1102, the commissioner shall examine a family trust company in accordance with RSA 383:13 and RSA 383-C:7-704.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
