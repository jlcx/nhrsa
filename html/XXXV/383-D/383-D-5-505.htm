TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-D
FAMILY TRUST COMPANY ACT

Article 5
Organization and Conversion

Section 383-D:5-505


[RSA 383-D:5-505 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-D:5-505 Action on Application. –
    Upon the completion of his or her review of the application, the commissioner shall issue a notice of decision and a charter in accordance with RSA 383-A:3-308(a) or a notice of denial in accordance with RSA 383-A:3-308(b). If the commissioner approves the application, then, in addition to the information that the notice must include under RSA 383-A:3-308(a), the notice of decision shall include a statement specifying each exemption granted or denied.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
