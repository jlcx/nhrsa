TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-D
FAMILY TRUST COMPANY ACT

Article 10
Reports, Certifications, and Other Filings

Section 383-D:10-1001

    383-D:10-1001 Reports and Certification. – (a) In addition to filing an annual report in accordance with RSA 383-A:5-511, a family trust company shall file with the commissioner an annual certification certifying that the family trust company is in compliance with the provisions of this chapter and the conditions and limitations of all granted exemptions. In its annual certification, a family trust company may change its designated relative in accordance with RSA 383-D:10-1002. The annual certification is due on the same day as its annual report.
    (b) A family trust company shall file quarterly reports in accordance with RSA 383-A:5-510, and it shall file copies of other documents as required under RSA 383-A:5-511.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
