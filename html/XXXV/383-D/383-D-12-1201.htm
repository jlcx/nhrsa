TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-D
FAMILY TRUST COMPANY ACT

Article 12
Dissolution

Section 383-D:12-1201


[RSA 383-D:12-1201 effective October 1, 2015.]
    383-D:12-1201 Voluntary Dissolution. –
    A family trust company may voluntarily dissolve in accordance with RSA 383-C:10-1001 through RSA 383-C:10-1007.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
