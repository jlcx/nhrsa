TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 383-D
FAMILY TRUST COMPANY ACT

Article 3
Qualifications; Safety and Soundness

Section 383-D:3-302

    383-D:3-302 Safety and Soundness. – For purposes of determining the safety or soundness of a family trust company or any act that a family trust company has taken or proposes to take, the commissioner shall consider the factors described in RSA 383-C:4-401. For purposes of those factors, a family trust company's market is serving family clients, and a family trust company's financial success is determined by the avoidance of net losses over multiple years.

Source. 2015, 272:16, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
