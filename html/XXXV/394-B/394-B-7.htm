TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Bylaws

Section 394-B:7


[RSA 394-B:7 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    394-B:7 Amendment. –
    The board of directors of a credit union may, by majority vote, amend the bylaws.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
