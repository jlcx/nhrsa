TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Corporate Credit Union

Section 394-B:67


[RSA 394-B:67 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    394-B:67 Reserves. –
    A corporate credit union shall be exempt from the regular reserve requirements of RSA 394-B:49 but shall be required to establish and maintain an equity reserve to meet losses, in accordance with rules adopted by the bank commissioner, pursuant to RSA 541-A.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
