TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Directors and Officers

Section 394-B:36


[RSA 394-B:36 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    394-B:36 Retirement Accounts. –
    I. A credit union may act as trustee or custodian of:
       (a) Individual retirement accounts authorized by federal and state law;
       (b) Pension funds of self-employed individuals or of a company or organization sponsoring the credit union;
       (c) Deferred compensation plans; and
       (d) Other similar retirement or pension plans.
    II. A credit union may act as trustee of pension and profit-sharing plans.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
