TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Directors and Officers

Section 394-B:29


[RSA 394-B:29 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    394-B:29 Qualifications. –
    Except in the case of a credit union with fewer than 11 members, no more than one member of the board of directors of a credit union shall be a member of the supervisory committee of such credit union nor shall any such director be an employee or compensated officer of the credit union.

Source. 1983, 195:1. 1993, 213:4, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
