TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Directors and Officers

Section 394-B:30

    394-B:30 Oath. – All directors and members of supervisory and credit committees, as well as all officers whom such directors may elect, shall be sworn to the faithful performance of their duties. A record of every such qualification shall be filed and preserved with the records of the corporation.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
