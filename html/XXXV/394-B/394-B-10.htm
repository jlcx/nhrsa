TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Meetings

Section 394-B:10

    394-B:10 Notice. – Notice of all meetings of the corporation, and of all meetings of the board of directors and of committees, shall be given in the manner prescribed by the bylaws.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
