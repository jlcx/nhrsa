TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Meetings

Section 394-B:9

    394-B:9 Special Meetings. – Special meetings of members may be called by a majority of the directors, or of the supervisory committee, and shall be called by the clerk upon written application of 10 percent or 100, whichever is less, of the members entitled to vote.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
