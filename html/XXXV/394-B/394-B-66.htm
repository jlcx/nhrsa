TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Corporate Credit Union

Section 394-B:66


[RSA 394-B:66 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    394-B:66 Fees. –
    The operating fees established by the bank commissioner pursuant to RSA 383:11 shall make allowances for the special purposes and operations of a corporate credit union.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
