TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Meetings

Section 394-B:8

    394-B:8 Annual Meeting. – The annual meetings of the corporation shall be held at such time and place as the bylaws prescribe, but must be held within 90 days after the close of the fiscal year.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
