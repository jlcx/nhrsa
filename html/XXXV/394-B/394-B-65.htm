TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Corporate Credit Union

Section 394-B:65

    394-B:65 Meetings. – The board of directors of a corporate credit union shall meet at least every 90 days in person or by means of telephone, as provided in the bylaws. The annual meeting of the members of the corporate credit union shall be held at such time as is prescribed in the bylaws. Each member shall be entitled to one vote irrespective of the number of shares held by the member.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
