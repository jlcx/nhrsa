TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Corporate Credit Union

Section 394-B:63

    394-B:63 Participation in Central System. – The corporate credit union may enter into agreements for the purpose of participation in any state or federal central liquidity facility or central financial system for credit unions, and for the purpose of aiding credit unions in establishing concentrated lines of credit with other financial institutions, and may act as a depositor and transmitter of funds to carry out such agreements.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
