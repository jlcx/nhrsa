TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 394-B:52


[RSA 394-B:52 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    394-B:52 Procedure. –
    Upon a vote dissolving the credit union, the members shall elect a committee of 3 persons to liquidate the assets of the credit union. The committee shall act under the control of the bank commissioner. Each paid-in share according to the amount paid in shall be entitled to its proportional part of the assets in liquidation after all deposits and debts have been paid.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
