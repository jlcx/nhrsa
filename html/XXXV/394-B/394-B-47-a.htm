TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Loans

Section 394-B:47-a

    394-B:47-a No Credit Committee. – If the bylaws do not provide for the establishment of a credit committee, the duties and responsibility set forth in RSA 394-B:47 shall be performed by the board of directors.

Source. 1993, 213:7, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
