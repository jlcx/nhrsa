TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Bylaws

Section 394-B:6

    394-B:6 Approval. – No credit union shall receive deposits or payments on account of shares, or make any loans, until its bylaws have been approved in writing by the bank commissioner, nor shall any amendments to its bylaws become operative until they have been so approved.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
