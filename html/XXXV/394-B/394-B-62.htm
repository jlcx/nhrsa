TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Corporate Credit Union

Section 394-B:62


[RSA 394-B:62 repealed by 2015, 272:52, XXIX, effective October 1, 2015.]
    394-B:62 Powers. –
    The corporate credit union may exercise such incidental powers or privileges as are conferred upon federal corporate credit unions, subject to the approval of the bank commissioner.

Source. 1983, 195:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
