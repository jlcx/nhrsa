TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 394-B
CREDIT UNIONS

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 394-B:52-a

    394-B:52-a Advantageous Federal Powers. – To the extent permitted by rules which may be adopted by the commissioner in accordance with RSA 394-A:7, any credit union shall have and may exercise any power, right, benefit or privilege, now or hereafter authorized for federal credit unions by federal legislation, regulation or ruling.

Source. 1993, 213:8, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
