TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386-A
INCORPORATION OF SAVINGS BANKS

Section 386-A:24

    386-A:24 Voluntary Dissolution. – A savings bank organized hereunder may be dissolved, as provided in RSA 293-A:14.01 through 293-A:14.34, whenever a majority of its incorporators (in the case of a mutual savings bank) or a majority of its special deposit or capital stock (in the case of a guaranty savings bank) shall have voted to authorize its dissolution. The court may order that liquidation be carried out under the supervision of the bank commissioner.

Source. 1965, 279:1, eff. Sept. 4, 1965.
