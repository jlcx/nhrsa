TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386-A
INCORPORATION OF SAVINGS BANKS

Section 386-A:17

    386-A:17 Certificate or Record as Evidence. – The secretary of state shall also cause a record of the certificate of incorporation to be made, and such certificate, or such record or a certified copy thereof, shall be conclusive evidence of the existence of such corporation.

Source. 1965, 279:1, eff. Sept. 4, 1965.
