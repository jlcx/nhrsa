TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386-A
INCORPORATION OF SAVINGS BANKS

Amended Powers

Section 386-A:28

    386-A:28 Petition to Board of Trust Company Incorporation. – Such savings bank shall thereupon file with the board of trust company incorporation a petition for approval of the proposed amendment, setting forth:
    I. The corporate action taken in compliance with the provisions of RSA 386-A:27.
    II. The proposed amendment adopted in accordance with RSA 386-A:27, together with amended articles of agreement or an amended charter, in duplicate, incorporating such amendment, certified by the clerk of such savings bank.

Source. 1977, 135:1, eff. Aug. 5, 1977.
