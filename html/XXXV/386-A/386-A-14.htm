TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 386-A
INCORPORATION OF SAVINGS BANKS

Section 386-A:14

    386-A:14 Approval of. – Such certificate shall be submitted to the commissioner, who shall examine it, and who may require such amendment thereof or such additional information as he or she may consider necessary. If the commissioner finds that the public convenience and advantage will be served by the establishment of such corporation, that the proposed management of such corporation will be of satisfactory character, knowledge and experience in the field of banking, and that the proceedings in other respects conform to the provisions of this chapter, he or she shall so certify and endorse his or her approval upon the certificate.

Source. 1965, 279:1, eff. Sept. 4, 1965. 2013, 56:13, eff. July 4, 2013.
