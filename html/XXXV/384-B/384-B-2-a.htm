TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384-B
BRANCH BANKING AND HOLDING COMPANY AFFILIATES

Section 384-B:2-a


[RSA 384-B:2-a repealed by 2015, 272:52, X, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384-B:2-a Loan Production Offices. –
    A bank chartered by this or any other state may engage in the business of loan origination or loan solicitation at a loan production office in this state.

Source. 1992, 229:6, eff. July 12, 1992.
