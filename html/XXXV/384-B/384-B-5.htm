TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384-B
BRANCH BANKING AND HOLDING COMPANY AFFILIATES

Section 384-B:5


[RSA 384-B:5 repealed by 2015, 272:52, X, effective October 1, 2015.]
    384-B:5 Injunction. –
    Upon an information or other proceeding the court may issue an injunction restraining such bank holding company or bank or its or their officers, directors, agents, and employees from further action in violation of any provision of this chapter or the conditions upon which any application was granted by the commissioner during the pendency of such proceeding, or permanently, and may make such other order as justice may require.

Source. 1963, 188:1. 1995, 67:5. 1996, 288:9, eff. June 10, 1996. 2013, 56:5, eff. July 4, 2013.
