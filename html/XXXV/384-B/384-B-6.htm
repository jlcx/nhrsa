TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384-B
BRANCH BANKING AND HOLDING COMPANY AFFILIATES

Section 384-B:6

    384-B:6 Existing Branch Offices. – Nothing herein shall be construed to make unlawful the continued operation of any branch office lawfully existing on June 30, 1979.

Source. 1963, 188:1. 1979, 123:5, eff. June 30, 1979.
