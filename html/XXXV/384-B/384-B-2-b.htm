TITLE XXXV
BANKS AND BANKING; LOAN ASSOCIATIONS; CREDIT UNIONS

CHAPTER 384-B
BRANCH BANKING AND HOLDING COMPANY AFFILIATES

Section 384-B:2-b

    384-B:2-b Rulemaking. – Pursuant to RSA 541-A, the bank commissioner may adopt such rules as he deems necessary for the administration and enforcement of this chapter. Such rules shall be consistent with the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 1992, 229:6, eff. July 12, 1992.
