TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 498-A
EMINENT DOMAIN PROCEDURE ACT

Board of Tax and Land Appeals

Section 498-A:22

    498-A:22 State Agencies. – With respect to any state agency, the expenses billed by the state treasurer to the agency may be a charge against the funds appropriated to the agency for the purchase of the parcel or parcels of land involved in the proceedings before the board.

Source. 1971, 526:1. 1982, 42:79, eff. Dec. 31, 1982.
