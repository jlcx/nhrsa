TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 498-A
EMINENT DOMAIN PROCEDURE ACT

Board of Tax and Land Appeals

Section 498-A:18

    498-A:18 Powers of Board. – The board and each of its members shall have the power to administer oaths and affirmations, to compel the attendance of witnesses and the production of books and documents, and to adjourn proceedings from time to time.

Source. 1971, 526:1. 1982, 42:79, eff. Dec. 31, 1982.
