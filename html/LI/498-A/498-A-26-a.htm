TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 498-A
EMINENT DOMAIN PROCEDURE ACT

Determination of Just Compensation

Section 498-A:26-a

    498-A:26-a Awarding of Costs to Prevailing Party. – If neither the condemnor nor the condemnee appeals from the award of the board as provided in RSA 498-A:27, the board shall award costs to the prevailing party.

Source. 1975, 331:1. 1981, 493:16. 1982, 42:79, eff. Dec. 31, 1982.
