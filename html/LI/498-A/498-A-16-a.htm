TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 498-A
EMINENT DOMAIN PROCEDURE ACT

Board of Tax and Land Appeals

Section 498-A:16-a

    498-A:16-a Filing Fee. – The condemnor shall pay a fee of $100 for each condemnation proceeding brought before the board. The filing fees collected under this chapter shall be deposited to the general fund.

Source. 1981, 493:12. 1982, 42:79. 1989, 408:77, eff. Aug. 4, 1989.
