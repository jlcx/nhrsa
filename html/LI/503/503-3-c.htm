CHAPTER 503
LITIGATION OF SMALL CLAIMS

Section 503:3-c


[RSA 503:3-c repealed by 2014, 194:6, I, effective July 1, 2015.]
    503:3-c Prejudgment Attachment Process. –
    The prejudgment attachment process under RSA 511-A shall apply to small claims actions.
