TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 500-A
JURORS

Section 500-A:3-a

    500-A:3-a Preparation of Master Jury List; Computer. – Notwithstanding RSA 500-A:3, in municipalities which have computerized lists, the names on the master jury list may be chosen by computer on a random basis rather than by random draw.

Source. 1990, 210:1, eff. June 1, 1990.
