TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 502-A
DISTRICT COURTS

Jurisdiction

Section 502-A:13

    502-A:13 Binding Over by District Court. – Each district court shall have jurisdiction to commit to jail, or bind over with sufficient sureties to the superior court, all persons charged with offenses exceeding its jurisdiction to try committed in the district in which the court is located.

Source. 1963, 331:1, eff. July 1, 1964.
