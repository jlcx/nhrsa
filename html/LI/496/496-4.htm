TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 496
TERMS OF THE SUPERIOR COURT

Section 496:4

    496:4 Jurors' Mileage. – When such adjournment involves transferring the jury, jurors so transferred shall be entitled to such extra mileage as may be reasonable, to be determined by the presiding justice.

Source. 1915, 158:2. 1921, 146:2. PL 318:3. RL 372:4.
