TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 494
THE JUDICIAL COUNCIL

Section 494:4

    494:4 Biennial Reports. – The council shall report to the governor and council biennially on its activities and shall submit with the report its recommendations, if any, for improving the administration of justice. The biennial report, and any and all other reports, studies and recommendations may be printed, based upon available funding, and made available to members of the general court, the supreme, superior, probate and district and municipal courts and to the public.

Source. 1945, 169:1, par. 4. RSA 494:4. 1992, 284:19, eff. Jan. 1, 1993.
