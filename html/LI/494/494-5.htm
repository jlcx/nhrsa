TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 494
THE JUDICIAL COUNCIL

Section 494:5

    494:5 Meetings. – The regular meetings of the council shall be held in January, April and October of each year, and at such other times as the council or its chairman may determine.

Source. 1945, 169:1, par. 5. 1951, 245:3. RSA 494:5. 1975, 107:1. 1981, 552:3. 1992, 284:20, eff. Jan. 1, 1993.
