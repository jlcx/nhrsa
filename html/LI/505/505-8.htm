TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 505
THE STATE REPORTER AND REPORTS

Section 505:8

    505:8 Style, Etc. of Reports. – The reports shall be published in volumes entitled "New Hampshire Reports,'' and shall give the extreme dates of the decisions on the title page. The size, style, and price of the volumes shall be prescribed by the justices of the court.

Source. CS 188:6. 1850, 961:6. 1855, 1659:32. 1862, 2597:1. GS 200:5. GL 219:2, 5. 1876, 25:8. PS 214:8, 9. 1893, 67:7. 1921, 43:1. PL 326:8. RL 382:8.
