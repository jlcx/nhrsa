TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 501-A
FEES FOR JUSTICES OF THE PEACE AND MUNICIPAL COURT CLERKS

Section 501-A:1

    501-A:1 Fees for Justices of the Peace for Administering Oaths. – For administering and certifying oaths, except the oaths of office of town officers, a justice of the peace shall be entitled to a fee of up to $5 for each oath.

Source. 1957, 244:1. 1967, 244:1. 1990, 16:1, eff. May 12, 1990.
