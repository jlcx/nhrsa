TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 490
SUPREME COURT

Justices, Jurisdiction, Sessions, Etc.

Section 490:4-a

    490:4-a Law Enforcement Officers; Wearing of Firearms. – Notwithstanding any other rule, regulation or order to the contrary, law enforcement officers shall be permitted to wear firearms in any courtroom in the state.

Source. 1975, 72:1, eff. June 7, 1975.
