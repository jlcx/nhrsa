TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 490
SUPREME COURT

Clerk and Messenger

Section 490:20

    490:20 Messenger. – The court may appoint a messenger, who shall perform such duties with reference to the courtroom and apartments occupied by the justices as they may prescribe, and who shall receive such compensation as the court shall certify to be just and reasonable.

Source. 1901, 78:11. PL 315:24. RL 369:19.
