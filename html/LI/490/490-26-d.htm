TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 490
SUPREME COURT

Court Fees, Fines, Forms, and Personnel

Section 490:26-d

    490:26-d Forms. – The judicial branch administrative council appointed pursuant to supreme court rule 54 shall establish such forms for all courts as are necessary for the effective administration of justice.

Source. 1985, 341:5. 2007, 48:1, eff. July 1, 2007.
