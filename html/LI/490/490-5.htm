TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 490
SUPREME COURT

Justices, Jurisdiction, Sessions, Etc.

Section 490:5

    490:5 Jurisdiction; By Special Provisions. – The supreme court shall have jurisdiction of all other proceedings and matters to be entered or heard therein by special provisions of law.

Source. RS 171:5. CS 181:8. 1855, 1659:11. GS 189:2. 1870, 2:1. GL 208:2. 1885, 42:3. PS 204:3. PL 315:3. RL 369:3.
