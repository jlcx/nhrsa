TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 490
SUPREME COURT

Employee Benefits

Section 490:28-a

    490:28-a Applicability. – The provisions of RSA 490:28 shall apply to all individuals who are state employees as of the effective date of this section, who previously transferred from the judicial branch to the executive branch.

Source. 2010, 11:2, eff. July 6, 2010.
