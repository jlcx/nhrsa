TITLE LI
COURTS

CHAPTER 490
SUPREME COURT

Justices, Jurisdiction, Sessions, Etc.

Section 490:5-d

    490:5-d Rating; Accreditation. – The commission shall rate each court inspected as herein provided as "accredited-excellent'', "accredited-satisfactory'', "not accredited'' or give each court a rating established by the commission which substantially reflects these rating categories, in accordance with the criteria set forth in RSA 490:5-c. The commission rating shall be a matter of public record and shall be transmitted in written form to each court.

Source. 1971, 382:1. 1981, 256:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1981.
