TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 377
RAILROAD TRANSPORTATION OF PASSENGERS, BAGGAGE AND FREIGHT

Passengers

Section 377:8

    377:8 Disorderly Conduct. – If a passenger or other person behaves in a noisy or disorderly manner, or is intoxicated or will not cease drinking intoxicating liquor of any kind upon a railway train or in a railroad or railway waiting room upon the request of the conductor of the train, or the person in charge of such waiting room, he shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1874, 98:5. GL 163:20. PS 160:8. 1907, 124:1. PL 251:10. RL 301:10. 1951, 203:60 par. 8. RSA 377:8. 1957, 111:2. 1973, 528:260, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
