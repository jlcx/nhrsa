TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 377
RAILROAD TRANSPORTATION OF PASSENGERS, BAGGAGE AND FREIGHT

Baggage

Section 377:14

    377:14 Amount. – Every passenger, in consideration of the fare paid by him, shall be entitled to have a reasonable amount of personal baggage carried by the same train on which he goes; but, if such baggage exceeds in value $100, the railroad shall not be liable for its loss or damage beyond that sum unless notice is given to them of its value and an extra charge is paid for the risk.

Source. 1850, 953:12. CS 150:70. GS 149:10. GL 163:10. PS 160:15. PL 251:25. RL 301:25. 1951, 203:60 par. 14, eff. Sept. 1, 1951.
