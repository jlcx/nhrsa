TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 377
RAILROAD TRANSPORTATION OF PASSENGERS, BAGGAGE AND FREIGHT

Heating Passenger Cars

Section 377:13

    377:13 Penalty. – If a railroad violates the provisions of RSA 377:12, it shall be guilty of a felony.
