TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 369-B
ELECTRIC RATE REDUCTION FINANCING AND COMMISSION ACTION

Section 369-B:3-b

    369-B:3-b Employee Protections. – In the event of divestiture or retirement of any or all of PSNH's generation assets, employee protections no less than those set forth in the then-current collective bargaining agreement shall be provided to affected employees.

Source. 2014, 310:3, eff. Sept. 30, 2014. 2015, 221:11, eff. July 9, 2015.
