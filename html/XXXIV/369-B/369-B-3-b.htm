TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 369-B
ELECTRIC RATE REDUCTION FINANCING AND COMMISSION ACTION

Section 369-B:3-b


[RSA 369-B:3-b effective September 30, 2014.]
    369-B:3-b Employee Protections. –
    In the event of divestiture or retirement of any or all of PSNH's generation assets, the employee protections set forth in Section X of the original proposed settlement defined in RSA 369-B:2, VIII, shall be provided to affected employees.

Source. 2014, 310:3, eff. Sept. 30, 2014.
