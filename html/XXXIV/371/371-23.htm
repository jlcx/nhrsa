TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 371
PROCEEDINGS TO ACQUIRE PROPERTY OR RIGHTS

Rights in Public Waters and Lands

Section 371:23

    371:23 Compensation. – Any compensation payable on account of any such license shall be paid to the state treasurer and, if derived from licenses affecting state forests or forestry reservations, shall be added to the forest improvement fund; otherwise it shall be a part of the general funds of the state.

Source. 1923, 6:1. PL 244:15. 1929, 113:1. RL 294:24. 1951, 203:48 par. 23, eff. Sept. 1, 1951.
