TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 371
PROCEEDINGS TO ACQUIRE PROPERTY OR RIGHTS

Eminent Domain

Section 371:5

    371:5 Notice by Publication. – Upon evidence satisfactory to the commission that any owner or his residence is unknown or uncertain, the notice may be by publication; and if no appearance is entered for such owner, the commission shall, upon application by the public utility, appoint a guardian ad litem to represent such owner in the proceedings.

Source. 1929, 85:1. RL 294:5. 1951, 203:48 par. 5, eff. Sept. 1, 1951.
