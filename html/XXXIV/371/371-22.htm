TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 371
PROCEEDINGS TO ACQUIRE PROPERTY OR RIGHTS

Rights in Public Waters and Lands

Section 371:22

    371:22 Deeds. – Any such license creating rights over, under or across any of the lands owned by the state shall be evidenced by an instrument executed in the name of the state by the governor and attested by the secretary of state and recorded in the county where the property is situated.

Source. 1923, 6:1. PL 244:14. 1929, 113:1. RL 294:23. 1951, 203:48 par. 22, eff. Sept. 1, 1951.
