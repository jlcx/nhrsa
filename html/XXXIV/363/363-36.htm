TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 363
THE PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION

Participation of Staff in Adjudicative Proceedings

Section 363:36

    363:36 Assessment of Costs. – The commission may recover additional costs which it incurs in the performance of its duties resulting from the designation of employees as staff advocates or decisional employees in the manner provided in RSA 363:27, I; RSA 365:37; or RSA 365:38.

Source. 1994, 414:1, eff. Aug. 10, 1994.
