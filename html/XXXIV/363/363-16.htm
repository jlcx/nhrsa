TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 363
THE PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION

Appointment, Qualification, Etc.

Section 363:16

    363:16 Quorum. – A majority of the commission shall constitute a quorum to issue orders or adopt rules, and any hearing or investigation may be held or conducted by 2 commissioners or by a single commissioner.

Source. 1911, 164:2. PL 237:16. RL 286:15. 1951, 203:10 par. 15. RSA 363:16. 1989, 240:5, eff. July 23, 1989.
