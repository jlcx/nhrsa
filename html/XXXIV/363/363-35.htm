TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 363
THE PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION

Participation of Staff in Adjudicative Proceedings

Section 363:35

    363:35 Separation of Function. – No employee designated as a staff advocate in an adjudicative proceeding may advise the commission, its presiding officer, individual commissioners, or any decisional employee designated as such in the same proceeding, with respect to matters at issue in the contested case.

Source. 1994, 414:1, eff. Aug. 10, 1994.
