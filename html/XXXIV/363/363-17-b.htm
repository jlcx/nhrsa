TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 363
THE PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION

Appointment, Qualification, Etc.

Section 363:17-b

    363:17-b Final Orders. – The commission shall issue a final order on all matters presented to it. The transcript or minutes of oral deliberations shall not constitute a final order. A final order shall include, but not be limited to:
    I. The identity of all parties;
    II. The positions of each party on each issue;
    III. A decision on each issue including the reasoning behind the decision; and
    IV. The concurrence or dissent of each commissioner participating in the decision.

Source. 1979, 494:4. 2000, 57:1, eff. June 16, 2000.
