TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 363
THE PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION

Appointment, Qualification, Etc.

Section 363:1

    363:1 Commission; Term. – There shall be a public utilities commission, composed of 3 commissioners who shall be full-time employees and who shall engage in no other gainful employment during their terms as members. Their term of office shall be for 6 years. Of the 3 commissioners, one shall be an attorney and a member of the New Hampshire Bar and one shall have either background or experience or both in one or more of the following: engineering, economics, accounting or finance.

Source. 1911, 164:2. PL 237:1. RL 286:1. 1951, 203:10 par. 1. RSA 363:1. 1979, 494:1. 2007, 156:11, eff. Aug. 17, 2007.
