TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 363
THE PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION

Appointment, Qualification, Etc.

Section 363:12-b

    363:12-b Prohibition on Future Employment. – No commissioner, executive director, finance director, general counsel, or chief engineer of the commission shall accept any employment with any utility under the control of the commission until one year after he or she shall become separated from the commission.

Source. 1979, 494:3. 2001, 237:3, eff. July 1, 2001.
