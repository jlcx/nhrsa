TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 363
THE PUBLIC UTILITIES COMMISSION

Appointment, Qualification, Etc.

Section 363:4-a

    363:4-a Commissioners, Duties. – The commissioners shall, by majority action, establish the budgetary requirements of the agency, act on the selection of department heads, select the attorneys employed by the commission, and establish practices and procedures of the agency.

Source. 1989, 240:4, eff. July 23, 1989.
