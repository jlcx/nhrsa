TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 374
GENERAL REGULATIONS

Reports, Etc.

Section 374:15

    374:15 Filing. – Every public utility shall file with the commission reports at such times, verified by oath in such manner, and setting forth such statistics and facts, as may be required by the commission.

Source. 1911, 164:8. PL 240:14. RL 289:14. 1951, 203:32, eff. Sept. 1, 1951.
