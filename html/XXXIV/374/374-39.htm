TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 374
GENERAL REGULATIONS

Investigation of Accidents

Section 374:39

    374:39 Reports. – Every public utility shall report to the commission accidents occurring in connection with the operation of its business wherein loss of life occurs or any person is injured, or of such a nature as to endanger the safety, health or property of its consumers or the public, as and whenever directed by such rules and regulations as the commission may prescribe.

Source. 1913, 145:16. PL 240:37. RL 289:37. 1951, 203:43, eff. Sept. 1, 1951.
