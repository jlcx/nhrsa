TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 374
GENERAL REGULATIONS

Reports, Etc.

Section 374:19

    374:19 False Statements, Etc. – No public utility shall willfully make any false statement or false entry in any report to the commission, or in any answer to any question lawfully asked by the commission.

Source. 1913, 145:8. PL 240:18. RL 289:18. 1951, 203:36, eff. Sept. 1, 1951.
