TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 374
GENERAL REGULATIONS

Investigation of Accidents

Section 374:38

    374:38 Manner. – Any such investigation may be made by the full commission, or by a single commissioner, or by an agent of the commission, in such manner as the commission may determine.

Source. 1911, 164:15. 1913, 145:16. PL 240:36. RL 289:36.
