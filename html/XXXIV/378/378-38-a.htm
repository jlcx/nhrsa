TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 378
RATES AND CHARGES

Least Cost Energy Planning

Section 378:38-a

    378:38-a Waiver by Commission. – The commission, by order, may waive for good cause any requirement under RSA 378:38, upon written request by a utility.

Source. 1997, 298:14, eff. June 20, 1997. 2014, 129:1, eff. Aug. 15, 2014.
