TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 378
RATES AND CHARGES

Least Cost Energy Planning

Section 378:42

    378:42 Existing Rate Plans. – Notwithstanding the requirements of RSA 378:37-41, nothing contained in this subdivision shall impair or change the provisions of any agreement or rate plan approved by the commission in accordance with RSA 362-C:6.

Source. 1994, 362:6, eff. June 8, 1994.
