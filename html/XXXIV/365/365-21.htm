TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 365
COMPLAINTS TO, AND PROCEEDINGS BEFORE, THE COMMISSION

Proceedings Before the Commission

Section 365:21

    365:21 Rehearings and Appeals. – The procedure for rehearings and appeals shall be that prescribed by RSA 541, except as herein otherwise provided.

Source. 1951, 203:11 par. 21, eff. Sept. 1, 1951.
