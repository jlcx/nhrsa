TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 365
COMPLAINTS TO, AND PROCEEDINGS BEFORE, THE COMMISSION

Complaints and Investigations

Section 365:6

    365:6 Inspection. – The commission may at any time personally, or by its experts or agents, inspect the property, works, system, plant, devices, appliances and methods used by any public utility, or its books, papers and records.

Source. 1911, 164:10. 1913, 145:9. PL 238:7. RL 287:7. 1951, 203:11 par. 6, eff. Sept. 1, 1951.
