TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 380
RAILROADS; FIRE DAMAGE AND PREVENTION

Entry on Land

Section 380:18

    380:18 Reports. – The department of resources and economic development or its authorized agents shall have the right to enter upon railroad or other property to ascertain facts, and from time to time shall report the same to the department of transportation.

Source. 1913, 155:5. PL 250:18. RL 300:18. 1951, 203:59 par. 18. RSA 380:18. 1985, 402:6, I(e)(8).
