TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 380
RAILROADS; FIRE DAMAGE AND PREVENTION

Entry on Land

Section 380:17

    380:17 Assistance. – The department of transportation may require the assistance of the department of resources and economic development and the director of the division of forests and lands in furnishing information pertinent to carrying out RSA 380:16.

Source. 1913, 155:4. PL 250:17. RL 300:17. 1951, 203:59 par. 17. RSA 380:17. 1985, 402:6, I(e)(8).
