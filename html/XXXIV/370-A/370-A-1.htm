TITLE XXXIV
PUBLIC UTILITIES

CHAPTER 370-A
USE OF CUSTOMER-OWNED EQUIPMENT

Section 370-A:1

    370-A:1 Definitions. – In this chapter:
    I. "Governmental agency'' means any state, county, city, town, village, district, organized fire mutual aid system dispatch or municipal agency, authority, department or other public instrumentality thereof.
    II. "Self-dialing telephone alarm'' means a device attached to a person's telephone system, designed to dial a designated telephone number when fire or unauthorized entry is detected at the location in which it is installed.

Source. 1979, 205:1, eff. Aug. 10, 1979.
