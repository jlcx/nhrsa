TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Management of Limited Liability Companies--General Provisions

Section 304-C:48

    304-C:48 Method of Appointment of Managers. –
    I. Persons may be appointed as managers of a limited liability company as provided in the operating agreement.
    II. Unless the operating agreement provides otherwise, the managers of a limited liability company shall be appointed by majority vote of the members.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
