TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Managers--General Provisions

Section 304-C:84

    304-C:84 Manager Resignations. – Unless the operating agreement provides otherwise, a manager may resign without liability upon 15 days' written notice to the limited liability company and the members.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
