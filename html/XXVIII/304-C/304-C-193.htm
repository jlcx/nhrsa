TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 304-C:193

    304-C:193 Resolution Of Cases Not Provided For In This Act. – In any case not provided for in this act, the rules of law and equity shall govern.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
