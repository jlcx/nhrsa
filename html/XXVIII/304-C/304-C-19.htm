TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Limited Liability Companies--Basic Statutory Characteristics

Section 304-C:19

    304-C:19 Legal Status of Limited Liability Company as Entity; Duration of Entity Status. –
    I. A limited liability company formed under this act shall be a legal entity separate and distinct from its members.
    II. The existence of a limited liability company as a separate legal entity shall continue until cancellation of the limited liability company's certificate of formation in accordance with RSA 304-C:142.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
