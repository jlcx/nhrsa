TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Limited Liability Companies--Basic Statutory Characteristics

Section 304-C:25

    304-C:25 Governing Law. – The laws of the state of New Hampshire govern:
    I. The internal affairs of a limited liability company; and
    II. The liability of a member as member and of a manager as manager for the debts, obligations, or other liabilities of a limited liability company.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
