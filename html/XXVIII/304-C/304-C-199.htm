TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 304-C:199

    304-C:199 Singular and Plural. – Unless the context otherwise requires, as used herein, the singular shall include the plural and the plural may refer to only the singular.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
