TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Managers--General Provisions

Section 304-C:79

    304-C:79 Managers' Voting Requirement. – Unless the operating agreement provides otherwise, each matter that the managers may decide by vote shall be decided by vote of a majority of the managers by number.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
