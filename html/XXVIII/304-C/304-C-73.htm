TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Managers--General Provisions

Section 304-C:73

    304-C:73 Contributions by Managers. – A manager of a limited liability company who is a member may make contributions to the limited liability company as a member and may share as a member in the profits and losses of the limited liability company and in distributions from it.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
