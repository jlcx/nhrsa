TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Members--Admissions, Classes, Information Rights, Etc.

Section 304-C:57

    304-C:57 Certificates of Membership Rights, Etc. – An operating agreement may provide that the limited liability company may issue certificates evidencing:
    I. Membership rights, including limited liability company interests; and
    II. Other rights relating to a limited liability company, including a limited liability company interest that has been transferred in accordance with the provisions of the operating agreement or this act.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
