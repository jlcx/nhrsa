TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Definitions

Section 304-C:15

    304-C:15 Membership Rights. – "Membership rights'' means the totality of the member's rights as a member under this act, including both economic rights, such as the member's limited liability company interest, and non-economic rights, such as the member's voting rights, if any.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
