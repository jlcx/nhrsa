TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Single-Member Limited Liability Companies--Special Provisions

Section 304-C:153

    304-C:153 Successor to Deceased Member of Single-Member Limited Liability Company Becomes Member. – Unless the operating agreement provides otherwise, a person who succeeds to the limited liability company interest of a deceased member of a single-member limited liability company is admitted as the member of the limited liability company.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
