TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Members--Admissions, Classes, Information Rights, Etc.

Section 304-C:58

    304-C:58 Rights and Duties of Members. – Unless the operating agreement provides otherwise, a member of a limited liability company shall have the rights and duties of members as provided in this act.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
