TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Management of Limited Liability Companies--General Provisions

Section 304-C:49

    304-C:49 Offices and Responsibilities of Managers. –
    I. The operating agreement may set forth the offices to be held by a manager and the manager's responsibilities.
    II. Unless the operating agreement provides otherwise, the manager or managers shall have general responsibility for managing the limited liability company.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
