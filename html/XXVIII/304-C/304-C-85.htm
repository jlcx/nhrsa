TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Contributions

Section 304-C:85

    304-C:85 Permitted Forms of Contributions. – The contribution of a member to a limited liability company may be in cash, property, or services rendered, or a promissory note or other obligation to contribute cash or property or to perform services.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
