TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Foreign Limited Liability Companies

Section 304-C:178

    304-C:178 Amendments to Applications to Register Foreign Limited Liability Companies. –
    I. A foreign limited liability company registered to transact business in New Hampshire shall amend its application for registration if it changes:
       (a) Its name; or
       (b) Its state or country of formation.
    II. The filing provisions of RSA 304-C:28 shall apply to the amendment of a foreign limited liability company's application for registration.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
