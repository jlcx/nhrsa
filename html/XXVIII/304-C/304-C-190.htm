TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Limited Liability Company Disputes

Section 304-C:190

    304-C:190 Right of Members to Bring Direct Actions. – Nothing in this subdivision shall be construed to restrict the right of a member or manager to bring a direct action on his or her own behalf against a member, a manager, or a limited liability company.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
