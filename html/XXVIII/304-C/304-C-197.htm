TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 304-C:197

    304-C:197 Filing of Certificates, Etc. Online. – The secretary shall, with reasonable promptness, adopt and implement comprehensive regulations permitting online filing of certificates of formation and other documents required to be filed under the act.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
