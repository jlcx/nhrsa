TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Managers--General Provisions

Section 304-C:77

    304-C:77 Permissibility of Manager Actions Not Requiring Manager Votes. – An operating agreement may provide for the taking of an action, including the amendment of the operating agreement, without the vote or approval of any manager or class or group of managers, including an action to create under the provisions of the operating agreement a class or group of membership rights, including limited liability company interests, that was not previously outstanding.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
