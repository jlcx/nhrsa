TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Managers--General Provisions

Section 304-C:74

    304-C:74 Offices and Responsibilities of Managers. – Unless the operating agreement provides otherwise, the managers shall have general responsibility for the management of the limited liability company.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
