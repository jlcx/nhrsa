TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Members--Admissions, Classes, Information Rights, Etc.

Section 304-C:59

    304-C:59 Resolution of Disputes About the Status of Persons as Members. – Any dispute as to whether a person was or is or will be entitled to become a member of a limited liability company and as to when the person was admitted or will be entitled to be admitted as a member shall be resolved in a proceeding in the superior court.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
