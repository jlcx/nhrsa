TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Member Meetings

Section 304-C:61

    304-C:61 Notices for Member Meetings and Voting Procedures. – An operating agreement that grants the members a right to vote may set forth provisions relating to (i) notice of the time, place, or purpose of any meeting at which any matter is to be voted on by any members, (ii) waiver of any such notice, (iii) action by vote without a meeting, (iv) the establishment of a record date, (v) quorum requirements, (vi) voting in person or by proxy, or (vii) any other matter with respect to the exercise of any such right to vote.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
