TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Definitions

Section 304-C:14

    304-C:14 Member. – "Member'' means a person who has been admitted to a limited liability company as a member as provided in RSA 304-C:53 or, in the case of a foreign limited liability company, in accordance with the laws of the state or foreign country or other foreign jurisdiction under which the foreign limited liability company is organized.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
