TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Managers--General Provisions

Section 304-C:75

    304-C:75 Duration of Managers' Terms. – Unless the operating agreement provides otherwise, the duration of a manager's term shall be indefinite and shall terminate only upon the manager's death, resignation, or removal.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
