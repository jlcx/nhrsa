TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 304-C:196

    304-C:196 Administrative Rulemaking. – The secretary of state may adopt rules, under RSA 541-A, necessary to implement the provisions of this act.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
