TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 304-C:204

    304-C:204 Reserved Power of State of New Hampshire to Alter or Repeal Act. – All provisions of this act may be altered from time to time or repealed, and all rights of members and managers are subject to this reservation.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
