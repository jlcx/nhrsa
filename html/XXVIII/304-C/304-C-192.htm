TITLE XXVIII
PARTNERSHIPS

CHAPTER 304-C
LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANIES

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 304-C:192

    304-C:192 Inapplicability of Rule That Statutes in Derogation of the Common Law Are to be Strictly Construed. – The rule that statutes in derogation of the common law are to be strictly construed shall have no application to this act.

Source. 2012, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
