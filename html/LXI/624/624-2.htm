TITLE LXI
ACTS REPEALED

CHAPTER 624
REPEALS AND REVISIONS

Section 624:2

    624:2 Citation. – This act shall not, in any citation or enumeration of the laws, be reckoned as one of the laws of the 1955 session of the general court; but in all citations this code shall be cited as the Revised Statutes Annotated, adding to the citation, when necessary, the number of the chapter and section.

Source. RS 230:2. CS 246:2. GS 273:2. GL 291:2. PS 288:3. PL 402:2. RL 466:2.
