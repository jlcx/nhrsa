TITLE LXI
ACTS REPEALED

CHAPTER 624
REPEALS AND REVISIONS

Section 624:9

    624:9 No Revival by Repeal. – No act which has been heretofore repealed shall be revived by the repeal contained in house bill no. 75 of the 1955 session of the general court of any of the acts therein repealed.

Source. RS 230:9. CS 246:9. GS 273:9. GL 291:9. PS 288:10. PL 402:9. RL 466:9.
