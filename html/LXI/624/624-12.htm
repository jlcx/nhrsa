TITLE LXI
ACTS REPEALED

CHAPTER 624
REPEALS AND REVISIONS

Section 624:12

    624:12 Implication from Repeal. – The repeal in house bill no. 75 of the 1955 session of the general court of any statute or part of a statute heretofore repealed shall not be construed as a declaration or implication that such statute or part of a statute has been in force at any time subsequent to the first repeal.

Source. RS 230:10. CS 246:10. GS 273:10. GL 291:10. PS 288:11. PL 402:10. RL 466:10.
