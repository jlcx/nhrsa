TITLE LXI
ACTS REPEALED

CHAPTER 624
REPEALS AND REVISIONS

Section 624:8

    624:8 Term of Office. – If the time of the beginning of a term of office is changed by the Revised Statutes Annotated, the incumbent of the office under the provisions of the former statute shall hold the office until the time fixed by the Revised Statutes Annotated for the beginning of the term, except in cases otherwise specially provided for.

Source. PS 288:9. PL 402:8. RL 466:8. 2007, 156:16, eff. Aug. 17, 2007.
