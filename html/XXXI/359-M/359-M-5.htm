TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 359-M
BAD FAITH ASSERTIONS OF PATENT INFRINGEMENT

Section 359-M:5

    359-M:5 Exemption. – A demand letter or assertion of patent infringement that includes a claim for relief arising under 35 U.S.C. section 271(e)(2) or 42 U.S.C. section 262 shall not be subject to the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 2014, 197:2, eff. July 11, 2014.
