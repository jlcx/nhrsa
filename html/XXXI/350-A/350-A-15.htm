TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 350-A
MODEL STATE TRADEMARK ACT

Section 350-A:15

    350-A:15 Severability. – If any provision hereof, or the application of such provision to any person or circumstance is held invalid, the remainder of this chapter shall not be affected thereby.

Source. 1969, 448:1, eff. Sept. 1, 1969.
