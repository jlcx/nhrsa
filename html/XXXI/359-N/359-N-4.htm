TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 359-N
REGULATION OF BIOMETRIC INFORMATION

Section 359-N:4

    359-N:4 Violation; Civil Action. – Any individual aggrieved by a violation of this chapter, including the loss or misuse of biometric data lawfully collected under RSA 359-N:2 or 359-N:3, may bring a civil action against a government agency under this section to obtain the following:
    I. An injunction of the agency practice;
    II. Actual damages or $2,500 for each violation, whichever is greater; and
    III. Reasonable attorney's fees and court costs.

Source. 2014, 162:1, eff. July 1, 2014.
