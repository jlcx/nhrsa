TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 358-E
DISTRIBUTORSHIP DISCLOSURE ACT

Section 358-E:2

    358-E:2 Prohibitions on the Advertising or Sale of Distributorships. – No person may advertise, offer, contract, sell, or promote any distributorship in this state unless the person has registered with the attorney general, as provided in RSA 358-E:3.

Source. 1977, 307:1, eff. Aug. 26, 1977.
