TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 347-A
REGULATION OF EQUIPMENT DEALERSHIPS

Section 347-A:9

    347-A:9 Remedies. – Nothing contained in this chapter shall bar the right of an agreement to provide for binding arbitration of disputes. Any arbitration shall be consistent with the provisions of this chapter and other appropriate state law. The place of any arbitration shall be in the city or county in which the dealer maintains the dealer's principal place of business in this state.

Source. 1995, 210:1, eff. June 12, 1995.
