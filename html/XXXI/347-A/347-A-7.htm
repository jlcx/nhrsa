TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 347-A
REGULATION OF EQUIPMENT DEALERSHIPS

Section 347-A:7

    347-A:7 Uniform Commercial Practice. – Nothing contained in this chapter shall be construed to release or terminate a perfected security interest of the supplier in the inventory of the dealer.

Source. 1995, 210:1, eff. June 12, 1995.
