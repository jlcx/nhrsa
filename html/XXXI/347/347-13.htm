TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 347
INSPECTION OF LUMBER

Section 347:13

    347:13 Shingles. – If any person shall export or ship for exportation any shingles branded O, or not branded according to law, he shall forfeit the same or the value thereof.

Source. RS 106:12. CS 109:12. GS 120:13. GL 131:13. PS 128:13. PL 167:13. RL 201:13.
