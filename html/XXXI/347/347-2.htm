TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 347
INSPECTION OF LUMBER

Section 347:2

    347:2 Cullers. – Cullers of hoops and staves shall view and cull all hoops, staves and heading previous to the exportation thereof, and shall give a certificate of the quantity, on payment therefor.

Source. RS 106:3. CS 109:3. GS 120:3. GL 131:3. PS 128:2. PL 167:2. RL 201:2.
