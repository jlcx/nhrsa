TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 347
INSPECTION OF LUMBER

Section 347:4

    347:4 Ship Timber. – All round ship timber shall be measured according to the following rule: a stick of timber 16 inches in diameter and 12 inches in length shall constitute one cubic foot, and in the same ratio for any other size and quantity; 40 feet shall constitute one ton.

Source. RS 106:5. CS 109:5. GS 120:5. GL 131:5. PS 128:4. PL 167:4. RL 201:4.
