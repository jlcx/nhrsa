TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 347
INSPECTION OF LUMBER

Section 347:14

    347:14 Surveyor's Fraud. – If any surveyor or culler shall be guilty of any fraud or deceit in surveying or culling, or shall connive at or allow any breach of this chapter, he shall be guilty of a violation.

Source. RS 106:13. CS 109:13. GS 120:14. GL 131:14. PS 128:14. PL 167:14. RL 201:14. RSA 347:14. 1973, 531:109, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
