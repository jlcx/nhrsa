TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 354-A
STATE COMMISSION FOR HUMAN RIGHTS

Fair Housing

Section 354-A:8

    354-A:8 Equal Housing Opportunity Without Discrimination a Civil Right. – The opportunity to obtain housing without discrimination because of age, sex, race, creed, color, marital status, familial status, physical or mental disability or national origin is hereby recognized and declared a civil right. In addition, no person shall be denied the benefit of the rights afforded by this section on account of that person's sexual orientation.

Source. 1992, 224:1. 1997, 108:13, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
