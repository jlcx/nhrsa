TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 358-C
UNFAIR, DECEPTIVE OR UNREASONABLE COLLECTION PRACTICES

Section 358-C:2

    358-C:2 General Prohibition. – No debt collector shall collect or attempt to collect a debt in an unfair, deceptive or unreasonable manner as defined in this chapter.

Source. 1975, 437:1, eff. Aug. 18, 1975.
