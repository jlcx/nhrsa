TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 359-O
SALE OF SYNTHETIC DRUGS

Section 359-O:3

    359-O:3 Prohibited Activities. – No person or business shall sell, barter, give, publicly display for sale or attempt to sell, possess, or transport, any material, compound, mixture, or preparation which contains any quantity of a synthetic drug or its optical, positional, and geometric isomers, salts, and salts of isomers, whenever the existence of such isomers, salts, and salts of isomers is possible within the specific chemical designation as defined in this chapter.

Source. 2015, 204:6, eff. July 6, 2015.
