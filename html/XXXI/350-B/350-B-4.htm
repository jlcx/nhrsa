TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 350-B
UNIFORM TRADE SECRETS ACT

Section 350-B:4

    350-B:4 Attorneys' Fees. – The court may award reasonable attorneys' fees to the prevailing party when:
    I. A claim of misappropriation is made in bad faith;
    II. A motion to terminate an injunction is made or resisted in bad faith; or
    III. Willful and malicious misappropriation exists.

Source. 1989, 220:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
