TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 350-B
UNIFORM TRADE SECRETS ACT

Section 350-B:8

    350-B:8 Uniformity of Application and Construction. – This chapter shall be applied and construed to effectuate its general purpose to make uniform the law with respect to the subject of this chapter among states enacting it.

Source. 1989, 220:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
