TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 350-B
UNIFORM TRADE SECRETS ACT

Section 350-B:6

    350-B:6 Statute of Limitations. – An action for misappropriation shall be brought within 3 years after the misappropriation is discovered or by the exercise of reasonable diligence should have been discovered. For the purposes of this section, a continuing misappropriation constitutes a single claim.

Source. 1989, 220:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
