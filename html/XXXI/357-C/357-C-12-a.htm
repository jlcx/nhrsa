TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 357-C
REGULATION OF BUSINESS PRACTICES BETWEEN MOTOR VEHICLE MANUFACTURERS, DISTRIBUTORS AND DEALERS

Section 357-C:12-a

    357-C:12-a Franchisor and Franchisee Registration. – Each franchisor and franchisee shall register annually with the New Hampshire motor vehicle industry board pursuant to rules adopted by the board in accordance with RSA 541-A.

Source. 2001, 209:10, eff. Jan. 1, 2002.
