TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 357-C
REGULATION OF BUSINESS PRACTICES BETWEEN MOTOR VEHICLE MANUFACTURERS, DISTRIBUTORS AND DEALERS

Section 357-C:14

    357-C:14 Construction. – In construing the provisions of this chapter, the courts may be guided by the interpretations of the Federal Trade Commission Act, as amended.

Source. 1981, 477:2, eff. Aug. 25, 1981.
