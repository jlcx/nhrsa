TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 359-P
FRM VICTIMS' CONTRIBUTION RECOVERY FUND

Section 359-P:10


[RSA 359-P:10 repealed by 2016, 293:6, I, effective July 1, 2023.]
    359-P:10 Claims Prior to January 1, 2006. –
    Claims for restitution assistance shall not be accepted for any losses that occurred prior to January 1, 2006 due to financial fraud.

Source. 2016, 293:2, eff. Aug. 20, 2016.
