TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 359-P
FRM VICTIMS' CONTRIBUTION RECOVERY FUND

Section 359-P:9


[RSA 359-P:9 repealed by 2016, 293:6, I, effective July 1, 2023.]
    359-P:9 Liability of State. –
    The state shall be under no obligation or liability under this chapter except to make payments to claimants to whom the recovery fund attorney or administrator has made awards of assistance, but only to the extent there is money available in the fund.

Source. 2016, 293:2, eff. Aug. 20, 2016.
