TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 359-P
FRM VICTIMS' CONTRIBUTION RECOVERY FUND

Section 359-P:8


[RSA 359-P:8 repealed by 2016, 293:6, I, effective July 1, 2023.]
    359-P:8 Award Not Subject to Other Processes. –
    An award made under this chapter shall not be subject to execution, attachment, garnishment, or other process. A claimant convicted of forgery, fraud, or deception in connection with a claim under this chapter shall forfeit an award paid to the claimant under this chapter.

Source. 2016, 293:2, eff. Aug. 20, 2016.
