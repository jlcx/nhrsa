TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 356-B
CONDOMINIUM ACT

II. Condominium Instruments

Section 356-B:13

    356-B:13 Complementarity of Condominium Instruments. – The condominium instruments shall be construed together and shall be deemed to incorporate one another to the extent that any requirement of this chapter as to the content of one shall be deemed satisfied if the deficiency can be cured by reference to any of the others. In the event of any conflict between the condominium instruments, the declaration shall control; but particular provisions shall control more general provisions, except that a construction conformable with the statute shall in all cases control over any construction inconsistent therewith.

Source. 1977, 468:1, eff. Sept. 10, 1977.
