TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 356-B
CONDOMINIUM ACT

IV. Administration and Enforcement

Section 356-B:64

    356-B:64 Penalties. – Any person who willfully violates any provision of this subdivision IV or of a rule adopted under it or any person who willfully, in an application for registration, makes any untrue statement of a material fact or omits to state a material fact shall be guilty of a class B felony if a natural person, or guilty of a felony if any other person.

Source. 1977, 468:1, eff. Sept. 10, 1977.
