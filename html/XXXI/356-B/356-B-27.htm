TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 356-B
CONDOMINIUM ACT

II. Condominium Instruments

Section 356-B:27

    356-B:27 Easement to Facilitate Conversion and Expansion. – Subject to any restrictions and limitations the condominium instruments may specify, the declarant shall have a transferable easement over and on the common areas for the purpose of making improvements on the submitted land and any additional land pursuant to the provisions of those instruments and of this chapter, and for the purpose of doing all things reasonably necessary and proper in connection therewith.

Source. 1977, 468:1, eff. Sept. 10, 1977.
