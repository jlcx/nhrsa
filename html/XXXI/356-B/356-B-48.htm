TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 356-B
CONDOMINIUM ACT

IV. Administration and Enforcement

Section 356-B:48

    356-B:48 Administration; Enforcement. – The provisions of this chapter shall be administered and enforced by the consumer protection and antitrust bureau, department of justice, established in RSA 21-M:9.

Source. 1977, 468:1. 1979, 171:1. 1985, 300:17.
