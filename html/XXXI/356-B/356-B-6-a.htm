TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 356-B
CONDOMINIUM ACT

I. General Principles

Section 356-B:6-a

    356-B:6-a Applicability of Chapter. –
    I. In the event of a conflict between the declaration or bylaws, the provisions of RSA 356-B shall control.
    II. A unit owners association may amend or revise the condominium documents to comply with the requirements of RSA 356-B without the need to obtain mortgagee approval if the changes do not affect the mortgagee's equity or security interest in the property.

Source. 2016, 311:4, eff. Aug. 1, 2016.
