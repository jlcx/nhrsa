TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 358-S
MARTIAL ARTS SCHOOLS

Section 358-S:4

    358-S:4 List of Membership Plans. –
    I. Each martial arts school operating in this state shall prepare a comprehensive list of the courses offered by the martial arts school and the respective price of each course. The list shall be shown to each prospective purchaser of a membership plan.
    II. A martial arts school is prohibited from selling courses not included in the list and in the registration statement required by RSA 358-S:2, I.
    III. This section shall not apply to scholarships given by martial arts schools.

Source. 2007, 281:1, eff. July 1, 2007.
