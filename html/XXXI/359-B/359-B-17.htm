TITLE XXXI
TRADE AND COMMERCE

CHAPTER 359-B
CONSUMER CREDIT REPORTING

Section 359-B:17

    359-B:17 Civil Liability for Negligent Noncompliance. – Any consumer reporting agency or user of information which is negligent in failing to comply with any requirement imposed under this title with respect to any consumer is liable to that consumer in an amount equal to the sum of:
    I. Any actual damages sustained by the consumer as a result of the failure or $1,000, whichever is greater;
    II. In the case of any successful action to enforce any liability under this section, the costs of the action together with reasonable attorney's fees as determined by the court.

Source. 1971, 430:1. 2006, 208:3, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
