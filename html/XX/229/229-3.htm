TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 229
HIGHWAY SYSTEM IN THE STATE

Section 229:3

    229:3 Turnpikes and System of Interstate and Defense Highways. – The turnpikes, as established by RSA 237, and the approved national system of interstate and defense highways, shall be a part of the primary state highway system.

Source. RSA 230:2-a. 1961, 4:1. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
