TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 232
COUNTIES AND COUNTY COMMISSIONERS

Laying Out Highways

Section 232:1-a

    232:1-a Petition to Lay Out. – Petitions for laying out or altering class IV, V or VI highways may be filed in the office of the clerk of the superior court in the following cases:
    I. When it passes over land not in any town; and
    II. When it passes over lands in 2 or more towns, one of which is in the county.

Source. 1982, 41:16, eff. May 28, 1982.
