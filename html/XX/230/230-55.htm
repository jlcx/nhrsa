TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 230
STATE HIGHWAYS

Discontinuance of Relocated Portions of Class I and
Class II Highways

Section 230:55

    230:55 Notice of Finding. – Whenever the commissioner of transportation shall alter or relocate any portion of any class I or class II highway, and finds that there is no further occasion to use such portion for class I or class II highway purposes, he shall post notice of such finding in 2 public places in the town in which land is situate and give notice in writing to the selectmen of such town.

Source. 1945, 188:1, part 8:1. 1950, 5:1, part 9:1, par. 2. RSA 237:1. 1981, 87:1. 1985, 402:6, I(b)(2).
