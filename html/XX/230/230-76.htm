TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 230
STATE HIGHWAYS

Planning and Laying Out Bicycle Routes

Section 230:76

    230:76 Cooperation Among Agencies. – For the purposes of this subdivision the department of resources and economic development shall assist the commissioner of transportation in matters relating to recreational use and the availability of government funds which may be used for purposes of this subdivision. The department of safety shall assist the commission in matters relating to bicycle safety.

Source. RSA 250:28. 1979, 486:2. 1981, 87:1. 1985, 402:6, I(b)(2).
