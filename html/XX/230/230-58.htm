TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 230
STATE HIGHWAYS

Discontinuance of Relocated Portions of Class I and
Class II Highways

Section 230:58

    230:58 Notice of Discontinuance. – Upon the filing of notice with the commissioner of transportation that such occasion does not exist, the commissioner shall post notice in 2 public places in such town that such portion of highway is thereupon discontinued.

Source. 1945, 188:1, part 8:4. 1949, 12:2. 1950, 5:1, part 9:1, par. 2. RSA 237:4. 1981, 87:1. 1985, 402:6, I(b)(2).
