TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 237
TURNPIKE SYSTEM

Regional Electronic Toll Collection

Section 237:16-b

    237:16-b Regional Electronic Toll Collection Authorized. – The commissioner is hereby authorized to execute all documents and perform all other acts necessary to enter into and carry out the provisions of a regional electronic toll collection system agreement with the Interagency Group in order to increase the efficiency of turnpike operation and to improve traffic management in the state and region.

Source. 2001, 247:1, eff. July 13, 2001.
