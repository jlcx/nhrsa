TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 234
BRIDGES AND BRIDGE AID

Portsmouth-Kittery Bridge

Section 234:57

    234:57 Declaration of Purpose. – It is hereby declared that the purposes of this subdivision are public and that the Authority shall be regarded as performing a governmental function in the carrying out of the provisions of this subdivision.

Source. 1936, 4:16. RSA 258:15. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
