TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 234
BRIDGES AND BRIDGE AID

Portsmouth-Kittery Bridge

Section 234:53

    234:53 Interim Expenses. – All expenses incurred by the Authority in the interim between the execution of the compact or agreement and the date money received from grants, bonds or revenues, whichever shall be available first, shall be a charge upon the highway funds in an amount not exceeding $2,500.

Source. 1936, 4:11. RSA 258:11. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
