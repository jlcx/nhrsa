TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 238-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE RAIL TRANSIT AUTHORITY

Section 238-A:2

    238-A:2 Authority Established. – There is hereby established the New Hampshire rail transit authority for the general purpose of developing and providing commuter rail or other similar forms of passenger rail service. The authority shall be an administratively attached agency, pursuant to RSA 21-G:10, to the department of transportation.

Source. 2007, 360:1, eff. July 17, 2007. 2009, 150:2, eff. July 8, 2009.
