TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 238-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE RAIL TRANSIT AUTHORITY

Section 238-A:12-a

    238-A:12-a Semiannual Reports. – The authority shall provide informational reports on its activities to the capital budget overview committee on March 30 and September 30 of each year.

Source. 2010, 201:2, eff. Aug. 21, 2010.
