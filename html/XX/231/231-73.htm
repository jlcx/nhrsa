TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Repair of Highways by Towns

Section 231:73

    231:73 Procedure. – The court may order such part of the expense to be paid to the town, or cause the highway to be put in repair in such manner as it thinks proper, and draw its order for such part upon the county treasurer.

Source. RS 55:19. CS 59:20. GS 66:3. GL 72:3. PS 73:3. PL 80:5. RL 96:5. 1945, 188:1, part 16:17. RSA 245:18. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
