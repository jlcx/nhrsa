TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Discontinuance of Class IV, V and VI Highways

Section 231:47

    231:47 Consent of Court. – Towns may not discontinue or discontinue as an open highway and make subject to gates and bars any highway concerning which proceedings are pending in court against the town for neglect or refusal to lay out, make or repair such highway, without the consent of the court.

Source. RS 54:2. CS 58:2. GS 65:2. GL 71:2. PS 72:2. PL 79:2. RL 95:2. 1945, 188:1, part 9:3. RSA 238:3. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
