TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Laying Out Highways

Section 231:7

    231:7 Applicable Provisions. – The provisions of RSA 230:9, 10, 11 and 12 are applicable to the mayor and aldermen of the city, the selectmen of the town and commissioners of a village district, in laying out class IV, V or VI highways.

Source. RS 49:9-11. CS 52:9-11. GS 61:9-11. GL 67:9, 10, 13. PS 67:10, 11, 14. PL 74:10, 11, 14. RL 90:11, 12, 15. 1945, 188:1, part 3:4-6. RSA 232:4-232:7. 1959, 294:1. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
