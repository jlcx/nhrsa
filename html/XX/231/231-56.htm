TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Special Limited Access Highways

Section 231:56

    231:56 Restrictions. – The provisions of RSA 230:47, 48, 52, 53 and 54 relative to exclusion of commercial enterprises, regulations and penalties, shall, insofar as may be consistent herewith, apply to the limited access highways hereby authorized.

Source. RSA 236-A:4. 1961, 25:1. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
