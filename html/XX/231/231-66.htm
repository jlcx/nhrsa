TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Repair of Highways by Towns

Section 231:66

    231:66 Compensation; Statements. – The compensation of such agents shall be fixed by the town or selectmen, and they shall render to the selectmen weekly statements of their expenditures, and shall receive money from the treasurer only on the order of the selectmen.

Source. 1893, 29:3. 1913, 14:1. 1915, 171:1. 1917, 49:1. PL 80:12. RL 96:12. 1945, 188:1, part 16:9. RSA 245:10. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
