TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Use of Highways by Aqueduct and Gas Companies

Section 231:186

    231:186 Liability for Damages. – They shall be liable for all damages occasioned to the town or city, or to any person, by an act so done or by any negligence connected therewith.

Source. 1854, 1527:1. GS 141:1. 1868, 1:40. GL 155:1. PS 82:2. PL 98:3. RL 114:3. 1945, 188:1, part 25:3. RSA 255:4. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
