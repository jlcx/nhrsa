TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Sidewalks

Section 231:111

    231:111 Construction of Sidewalks. – The mayor and aldermen of any city, upon petition, may construct sidewalks therein, with or without edgestones, and covered with any appropriate material, and for that purpose may widen and straighten any highway as in other cases, except that the notice of proceedings shall state that the construction of a sidewalk is contemplated.

Source. 1875, 36:1. GL 78:1. PS 79:1. PL 95:1. RL 111:1. 1945, 188:1, part 22:1. RSA 252:1. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
