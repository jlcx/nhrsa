TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Street Names and Markers

Section 231:136

    231:136 Where in Force. – RSA 231:134 and RSA 231:135 shall be in force in cities and in such towns as shall, at any regular meeting by a majority vote, adopt the same.

Source. 1911, 79:4. PL 93:8. RL 109:8. 1945, 188:1, part 21:4. RSA 251:4. 1981, 87:1. 1991, 53:4, eff. July 5, 1991.
