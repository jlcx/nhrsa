TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Street Names and Markers

Section 231:135

    231:135 Lost Bounds; Exceptions. – All streets affected by this subdivision, the bounds of which have been lost, shall be resurveyed so that the provisions of this subdivision may be carried out. Principal streets which have curbings and corners of stone or other similar material, or permanent, durable sidewalks on both sides thereof, shall not be subject to the provisions of this section; but all streets hereafter laid out shall be constructed in accordance with this subdivision.

Source. 1911, 79:3. PL 93:7. RL 109:7. 1945, 188:1, part 21:3. RSA 251:3. 1981, 87:1. 1991, 53:3, eff. July 5, 1991.
