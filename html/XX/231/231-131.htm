TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Parking Meters

Section 231:131

    231:131 Revenue. – The revenue derived from the use of such meters shall be expended to finance the purchase, maintenance and policing of such meters or to maintain and improve streets and highways, or to acquire, construct, improve, maintain and manage public parking areas and public transportation systems, or for any combination of the foregoing purposes, but for no purpose not expressly authorized in this section.

Source. 1947, 74:2. RSA 249:2. 1981, 87:1. 1993, 14:1, eff. May 25, 1993.
