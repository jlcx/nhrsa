TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Repair of Highways by Towns

Section 231:68

    231:68 Accounts. – Highway agents shall keep accurate accounts, showing in detail all moneys received by them, from whom and when received and all moneys paid out by them, to whom and for what purpose. They shall settle their accounts before January 1 annually, and the same shall be printed in the annual town report in detail.

Source. 1893, 29:6; 70:1. PL 80:14. RL 96:14. 1945, 188:1, part 16:11. RSA 245:12. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
