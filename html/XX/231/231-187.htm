TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Use of Highways by Aqueduct and Gas Companies

Section 231:187

    231:187 Petition for Easement. – Selectmen, upon petition of any aqueduct or gaslight corporation or company, or of any person who supplies water or gas to people for hire, may lay out for the petitioner an easement to place and maintain pipes and other structures for conveying water or gas in any unaccepted street or private way in the town if they find that the public good requires it.

Source. 1877, 55:1, 2. GL 155:2, 3. PS 82:3. PL 98:4. RL 114:4. 1945, 188:1, part 25:4. RSA 255:5. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
