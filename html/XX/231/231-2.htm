TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Laying Out Highways

Section 231:2

    231:2 Class IV Compact Section Highways. – All class IV highways shall be wholly constructed, reconstructed and maintained by the city or town in which they are located, and no state funds shall be expended thereon except as may be authorized by RSA 235.

Source. 1945, 188:1, part 2:7. 1949, 79:2. RSA 231:7. 1955, 333:1. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
