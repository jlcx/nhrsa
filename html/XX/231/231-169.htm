TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Lines of Telegraph and Other Companies in Highways

Section 231:169

    231:169 Joint Licenses. – Joint licenses for erecting or installing and maintaining any jointly owned poles, structures, conduits, cables and wires may be granted under the provisions hereof to 2 or more petitioners.

Source. 1935, 100:1. 1937, 102:5. RL 113:11. 1945, 188:1, part 24:10. RSA 254:11. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
