TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Repair of Highways by Towns

Section 231:58

    231:58 Collection. – All appropriations for the repair of highways in towns shall be committed to the collector of taxes and be collected as other taxes.

Source. RS 55:12. CS 59:13. GS 66:22. GL 72:22. PS 73:5. 1893, 29:2. 1899, 97:1. PL 80:2. RL 96:2. 1945, 188:1, part 16:2. RSA 245:2. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
