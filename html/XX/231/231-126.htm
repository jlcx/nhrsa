TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Public Parking Facilities

Section 231:126

    231:126 Abatement of Assessments. – For good cause shown, the assessors may abate any such assessment made by them or by their predecessors.

Source. RSA 252-A:13. 1969, 493:1. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
