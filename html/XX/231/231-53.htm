TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Special Limited Access Highways

Section 231:53

    231:53 Limited Access Highways. – Notwithstanding any other provisions of law, selectmen of towns and mayors and aldermen of cities are hereby authorized to lay out limited access highways.

Source. RSA 236-A:1. 1961, 25:1. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
