TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Trees and Roadside Growth

Section 231:143

    231:143 Appropriation. – Such sums of money as the town may appropriate, or as are available, may be used to carry out the provisions of RSA 231:139-142.

Source. 1909, 93:1. 1925, 89:1. PL 93:26. RL 109:26. 1945, 188:1, part 23:5. RSA 253:5. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
