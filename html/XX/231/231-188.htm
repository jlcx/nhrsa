TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Use of Highways by Aqueduct and Gas Companies

Section 231:188

    231:188 Procedure. – They shall proceed upon such petition the same as in the laying out of a highway; and the owner of the land in which the easement is taken shall have like remedy by appeal.

Source. 1877, 55:2, 3. GL 155:3, 4. PS 82:4. PL 98:5. RL 114:5. 1945, 188:1, part 25:5. RSA 255:6. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
