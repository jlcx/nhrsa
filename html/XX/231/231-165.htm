TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Lines of Telegraph and Other Companies in Highways

Section 231:165

    231:165 Services and Fees. –
    I. If the proprietors of the line are the petitioners, they shall pay the town clerk for his services and fees.
    II. If a landowner is the petitioner he shall advance the payment for such services and fees, and if his petition is sustained he may recover the same from the proprietors of the line.
    III. The town clerk shall be entitled to a fee of $10, which shall be remitted when the license is recorded.

Source. PS 81:8. PL 97:9. 1935, 100:1. RL 113:6. 1945, 188:1, part 24:6. RSA 254:7. 1981, 87:1; 360:1. 1991, 252:1, eff. Aug. 9, 1991.
