TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Laying Out Highways

Section 231:15

    231:15 Assessment of Damages. – The selectmen shall assess the damages sustained by each owner of land or other property taken for such highway.

Source. RS 40:13. CS 52:16. GS 61:15. GL 67:19. PS 67:18. PL 74:18. RL 90:19. 1945, 188:1, part 5:12. RSA 234:12. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
