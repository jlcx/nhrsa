TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Street Names and Markers

Section 231:134

    231:134 Corners and Angles. – The corners and angles of all streets shall be marked by a durable marker of stone, metal or other material, of such size and construction that it can be readily found.

Source. 1911, 79:2. PL 93:6. RL 109:6. 1945, 188:1, part 21:2. RSA 251:2. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
