TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Lines of Telegraph and Other Companies in Highways

Section 231:178

    231:178 Service of Notice. – Such notice may be served by any agent of the department of transportation or by the highway agent of any city or town on such utility or any agent or officer thereof.

Source. 1945, 188:1, part 24:19. RSA 254:20. 1981, 87:1. 1985, 402:6, I(a)(3).
