TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Lines of Telegraph and Other Companies in Highways

Section 231:181

    231:181 Time of Removal. – All such poles shall be removed within the time designated, and, if not removed by the date stated in such notice, may be forthwith removed by the agency giving notice at the expense of the owner.

Source. 1945, 188:1, part 24:22. RSA 254:23. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
