TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Lines of Telegraph and Other Companies in Highways

Section 231:174

    231:174 No Prescriptive Right. – No enjoyment by a person, copartnership, or corporation for any length of time of the privilege of having or maintaining wires and their supports and appurtenances in, upon, over, or attached to any building or land of other persons, shall create an easement or raise any presumption of a grant thereof.

Source. 1881, 54:2. PS 81:9. PL 97:12. 1935, 100:1. RL 113:16. 1945, 188:1, part 24:15. RSA 254:16. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
