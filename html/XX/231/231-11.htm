TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Laying Out Highways

Section 231:11

    231:11 Hearing. – At the time and place so appointed the selectmen shall make a personal examination of the several routes proposed, and of the highways for which such new highway is designed to be a substitute, shall hear all parties interested who may attend and any evidence they may offer, and may adjourn as they see cause.

Source. RS 49:7. CS 52:7. GS 61:7. GL 67:7. PS 67:8. PL 74:8. RL 90:9. 1945, 188:1, part 5:8. RSA 234:8. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
