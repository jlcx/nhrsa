TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Laying Out Highways

Section 231:16

    231:16 Return. – They shall make a return of the highway or any alteration by them laid out, describing the same and the width thereof, and cause the same to be recorded by the town clerk.

Source. RS 49:12. CS 52:15. GS 61:14. GL 67:18. PS 67:17. PL 74:17. 1937, 97:1. RL 90:18. 1945, 188:1, part 5:13. RSA 234:13. 1971, 526:5. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
