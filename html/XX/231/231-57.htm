TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Repair of Highways by Towns

Section 231:57

    231:57 Levy of Highway Taxes. – Each town shall, at each annual meeting, raise and appropriate for the repair of highways and bridges within such town a sum not less than 1/4 of one percent of the valuation of the ratable estate on which other taxes are assessed by the town; and in addition thereto may raise as much more as it may deem necessary; providing, that no town shall be required to raise under the provisions of this subdivision more than $50 per mile for the repair of highways and bridges regularly maintained within the town.

Source. RS 55:1. CS 59:1. GS 66:1. GL 72:1. PS 73:1. 1893, 29:2. 1899, 29:2. PL 80:1. RL 96:1. 1945, 188:1, part 16:1. RSA 245:1. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
