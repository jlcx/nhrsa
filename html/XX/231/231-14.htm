TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Laying Out Highways

Section 231:14

    231:14 Apportionment of Cost. – The cost of such laying out or altering shall be apportioned between the towns by the selectmen acting as aforesaid; and their return shall not take effect until the apportionment is made.

Source. 1859, 2219:3. GS 61:19. GL 67:23. PS 67:21. PL 74:21. RL 90:22. 1945, 188:1, part 5:11. RSA 234:11. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
