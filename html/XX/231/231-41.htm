TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Laying Out Rights-of-Way for Removal of Lumber

Section 231:41

    231:41 Use and Discontinuance. – The selectmen may fix the length of time and conditions under which such right-of-way may be used and may order the same closed or discontinued when in their judgment it is necessary.

Source. RSA 234-A:2. 1963, 274:2. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
