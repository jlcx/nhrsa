TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Laying Out Highways

Section 231:1

    231:1 Class IV, V and VI. – All class IV highways not financed in whole or in part with federal aid highway funds, and class V and VI highways shall be laid out by the mayor and aldermen of the city, the selectmen of the town or the commissioners of a village district formed for the purpose of RSA 52:1, I(m) in which such highways are located, or by the superior court as hereinafter provided. In the case of a village district formed for the purpose of RSA 52:1, I(m), references in this title to "town'' and "selectmen'' shall be deemed to be references to "village district'' and "village district commissioners'', respectively.

Source. 1945, 188:1, part 3:2. RSA 232:2. 1967, 157:2. 1975, 455:3. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
