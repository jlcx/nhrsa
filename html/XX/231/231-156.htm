TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Trees and Roadside Growth

Section 231:156

    231:156 Penalty. – Any person who violates any provision of this subdivision or any rule or regulation thereunder made by the commissioner of transportation shall be guilty of a violation if a natural person, or guilty of a misdemeanor if any other person. Any person shall be liable for all damage occasioned thereby.

Source. 1901, 98:8. PL 93:37. RL 109:37. 1945, 188:1, part 23:16. 1950, 5:1, part 9:1, par. 2. RSA 253:16. 1973, 530:34. 1981, 87:1. 1985, 402:6, I(b)(3).
