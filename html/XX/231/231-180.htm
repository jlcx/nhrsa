TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Lines of Telegraph and Other Companies in Highways

Section 231:180

    231:180 Defining Location. – The location defined in such notice of any pole so removed, together with the wires thereon, shall be of the same validity as if made under a permit by the commissioner of transportation in case of state maintained highways or selectmen or mayor and aldermen in case of other highways.

Source. 1945, 188:1, part 24:21. RSA 254:22. 1981, 87:1. 1985, 402:6, I(b)(3).
