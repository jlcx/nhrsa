TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Lines of Telegraph and Other Companies in Highways

Section 231:159

    231:159 Applicability of Subdivision. – The provisions of this subdivision apply to all cities and towns now or hereafter incorporated, except such provisions thereof as may have been or are hereafter specifically amended or repealed in the act of incorporation.

Source. 1949, 287:1. RSA 254:1. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
