TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 231
CITIES, TOWNS AND VILLAGE DISTRICT HIGHWAYS

Use of Highways by Aqueduct and Gas Companies

Section 231:189

    231:189 Malicious Injury to Aqueducts or Pipes; Penalty. – Any person who shall wantonly and maliciously injure any aqueduct, or the pipes, logs or other property of any gaslight company, aqueduct company or person, shall be liable to pay treble damages to such company or person in an action on the case, and shall be guilty of a misdemeanor if a natural person, or guilty of a felony if any other person.

Source. 1854, 1527:2. GS 141:2. 1868, 1:41. GL 155:5. PS 82:5. PL 98:6. RL 114:6. 1945, 188:1, part 25:6. RSA 255:7. 1973, 529:45. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
