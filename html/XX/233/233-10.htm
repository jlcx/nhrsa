TITLE XX
TRANSPORTATION

CHAPTER 233
RECREATIONAL ROADS

Section 233:10

    233:10 Class V Public Roads to Private Recreational Areas. – Any class V public road which leads to a private recreational area shall be maintained either by the operator of such area or by the town or towns in which said road is located unless otherwise specifically provided.

Source. RSA 231:8-a. 1965, 228:4. 1981, 87:1, eff. April 20, 1981.
