TITLE XIX
PUBLIC RECREATION

CHAPTER 216-B
HAMPTON HARBOR CHANNEL AND BEACH EROSION CONTROL

Section 216-B:5

    216-B:5 Contracts for Construction. – Any contracts by the state shall be administered by the commissioner of transportation in accordance with RSA 228 as amended.

Source. 1963, 333:1. 2004, 257:33, eff. June 15, 2004.
