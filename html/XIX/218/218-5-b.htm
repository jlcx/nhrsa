TITLE XIX
PUBLIC RECREATION

CHAPTER 218
DIRECTOR OF PARKS AND RECREATION

Section 218:5-b

    218:5-b Discounts; Division of Parks and Recreation. – The director of the division of parks and recreation is hereby instructed to issue day use coupon books reflecting a 20 percent discount from the usual rates charged at state parks during the summer months.

Source. 1973, 503:1. 1977, 384:3, eff. Sept. 3, 1977.
