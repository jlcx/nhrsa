TITLE XIX
PUBLIC RECREATION

CHAPTER 218
DIRECTOR OF PARKS AND RECREATION

Section 218:16

    218:16 Passes. – In recognition of promotional or reciprocal cooperation, the director of parks and recreation, with the approval of the commissioner, may grant or permit the Cannon Mountain manager to grant special daily or seasonal passes to persons to use recreational or other areas under his or her jurisdiction under the following conditions: no more than one such pass, including not more than one other person, shall be assigned to an individual and use of the same shall not be transferable to others than members of the family of said recipient. Any pass of seasonal duration shall include individualized identification. The commissioner shall establish other conditions for the issuance of said passes and the director shall cancel said passes for any violation of said conditions.

Source. 1967, 352:3, eff. July 3, 1967. 2016, 207:6, eff. June 6, 2016.
