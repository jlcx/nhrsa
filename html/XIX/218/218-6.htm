TITLE XIX
PUBLIC RECREATION

CHAPTER 218
DIRECTOR OF PARKS AND RECREATION

Section 218:6

    218:6 Procuring Insurance. – The director of the division of parks and recreation, subject to the approval of the commissioner of resources and economic development, shall have power to protect merchandise purchased for resale situated in the forestry and recreation building at Franconia Notch, Franconia, New Hampshire, against fire and water damage, and such insurance shall be purchased through the department of administrative services, division of procurement and support services.

Source. 1951, 98:1. RSA 218:6. 1994, 158:13, eff. May 23, 1994. 2014, 327:56, eff. Aug. 2, 2014.
