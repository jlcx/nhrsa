TITLE XIX
PUBLIC RECREATION

CHAPTER 227-D
MONADNOCK ADVISORY COMMISSION

Section 227-D:3

    227-D:3 Tax Exemption. – The land presently owned by the Society for the Protection of New Hampshire Forests on Gap mountain in Troy shall hereafter be exempt from local property taxes normally imposed by the town of Troy so long as the Society and the state of New Hampshire as lessee of the Society for the Protection of New Hampshire Forests shall use said lands for recreational, wildlife, open space and forestry purposes only. Any leases adopted under this section shall not preclude the department of resources and economic development from charging fees for the use of lands specified in this section.

Source. 1975, 432:1, eff. July 1, 1975.
