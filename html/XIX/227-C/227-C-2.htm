TITLE XIX
PUBLIC RECREATION

CHAPTER 227-C
HISTORIC PRESERVATION

General Provisions

Section 227-C:2

    227-C:2 State Historic Preservation Office. – The division of historical resources, department of cultural resources established by RSA 21-K:7 shall also be known as the state historic preservation office. The director, division of historical resources shall also be known as the state historic preservation officer.

Source. 1974, 32:1. 1985, 345:3. 1990, 73:1. 1998, 363:3, eff. Aug. 25, 1998.
