TITLE XIX
PUBLIC RECREATION

CHAPTER 216-D
APPALACHIAN NATIONAL SCENIC TRAIL

Section 216-D:4

    216-D:4 Use of Funds. – The department of resources and economic development is hereby authorized to use any funds which may become available to carry out this chapter. Any available funds from the United States Land and Water Conservation Fund or other federal assistance programs may also be used to accomplish this purpose.

Source. 1971, 279:1, eff. Aug. 22, 1971.
