TITLE XIX
PUBLIC RECREATION

CHAPTER 216-A
EXPANSION OF STATE PARK SYSTEM

Section 216-A:3-b

    216-A:3-b Authority for Payment. – The governor is authorized to draw his warrant for the payments authorized under the provisions of RSA 216-A:3-a out of any money in the treasury not otherwise appropriated.

Source. 1963, 308:1, eff. April 1, 1964.
