TITLE XIX
PUBLIC RECREATION

CHAPTER 216-A
EXPANSION OF STATE PARK SYSTEM

Conservation Corps

Section 216-A:17

    216-A:17 Applicability of Other Statutes. – All provisions of RSA 216-F, multi-use statewide trail system, shall be applicable to the department for the purposes of carrying out this chapter.

Source. 1990, 263:3, eff. April 28, 1990.
