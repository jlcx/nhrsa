TITLE XIX
PUBLIC RECREATION

CHAPTER 216-A
EXPANSION OF STATE PARK SYSTEM

Conservation Corps

Section 216-A:7

    216-A:7 Conservation Corps Established. – The director of the division of parks and recreation, in cooperation with the commissioner of the department of health and human services, shall establish a conservation corps for the purpose of employing New Hampshire youth with an emphasis on employing young people who need educational and vocational assistance to perform successfully in school, in their communities, and in their personal lives. The services of these young people shall be used to create a hiking trail extending the length of the state or such other resource management and conservation projects deemed appropriate and necessary.

Source. 1988, 265:1. 1994, 212:2. 1995, 310:182, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
