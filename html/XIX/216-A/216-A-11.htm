TITLE XIX
PUBLIC RECREATION

CHAPTER 216-A
EXPANSION OF STATE PARK SYSTEM

Conservation Corps

Section 216-A:11

    216-A:11 New Hampshire Heritage Trail. – Pursuant to RSA 4:43, the general court hereby designates and names the trail as authorized by RSA 216-A:7 and 8 as the New Hampshire Heritage Trail.

Source. 1988, 265:1. 1990, 263:2, eff. April 28, 1990.
