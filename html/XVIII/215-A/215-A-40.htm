TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 215-A
OFF HIGHWAY RECREATIONAL VEHICLES AND TRAILS

Budget

Section 215-A:40

    215-A:40 Operating Budget. – The commissioner of the department of resources and economic development shall ensure that all sources of funding of the bureau of trails are reflected in the operating budget documents submitted by the department.

Source. 1987, 292:8. 1993, 53:11, eff. June 15, 1993.
