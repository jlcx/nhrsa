TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 214
LICENSES

Hunting, Fishing, and Trapping

Section 214:14-c

    214:14-c One-Day Licenses. – The executive director is hereby authorized to establish within state parks, in cooperation with the department of resources and economic development, such license agencies as he determines necessary to distribute a special one-day fishing license for the purpose of fishing within state parks when the land totally encompasses the entire lake or pond. Rules, fees, and licensing procedures shall be adopted by the executive director in accordance with RSA 541-A.

Source. 1985, 291:17, eff. Jan. 1, 1986.
