TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 214
LICENSES

Special Licenses

Section 214:33

    214:33 Federal Permit. – Any person holding a scientific or taxidermy permit must also hold such license or permit as is required under federal laws.

Source. 1935, 124:7. RL 247:22. 1997, 10:22, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
