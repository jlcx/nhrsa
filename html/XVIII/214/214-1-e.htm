TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 214
LICENSES

Hunting, Fishing, and Trapping

Section 214:1-e

    214:1-e Fishing Without a License. – The executive director may adopt rules under RSA 541-A establishing one day per year on which persons normally required to hold a license under RSA 214:1 may fish by angling only, in any public waters within the jurisdiction of the state without a license.

Source. 1989, 101:1, eff. May 1, 1989.
