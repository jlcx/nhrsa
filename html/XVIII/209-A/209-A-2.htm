TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 209-A
FALCONRY

Section 209-A:2

    209-A:2 Purpose. – Notwithstanding RSA 207:3 or any law to the contrary, the general court recognizes that falconry is a legal means of taking game and a legal basis for keeping otherwise protected raptorial birds.

Source. 1987, 130:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1988.
