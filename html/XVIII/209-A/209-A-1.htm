TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 209-A
FALCONRY

Section 209-A:1

    209-A:1 Definitions. – In this chapter:
    I. "Department'' means the department of fish and game.
    II. "Executive director'' means the executive director of the department of fish and game.
    III. "Falconry'' means the sport of taking quarry by means of a trained raptor.
    IV. "Raptor'' means a live migratory bird of the family Accipitridae or of the family Falconidae.

Source. 1987, 130:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1988.
