TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 210
FUR-BEARING ANIMALS

Section 210:2

    210:2 Raccoons. – No person shall hunt raccoons at night by the use of a rifle, revolver, or pistol larger than .22 caliber long rifle or by the use of shotgun shells carrying shot larger than number 4. Lights may be used to take or attempt to take raccoons, except by use of a light from a motor vehicle.

Source. 1935, 124:4. 1937, 132:1. 1939, 215:1. RL 244:3. 1947, 116:1. 1953, 189:1. RSA 210:2. 1959, 151:1. 1967, 27:1; 208:1. 1969, 404:1. 1977, 23:1. 1981, 148:3. 1989, 33:1, eff. June 11, 1989.
