TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 210
FUR-BEARING ANIMALS

Licenses for Trapping and Fur Buying

Section 210:20

    210:20 Expiration Date. – Notwithstanding any of the general provisions for the expiration of fish and game licenses, any license to trap fur-bearing animals and any license to buy furs under RSA 214:24 shall expire on June 30 of each year.

Source. 1969, 93:1, eff. June 30, 1970.
