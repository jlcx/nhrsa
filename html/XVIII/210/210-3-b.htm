TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 210
FUR-BEARING ANIMALS

Section 210:3-b

    210:3-b Marine Mammals. – It shall be unlawful for any person to possess or take any marine mammals except as provided for in the Marine Mammal Protection Act and under the rules adopted by the executive director pursuant to RSA 207:14 and RSA 211:62. Notwithstanding any provisions to the contrary, whoever violates this section shall be guilty of a violation.

Source. 1969, 452:1. 1977, 588:37. 1993, 51:1, eff. June 15, 1993.
