TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 210
FUR-BEARING ANIMALS

Traps, Spring Guns, and Snares

Section 210:15

    210:15 Protection of Bear Traps. – A person who sets or causes to be set a bear trap shall build in a suitable manner, and maintain around the same, a railing or guard not less than 3 feet high, and shall post not less than 2 painted signs, with the words "Bear Trap'' thereon in letters not less than 3 inches in height, on such railing or guard.

Source. 1935, 124:4. 1937, 188:16. RL 244:15.
