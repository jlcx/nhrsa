TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 210
FUR-BEARING ANIMALS

Section 210:4

    210:4 Muskrat House, Dens, Etc. – No person shall at any time destroy or injure a muskrat house, den or burrow, or place a trap within 15 feet thereof. No person shall at any time injure or destroy the house, den or burrow used by any game animal or fur-bearing animal.

Source. 1935, 124:4. RL 244:5. RSA 210:4. 1975, 33:1, eff. May 16, 1975.
