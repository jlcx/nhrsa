TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 210
FUR-BEARING ANIMALS

Traps, Spring Guns, and Snares

Section 210:12

    210:12 Time for. – No person shall set or arrange any trap prior to the first day of the open season for trapping any particular fur-bearing animal.

Source. 1947, 42:1, eff. Mar. 18, 1947.
