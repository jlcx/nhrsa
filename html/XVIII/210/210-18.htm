TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 210
FUR-BEARING ANIMALS

Traps, Spring Guns, and Snares

Section 210:18

    210:18 Damage to Domestic Animals. – Any person causing injury or damage to domestic animals, except for dogs at large pursuant to RSA 466:33, by the aid or use of traps shall be liable to the owner therefor. An injury to a licensed dog at large shall be reported to the town or city listed on the dog's tag, and to the owner of the dog if identifiable.

Source. 1935, 124:4. RL 244:18. 2004, 65:1, eff. July 3, 2004.
