TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 210
FUR-BEARING ANIMALS

Traps, Spring Guns, and Snares

Section 210:17

    210:17 Snares. – The executive director may adopt rules, under RSA 541-A, relative to the use of snares for the taking of wildlife.

Source. 1935, 124:4. RL 244:18. RSA 210:17. 1986, 233:1, 3, I. 2001, 92:1, eff. Aug. 20, 2001.
