TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 210
FUR-BEARING ANIMALS

Traps, Spring Guns, and Snares

Section 210:16

    210:16 Spring Guns. – No person shall set or use at any time any device, the object of which is to discharge a firearm, for the purpose of taking game or fur-bearing animals.

Source. 1935, 124:4. RL 244:16.
