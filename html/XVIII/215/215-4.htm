TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 215
REGISTRATION AND LICENSING OF GUIDES

Section 215:4

    215:4 Fee for License. – The executive director shall set the fees for resident and nonresident licenses to guide under this chapter pursuant to RSA 206:10, I.

Source. 1935, 124:8. RL 248:4. 1951, 181:6. RSA 215:4. 1961, 190:2. 1975, 168:1. 1983, 251:2. 1986, 214:15, eff. Nov. 15, 1986. 2015, 186:30, eff. July 1, 2015.
