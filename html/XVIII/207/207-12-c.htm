TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Dogs

Section 207:12-c

    207:12-c Use and Training of Leashed Tracking Dogs. – Notwithstanding any provision of this title to the contrary, a handler with a leashed dog may track wounded dear, moose, or bear. The executive director shall adopt rules pursuant to RSA 541-A, relative to the method and manner of take, time periods for training or using leashed tracking dogs, licensing requirements and fees, possession of recovered deer, moose, or bear, notification requirements, and other provisions deemed necessary to effectively regulate this practice. No person shall use an intact carcass of a deer, moose, or bear for training purposes, but may use samples of blood, tissue, and hide of a deer, moose, or bear legally taken or acquired in this state.

Source. 2006, 65:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
