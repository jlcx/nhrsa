TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Collecting Seaweed

Section 207:50

    207:50 Piling Below High-water Mark. – If any person shall pile below high-water mark, for the purpose of hauling it away, any seaweed or rockweed shall be guilty of a violation.

Source. 1973, 532:10, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
