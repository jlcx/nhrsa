TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Collecting Seaweed

Section 207:52

    207:52 Uprooting. – If any person shall pull or take any growing rockweed or sea moss from the rocks, banks or shores of this state, except by cutting same with a knife so as not to detach or injure its roots, he shall be guilty of a violation.

Source. 1973, 532:10, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
