TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Wildlife Damage Control

Section 207:24

    207:24 Appeal From Executive Director. – If the person sustaining the damage pursuant to RSA 207:23-a is dissatisfied with the finding of the executive director, either as to the cause of the damage or the amount thereof, the person may appeal therefrom within 30 days to the superior court in the county where the damage was sustained.

Source. 1935, 124:1. RL 241:22. 1999, 344:5, eff. Nov. 18, 1999.
