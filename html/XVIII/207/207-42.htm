TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Closing Waters to Protect Ice

Section 207:42

    207:42 Expense. – The expense of separating, by suitable marks, monuments or boundaries, the area on which ice is to be harvested shall be borne by the petitioner.

Source. 1935, 124:1. RL 241:41.
