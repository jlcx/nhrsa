TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Dogs

Section 207:13-b

    207:13-b Treed Animals. – It shall be unlawful for any person to shoot any wildlife which has been treed or cornered by a dog, unless the owner of said dog, or a member of his hunting party, is present when the animal is shot. Persons violating the provisions of this section shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1971, 21:1. 1973, 528:113. 1977, 247:4, eff. Aug. 19, 1977.
