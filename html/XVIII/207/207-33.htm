TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Season Closed by Proclamation, Etc.

Section 207:33

    207:33 Woodland Defined. – Woodland includes cut-over land, slash, and such other land as bears a sufficient amount of wood growth, wood, weeds, grass or other growth as to be likely to be burned over.

Source. 1935, 124:1. RL 241:31.
