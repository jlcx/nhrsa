TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Foreign Sporting Clubs

Section 207:43

    207:43 Rights Limited. – No foreign corporation, association, club or similar organization shall hold or acquire property in New Hampshire for the purpose of hunting, fishing, sporting or recreation, without first becoming incorporated in this state. Before becoming incorporated in this state, the purposes of such corporation shall be approved by the executive director.

Source. 1935, 124:1. RL 241:42.
