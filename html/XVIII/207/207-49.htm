TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Collecting Seaweed

Section 207:49

    207:49 Without Leave of Owner of Marsh or Flats. – If any person shall collect or carry away from any salt marsh or flats any flats-weed or any seaweed thrown thereon by the sea or tide, without leave of the owner of the marsh or flats, he shall be guilty of a violation.

Source. 1973, 532:10, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
