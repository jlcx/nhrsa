TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Import, Possession, or Release of Wildlife

Section 207:15-a

    207:15-a Seizure. – All fish or the fry thereof, living wild birds or the eggs thereof or wild animals imported or released in this state contrary to the provisions of this subdivision shall be seized and forfeited as provided in RSA 207:17.

Source. 1961, 11:1, eff. April 28, 1961.
