TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Season Closed by Proclamation, Etc.

Section 207:34

    207:34 Notice. – Such proclamation shall be published in 2 or more newspapers of the state and shall be posted in such places and in such manner as the governor may order.

Source. 1935, 124:1. RL 241:32.
