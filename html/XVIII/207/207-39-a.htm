TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Injuring Property or Person

Section 207:39-a

    207:39-a Shooting Domestic Animals; Liability. – Any person, while actually engaged in hunting or in the pursuit of wild animals or wild birds, who causes death, injury, or damage to domestic animals, ducks, or fowl shall be liable for damages to the owner of the animals.

Source. 1965, 179:1. 1971, 89:1. 2005, 289:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2006.
