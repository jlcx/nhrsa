TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Season Closed by Proclamation, Etc.

Section 207:35

    207:35 Violations. – No person shall drop a lighted cigarette, lighted cigar, lighted match or other article likely to cause fire, within 200 yards of any woodlands during the time such woodlands are closed by such proclamation.

Source. 1935, 124:1. RL 241:33.
