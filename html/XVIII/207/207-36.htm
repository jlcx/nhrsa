TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Injuring Property or Person

Section 207:36

    207:36 Injuring Property. – No person shall tear down, damage or destroy any fence, wall, lock, boat or dock, or leave open any gate or bars, or trample or destroy any crop, on land of another person, while taking, trapping, hunting, or pursuing any fish, game or fur-bearing animal.

Source. 1935, 124:1. RL 241:34. RSA 207:36. 1959, 30:1, eff. May 31, 1959.
