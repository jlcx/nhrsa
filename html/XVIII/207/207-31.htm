TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Season Closed by Proclamation, Etc.

Section 207:31

    207:31 Declaring. – The governor and council, upon the joint recommendation of the executive director of the fish and game department and the director, division of forests and lands, department of resources and economic development, when, in their opinion, the danger of starting fires in the woodlands of the state, during periods of protracted drouth or excessive dryness requires extraordinary precautions, may, by official proclamation, declare any or all of the woodlands of the state closed to hunters, fishermen, trappers and other persons whose presence in such woodlands might create a fire hazard under the circumstances, for such time as they may designate.

Source. 1935, 124:1. RL 241:29.
