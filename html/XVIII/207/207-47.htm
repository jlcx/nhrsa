TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Penalties

Section 207:47

    207:47 For Disobeying Officer. – Any person who shall refuse or neglect to stop when signalled to stop by any conservation officer who is in uniform or who refuses on demand of such officer to produce his fish and game license or to permit such officer to take the license or certificate in hand for the purpose of examination shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1949, 52:2. RSA 207:47. 1973, 528:118, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
