TITLE XVIII
FISH AND GAME

CHAPTER 207
GENERAL PROVISIONS AS TO FISH AND GAME

Injuring Property or Person

Section 207:38-a

    207:38-a Hunting Related Shootings. – Any person while hunting or in the field who shall cause any injury by shooting another human being shall report immediately to the nearest conservation officer or law enforcement officer giving his or her name, address, date of birth, hunting license number and any other information needed to identify that person. Whoever violates the provisions of this section shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1955, 66:2. 1973, 531:50. 1977, 343:2. 1997, 188:3, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
