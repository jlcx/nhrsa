TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 6
ENFORCEMENT AND MODIFICATION OF SUPPORT ORDER AFTER
REGISTRATION

Part B
Contest of Validity or Enforcement

Section 546-B:46

    546-B:46 Confirmed Order. – Confirmation of a registered order, whether by operation of law or after notice and hearing, precludes further contest of the order with respect to any matter that could have been asserted at the time of registration.

Source. 1997, 263:32, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
