TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 9
MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

Section 546-B:58


[RSA 546-B:58 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA 546-B:58 set out below.]
    546-B:58 Short Title. –
    This chapter may be cited as the Uniform Interstate Family Support Act.

[RSA 546-B:58 effective January 1, 2016; see also RSA 546-B:58 set out above.]


    546-B:58 Transitional Provision. --
    This chapter applies to proceedings begun on or after January 1, 2016 to establish a support order or determine parentage of a child or to register, recognize, enforce, or modify a prior support order, determination, or agreement, whenever issued or entered.

Source. 2015, 75:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
