TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 3
CIVIL PROVISIONS OF GENERAL APPLICATION

Section 546-B:30

    546-B:30 Receipt and Disbursement of Payments. – A support enforcement agency or other tribunal of this state shall disburse promptly any amounts received pursuant to a support order, as directed by the order. The agency or tribunal shall furnish to a requesting party or tribunal of another state a certified statement by the custodian of the record of the amounts and dates of all payments received.

Source. 1997, 263:32, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
