TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 3
CIVIL PROVISIONS OF GENERAL APPLICATION

Section 546-B:13


[RSA 546-B:13 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA 546-B:13 set out below.]
    546-B:13 Action by Minor Parent. –
    A minor parent, or a guardian or other legal representative of a minor parent, may maintain a proceeding on behalf of or for the benefit of the minor's child.

[RSA 546-B:13 effective January 1, 2016; see also RSA 546-B:13 set out above.]


    546-B:13 Proceeding by Minor Parent. --
    A minor parent, or a guardian or other legal representative of a minor parent, may maintain a proceeding on behalf of or for the benefit of the minor's child.

Source. 2015, 75:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
