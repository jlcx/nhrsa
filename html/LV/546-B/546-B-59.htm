TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 9
MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

Section 546-B:59

    546-B:59 Severability Clause. – If any provision of this chapter or its application to any person or circumstance is held invalid, the invalidity does not affect other provisions or applications of this chapter which can be given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to this end the provisions of this chapter are severable.

Source. 1997, 263:32, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
