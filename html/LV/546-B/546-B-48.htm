TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 6
ENFORCEMENT AND MODIFICATION OF SUPPORT ORDER AFTER
REGISTRATION

Part C
Registration and Modification of Child Support Order

Section 546-B:48


[RSA 546-B:48 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA 546-B:48 set out below.]
    546-B:48 Effect of Registration for Modification. –
    A tribunal of this state may enforce a child support order of another state registered for purposes of modification, in the same manner as if the order had been issued by a tribunal of this state, but the registered order may be modified only if the requirements of RSA 546-B:49, relative to modification of child support order of another state, have been met.

[RSA 546-B:48 effective January 1, 2016; see also RSA 546-B:48 set out above.]


    546-B:48 Effect of Registration for Modification. --
    A tribunal of this state may enforce a child support order of another state registered for purposes of modification, in the same manner as if the order had been issued by a tribunal of this state, but the registered order may be modified only if the requirements of RSA 546-B:49 or RSA 546-B:51 have been met.

Source. 2015, 75:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
