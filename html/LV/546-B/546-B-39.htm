TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 6
ENFORCEMENT AND MODIFICATION OF SUPPORT ORDER AFTER
REGISTRATION

Part A
Registration and Enforcement of Support Order

Section 546-B:39

    546-B:39 Registration of Order for Enforcement. – A support order or an income-withholding order issued by a tribunal of another state may be registered in this state for enforcement.

Source. 1997, 263:32, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
