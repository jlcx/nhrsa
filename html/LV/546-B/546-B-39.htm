TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 6
ENFORCEMENT AND MODIFICATION OF SUPPORT ORDER AFTER
REGISTRATION

Part A
Registration and Enforcement of Support Order

Section 546-B:39


[RSA 546-B:39 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA 546-B:39 set out below.]
    546-B:39 Registration of Order for Enforcement. –
    A support order or an income-withholding order issued by a tribunal of another state may be registered in this state for enforcement.

[RSA 546-B:39 effective January 1, 2016; see also RSA 546-B:39 set out above.]


    546-B:39 Registration of Order for Enforcement. --
    A support order or income-withholding order issued in another state or a foreign support order may be registered in this state for enforcement.

Source. 2015, 75:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
