TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 9
MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

Section 546-B:57


[RSA 546-B:57 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA 546-B:57 set out below.]
    546-B:57 Rulemaking Authority. –
    The commissioner of the department of health and human services may adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, which are deemed necessary for the efficient administration of the duties with which the department is charged under Title IV-D of the Social Security Act, as amended, to provide interstate child support services, provided that such rules shall not be inconsistent with the provisions of this chapter.
