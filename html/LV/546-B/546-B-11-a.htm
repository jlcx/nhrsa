TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 2
JURISDICTION

Part C
Reconciliation of Multiple Orders

Section 546-B:11-a


[RSA 546-B:11-a effective January 1, 2016.]
    546-B:11-a Application of This Chapter to Nonresident Subject to Personal Jurisdiction. –
    A tribunal of this state exercising personal jurisdiction over a nonresident in a proceeding under this chapter, under other law of this state relating to a support order, or recognizing a foreign support order may receive evidence from outside this state pursuant to RSA 546-B:27, communicate with a tribunal outside this state pursuant to RSA 546-B:28, and obtain discovery through a tribunal outside this state pursuant to RSA 546-B:29. In all other respects, Articles 3 through 6 do not apply, and the tribunal shall apply the procedural and substantive law of this state.

Source. 2015, 75:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
