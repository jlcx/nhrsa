TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 5
ENFORCEMENT OF ORDER OF ANOTHER STATE WITHOUT REGISTRATION

Section 546-B:35

    546-B:35 Immunity From Civil Liability. – An employer who complies with an income-withholding order issued in another state in accordance with this article is not subject to civil liability to an individual or agency with regard to the employer's withholding child support from the obligor's income.

Source. 1997, 263:32, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
