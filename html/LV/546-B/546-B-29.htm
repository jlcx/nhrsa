TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 3
CIVIL PROVISIONS OF GENERAL APPLICATION

Section 546-B:29

    546-B:29 Assistance With Discovery. – A tribunal of this state may:
    I. Request a tribunal of another state to assist in obtaining discovery.
    II. Upon request, compel a person over whom it has jurisdiction to respond to a discovery order issued by a tribunal of another state.

Source. 1997, 263:32, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
