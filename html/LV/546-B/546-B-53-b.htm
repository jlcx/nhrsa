TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE7
DETERMINATION OF PARENTAGE

Section 546-B:53-b


[RSA 546-B:53-b effective January 1, 2016.]
    546-B:53-b Relationship of Department of Health and Human Services to United States Central Authority. –
    The department of health and human services of this state is recognized as the agency designated by the United States central authority to perform specific functions under the Convention.

Source. 2015, 75:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
