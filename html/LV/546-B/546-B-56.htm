TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 9
MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

Section 546-B:56

    546-B:56 Uniformity of Application and Construction. – In applying and construing this uniform act consideration must be given to the need to promote uniformity of the law with respect to its subject matter among states that enact it.

Source. 2015, 75:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
