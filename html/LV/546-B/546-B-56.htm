TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 9
MISCELLANEOUS PROVISIONS

Section 546-B:56


[RSA 546-B:56 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA 546-B:56 set out below.]
    546-B:56 Uniformity of Application and Construction. –
    This chapter shall be applied and construed to effectuate its general purpose to make uniform the law with respect to the subject of this chapter among states enacting it.

[RSA 546-B:56 effective January 1, 2016; see also RSA 546-B:56 set out above.]


    546-B:56 Uniformity of Application and Construction. --
    In applying and construing this uniform act consideration must be given to the need to promote uniformity of the law with respect to its subject matter among states that enact it.

Source. 2015, 75:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
