TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE 2
JURISDICTION

Section 546-B:4

    546-B:4 Duration of Personal Jurisdiction. – Personal jurisdiction acquired by a tribunal of this state in a proceeding under this chapter or other law of this state relating to a support order continues as long as a tribunal of this state has continuing exclusive jurisdiction to modify its order or continuing jurisdiction to enforce its order as provided by RSA 546-B:7, RSA 546-B:8, and RSA 546-B:11-b.

Source. 2015, 75:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
