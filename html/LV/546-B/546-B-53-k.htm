TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 546-B
UNIFORM INTERSTATE FAMILY SUPPORT ACT

ARTICLE7
DETERMINATION OF PARENTAGE

Section 546-B:53-k


[RSA 546-B:53-k effective January 1, 2016.]
    546-B:53-k Personal Information; Limit on Use. –
    Personal information gathered or transmitted under this article may be used only for the purposes for which it was gathered or transmitted.

Source. 2015, 75:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
