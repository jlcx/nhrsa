TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 541-B
CLAIMS AGAINST THE STATE

Section 541-B:20

    541-B:20 Right to Jury Trial. – In any action in the superior court pursuant to this chapter there shall be a right to a jury trial and the action shall be tried to a justice of the superior court in the ordinary course.

Source. 1985, 412:13, eff. July 3, 1985.
