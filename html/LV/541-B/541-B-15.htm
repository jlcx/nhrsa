TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 541-B
CLAIMS AGAINST THE STATE

Section 541-B:15

    541-B:15 Claimants' Rights Against Others. – The adjudication by the board or the superior court on any claim before it shall not deprive the claimant of any other legal rights he may have against another party.

Source. 1977, 595:2. 1985, 412:12, eff. July 3, 1985.
