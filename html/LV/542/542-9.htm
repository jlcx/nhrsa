TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 542
ARBITRATION OF DISPUTES

Section 542:9

    542:9 Judgment Upon Award. – Upon the granting of an order confirming, modifying, or correcting an award, judgment may be entered in conformity therewith in the court wherein the order was granted.

Source. 1929, 147:1. RL 415:9.
