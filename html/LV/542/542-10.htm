TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 542
ARBITRATION OF DISPUTES

Section 542:10

    542:10 Appeal. – An appeal may be taken from an order confirming, modifying, correcting, or vacating an award, or from a judgment entered upon an award as in the case of appeals from the superior to the supreme court.

Source. 1929, 147:1. RL 415:10.
