TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 544
REMEDIES FOR CERTAIN NUISANCES

Section 544:12

    544:12 Continuance. – If the hearing be then continued at the instance of any defendant the temporary writ as prayed shall be granted as a matter of course.

Source. 1919, 95:4. PL 360:12. RL 418:12.
