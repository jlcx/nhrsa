TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 544
REMEDIES FOR CERTAIN NUISANCES

Section 544:14

    544:14 Extending Time of Answer. – The court may allow additional time for answering; provided, that such extension shall not prevent the issuing of said temporary injunction as prayed for.

Source. 1919, 95:4. PL 360:14. RL 418:14.
