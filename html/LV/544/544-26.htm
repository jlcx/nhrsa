TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 544
REMEDIES FOR CERTAIN NUISANCES

Section 544:26

    544:26 New Complainant. – If the proceeding is continued more than one term any person who is a citizen of the state, or has an office therein, or the attorney-general or the county attorney, may be substituted for the complainant and prosecute to judgment.

Source. 1919, 95:5. PL 360:26. RL 418:26. RSA 544:26. 1959, 12:1.
