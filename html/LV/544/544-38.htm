TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 544
REMEDIES FOR CERTAIN NUISANCES

Section 544:38

    544:38 Conviction as Evidence. – The finding of the defendant guilty in such criminal proceedings, unless reversed or set aside, shall be conclusive as against such defendant as to the existence of the nuisance.

Source. 1919, 95:7. PL 360:38. RL 418:38.
