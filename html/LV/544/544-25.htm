TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 544
REMEDIES FOR CERTAIN NUISANCES

Section 544:25

    544:25 Refusal to Dismiss. – If the court is of the opinion that the proceeding ought not to be dismissed he may direct the county attorney to prosecute it to judgment at the expense of the county.

Source. 1919, 95:5. PL 360:25. RL 418:25. RSA 544:25. 1959, 12:1.
