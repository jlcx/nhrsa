TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 541-A
ADMINISTRATIVE PROCEDURE ACT

Section 541-A:28

    541-A:28 Executive Orders and Opinions of the Attorney General. – The secretary of state shall transmit each executive order received from the governor to the director of legislative services, who shall publish the text of the executive order in the rulemaking register. The attorney general shall transmit a copy of every non-confidential opinion relative to rulemaking or rulemaking authority issued by the attorney general or in the attorney general's name to the director of legislative services. The director shall publish such opinions in the rulemaking register.

Source. 1994, 412:1. 2000, 288:19, eff. July 1, 2000.
