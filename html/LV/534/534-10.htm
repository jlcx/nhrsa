TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 534
HABEAS CORPUS

Section 534:10

    534:10 How Returnable. – The writ, when issued by the court, may be made returnable to the court or a justice thereof in any county immediately, or at a time and place therein directed.

Source. RS 202:10. CS 215:10. GS 224:10. GL 243:10. PS 239:10. PL 350:10. RL 406:10.
