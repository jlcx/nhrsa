TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 534
HABEAS CORPUS

Section 534:12

    534:12 Mode of Return. – In all cases the writ shall be made returnable in such mode as to secure to the applicant the enjoyment of it in the most free, easy, cheap and expeditious manner.

Source. RS 202:12. CS 215:12. GS 224:12. GL 243:12. PS 239:12. PL 350:12. RL 406:12.
