TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 534
HABEAS CORPUS

Penalties, etc.

Section 534:32

    534:32 Survival of Right of Action. – The right of action in any such case shall not cease by the death of either party, but shall survive for and against the personal representatives of the deceased.

Source. RS 202:31. CS 215:31. GS 224:32. GL 243:32. 1885, 11:1. PS 239:32. PL 350:32. RL 406:32.
