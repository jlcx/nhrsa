TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 534
HABEAS CORPUS

Section 534:18

    534:18 Place. – If the court is not in session when any such writ is made returnable it shall be returned to any justice of the court as if it were issued by and returnable to him.

Source. RS 202:19. CS 215:19. GS 224:19. GL 243:19. PS 239:19. PL 350:18. RL 406:18.
