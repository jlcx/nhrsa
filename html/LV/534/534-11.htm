TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 534
HABEAS CORPUS

Section 534:11

    534:11 When Issued by Justice. – If the writ is issued by a justice of the court it may be made returnable before himself, the court or any justice thereof at a place certain, immediately or at other time therein directed.

Source. RS 202:11. CS 215:11. GS 224:11. GL 243:11. PS 239:11. PL 350:11. RL 406:11.
