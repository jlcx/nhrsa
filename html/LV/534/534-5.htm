TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 534
HABEAS CORPUS

Section 534:5

    534:5 Denial. – If by such copy it appears that the person is lawfully imprisoned or restrained of his liberty by virtue thereof, a writ of habeas corpus shall not be granted.

Source. RS 202:5. CS 215:5. GS 224:5. GL 243:5. PS 239:5. PL 350:5. RL 406:5.
