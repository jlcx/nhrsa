TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 534
HABEAS CORPUS

Section 534:1

    534:1 Right. – A person imprisoned or otherwise restrained of his personal liberty, by an officer or other person, except in the cases mentioned in the following section, is entitled of right to a writ of habeas corpus according to the provisions of this chapter.

Source. RS 202:1. CS 215:1. GS 224:1. GL 243:1. PS 239:1. PL 350:1. RL 406:1.
