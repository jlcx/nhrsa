TITLE LV
PROCEEDINGS IN SPECIAL CASES

CHAPTER 534
HABEAS CORPUS

Penalties, etc.

Section 534:31

    534:31 Limitation of Actions and Prosecutions. – All actions and prosecutions for any offense against the provisions of this chapter shall be begun within two years next after the commission of the offense, saving to all persons in prison the right to sue or prosecute at any time within two years after such impediment is removed.

Source. RS 202:30. CS 215:30. GS 224:31. GL 243:31. PS 239:31. PL 350:31. RL 406:31.
