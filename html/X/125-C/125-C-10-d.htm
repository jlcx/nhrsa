TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 125-C
AIR POLLUTION CONTROL

Section 125-C:10-d

    125-C:10-d Sulfur Limits of Certain Liquid Fuels. –
    I. No person shall sell, offer for sale, supply, distribute for sale or use, except for fuel remaining in storage for a device not requiring a permit pursuant to RSA 125-C:11, any of the following liquid fuels:
       (a) No. 2 oil, also referred to as distillate oil, with a sulfur content greater than 0.0015 percent by weight;
       (b) No. 4 oil with a sulfur content greater than 0.25 percent by weight; or
       (c) Nos. 5 or 6 oil, also referred to as residual oil, with a sulfur content greater than 0.5 percent by weight.
    II. The commissioner may temporarily allow the use of non-conforming fuels with respect to paragraph I if there is a demonstrated need to do so based on an acute shortage of supply.

Source. 2016, 94:1, eff. July 1, 2018.
