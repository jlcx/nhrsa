TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 130
SANITARY INSPECTORS

Section 130:3

    130:3 Inspection of Food Products. – The inspectors shall investigate food manufacturing processes and food products offered, or to be offered, for sale in this state, and may take samples of food for examination at the laboratories of the department of health and human services.

Source. 1909, 163:3. PL 136:3. RL 161:3. 1995, 310:181, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
