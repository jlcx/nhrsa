TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-Z
TERMINAL PATIENTS' RIGHT TO TRY ACT

Section 126-Z:4

    126-Z:4 Private Cause of Action. – Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to create a private cause of action against a manufacturer of an investigational drug, biological product, or device or against any other person or entity involved in the care of an eligible patient using the investigational drug, biological product, or device for any harm done to the eligible patient resulting from the investigational drug, biological product, or device, if the manufacturer or other person or entity is complying in good faith with the terms of this chapter and has exercised reasonable care.

Source. 2016, 206:2, eff. June 6, 2016.
