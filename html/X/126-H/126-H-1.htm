TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-H
HEALTHY KIDS CORPORATION

Section 126-H:1

    126-H:1 Definitions. – In this chapter:
    I. "Board'' means the healthy kids board established in RSA 126-H:3.
    II. "Corporation'' means the healthy kids corporation established in this chapter.

Source. 1993, 312:2, eff. Aug. 22, 1993.
