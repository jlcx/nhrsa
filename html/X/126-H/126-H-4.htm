TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-H
HEALTHY KIDS CORPORATION

Section 126-H:4

    126-H:4 Meetings of Board. – Meetings shall be held at the call of the chairperson or when 5 members so request. Seven members of the board shall constitute a quorum and the affirmative vote of 7 members shall be necessary for any action taken by the authority. No vacancy in the membership of the board shall impair the right of a quorum to exercise all the rights and perform all the duties of the corporation.

Source. 1993, 312:2, eff. Aug. 22, 1993.
