TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 125
GENERAL PROVISIONS

Licensing and Registering Boards

Section 125:22

    125:22 Prescribing Uniform Records. – As far as practicable and after consultation with the professional and trade licensing and registering boards within the department of health and human services the commissioner shall prescribe uniform procedures for all secretarial and recording activities of the boards.

Source. 1950, 5, part 22:1, par. 30. RSA 125:22. 1983, 291:1, I. 1986, 198:7. 1995, 310:69, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
