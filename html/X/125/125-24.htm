TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 125
GENERAL PROVISIONS

Licensing and Registering Boards

Section 125:24

    125:24 Assigning Office Space. – The commissioner shall provide necessary office space for each professional and trade licensing and registering board within the department of health and human services.

Source. 1950, 5, part 22:1, par. 32. RSA 125:24. 1983, 291:1, I. 1986, 198:8. 1995, 310:71, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
