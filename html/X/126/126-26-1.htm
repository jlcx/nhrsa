TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126
VITAL RECORDS AND HEALTH STATISTICS

Health Care Data

Section 126:26


[RSA 126:26 effective June 30, 2015; see also RSA 126:26 set out above.]
    126:26 Data Review. –
    The department of health and human services shall prepare a report relative to the data collected under RSA 126:25, I on or before November 15, 1996, and annually thereafter, to the speaker of the house of representatives and president of the senate. The report shall contain, but not be limited to, an analysis and evaluation of the data collected and recommendations for improved efficiencies and for health care cost containment. The department of health and human services is also authorized to prepare periodic reports on price and utilization of health services for the purpose of encouraging competition.

Source. 1985, 378:2. 1995, 310:52, eff. Nov. 1, 1995. 2012, 282:3, eff. June 30, 2015.
