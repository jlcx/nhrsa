TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 146-F
MOTOR OIL DISCHARGE CLEANUP FUND

Section 146-F:2

    146-F:2 Definitions. – In this chapter:
    I. "Motor oil'' means any lubricating oil, which is reclaimable, classified for use in an internal combustion engine, transmission, gear box, or differential for a motor vehicle, boat, or off-highway recreational vehicle, or commercial or household power equipment.
    II. "Motor oil storage facility'' means a location consisting of a system of storage tanks, pipes, pumps, and appurtenant structures, singly or in any combination with a total storage capacity of less than or equal to 45,000 gallons, which is, or has been, used for the storage of motor oil or used motor oil.

Source. 1995, 282:1, eff. July 1, 1995. 2013, 37:1, eff. July 1, 2013.
