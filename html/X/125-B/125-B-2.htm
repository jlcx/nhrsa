TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 125-B
NEW ENGLAND COMPACT ON RADIOLOGICAL HEALTH PROTECTION

Section 125-B:2

    125-B:2 State Agency. – The state radiation control agency shall formulate and keep current a radiation incident plan for this state, in accordance with the duty assumed pursuant to Article III(a) of this compact.

Source. 1967, 131:1, eff. July 18, 1967.
