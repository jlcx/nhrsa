TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 125-B
NEW ENGLAND COMPACT ON RADIOLOGICAL HEALTH PROTECTION

Section 125-B:3

    125-B:3 Compact Administrator. – The compact administrator for this state, as required by Article VI of the compact, shall be the director of the state radiation control agency.

Source. 1967, 131:1, eff. July 18, 1967.
