TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 149-M
SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT

Section 149-M:3

    149-M:3 Achieving Goals; Hierarchy. – The general court supports integrated solid waste disposal solutions which are environmentally safe and economically sound. The general court endorses, in order of preference, the following waste management methods:
    I. Source reduction.
    II. Recycling and reuse.
    III. Composting.
    IV. Waste-to-energy technologies (including incineration).
    V. Incineration without resource recovery.
    VI. Landfilling.

Source. 1996, 251:2, eff. Aug. 9, 1996.
