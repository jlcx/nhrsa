TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 125-A
NORTHERN NEW ENGLAND MEDICAL NEEDS COMPACT

Section 125-A:6

    125-A:6 Takes Effect. – This chapter shall not be effective unless and until the governor of this state shall receive authenticated copies from the governors of the states of Maine and Vermont of the acts ratifying said compact.

Source. 1957, 141:2, eff. May 29, 1957.
