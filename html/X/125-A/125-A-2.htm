TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 125-A
NORTHERN NEW ENGLAND MEDICAL NEEDS COMPACT

Section 125-A:2

    125-A:2 Copies to be Sent. – The secretary of state shall send authenticated copies of this chapter to the governor of each of the other 2 states party to this compact.

Source. 1957, 141:1, eff. upon ratification of compact by Maine and Vermont.
