TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 143-A
FOOD SERVICE LICENSURE

Section 143-A:9-b

    143-A:9-b Sale of Synthetic Drugs Prohibited. – Any licensee who sells or distributes any substance containing a synthetic drug as defined in RSA 359-O:2 in any food service establishment or retail food store shall be guilty of a violation. Any licensee who violates this section shall be subject to the provisions of RSA 143-A:7.

Source. 2015, 204:5, eff. July 6, 2015.
