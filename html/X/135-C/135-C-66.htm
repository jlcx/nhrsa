TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 135-C
NEW HAMPSHIRE MENTAL HEALTH SERVICES SYSTEM

Anna Philbrook Center

Section 135-C:66

    135-C:66 Access of Records. – Notwithstanding any other provisions of law, records regarding children placed at Philbrook center pursuant to RSA 169-B, 169-C, or 169-D shall be exchanged between employees of the department to facilitate coordinated care for those children and their families. The confidentiality of such information shall be maintained according to applicable law.

Source. 1990, 3:45. 1994, 212:2. 1995, 310:89, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
