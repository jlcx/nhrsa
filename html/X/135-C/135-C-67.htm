TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 135-C
NEW HAMPSHIRE MENTAL HEALTH SERVICES SYSTEM

Anna Philbrook Center

Section 135-C:67

    135-C:67 Admission and Discharge. – The commissioner shall adopt rules relative to eligibility criteria and procedures for admission to and discharge from the Philbrook center.

Source. 1990, 3:45. 1994, 212:2. 1995, 310:90, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
