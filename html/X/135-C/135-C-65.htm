TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 135-C
NEW HAMPSHIRE MENTAL HEALTH SERVICES SYSTEM

Anna Philbrook Center

Section 135-C:65

    135-C:65 Admission Limitation. – Children subject to proceedings in juvenile court may be admitted to the Philbrook center for evaluation, care, or treatment only upon prior approval of the commissioner or designee.

Source. 1990, 3:45. 1995, 310:183, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
