TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 135-C
NEW HAMPSHIRE MENTAL HEALTH SERVICES SYSTEM

Nonemergency Involuntary Admissions

Section 135-C:38

    135-C:38 Copies of Petition. – Subsequent to receipt of the petition for involuntary admission, the clerk shall, within 2 days of receipt of the petition, forward 2 copies to the person sought to be admitted.

Source. 1986, 212:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1987. 2011, 88:20, eff. July 1, 2011.
