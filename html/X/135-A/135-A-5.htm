TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 135-A
INTERSTATE COMPACT ON MENTAL HEALTH

Section 135-A:5

    135-A:5 Approval by Court. – The compact administrator is hereby directed to consult with the immediate family of any proposed transferee and, in the case of a proposed transferee from an institution in this state to an institution in another party state, to take no final action without approval of the probate court.

Source. 1957, 215:1, eff. Sept. 1, 1957.
