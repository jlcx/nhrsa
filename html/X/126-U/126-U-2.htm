TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-U
LIMITING THE USE OF CHILD RESTRAINT PRACTICES IN SCHOOLS AND TREATMENT FACILITIES

Section 126-U:2


[RSA 126-U:2 effective until September 30, 2014; see also RSA 126-U:2 set out below.]
    126-U:2 Written Policies Required. –
    Each facility and school shall have a written policy and procedures for managing the behavior of children. Such policy shall describe how and under what circumstances restraint is used and shall be provided to the parent, guardian, or legal representative of each child at such facility or school.

Source. 2010, 375:2, eff. Sept. 1, 2010.
