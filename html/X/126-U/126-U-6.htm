TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-U
LIMITING THE USE OF CHILD RESTRAINT PRACTICES IN SCHOOLS AND TREATMENT FACILITIES

Section 126-U:6

    126-U:6 Schools Limited to Physical Restraint. – Use of restraint in schools shall be limited to physical restraint as permitted by this chapter. Schools shall not use medication restraint and shall not use mechanical restraint except as otherwise permitted in the transportation of children pursuant to RSA 126-U:12.

Source. 2010, 375:2, eff. Sept. 1, 2010.
