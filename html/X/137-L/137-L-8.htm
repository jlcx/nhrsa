TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 137-L
NEW HAMPSHIRE POLST REGISTRY ACT

Section 137-L:8


[RSA 137-L:8 effective July 1, 2015.]
    137-L:8 Immunity. –
    Any person reporting information to the POLST registry or acting on information obtained from the POLST registry in good faith shall be immune from any civil or criminal liability that might otherwise be incurred or imposed with respect to the reporting of information to the POLST registry or acting on information obtained from the POLST registry.

Source. 2014, 328:1, eff. July 1, 2015.
