TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 137-L
NEW HAMPSHIRE POLST REGISTRY ACT

Section 137-L:6


[RSA 137-L:6 effective July 1, 2015.]
    137-L:6 Application of Chapter. –
    Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to require an individual to have a POLST or to require a health professional to authorize or execute a POLST. A POLST may be revoked at any time by the patient.

Source. 2014, 328:1, eff. July 1, 2015.
