TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 125-H
HEALTH RISK ASSESSMENTS

Section 125-H:6

    125-H:6 Reports. –
    I. The commissioner shall prepare written reports detailing the results of any environmental health risk assessment undertaken pursuant to this chapter.
    II. The commissioner shall make these reports available to those state or local officials responsible for risk management decisions and activities.

Source. 1986, 202:13. 1995, 310:183, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
