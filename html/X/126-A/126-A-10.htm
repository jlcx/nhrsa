TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-A
DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES

Section 126-A:10

    126-A:10 Salaries. – The annual salaries of the commissioner of health and human services, deputy commissioner of health and human services, senior division director, division directors, and unclassified employees of the department shall be as prescribed by RSA 94:1-a.

Source. 1995, 310:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
