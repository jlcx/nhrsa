TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-A
DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES

Community Living Facilities

Section 126-A:24

    126-A:24 Placement. – Community living facilities serving persons with developmental disabilities shall be considered a part of the service delivery system as defined in RSA 171-A.

Source. 1995, 310:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
