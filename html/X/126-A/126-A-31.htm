TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-A
DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES

Emergency Shelter Program

Section 126-A:31

    126-A:31 Rulemaking. – The commissioner may adopt such rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, as the commissioner deems reasonable and necessary to carry out the provisions of this subdivision.

Source. 1995, 310:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
