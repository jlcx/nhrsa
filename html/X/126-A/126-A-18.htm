TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-A
DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES

Section 126-A:18

    126-A:18 Primary Preventative Health Services. – The commissioner shall:
    I. (a) Develop primary preventive health services for low-income and underserved populations.
       (b) Establish a network of primary care and family support services for children with chronic illness.
       (c) Recruit and establish retention of primary care practitioners in rural communities and areas of high primary care needs.
       (d) Provide technical assistance to communities, health care agencies, and primary care providers developing comprehensive care services.
    II. On June 30, 1999, any unexpended funds appropriated for the purposes of this section shall lapse to the fund from which they were appropriated.

Source. 1995, 310:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
