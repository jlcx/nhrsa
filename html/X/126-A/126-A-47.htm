TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-A
DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES

Office of Reimbursements

Section 126-A:47

    126-A:47 Reports. – The superintendent of such institutions shall forward forthwith to the commissioner any change in population at such institutions, any change affecting the rates charged patients or residents, and any other changes affecting expenses incurred by a patient or resident.

Source. 1995, 310:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
