TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-A
DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES

Emergency Shelter Program

Section 126-A:26

    126-A:26 Program Established. – There is hereby established an emergency shelter program to assist in providing safe and sanitary shelters on a short-term emergency or transitional basis for persons who are destitute, mentally ill, abandoned, or developmentally disabled, and other poor persons.

Source. 1995, 310:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
