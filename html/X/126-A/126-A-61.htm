TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-A
DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES

Housing Security Guarantee Program

Section 126-A:61

    126-A:61 Rulemaking Authority. – The department shall adopt rules, under RSA 541-A, relative to matters as are necessary to carry out the powers and duties of the department under this subdivision.

Source. 1995, 310:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
