TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-A
DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES

Poison Information and Control Center

Section 126-A:49

    126-A:49 Poison Information and Treatment. – The commissioner shall develop or designate a statewide program for poison information and treatment. In connection with this program, the commissioner shall be responsible for establishment or designation of a poison information and treatment center which shall make available to New Hampshire residents information and medical consultation on a daily, 24-hour basis.

Source. 1995, 310:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
