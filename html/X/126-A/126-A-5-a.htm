TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-A
DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES

Section 126-A:5-a

    126-A:5-a Access to Budget and Expenditures for Persons Receiving State Services. – The commissioner of the department of health and human services and the area agencies shall provide to any person, or that person's guardian, who is receiving state services pursuant to a plan with an individualized budget, a copy of such budget and the expenditures made under such budget.

Source. 2013, 144:108, eff. July 1, 2013.
