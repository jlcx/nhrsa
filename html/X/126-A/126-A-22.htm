TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-A
DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES

Community Living Facilities

Section 126-A:22

    126-A:22 Rates for Community Living Facilities. –
    I. The commissioner shall establish rates, by rules adopted under RSA 541-A, sufficient to provide a reasonable subsistence compatible with decency and health for persons in placement under this program. Payments hereunder may be made monthly by the commissioner from appropriated funds.
    II. The commissioner may establish rates pursuant to RSA 167:7, I-a for certain programs administered by the department.

Source. 1995, 310:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
