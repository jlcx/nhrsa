TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 147
NUISANCES; TOILETS; DRAINS; EXPECTORATION; RUBBISH AND WASTE

Section 147:22

    147:22 Penalty. – Any person violating the provisions of RSA 147:18 to 21 shall be guilty of a violation.
