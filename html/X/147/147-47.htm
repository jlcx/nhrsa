TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 147
NUISANCES; TOILETS; DRAINS; EXPECTORATION; RUBBISH AND WASTE

Solid Waste Management

Section 147:47

    147:47 Resource Recovery Facilities. – No municipality in this state shall be prohibited by any provision of local or state law from entering into long-term contracts for the supply of solid waste to resource recovery facilities.

Source. 1977, 581:2, eff. July 18, 1977.
