TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-K
YOUTH ACCESS TO AND USE OF TOBACCO PRODUCTS

Section 126-K:1

    126-K:1 Purpose. – The purpose of this chapter is to protect the children of New Hampshire from the possibility of addiction, disability, and death resulting from the use of tobacco products by ensuring that tobacco products will not be supplied to minors.

Source. 1997, 338:8, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
