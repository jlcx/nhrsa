TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-Q
NEW HAMPSHIRE VACCINE ASSOCIATION

Section 126-Q:8

    126-Q:8 Immunity From Liability. – There shall be no liability on the part of and no cause of action of any nature shall arise against any association member or its agents or employees, the association or its agents or employees, members of the board of directors, or the commissioner or the commissioner's representatives, for any action or omission by them in the performance of their powers and duties under this chapter.

Source. 2002, 279:2, eff. June 17, 2002.
