TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-C
INTERSTATE COMPACT ON THE MENTALLY DISORDERED OFFENDER

Section 126-C:3

    126-C:3 Contracts Authorized. – The department of health and human services may negotiate and enter into contracts on behalf of this state pursuant to Article III of the compact and may perform such contracts; provided that no funds, personnel, facilities, equipment, supplies, or materials shall be pledged for, committed or used on account of any such contract, unless legally available therefor.

Source. 1969, 442:1. 1979, 246:1. 1983, 291:1, I. 1995, 310:77, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
