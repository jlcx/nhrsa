TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 130-A
LEAD PAINT POISONING PREVENTION AND CONTROL

Section 130-A:16

    130-A:16 Penalty. – Any person who violates any provision of this chapter shall be guilty of a misdemeanor for each day that a violation exists. When lead hazard reduction has been ordered, the period of violation shall be calculated from the last date for reduction stated in the order issued under RSA 130-A:7 or in any extension of the order.

Source. 1993, 325:2. 1997, 165:18, eff. Aug. 8, 1997.
