TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 141-E
ASBESTOS MANAGEMENT AND CONTROL

Section 141-E:17

    141-E:17 Civil Forfeiture. – Any person who violates any provision of RSA 141-E or any rule adopted under it, or any term or condition of a license, certificate, or order issued under RSA 141-E, shall be subject to a civil forfeiture of up to $25,000 for each day of a continuing violation, in addition to enforcement by injunctive relief.

Source. 2000, 275:1, eff. July 1, 2000.
