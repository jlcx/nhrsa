TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 143
SANITARY PRODUCTION AND DISTRIBUTION OF FOOD

Manufacture and Sale of Beverages

Section 143:17

    143:17 Penalty for Violating Requirements. – Whoever violates any provision of this subdivision, or any rule or regulation lawfully made hereunder, shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1925, 107:7. PL 137:16. RL 162:19. RSA 143:17. 1973, 528:51, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
