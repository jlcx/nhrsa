TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 143
SANITARY PRODUCTION AND DISTRIBUTION OF FOOD

Manufacture and Sale of Beverages

Section 143:19-a

    143:19-a Exemption. – The provisions of this subdivision shall not apply to persons, firms, and corporations duly licensed under the provisions of the milk sanitation code, RSA 184:79-102.

Source. 1963, 289:3, eff. July 1, 1963.
