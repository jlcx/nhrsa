TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 143
SANITARY PRODUCTION AND DISTRIBUTION OF FOOD

Shellfish

Section 143:27

    143:27 Penalty. – Whoever violates any provision of this subdivision, or regulations made under authority of the preceding RSA 143:26, shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1951, 25:3, par. 33. RSA 143:27. 1973, 528:52, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
