TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-T
COMMISSION ON PRIMARY CARE WORKFORCE ISSUES

Section 126-T:5


[RSA 126-T:5 repealed by 2010, 114:3, effective November 1, 2015.]
    126-T:5 Authority to Accept Gifts. –
    The commission may accept any gifts, donations, or grants from any source whatsoever provided such gifts, donations, or grants shall be used exclusively in the furtherance of the duties of the commission.

Source. 2010, 114:1, eff. June 1, 2010.
