TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 137-F
HEARING CARE PROVIDERS

Section 137-F:17

    137-F:17 Prohibited Acts. – No person shall:
    I. Practice audiology or act or advertise as an audiologist without holding a license issued pursuant to this chapter.
    II. Fail to comply with an order of the board issued pursuant to this chapter.
    III. Fail to comply with a rule adopted pursuant to this chapter.
    IV. Hire as an audiologist any person not holding a license issued pursuant to this chapter.
    V. Practice audiology in violation of this chapter.

Source. 1996, 236:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1997.
