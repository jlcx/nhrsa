TITLE X
PUBLIC HEALTH

CHAPTER 126-N
PEER SUPPORT PROGRAMS

Section 126-N:1

    126-N:1 Purpose. – The purpose of this chapter is to recognize the effectiveness of mutual peer supports to the capacity of individuals with disabilities to participate fully in the workforce, in their families, and in the life of their community.

Source. 2000, 100:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2001.
