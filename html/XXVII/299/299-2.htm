TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF COMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 299
CORPORATE MORTGAGES AND REORGANIZATION AFTER FORECLOSURE

Mortgages

Section 299:2

    299:2 After-acquired Property. – Except as otherwise provided, corporate mortgage security for bonded or other indebtedness may include after-acquired property of the following classes: Real estate, franchises, fixtures, machinery and rolling stock.

Source. 1901, 66:1. 1905, 111:1. PL 230:2. RSA 299:2. 1959, 247:2, eff. July 1, 1961.
