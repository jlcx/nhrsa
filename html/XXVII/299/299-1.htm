TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF COMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 299
CORPORATE MORTGAGES AND REORGANIZATION AFTER FORECLOSURE

Mortgages

Section 299:1

    299:1 Franchises. – A corporation which may lawfully mortgage its property may also include its franchises in such mortgage.

Source. 1905, 61:1. PL 230:1. RL 279:1.
