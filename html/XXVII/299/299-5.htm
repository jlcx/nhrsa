TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF COMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 299
CORPORATE MORTGAGES AND REORGANIZATION AFTER FORECLOSURE

Reorganization of Corporations After Foreclosure

Section 299:5

    299:5 Conveyance by Trustee. – The trustee under such mortgage may, by auction sale, convey to said purchaser or to said new corporation all the right, title and interest under the mortgage and by virtue of the foreclosure thereof, and thereupon said trustees shall be discharged.

Source. 1895, 52:1. PL 230:5. RL 279:5.
