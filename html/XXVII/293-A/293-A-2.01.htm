TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF COMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

Incorporation

Section 293-A:2.01


[RSA 293-A:2.01 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA 293-A:2.01 set out below.]
    293-A:2.01 Incorporators. –
    One or more persons may act as the incorporator or incorporators of a corporation by delivering articles of incorporation and the certificate required by RSA 421-B:11, II(a) a to the secretary of state for filing.

[RSA 293-A:2.01 effective January 1, 2016; see also RSA 293-A:2.01 set out above.]


    293-A:2.01 Incorporators. --
    One or more persons may act as the incorporator or incorporators of a corporation by delivering articles of incorporation to the secretary of state for filing.
