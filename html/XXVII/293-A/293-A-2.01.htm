TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF CMOMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

Incorporation

Section 293-A:2.01

    293-A:2.01 Incorporators. – One or more persons may act as the incorporator or incorporators of a corporation by delivering articles of incorporation and the certificate required by RSA 421-B:11, II(a) a to the secretary of state for filing.

Source. 2013, 142:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2014.
