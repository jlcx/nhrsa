TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF CMOMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

Dissolution

C. Judicial Dissolution

Section 293-A:14.33

    293-A:14.33 Decree of Dissolution. – (a) If after a hearing the court determines that one or more grounds for judicial dissolution described in RSA 293-A:14.30 exist, it may enter a decree dissolving the corporation and specifying the effective date of the dissolution, and the clerk of the court shall deliver a certified copy of the decree to the secretary of state, who shall file it and at the same time shall mail notice of such decree to the department of revenue administration.
    (b) After entering the decree of dissolution, the court shall direct the winding up and liquidation of the corporation's business and affairs in accordance with RSA 293-A:14.05 and the notification of claimants in accordance with RSA 293-A:14.06 and RSA 293-A:14.07.

Source. 2013, 142:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2014.
