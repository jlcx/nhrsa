TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF COMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

Transition Provisions

Section 293-A:17.01

    293-A:17.01 Application to Existing Domestic Corporations. – This chapter applies to all domestic corporations in existence on January 1, 1993, that were incorporated under any general statute of this state providing for incorporation of corporations for profit if power to amend or repeal the statute under which the corporation was incorporated was reserved.

Source. 1992, 255:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1993.
