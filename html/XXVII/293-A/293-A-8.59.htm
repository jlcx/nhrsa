TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF CMOMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

Directors and Officers

E. Indemnification and Advance for Expenses

Section 293-A:8.59

    293-A:8.59 Exclusivity of Subdivision. – A corporation may provide indemnification or advance expenses to a director or an officer only as permitted by this subdivision.

Source. 2013, 142:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2014.
