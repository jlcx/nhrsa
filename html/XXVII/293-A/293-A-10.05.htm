TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF COMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

Amendment of Articles of Incorporation and Bylaws

A. Amendment of Articles of Incorporation

Section 293-A:10.05

    293-A:10.05 Amendment Before Issuance of Shares. – If a corporation has not yet issued shares, its incorporators or board of directors may adopt one or more amendments to the corporation's articles of incorporation.

Source. 1992, 255:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1993.
