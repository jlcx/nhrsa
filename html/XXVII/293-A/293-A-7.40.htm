TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF CMOMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

Shareholders

D. Derivative Proceedings

Section 293-A:7.40

    293-A:7.40 Definitions. – In this subdivision:
    (1) "Derivative proceeding'' means a civil suit in the right of a domestic corporation or, to the extent provided in RSA 293-A:7.47 in the right of a foreign corporation.
    (2) "Shareholder'' includes a beneficial owner whose shares are held in a voting trust or held by a nominee on the beneficial owner's behalf.

Source. 2013, 142:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2014.
