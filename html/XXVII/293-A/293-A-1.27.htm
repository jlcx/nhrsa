TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF CMOMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

General Provisions

B. Filing Documents

Section 293-A:1.27

    293-A:1.27 Evidentiary Effect of Copy of Filed Document. – A certificate from the secretary of state delivered attached or affixed to a copy of a document filed by the secretary of state, bearing his or her signature and the seal of this state (both of which may be in facsimile) is conclusive evidence that the original document is on file with the secretary of state.

Source. 2013, 142:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2014.
