TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF CMOMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

Foreign Corporations

B. Withdrawal or Transfer of Authority

Section 293-A:15.21

    293-A:15.21 Automatic Withdrawal Upon Certain Conversions. – A foreign corporation authorized to transact business in this state that converts to any form of domestic filing entity shall be deemed to have withdrawn on the effective date of the conversion.

Source. 2013, 142:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2014.
