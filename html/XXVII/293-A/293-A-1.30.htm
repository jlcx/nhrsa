TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF COMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

General Provisions

C. Secretary of State

Section 293-A:1.30

    293-A:1.30 Powers. – The secretary of state has the power reasonably necessary to perform the duties required of him by this chapter.

Source. 1992, 255:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1993.
