TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF COMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

Incorporation

Section 293-A:2.06

    293-A:2.06 Bylaws. – (a) The incorporators or board of directors of a corporation shall adopt initial bylaws for the corporation.
    (b) The bylaws of a corporation may contain any provision for managing the business and regulating the affairs of the corporation that is not inconsistent with law or the articles of incorporation.

Source. 1992, 255:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1993.
