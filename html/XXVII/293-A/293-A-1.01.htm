TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF CMOMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

General Provisions

A. Short Title and Reservation of Power

Section 293-A:1.01

    293-A:1.01 Short Title. – This chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "New Hampshire Business Corporation Act.''

Source. 2013, 142:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2014.
