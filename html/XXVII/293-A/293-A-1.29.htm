TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF CMOMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE BUSINESS CORPORATION ACT

General Provisions

B. Filing Documents

Section 293-A:1.29

    293-A:1.29 Penalty for Signing False Document. – (a) A person commits an offense by signing a document that the person knows is false in any material respect with intent that the document be delivered to the secretary of state for filing.
    (b) An offense under this section is a misdemeanor.

Source. 2013, 142:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2014.
