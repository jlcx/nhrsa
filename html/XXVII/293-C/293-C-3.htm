TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF CMOMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293-C
BENEFIT CORPORATIONS

Preliminary Provisions

Section 293-C:3


[RSA 293-C:3 effective January 1, 2015.]
    293-C:3 Incorporation of Benefit Corporation. –
    A benefit corporation shall be incorporated in accordance with RSA 293-A:2.01 through RSA 293-A:2.07, but its articles of incorporation shall also state that it is a benefit corporation.

Source. 2014, 216:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
