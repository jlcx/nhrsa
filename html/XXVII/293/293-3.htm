TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF COMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 293
CORPORATE CHARTERS

Avoidance of Charters for Nonpayment, Fraud, Etc.

Section 293:3

    293:3 Nonpayment of Fees. – Acts of the legislature which come within the provisions of the 2 preceding sections shall be void 30 days after notice from the secretary of state demanding payment of fees therein specified, if the payment of said sums is not made.

Source. 1877, 34:3. GL 13:7. PS 14:17. PL 13:7. RL 20:7. 1953, 78:3, eff. April 10, 1953.
