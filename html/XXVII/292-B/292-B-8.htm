TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF CMOMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 292-B
UNIFORM PRUDENT MANAGEMENT OF INSTITUTIONAL FUNDS ACT

Section 292-B:8

    292-B:8 Application to Existing Institutional Funds. – This chapter applies to institutional funds existing on or established after July 1, 2008. As applied to institutional funds existing on July 1, 2008, this chapter governs only decisions made or actions taken on or after that date.

Source. 2008, 75:1, eff. July 1, 2008.
