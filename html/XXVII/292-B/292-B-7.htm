TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF COMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 292-B
UNIFORM PRUDENT MANAGEMENT OF INSTITUTIONAL FUNDS ACT

Section 292-B:7

    292-B:7 Reviewing Compliance. – Compliance with this chapter is determined in light of the facts and circumstances existing at the time a decision is made or action is taken, and not by hindsight.

Source. 2008, 75:1, eff. July 1, 2008.
