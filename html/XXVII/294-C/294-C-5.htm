TITLE XXVII
CORPORATIONS, ASSOCIATIONS, AND PROPRIETORS OF CMOMMON LANDS

CHAPTER 294-C
FRAUDULENT OR UNAUTHORIZED ISSUE OF STOCK

Section 294-C:5

    294-C:5 False Records. – No officer, agent, or servant of a corporation shall fraudulently make any false entry or record in any book of the corporation, with intent to defraud, which is not prohibited by RSA 294-C:4.

Source. PS 273:14. PL 387:22. RL 450:21. RSA 580:21. 1973, 532:22, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
