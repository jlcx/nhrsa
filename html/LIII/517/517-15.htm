TITLE LIII
PROCEEDINGS IN COURT

CHAPTER 517
DEPOSITIONS

Commissioners to Take Depositions

Section 517:15

    517:15 Appointment. – Upon petition the superior court may appoint some suitable person as commissioner to take depositions outside this state, for use in causes pending in or returnable to said court.

Source. 1903, 21:1. PL 337:15. RL 393:15. RSA 517:15. 1967, 132:29, eff. July 18, 1967.
