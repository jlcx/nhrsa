TITLE LIII
PROCEEDINGS IN COURT

CHAPTER 524-A
REVISED UNIFORM ENFORCEMENT OF FOREIGN JUDGMENTS ACT

Section 524-A:6

    524-A:6 Optional Procedure. – The right of a judgment creditor to bring an action to enforce his judgment instead of proceeding under this chapter remains unimpaired.

Source. 1994, 266:1, eff. June 6, 1994.
