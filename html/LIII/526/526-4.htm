TITLE LIII
PROCEEDINGS IN COURT

CHAPTER 526
NEW TRIALS

Section 526:4

    526:4 Time. – A new trial shall not be granted unless the petition is filed within three years after the rendition of the judgment complained of, or the failure of the suit.

Source. RS 192:6. CS 205:6. GS 215:8. GL 234:4. PS 230:4. PL 342:4. RL 398:4.
