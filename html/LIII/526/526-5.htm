TITLE LIII
PROCEEDINGS IN COURT

CHAPTER 526
NEW TRIALS

Section 526:5

    526:5 Effect of Order. – Whenever a new trial is granted the action shall be brought forward on the docket of the court, and shall be tried as if no judgment had been rendered therein.

Source. RS 192:7. CS 205:7. GS 215:9. GL 234:5. PS 230:5. PL 342:5. RL 398:5.
