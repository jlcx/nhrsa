TITLE LIII
PROCEEDINGS IN COURT

CHAPTER 526
NEW TRIALS

Section 526:3

    526:3 Costs; Grant on Terms. – Costs may be allowed to either party upon such petition, and the new trial may be granted upon such terms as the court may deem just.

Source. RS 192:4. CS 205:4. GS 215:7. GL 234:3. PS 230:3. PL 342:3. RL 398:3.
