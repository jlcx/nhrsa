TITLE LIII
PROCEEDINGS IN COURT

CHAPTER 526
NEW TRIALS

Section 526:1

    526:1 When Granted. – A new trial may be granted in any case when through accident, mistake or misfortune justice has not been done and a further hearing would be equitable.

Source. RS 192:2. CS 205:2. GS 215:5. 1878, 64:1. GL 234:1. PS 230:1. PL 342:1. RL 398:1.
