TITLE VI
PUBLIC OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES

CHAPTER 94
COMPENSATION OF CERTAIN STATE OFFICERS

Section 94:3

    94:3 Yearly Service Increases. – For each year after July 1, 1953, upon recommendation of the appointing authority, the holder of any position mentioned in RSA 94:1-a shall be entitled to an annual increase in salary amounting to 1/5 of the difference between the minimum and maximum salary for said position but in no case shall the total salary exceed the maximum as herein established.

Source. 1953, 265:3. RSA 94:3. 1977, 600:73, eff. June 17, 1977.
