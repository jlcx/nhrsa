TITLE VI
PUBLIC OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES

CHAPTER 95
PERSONAL INTEREST IN BUSINESS TRANSACTIONS WITH PUBLIC

Section 95:1

    95:1 Public Officials Barred From Certain Private Dealings. – No person holding a public office, as such, in state or any political subdivision governmental service shall, by contract or otherwise, except by open competitive bidding, buy real estate, sell or buy goods, commodities, or other personal property of a value in excess of $200 at any one sale to or from the state or political subdivision under which he holds his public office.

Source. 1941, 85:1. RL 43:1. RSA 95:1. 1969, 310:1. 1975, 71:1, eff. June 6, 1975.
