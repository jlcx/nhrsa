TITLE VI
PUBLIC OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES

CHAPTER 99
ADJUSTMENT OF SALARIES OF STATE EMPLOYEES

Section 99:4-b

    99:4-b Certain Legislative Salary Increases. – The amount of all salary increases granted to the legislative budget assistant, the director of legislative services, or to any employee of either or both offices shall be a charge against the salary adjustment fund.

Source. 1972, 60:63, eff. Mar. 27, 1972.
