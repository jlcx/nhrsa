TITLE VI
PUBLIC OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES

CHAPTER 99
ADJUSTMENT OF SALARIES OF STATE EMPLOYEES

Section 99:3-a

    99:3-a Attendants. – Employees in the several attendant classifications within state institutions who have successfully completed an in-service training program approved by the personnel commission shall receive a one-salary grade increase to a new classification of psychiatric aide.

Source. 1965, 73:4, eff. July 2, 1965.
