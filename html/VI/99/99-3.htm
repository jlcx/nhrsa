TITLE VI
PUBLIC OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES

CHAPTER 99
ADJUSTMENT OF SALARIES OF STATE EMPLOYEES

Section 99:3


[RSA 99:3 effective until January 9, 2015; see also RSA 99:3 set out below.]
    99:3 Increase in Salary. –
    Notwithstanding the provisions of RSA 273-A or any other provision of law to the contrary, classified employees of the state as of July 11, 2014, shall be placed in the corresponding steps in the new salary ranges as their length of service justifies and their salaries shall be in accordance with the salary scales set forth in RSA 99:1-a. The provisions hereof shall not be construed as affecting so-called longevity payments which shall be in addition to the regular salary scale.
