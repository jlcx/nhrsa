TITLE VI
PUBLIC OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES

CHAPTER 93-B
OFFICIALS AND EMPLOYEES BONDS

Section 93-B:3

    93-B:3 Amount of Coverage. – The director of the division of purchase and property shall purchase such blanket bond or bonds as required under this chapter or as otherwise specified by law.

Source. 1973, 329:1. 1994, 158:8, eff. May 23, 1994.
