TITLE VI
PUBLIC OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES

CHAPTER 99-E
SUGGESTION AND EXTRAORDINARY SERVICE AWARD PROGRAM

Section 99-E:10

    99-E:10 Filings Not Required. – Members of the state suggestion and extraordinary service award evaluation committee, and members of any departmental award evaluation committee, shall not, unless otherwise required by virtue of another position held by the member, be subject to the financial reporting requirements of RSA 15-A.

Source. 2005, 258:1, eff. Sept. 14, 2005. 2008, 89:4, eff. July 20, 2008.
