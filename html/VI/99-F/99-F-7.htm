TITLE VI
PUBLIC OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES

CHAPTER 99-F
VETERANS' AND DISABLED VETERANS' PREFERENCE IN PUBLIC EMPLOYMENT

Section 99-F:7

    99-F:7 Rulemaking. – The department of administrative services shall adopt rules pursuant to RSA 541-A to implement the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 2014, 264:2, eff. July 28, 2014.
