TITLE VI
PUBLIC OFFICERS AND EMPLOYEES

CHAPTER 99-F
VETERANS' AND DISABLED VETERANS' PREFERENCE IN PUBLIC EMPLOYMENT

Section 99-F:3

    99-F:3 Eligibility for Employment Preference. –
    I. A veteran or disabled veteran may use the preference for a position for state employment at any time after discharge or release from service in the armed forces of the United States.
    II. A veteran or disabled veteran may claim the preference an unlimited number of times.

Source. 2014, 264:2, eff. July 28, 2014.
