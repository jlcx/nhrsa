TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 9
BUDGET AND APPROPRIATIONS; REVOLVING FUNDS

The Budget

Section 9:9-b


[RSA 9:9-b effective until July 1, 2013; see also RSA 9:9-b set out below.]
    9:9-b Allocation of Gross Appropriations From Highway Fund. –
    For each fiscal year of the biennium, gross highway fund appropriations net of highway block grant aid, shall be allocated in the operating budget as follows:
    I. Department of transportation, including the office of information technology: Not less than 70.75 percent.

[Paragraph II suspended for the biennium ending June 30, 2013 by 2011, 224:109, effective July 1, 2011.]


    II. Department of safety: Not to exceed 28 percent.
    III. Other projects that comply with part 2, article 6-a of the New Hampshire constitution, relative to the use of highway funds: Not to exceed 1.25 percent.

Source. 2003, 263:1, eff. Sept. 12, 2003. 2008, 237:1, eff. July 1, 2009; 237:2, eff. July 1, 2011.
