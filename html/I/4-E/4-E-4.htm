TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 4-E
STATE ENERGY STRATEGY

Section 4-E:4


[RSA 4-E:4 repealed by 2013, 276:5, effective December 31, 2014.]
    4-E:4 Costs. –
    The office of energy and planning is authorized to expend up to $200,000 from the energy efficiency portion of the system benefits charge, the renewable energy fund established under RSA 362-F:10, the energy efficiency fund established under RSA 125-O:23, or other funding sources under the jurisdiction of the public utilities commission for the development of the strategy, and may utilize any other sources available to the office necessary to fulfill the requirements of this chapter. The office of energy and planning is encouraged to seek other funding to support the development and updating of the strategy.

Source. 2013, 276:3, eff. July 24, 2013.
