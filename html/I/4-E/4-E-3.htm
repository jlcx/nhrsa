TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 4-E
STATE ENERGY STRATEGY

Section 4-E:3


[RSA 4-E:3 repealed by 2013, 276:5, effective December 31, 2014.]
    4-E:3 Public Participation. –
    The state energy advisory council shall provide duly noticed opportunities for public and stakeholder participation in the development of the strategy. In addition, at least 4 duly noticed public hearings shall be held in different regions of the state to seek public input during the development of the energy strategy. Public comment shall be sought by the office of energy and planning, the senate energy and natural resources committee, and the house science, technology and energy committee prior to updating the strategy.

Source. 2013, 276:3, eff. July 24, 2013.
