TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-I
DEPARTMENT OF ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES

General Provisions

Section 21-I:4

    21-I:4 Office Established. – There is hereby established an office of the commissioner consisting of the following 6 units:
    I. Financial data management unit.
    II. State budget.
    III. Internal audit.
    IV. Operational analysis.
    V. Cost containment.
    VI. Risk management.

Source. 1983, 416:40. 1985, 399:8. 1989, 345:1. 2006, 70:1, 2, eff. June 24, 2006.
