TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-I
DEPARTMENT OF ADMINISTRATIVE SERVICES

State Facility Energy Cost Reduction

Section 21-I:19-f

    21-I:19-f Energy Efficiency Fund. – There is hereby established an energy efficiency fund into which shall only be deposited moneys received by the state for participating in demand response programs. The state treasurer may invest moneys in the fund as provided by law, with interest received on such investment credited to the fund. Moneys in the fund shall be nonlapsing and continually appropriated to the division of plant and property to be used exclusively to fund energy efficiency projects and energy efficiency contracts and to reimburse state agencies for demand response program expenses.

Source. 2008, 166:3, eff. July 1, 2008. 2014, 327:21, eff. Aug. 2, 2014.
