TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-P
DEPARTMENT OF SAFETY

Transport of Hazardous Materials and Waste

Section 21-P:16

    21-P:16 Definitions. – In this subdivision:
    I. "Hazardous materials'' means "hazardous materials'' as defined in RSA 147-B:2, VIII.
    II. "Waste'' means "hazardous waste'' as defined in RSA 147-B:2, VII.

Source. 1987, 124:1, eff. July 1, 1987.
