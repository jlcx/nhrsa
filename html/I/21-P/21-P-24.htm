TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-P
DEPARTMENT OF SAFETY

Transport of Hazardous Materials and Waste

Section 21-P:24

    21-P:24 Effect on Other Laws. – Nothing contained in this chapter shall be construed to modify, limit, or be inconsistent with the duties and authority conferred upon the department of environmental services under RSA 147-A, 147-B, 147-C, and 147-D.

Source. 1987, 124:1. 1996, 228:108, eff. July 1, 1996.
