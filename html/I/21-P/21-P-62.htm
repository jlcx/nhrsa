TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-P
DEPARTMENT OF SAFETY

State Highway Safety Program

Section 21-P:62

    21-P:62 Staff. – Subject to state personnel regulations and within budgetary appropriations, the commissioner shall appoint such field representatives and secretarial and clerical assistants as may be needed to carry out the purpose of this chapter.

Source. 2015, 276:159, eff. July 1, 2015.
