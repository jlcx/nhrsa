TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-P
DEPARTMENT OF SAFETY

Fire Standards and Training Commission

Section 21-P:33

    21-P:33 Delegation. – The commission may delegate to the division of fire standards and training and emergency medical services any powers and duties enumerated in this subdivision.

Source. 1994, 389:4. 2004, 171:28, eff. July 24, 2004.
