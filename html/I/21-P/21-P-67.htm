TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-P
DEPARTMENT OF SAFETY

Substance Abuse Enforcement Program

Section 21-P:67


[RSA 21-P:67 effective until July 1, 2017; see also RSA 21-P:67 set out below.]
    21-P:67 Rulemaking. –
    The commissioner of safety shall adopt rules to implement this subdivision. Notwithstanding any other provisions of law, such rules shall be exempt from the provisions of RSA 541-A.

[RSA 21-P:67 effective July 1, 2017; see also RSA 21-P:67 set out above.]


21-P:67 Rulemaking. --
    The commissioner of safety shall adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, to implement this subdivision.

Source. 2016, 277:2, eff. June 17, 2016; 277:3, eff. July 1, 2017.
