TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 4-C
OFFICE OF ENERGY AND PLANNING

Regional and Municipal Assistance

Section 4-C:9

    4-C:9 Coordination at State Level. – The office of energy and planning shall coordinate efforts by state agencies to provide technical assistance to municipal governments in areas related to growth management and resource protection.

Source. 1987, 283:3. 2003, 319:9. 2004, 257:44, eff. July 1, 2004.
