TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 6-D
DRINKING WATER AND GROUNDWATER TRUST FUND

Section 6-D:2

    6-D:2 Annual Report. – The state treasurer shall prepare and file an annual report regarding the status of the trust fund with the New Hampshire drinking water and groundwater advisory commission established pursuant to RSA 485-F:4.

Source. 2016, 11:1, eff. Mar. 31, 2016.
