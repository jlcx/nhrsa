TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 5-C
VITAL RECORDS ADMINISTRATION

Confidentiality and Disclosure of Information

Section 5-C:110

    5-C:110 Public Use Statistical File. – The department shall make available a public use statistical file containing vital record information of all New Hampshire residents. The public use statistical file shall not contain any identifying personal information or information to constructively identify an individual.

Source. 2005, 268:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2006.
