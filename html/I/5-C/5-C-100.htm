TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 5-C
VITAL RECORDS ADMINISTRATION

Registrar's Duties

Section 5-C:100

    5-C:100 Search Fees. –
    I. The fee for making a record search shall be in accordance with RSA 5-C:10.
    II. The search fee shall be payable regardless of whether the record is found.
    III. The search fee shall include one certified copy of the record if a copy is desired and if it has been determined that the requestor has a direct and tangible interest in such record.

Source. 2005, 268:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2006.
