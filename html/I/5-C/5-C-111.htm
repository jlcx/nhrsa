TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 5-C
VITAL RECORDS ADMINISTRATION

Hearings

Section 5-C:111

    5-C:111 Hearings. – If aggrieved by any decision for denial of access to vital records documents under this chapter, an individual may request, within 30 days of the denial, an administrative due process hearing.

Source. 2005, 268:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2006.
