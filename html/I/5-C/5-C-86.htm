TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 5-C
VITAL RECORDS ADMINISTRATION

Forms and Procedures for Amendments and Corrections to Vital Records and Retention of Vital Record Documents

Section 5-C:86

    5-C:86 Forms to Amend Birth Records. – The division shall provide forms to amend birth records and shall require such information as the division deems appropriate given the year of birth.

Source. 2005, 268:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2006.
