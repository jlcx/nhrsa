TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 5-C
VITAL RECORDS ADMINISTRATION

Birth Registration Forms and Procedures

Section 5-C:37

    5-C:37 Preparation of the Birth Record of a Foundling. – Whoever assumes the custody of a foundling shall report such within 6 days to the clerk of the custodian's city or town of residence. The clerk of the town or city shall assist in the completion of the birth record of a foundling, pursuant to RSA 5-C:36, and file the completed birth record with the division.

Source. 2005, 268:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2006.
