TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 4
POWERS OF THE GOVERNOR AND COUNCIL IN CERTAIN CASES

Observances Proclaimed by Governor

Section 4:13-r

    4:13-r Blue Star Mother's Day. – The governor shall annually proclaim the first Sunday after Mother's Day as Blue Star Mother's Day recognizing and honoring all mothers who now have, or have had, children honorably serving in the United States armed forces. The governor shall urge the citizens of this state to observe this day with appropriate events.

Source. 2012, 274:1, eff. June 19, 2012.
