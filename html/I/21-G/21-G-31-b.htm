TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-G
ORGANIZATION OF EXECUTIVE BRANCH

Code of Ethics

Section 21-G:31-b

    21-G:31-b Interpretive Rulings; Procedure. – The committee may initiate or any person may request an interpretive ruling explaining or clarifying a law, guideline, or rule of general applicability within the committee's jurisdiction. If the committee determines that a requested interpretive ruling will be helpful to those individuals under the committee's jurisdiction, it shall take up the request in public, receive testimony and other information that it deems helpful on the issue, and render a public ruling.

Source. 2016, 57:12, eff. July 4, 2016.
