TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-L
DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION

State Infrastructure Bank

Section 21-L:19


[RSA 21-L:19 effective as provided in 2014, 290:4.]
    21-L:19 State Infrastructure Bank; Purpose. –
    The general court recognizes the importance of a sound transportation infrastructure system for public safety and the state's economic vitality. It is hereby declared to be in the public interest to promote the surface transportation infrastructure system by providing a financial vehicle to enable state and local transportation projects to proceed.

Source. 2014, 290:1, eff. as provided in 2014, 290:4.
