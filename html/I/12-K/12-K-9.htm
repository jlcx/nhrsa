TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 12-K
DEPLOYMENT OF PERSONAL WIRELESS SERVICE FACILITIES

Section 12-K:9

    12-K:9 Rulemaking. – The director of the office of energy and planning, after holding a public hearing, shall adopt rules under RSA 541-A to provide sufficient information to municipalities, other state agencies, wireless companies doing business or seeking to do business in this state, and the public.

Source. 2000, 240:1, eff. Aug. 7, 2000. 2003, 319:9, eff. July 1, 2003. 2004, 257:44, eff. July 1, 2004. 2012, 171:3, eff. Aug. 10, 2012.
