TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21
STATUTORY CONSTRUCTION

Section 21:52

    21:52 Under Oath. – The words "under oath'' when used in connection with a document to be filed with the court shall be satisfied by electronic signature "under the penalty of perjury'' if the document is filed with the court electronically.

Source. 2015, 22:1, eff. May 5, 2015.
