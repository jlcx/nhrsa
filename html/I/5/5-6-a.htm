TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 5
DEPARTMENT OF STATE

Department and Secretary of State

Section 5:6-a

    5:6-a Election Procedure Manual. – The secretary of state shall prepare and distribute an election procedure manual as provided in RSA 652:22.
