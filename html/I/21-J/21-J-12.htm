TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-J
DEPARTMENT OF REVENUE ADMINISTRATION

General Provisions

Section 21-J:12

    21-J:12 Document Processing Division. – There is established within the department the division of document processing for processing all tax returns filed with the department.

Source. 1985, 204:1. 1988, 232:4. 1991, 269:1. 2011, 224:198, eff. July 1, 2011.
