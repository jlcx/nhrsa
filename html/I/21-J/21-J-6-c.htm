TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-J
DEPARTMENT OF REVENUE ADMINISTRATION

General Provisions

Section 21-J:6-c

    21-J:6-c Central Tax Services Unit. – There is hereby established within the department of revenue administration a central tax services unit, which shall be responsible for providing general assistance to the public for all taxes administered by the department.

Source. 2005, 218:3. 2011, 23:1, eff. June 27, 2011. 2013, 247:14, eff. July 24, 2013.
