TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-J
DEPARTMENT OF REVENUE ADMINISTRATION

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 21-J:26

    21-J:26 Witness Fees. – Witnesses summoned to appear before the commissioner shall receive the same fees for travel and attendance as if summoned to appear before and in attendance upon the superior court.

Source. 1985, 204:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
