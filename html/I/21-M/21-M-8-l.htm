TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-M
DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE

Section 21-M:8-l

    21-M:8-l Restitution to the Victims' Assistance Fund. – Notwithstanding RSA 651:63, a court may order restitution to the victims' assistance fund as a part of a sentence, to the extent that moneys were awarded to the victim of the crime from the victims' assistance fund, if the court finds that:
    I. [Repealed.]
    II. Restitution shall replenish the victims' assistance fund.
    III. The victims' assistance fund has not been reimbursed for the moneys expended from another source.

Source. 1993, 42:1. 1996, 286:21, I, eff. July 1, 1997.
