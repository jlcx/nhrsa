TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 12-A
DEPARTMENT OF RESOURCES AND ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT

Reports on Economic Development Loans and Grants

Section 12-A:34

    12-A:34 Review of Reports Required. – For the purpose of ensuring comparability of impact reports on economic development programs issued under RSA 12-A:33, and RSA 162-A:23-a, the department of resources and economic development, in consultation with the legislative budget assistant, shall periodically review such reports at least once every 5 years and make recommendations to be utilized by the agencies making such reports for an improved and consistent methodology for assessing the quantity and quality of jobs created and saved and the growth potential and environmental impacts of such programs. This section shall not apply to promotional literature.

Source. 1996, 189:2. 2001, 290:5. 2008, 356:10. 2011, 224:116, eff. July 1, 2011.
