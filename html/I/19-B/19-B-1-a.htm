TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 19-B
COMMISSION ON THE STATUS OF WOMEN

Section 19-B:1-a


[RSA 19-B:1-a repealed by 2011, 231:12, I, effective upon merger with qualified voluntary corporation as provided by 2011, 231:13.]
    19-B:1-a Consecutive Terms. –
    Members of the commission shall be limited to 2 consecutive terms.

Source. 1981, 357:5, eff. Jan. 1, 1985.
