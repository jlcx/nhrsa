TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 21-O
DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENTAL SERVICES

Pollution Prevention

Section 21-O:16


[RSA 21-O:16 repealed effective July 1, 2020; see history notes following this section.]
    21-O:16 Pollution Prevention Coordinator. –
    There is established in the office of the commissioner, department of environmental services, the position of state pollution prevention coordinator. The coordinator shall be a classified employee qualified by reason of education and experience. It is the intent of the legislature that coordination of pollution prevention efforts shall complement and reinforce existing state, federal, local, and private pollution prevention efforts.

Source. 1996, 8:3, eff. July 1, 1996.
