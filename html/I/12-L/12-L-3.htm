TITLE I
THE STATE AND ITS GOVERNMENT

CHAPTER 12-L
MCAULIFFE-SHEPARD DISCOVERY CENTER

Section 12-L:3


[RSA 12-L:3 repealed by 2012, 173:8, I, effective March 31, 2013.]
    12-L:3 Commission Established. –
    There is hereby established a McAuliffe-Shepard discovery center commission which shall be responsible for the management and operation of the discovery center, including the planetarium located in Concord, and the buildings and grounds related thereto.

Source. 2001, 136:2, eff. July 1, 2001. 2009, 13:3, eff. April 17, 2009.
