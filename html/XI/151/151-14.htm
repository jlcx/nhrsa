TITLE XI
HOSPITALS AND SANITARIA

CHAPTER 151
RESIDENTIAL CARE AND HEALTH FACILITY LICENSING

Section 151:14

    151:14 Report. – The department of health and human services shall prepare and publish a biennial report of its activities and operations hereunder and shall make such information available to the governor and council.

Source. 1947, 216:1, par. 14. RSA 151:14. 1973, 140:13. 1983, 291:1, I. 1995, 310:181, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
