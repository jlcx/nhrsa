TITLE XI
HOSPITALS AND SANITARIA

CHAPTER 151
RESIDENTIAL CARE AND HEALTH FACILITY LICENSING

Section 151:3

    151:3 Existing Facilities. – Facilities subject hereto which are already in operation at the time of enactment hereof shall be given a reasonable time from the date of enactment of this law within which to comply with the rules and regulations and minimum standards provided for herein.

Source. 1947, 216:1, par. 3. RSA 151:3. 1979, 399:4, eff. Aug. 22, 1979.
