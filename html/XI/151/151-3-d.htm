TITLE XI
HOSPITALS AND SANITARIA

CHAPTER 151
RESIDENTIAL CARE AND HEALTH FACILITY LICENSING

Section 151:3-d


[RSA 151:3-d effective October 1, 2014.]
    151:3-d Verification of Medical Technician Registration. –
    Every facility administrator, or designee, for any health care facility licensed under this chapter shall verify with the board of registration of medical technicians established under RSA 328-I:2, prior to employing a medical technician, as defined in RSA 328-I:1, VI, that such medical technician is registered with the board.

Source. 2014, 295:2, eff. Oct. 1, 2014.
