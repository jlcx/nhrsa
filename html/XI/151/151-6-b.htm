TITLE XI
HOSPITALS AND SANITARIA

CHAPTER 151
RESIDENTIAL CARE AND HEALTH FACILITY LICENSING

Section 151:6-b

    151:6-b Report of Disciplinary Action. – Every facility administrator, or designee, for any health care facility licensed under this chapter shall report to the board of medicine, the board of nursing, or the board of registration of medical technicians any disciplinary or adverse action taken against a licensee or registrant of the board. Such report shall be made within 30 days after such action is taken. Actions reported shall only involve misconduct sufficient to support disciplinary proceedings by the board and shall include all situations in which allegations of misconduct are settled by voluntary resignation without adverse action.

Source. 2001, 228:1, eff. Sept. 9, 2001. 2005, 293:3, eff. July 1, 2005 at 12:01 a.m. 2014, 295:3, eff. Oct. 1, 2014.
