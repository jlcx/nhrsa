TITLE XI
HOSPITALS AND SANITARIA

CHAPTER 151
RESIDENTIAL CARE AND HEALTH FACILITY LICENSING

Section 151:6-b


[RSA 151:6-b effective until October 1, 2014; see also RSA 151:6-b set out below.]
    151:6-b Report of Disciplinary Action. –
    Every facility administrator, or designee, for any health care facility licensed under this chapter shall report to the board of medicine or the board of nursing any disciplinary or adverse action taken against a licensee of the board. Such report shall be made within 30 days after such action is taken. Actions reported shall only involve misconduct sufficient to support disciplinary proceedings by the board and shall include all situations in which allegations of misconduct are settled by voluntary resignation without adverse action.

Source. 2001, 228:1, eff. Sept. 9, 2001. 2005, 293:3, eff. July 1, 2005 at 12:01 a.m.
