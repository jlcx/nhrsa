TITLE XI
HOSPITALS AND SANITARIA

CHAPTER 151
RESIDENTIAL CARE AND HEALTH FACILITY LICENSING

Section 151:18

    151:18 Disposition of Fees. – All fees received from licenses under the provisions of this chapter shall be kept by the state treasurer in a separate fund to be paid out to the department of health and human services for purposes of this chapter only.

Source. 1947, 216:1, par. 19. RSA 151:18. 1983, 291:1, I. 1995, 310:181, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
