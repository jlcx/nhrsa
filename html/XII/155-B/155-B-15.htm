TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 155-B
HAZARDOUS AND DILAPIDATED BUILDINGS

Section 155-B:15

    155-B:15 Appeal. – A party aggrieved by the judgment of a municipal or district court upon issue joined in such case may, within 15 days after the rendition of the judgment, appeal to the superior court therefrom and the superior court shall hear said appeal forthwith.

Source. 1967, 334:1, eff. Sept. 1, 1967.
