TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 155-B
HAZARDOUS AND DILAPIDATED BUILDINGS

Section 155-B:14

    155-B:14 Local Acts and Charter Provisions. – The provisions of this chapter are supplementary to other statutory and charter provisions and do not limit the authority of any city or town to enact and enforce ordinances on the same subject.

Source. 1967, 334:1, eff. Sept. 1, 1967.
