TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 173-C
CONFIDENTIAL COMMUNICATIONS BETWEEN VICTIMS AND COUNSELORS

Section 173-C:4

    173-C:4 Partial Waiver. – Waiver as to a specific portion of communication between the victim and the counselor shall not constitute a waiver of the privilege as to other portions of the confidential communication between victim and counselor, relating to the alleged crime.

Source. 1985, 98:1, eff. May 10, 1985.
