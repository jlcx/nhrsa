TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 167
PUBLIC ASSISTANCE TO BLIND, AGED, OR DISABLED PERSONS, AND TO DEPENDENT CHILDREN

Section 167:11


[RSA 167:11 suspended for each fiscal year of the biennium ending June 30, 2013 as provided by 2011, 224:327, II.]
    167:11 Funeral Expenses. –
    On the death of the recipient reasonable funeral expenses may be paid subject to the rules and regulations of the department if the estate of the deceased is insufficient to pay the same; provided, however, that this section shall not apply to recipients of medical assistance.

Source. 1937, 202:17. RL 126:17. RSA 167:11. 1961, 271:4. 1975, 65:1. 1995, 310:175, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
