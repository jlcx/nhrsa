TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 155-E
LOCAL REGULATION EXCAVATIONS

Section 155-E:6

    155-E:6 Application for Amendment. – When the scope of a project for which an excavation permit has been issued is proposed to be altered so as to affect either the size or location of the excavation, the rate of removal or the plan for reclamation, the owner shall submit an application for amendment of his excavation permit which application shall be subject to approval in the same manner as provided for an excavation permit.

Source. 1979, 481:2. 1989, 363:12, eff. Aug. 4, 1989.
