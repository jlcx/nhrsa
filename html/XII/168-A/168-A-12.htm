TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 168-A
UNIFORM ACT ON PATERNITY

Section 168-A:12

    168-A:12 Statute of Limitations. – No proceeding under this chapter shall be maintained unless commenced within 18 years of the date of birth of the child in question.

Source. 1971, 530:1. 1977, 387:1. 1985, 331:3, eff. Oct. 1, 1985.
