TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 168-A
UNIFORM ACT ON PATERNITY

Section 168-A:3

    168-A:3 Limitations on Recovery From Father's Estate. – The obligation of the estate of the father for liabilities under this chapter is limited to amounts accrued prior to his death and such sums as may be payable for dependence under other laws.

Source. 1971, 530:1, eff. Sept. 5, 1971.
