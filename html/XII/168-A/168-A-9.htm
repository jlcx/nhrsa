TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 168-A
UNIFORM ACT ON PATERNITY

Section 168-A:9

    168-A:9 Venue. – A proceeding under this chapter may be brought in the county where the alleged father is present or has property or in the county where the mother resides.

Source. 1971, 530:1, eff. Sept. 5, 1971.
