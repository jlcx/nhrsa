TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 168-A
UNIFORM ACT ON PATERNITY

Section 168-A:13

    168-A:13 Social Security Numbers. – At the conclusion of a paternity action filed pursuant to this chapter in which paternity is established, the court shall also order the mother and father to supply their social security numbers to the registrar of vital records, in accordance with RSA 5-C:25, and to the department of health and human services.

Source. 1990, 250:5. 1995, 310:181. 2003, 319:59. 2005, 268:4, eff. Jan. 1, 2006.
