TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 168-A
UNIFORM ACT ON PATERNITY

Section 168-A:10

    168-A:10 Uniformity of Interpretation. – This chapter shall be so interpreted and construed as to effectuate its general purpose to make uniform the law of those states which enact it.

Source. 1971, 530:1, eff. Sept. 5, 1971.
