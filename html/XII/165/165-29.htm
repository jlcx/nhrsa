TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 165
AID TO ASSISTED PERSONS

Liability for Support, and Recovery Over

Section 165:29

    165:29 Enforcement of Liens. – Liens arising under RSA 165:28 or RSA 165:28-a may be enforced by a bill in equity.

Source. 1965, 42:1. 1981, 503:3, eff. Aug. 28, 1981.
