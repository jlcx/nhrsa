TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 161-J
ASSISTED LIVING RESIDENCES, INDEPENDENT LIVING RETIREMENT COMMUNITIES, AND HOUSING FOR OLDER PERSONS

Section 161-J:3

    161-J:3 Applicability. – This chapter shall apply to assisted living residences as defined in RSA 161-J:2, II, independent living retirement communities as defined in RSA 161-J:2, III-a, and housing for older persons as defined in RSA 161-J:2, III. This chapter shall not apply to continuing care communities as defined in RSA 420-D or condominiums as defined in RSA 356-B:3, V.

Source. 2002, 192:1. 2003, 114:4, eff. Jan. 1, 2004.
