TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 161-J
ASSISTED LIVING RESIDENCES, INDEPENDENT LIVING RETIREMENT COMMUNITIES, AND HOUSING FOR OLDER PERSONS

Section 161-J:9

    161-J:9 Use of Term "Assisted Living.'' – The term "assisted living'' may only be used in a title, brochure, admission agreement, or other written or promotional materials by an entity that is licensed pursuant to RSA 151.

Source. 2003, 114:8, eff. Jan. 1, 2004.
