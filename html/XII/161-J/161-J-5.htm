TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 161-J
ASSISTED LIVING RESIDENCES, INDEPENDENT LIVING RETIREMENT COMMUNITIES, AND HOUSING FOR OLDER PERSONS

Section 161-J:5

    161-J:5 Standard Disclosure Summary. –
    I. The standard disclosure summary, which shall accompany a residential services agreement, shall be in a form adopted by the commissioner of the department of health and human services by rule pursuant to RSA 541-A.
    II. A copy of the residential services agreement shall be given to prospective residents upon request and at least 24 hours prior to signing.

Source. 2002, 192:1. 2003, 114:6, eff. Jan. 1, 2004.
