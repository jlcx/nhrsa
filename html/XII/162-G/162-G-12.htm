TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 162-G
ACQUISITION, DEVELOPMENT AND DISPOSAL OF INDUSTRIAL LAND AND FACILITIES

Section 162-G:12

    162-G:12 Bonds Exempt From Taxation. – All bonds and the interest thereon shall be exempt from taxation in the state of New Hampshire.

Source. 1972, 57:1, eff. May 26, 1972.
