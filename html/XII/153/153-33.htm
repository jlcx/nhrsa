TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 153
STATE BOARD OF FIRE CONTROL

Fuel Gas Fitters

Section 153:33

    153:33 Appeals. – Any person or business entity affected by a final decision of the commissioner may appeal such final decision pursuant to RSA 541.

Source. 2006, 206:3, eff. Jan. 1, 2007. 2008, 170:4, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
