TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 153
STATE BOARD OF FIRE CONTROL

Miscellaneous

Section 153:20

    153:20 Witness Fees. – Each person summoned and testifying before the state fire marshal shall receive from the state treasurer, on certificate of the state fire marshal, for witness fees and mileage, such sums as are provided for similar activities in the superior courts of this state.

Source. 1947, 251:1, par. 18. RSA 153:20. 1971, 486:15, eff. July 2, 1971.
