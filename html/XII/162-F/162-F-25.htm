TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 162-F
DECOMMISSIONING OF NUCLEAR ELECTRIC GENERATING FACILITIES

Section 162-F:25

    162-F:25 Penalty. – Any person who willfully violates any order or determination of a committee shall be guilty of a felony.

Source. 1981, 109:2. 2001, 193:12, eff. Sept. 3, 2001.
