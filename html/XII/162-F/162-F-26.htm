TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 162-F
DECOMMISSIONING OF NUCLEAR ELECTRIC GENERATING FACILITIES

Section 162-F:26

    162-F:26 Appeal. – Any person who is aggrieved by an order or a decision of a nuclear decommissioning financing committee may appeal under RSA 541.

Source. 1981, 109:2, eff. May 4, 1981.
