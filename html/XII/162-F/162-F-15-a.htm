TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 162-F
DECOMMISSIONING OF NUCLEAR ELECTRIC GENERATING FACILITIES

Section 162-F:15-a

    162-F:15-a Rulemaking. – The committee shall adopt rules under RSA 541-A relative to the conduct of hearings under RSA 162-F:21.

Source. 1993, 330:5. 2012, 171:11, eff. Aug. 10, 2012.
