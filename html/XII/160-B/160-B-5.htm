TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 160-B
FIREWORKS

Section 160-B:5

    160-B:5 Issuance of Permit; Penalty. –
    I. No person shall issue a permit or license to display fireworks contrary to this chapter.
    II. Any person who violates the provisions of this section shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1991, 286:2, eff. March 1, 1992.
