TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 172
STUDY, TREATMENT AND CARE OF INEBRIATES

Section 172:7

    172:7 Assignment of Functions. – The commissioner may assign the functions provided for in this chapter to the staff of the department as the commissioner deems appropriate.

Source. 1947, 254:6. 1949, 313:7. 1950, 5, part 19:4. RSA 172:7. 1979, 378:14, 33. 1983, 291:1, II. 1995, 310:146, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
