TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 172
STUDY, TREATMENT AND CARE OF INEBRIATES

Section 172:6

    172:6 Director. – The governor, with the advice and consent of the council, shall appoint an office director in accordance with RSA 4:12. The director shall be compensated in accordance with RSA 94.

Source. 1947, 254:5. 1949, 313:1, par. 6. 1950, 5, part 19:4. RSA 172:6. 1979, 378:13; 378:32, eff. July 1, 1980.
