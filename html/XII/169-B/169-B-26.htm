TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 169-B
DELINQUENT CHILDREN

Section 169-B:26


[RSA 169-B:26 effective until July 1, 2015; see also RSA 169-B:26 set out below.]
    169-B:26 Petition by Minor. –
    At any time prior to hearing pursuant to RSA 169-B:16, a minor who is charged with an act of delinquency committed after the minor's sixteenth birthday may petition the court to be tried as an adult and to have such case dealt with in the same manner as any other criminal prosecution.

Source. 1979, 361:2. 1995, 302:19; 308:107, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
