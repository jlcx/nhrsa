TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 161-N
HOME HEALTH CARE AGENCY SERVICES

Section 161-N:1

    161-N:1 Purpose. – Older adults and people with disabilities, including children with special health care needs, require the support of home health care services. The purpose of this chapter is to ensure medically-necessary home health care services are available to Medicaid recipients at their residences and in non-institutional locations outside of the residence in which normal life activities take place.

Source. 2016, 273:1, eff. June 16, 2016.
