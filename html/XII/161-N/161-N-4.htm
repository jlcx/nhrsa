TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 161-N
HOME HEALTH CARE AGENCY SERVICES

Section 161-N:4

    161-N:4 Rulemaking. – The commissioner shall adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, relative to:
    I. Further definition of home health care services under RSA 161-N:3.
    II. Content and format of forms required under this chapter.
    III. Any other matter necessary to the proper administration of this chapter.

Source. 2016, 273:1, eff. June 16, 2016.
