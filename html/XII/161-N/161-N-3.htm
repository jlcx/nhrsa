TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 161-N
HOME HEALTH CARE AGENCY SERVICES

Section 161-N:3

    161-N:3 Home Health Care Services Location. – Medicaid coverage of medically-necessary home health care services shall be available to Medicaid recipients at their residences and in any non-institutional setting in which normal life activities take place.

Source. 2016, 273:1, eff. June 16, 2016.
