TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 170-A
INTERSTATE COMPACT ON THE PLACEMENT OF CHILDREN

Section 170-A:2

    170-A:2 Financial Responsibility. – Financial responsibility for any child placed pursuant to the provisions of the Interstate Compact on the Placement of Children shall be determined in accordance with the provisions of Article V of the compact in the first instance. However, in the event of partial or complete default of performance under the compact, the provisions of RSA 546-A and RSA 546-B shall apply.

Source. 1965, 366:1. 1997, 263:24, eff. Aug. 18, 1997.
