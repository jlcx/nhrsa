TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 170-A
INTERSTATE COMPACT ON THE PLACEMENT OF CHILDREN

Section 170-A:7

    170-A:7 Rulemaking. – The compact administrator of the Interstate Compact on the Placement of Children shall adopt rules under Article VII of the compact in accordance with RSA 541-A.

Source. 1983, 242:10, eff. June 18, 1983.
