TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 170-C
TERMINATION OF PARENTAL RIGHTS

Section 170-C:15

    170-C:15 Appeals. – Any party aggrieved by any order or decree of the court may appeal in accordance with RSA 567. The pendency of an appeal, or an application therefor, shall not suspend the order of the court regarding the child.

Source. 1973, 523:1, eff. Aug. 31, 1973.
