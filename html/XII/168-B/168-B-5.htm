TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 168-B
SURROGACY

Rules of Parentage

Section 168-B:5

    168-B:5 Effect of Noncompliance. – Noncompliance with the requirements of this chapter shall not affect the determination of parenthood under this subdivision, nor shall breach of a judicially preauthorized surrogacy contract affect transfer of parentage under RSA 168-B:23, IV.

Source. 1990, 87:2, eff. Jan. 1, 1991.
