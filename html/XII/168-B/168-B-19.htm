TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 168-B
SURROGACY

Surrogacy

Section 168-B:19

    168-B:19 Medical Evaluation. – General requirements for a medical evaluation shall include the following:
    I. Gamete donors, recipients of insemination, participants in in vitro fertilization and preembryo transfer shall be medically evaluated in accordance with RSA 168-B:10 and 168-B:12-14.
    II. The surrogate and the intended parents shall receive genetic counseling, if the surrogate is 35 years of age or older.

Source. 1990, 87:2, eff. Jan. 1, 1991.
