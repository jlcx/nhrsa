TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 168-B
SURROGACY

Artificial Insemination

Section 168-B:11

    168-B:11 Liability of Sperm Donor for Support. – A sperm donor may be liable for support only if he signs an agreement with the other parties to that effect.

Source. 1990, 87:2, eff. Jan. 1, 1991.
