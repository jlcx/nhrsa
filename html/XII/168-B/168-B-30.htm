TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 168-B
SURROGACY

Liabilities and Immunities for Participants of Insemination, In Vitro
Fertilization, Preembryo Transfer and Surrogacy

Section 168-B:30

    168-B:30 Liabilities. –
    I. A person who acts in negligent noncompliance with this chapter:
       (a) Shall be liable for resulting damages; and
       (b) May be jointly and severally liable for child support to the resulting child under the laws of this state.
    II. Any person or entity who violates RSA 168-B:16, IV shall be guilty of a misdemeanor. Each violation shall constitute a separate offense.
    III. The sanctions provided in this section shall be in addition to any other sanctions provided under applicable law.

Source. 1990, 87:2, eff. Jan. 1, 1991.
