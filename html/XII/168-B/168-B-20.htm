TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 168-B
SURROGACY

Judicial Preauthorization

Section 168-B:20

    168-B:20 Jurisdiction. – A petition for preauthorization of a surrogacy arrangement shall be brought in the probate court for the county in which the surrogate or the intended parents reside at the time the petition is filed. The surrogate or the intended parents shall have resided in New Hampshire for at least 6 months prior to the date the petition for preauthorization is filed.

Source. 1990, 87:2, eff. Jan. 1, 1991.
