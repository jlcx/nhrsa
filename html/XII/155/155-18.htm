TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 155
FACTORIES, TENEMENTS, SCHOOLHOUSES, AND PLACES OF PUBLIC ACCOMMODATION, RESORT OR ASSEMBLY

Places of Assembly

Section 155:18

    155:18 License Required. – No person shall own or operate a place of assembly within this state unless licensed so to do by the licensing agency of the city, town or village district where said place of assembly is located in accordance with the regulations herein promulgated. In the application of this act to existing places of assembly the licensing agency may modify such of its provisions as would require structural changes if in his opinion adequate safety may be obtained otherwise and provided that a permanent record is kept of such modifications and the reasons therefor.

Source. 1943, 153:2, eff. May 5, 1943.
