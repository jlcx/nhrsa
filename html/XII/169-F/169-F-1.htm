TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 169-F
COURT ORDERED PLACEMENTS

Section 169-F:1

    169-F:1 Purpose and Application of Chapter. – This chapter shall apply to any court ordered placement of any minor pursuant to RSA 169-B or any child pursuant to RSA 169-C or RSA 169-D, for the purposes of the effective implementation of any such placement.

Source. 1987, 402:35, eff. July 25, 1987.
