TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 161-E
PERSONAL CARE FOR THE SEVERELY PHYSICALLY DISABLED

Section 161-E:3

    161-E:3 Federal Funds. – The commissioner of the department of health and human services shall attempt to obtain such federal funds as are available to provide the services authorized under this chapter. The commissioner shall be authorized to expend such monies as he receives from the federal or state government or any other source for the purposes of this chapter.

Source. 1979, 275:1. 1995, 310:182, 183, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
