TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 161-E
PERSONAL CARE FOR THE SEVERELY PHYSICALLY DISABLED

Section 161-E:2

    161-E:2 Services Provided. – The commissioner of the department of health and human services shall develop a program under which severely physically disabled persons eligible for coverage under medical assistance programs are provided with personal care attendants to provide on a periodic basis medically oriented long-term maintenance and supportive care to assist them, as is required in each individual case, to maintain themselves in their homes and gain greater control over their own lives.

Source. 1979, 275:1. 1995, 310:182, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
