TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 169-A
INTERSTATE COMPACT FOR JUVENILES

Section 169-A:9-a

    169-A:9-a Conditional Release. – An adjudicated juvenile placed on probation or parole as such term is used in the interstate compact on juveniles is on conditional release as defined in RSA 169-B:2, VIII.

Source. 1987, 402:15, eff. Jan. 1, 1988.
