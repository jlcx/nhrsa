TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 169-A
INTERSTATE COMPACT FOR JUVENILES

Section 169-A:9

    169-A:9 Juvenile Delinquent. – The term "delinquent juvenile'' as used in the interstate compact on juveniles shall include a delinquent as defined in RSA 169-B:2, IV.

Source. 1955, 121:7. 1957, 174:1. 1987, 402:15, eff. Jan. 1, 1988.
