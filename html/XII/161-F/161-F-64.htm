TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 161-F
ELDERLY AND ADULT SERVICES

Homemaker Services

Section 161-F:64

    161-F:64 Review. – On or before December 1 of each year, the commissioner shall submit a report on the program with recommendations for improvements to the legislature.

Source. 1989, 7:1. 1995, 310:183, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
