TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 161-F
ELDERLY AND ADULT SERVICES

Companion Animals

Section 161-F:32

    161-F:32 Damage Deposit. – If companion animals are permitted, tenants desiring to have them as pets shall post, with the management of the building, a damage deposit which shall be a sum of money not to exceed 1 1/2 times the amount of a month's rent paid by the tenant.

Source. 1989, 7:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
