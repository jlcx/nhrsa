TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 170-E
CHILD DAY CARE, RESIDENTIAL CARE, AND CHILD-PLACING AGENCIES

Child Day Care Licensing

Section 170-E:20

    170-E:20 Notice of Death. – If any child under the control of any licensed child day care agency dies, the licensee shall give notice of such event to the department within 24 hours.

Source. 1990, 257:8, eff. Jan. 1, 1991.
