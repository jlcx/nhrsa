TITLE XII
PUBLIC SAFETY AND WELFARE

CHAPTER 162-A
BUSINESS FINANCE AUTHORITY

Section 162-A:22

    162-A:22 Unified Contingent Credit Limit. – The total amount of state guarantees in force under RSA 162-A:7-a, RSA 162-A:8, RSA 162-A:10, III, RSA 162-A:13, RSA 162-A:13-a, RSA 162-A:17, and RSA 162-I:9-b shall not exceed in the aggregate at any time $50,000,000 plus interest, provided that such amount shall be increased to $80,000,000 plus interest on January 1, 1993, and to $95,000,000 plus interest on January 1, 1994.

Source. 1992, 262:3. 1993, 335:14. 2000, 10:4, eff. July 1, 2000.
