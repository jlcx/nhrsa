TITLE XLIV
GUARDIANS AND CONSERVATORS

CHAPTER 464-C
UNIFORM ADULT GUARDIANSHIP AND PROTECTIVE PROCEEDINGS JURISDICTION ACT

Article 1
General Provisions

Section 464-C:3


[RSA 464-C:3 effective January 1, 2016.]
    464-C:3 International Application of Chapter. –
    A court of this state may treat a foreign country as if it were a state for the purpose of applying this article and articles 2, 3, and 5.

Source. 2015, 79:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
