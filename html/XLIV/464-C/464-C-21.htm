TITLE XLIV
GUARDIANS AND CONSERVATORS

CHAPTER 464-C
UNIFORM ADULT GUARDIANSHIP AND PROTECTIVE PROCEEDINGS JURISDICTION ACT

Article 5
Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 464-C:21

    464-C:21 Uniformity of Application and Construction. – In applying and construing this uniform act, consideration shall be given to the need to promote uniformity of the law with respect to its subject matter among states that enact it.

Source. 2015, 79:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
