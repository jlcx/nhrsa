TITLE XLIV
GUARDIANS AND CONSERVATORS

CHAPTER 464-C
UNIFORM ADULT GUARDIANSHIP AND PROTECTIVE PROCEEDINGS JURISDICTION ACT

Article 2
Jurisdiction

Section 464-C:8

    464-C:8 Exclusive Basis. – This chapter provides the exclusive jurisdictional basis for a court to address and adjudicate a case falling within the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 2015, 79:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
