TITLE XLIV
GUARDIANS AND CONSERVATORS

CHAPTER 463-A
UNIFORM TRANSFERS TO MINORS ACT

Section 463-A:26

    463-A:26 Severability. – If any provisions of this chapter or its application to any person or circumstance is held invalid, the invalidity does not affect other provisions or applications of this chapter which can be given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to this end provisions of this chapter are severable.

Source. 1985, 197:1, eff. July 30, 1985.
