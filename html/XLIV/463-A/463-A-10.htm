TITLE XLIV
GUARDIANS AND CONSERVATORS

CHAPTER 463-A
UNIFORM TRANSFERS TO MINORS ACT

Section 463-A:10

    463-A:10 Single Custodianship. – A transfer may be made only for one minor, and only one person may be the custodian. All custodial property held under the provisions of this chapter by the same custodian for the benefit of the same minor constitutes a single custodianship.

Source. 1985, 197:1, eff. July 30, 1985.
