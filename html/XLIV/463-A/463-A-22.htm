TITLE XLIV
GUARDIANS AND CONSERVATORS

CHAPTER 463-A
UNIFORM TRANSFERS TO MINORS ACT

Section 463-A:22

    463-A:22 Construction. – Notwithstanding the provisions of RSA 21:44 and RSA 21-B, in construing this chapter, the provisions of RSA 463-A:1 relative to the definition of "adult'' and "minor'' shall apply.

Source. 1985, 197:1, eff. July 30, 1985.
