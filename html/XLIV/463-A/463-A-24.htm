TITLE XLIV
GUARDIANS AND CONSERVATORS

CHAPTER 463-A
UNIFORM TRANSFERS TO MINORS ACT

Section 463-A:24

    463-A:24 Uniformity of Application and Construction. – This chapter shall be applied and construed to effectuate its general purpose to make uniform the law with respect to the subject of this chapter among states enacting it.

Source. 1985, 197:1, eff. July 30, 1985.
