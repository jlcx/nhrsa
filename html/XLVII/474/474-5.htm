TITLE XLVII
BOUNDARIES, FENCES AND COMMON FIELDS

CHAPTER 474
FENCING AND RENEWING BOUNDS OF COMMON FIELDS

Section 474:5

    474:5 Renewing Bounds. – Owners of adjoining improved lands where there is no partition fence shall, once in every 5 years, run the lines and renew the bounds between them, and any such owner neglecting without sufficient excuse to attend and renew the bounds, upon 6 days' previous notice given by any other such owner, shall forfeit $5 to the owner giving the notice.

Source. RS 136:23. CS 142:23. GS 128:24. GL 142:24. PS 143:26. PL 219:26. RL 269:26.
