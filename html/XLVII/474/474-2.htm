TITLE XLVII
BOUNDARIES, FENCES AND COMMON FIELDS

CHAPTER 474
FENCING AND RENEWING BOUNDS OF COMMON FIELDS

Section 474:2

    474:2 Meeting of Owners. – When several owners of land agree to improve the same in one common field, any justice, on application of 2 or more owners, may call a meeting of such owners, and the majority of them, when met, may determine in what manner the common field shall be fenced.

Source. RS 136:20. CS 142:20. GS 128:21. GL 142:21. PS 143:23. PL 219:23. RL 269:23.
