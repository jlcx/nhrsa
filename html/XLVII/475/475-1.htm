TITLE XLVII
BOUNDARIES, FENCES AND COMMON FIELDS

CHAPTER 475
FENCING WATERWAYS

Section 475:1

    475:1 "Owner'' Defined. – The actual occupant of land shall be deemed the owner thereof for any purposes of this chapter.

Source. RS 136:18. CS 142:18. GS 128:19. GL 142:19. PS 142:16. PL 219:16. RL 269:16.
