TITLE XLVII
BOUNDARIES, FENCES AND COMMON FIELDS

CHAPTER 476
SPITE FENCES

Section 476:2

    476:2 Remedy. – Any owner or occupant injured either in his comfort or the enjoyment of his estate by such nuisance may have an action on the case for the damage sustained thereby.

Source. 1887, 91:2. PS 143:29. PL 219:33. RL 269:33.
