TITLE XLVII
BOUNDARIES, FENCES AND COMMON FIELDS

CHAPTER 473
DIVISION FENCES AND THEIR MAINTENANCE

Section 473:3

    473:3 Proof of Division. – Such division may be established by usage and acquiescence of the parties, and those under whom they claim, for 20 years.

Source. 1862, 2614. GS 128:3. GL 142:3. PS 143:3. PL 219:3. RL 269:3.
