TITLE XLVII
BOUNDARIES, FENCES AND COMMON FIELDS

CHAPTER 473
DIVISION FENCES AND THEIR MAINTENANCE

Section 473:10

    473:10 Recovery. – The person so building or repairing may demand and recover double the appraised value thereof of the delinquent party, with costs of suit, in an action of assumpsit.

Source. RS 136:8. CS 142:8. GS 128:9. GL 142:9. PS 143:11. PL 219:10. RL 269:10.
