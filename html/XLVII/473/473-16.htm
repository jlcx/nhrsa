TITLE XLVII
BOUNDARIES, FENCES AND COMMON FIELDS

CHAPTER 473
DIVISION FENCES AND THEIR MAINTENANCE

Section 473:16

    473:16 Who Deemed Owner. – The actual occupant of land shall be deemed the owner thereof for any of the purposes of this chapter.

Source. RS 136:18. CS 142:18. GS 128:19. GL 142:19. PS 143:16. PL 219:16. RL 269:16.
