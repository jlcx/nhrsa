TITLE XLVII
BOUNDARIES, FENCES AND COMMON FIELDS

CHAPTER 473
DIVISION FENCES AND THEIR MAINTENANCE

Section 473:1

    473:1 Duty. – The owners of adjoining lands under improvement shall build and repair the partition fence between them in equal shares.

Source. RS 136:1. CS 142:1. GS 128:1. GL 142:1. PS 143:1. PL 219:1. RL 269:1.
