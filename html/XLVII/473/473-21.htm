TITLE XLVII
BOUNDARIES, FENCES AND COMMON FIELDS

CHAPTER 473
DIVISION FENCES AND THEIR MAINTENANCE

Procedure Before Fence-Viewers, and Their Fees

Section 473:21

    473:21 Penalty. – Any fence-viewer neglecting without sufficient cause to attend and perform any official duty shall be guilty of a violation.

Source. RS 136:19. CS 142:19. GS 128:20. GL 142:20. PS 143:21. PL 219:21. RL 269:21. RSA 473:21. 1973, 531:133, eff. Oct. 31, 1973.
