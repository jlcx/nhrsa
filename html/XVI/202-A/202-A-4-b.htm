TITLE XVI
LIBRARIES

CHAPTER 202-A
PUBLIC LIBRARIES

Section 202-A:4-b

    202-A:4-b Contracts for Services. – Any town may contract with another town or city, or with an institution or other organization, for any library service. If a town meeting votes to enter into such a contract, the town shall raise and appropriate sufficient money to carry out the contract.

Source. 1981, 499:4, eff. Aug. 28, 1981.
