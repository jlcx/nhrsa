TITLE XVI
LIBRARIES

CHAPTER 201-B
INTERSTATE LIBRARY COMPACT

Section 201-B:3

    201-B:3 Definition. – As used in the compact, "state library agency'', with reference to this state, means the commissioner of cultural resources.

Source. 1963, 106:1. 1985, 268:6, I(a). 1990, 73:1. 1998, 363:3, eff. Aug. 25, 1998.
