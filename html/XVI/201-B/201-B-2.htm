TITLE XVI
LIBRARIES

CHAPTER 201-B
INTERSTATE LIBRARY COMPACT

Section 201-B:2

    201-B:2 Limitation on Capital Expenditures. – No city or town of this state shall be party to a library agreement which provides for the construction or maintenance of a library pursuant to Article III, (c)(7) of the compact, nor pledge its credit in support of such a library, or contribute to the capital financing thereof, except after compliance with any laws applicable to such cities or towns relating to or governing capital outlays and the pledging of credit.

Source. 1963, 106:1, eff. July 23, 1963.
