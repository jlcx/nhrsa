TITLE XVI
LIBRARIES

CHAPTER 201-E
MUSEUM PROPERTY ACT

Section 201-E:7

    201-E:7 Rulemaking. – The commissioner of the department of cultural resources shall adopt rules under RSA 541-A relative to:
    I. The form of written notification to persons loaning property under RSA 201-E:4, V.
    II. Procedures for recordkeeping.

Source. 1989, 87:2. 1990, 73:1. 1998, 363:3, eff. Aug. 25, 1998.
