TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 326-D
MIDWIFERY

Section 326-D:9

    326-D:9 Report. – The council shall report biennially to the commissioner of the department of health and human services concerning the practice of midwifery in this state. The report shall include any recommendations of the council to increase the quality and safety of midwife services and generally to assure competence in the practice.

Source. 1999, 213:8, eff. July 6, 1999.
