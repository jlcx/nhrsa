TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 314
ELECTROLOGISTS

Section 314:10

    314:10 Fees. –
    I. The fee for an initial biennial license and for renewal of the biennial license issued under this chapter shall be $110. The executive director shall establish by rule a schedule of fees for applications, examinations, and license replacement.
    II. Fees collected shall be appropriated to the department and used to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 1994, 202:2. 1995, 310:175, 183. 2014, 167:12, eff. July 1, 2014. 2015, 276:90, eff. July 1, 2015.
