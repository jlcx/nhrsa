TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 325
EMBALMERS AND FUNERAL DIRECTORS

Section 325:25

    325:25 Renewal of Licenses. –
    I. Every person licensed to practice under this chapter, except as provided in RSA 325:29, shall apply to the board for annual license renewal. The board shall require each licensee to show proof of meeting the continuing education requirement of RSA 325:28-a within the preceding year. Payment shall be made to the board secretary of the renewal fee established in RSA 325:12-a.
    II. All licenses shall automatically lapse unless a timely and complete renewal application has been filed.

Source. 1935, 95:1. RL 168:25. RSA 325:25. 1957, 77:4. 1973, 354:3. 1977, 563:1. 1981, 487:18. 1996, 283:4, eff. Jan. 1, 1997. 2014, 167:15, eff. July 1, 2014.
