TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 313-A
BARBERING, COSMETOLOGY, AND ESTHETICS

Section 313-A:6

    313-A:6 Compensation. – Each member of the board shall receive as compensation the sum of $45 for each day actually devoted to the work of the board and shall be reimbursed for necessary traveling expenses incurred in the discharge of such duty, provided that the total expenditures hereby authorized shall not exceed the amount of moneys received by the state treasurer from the board during the current year.

Source. 2000, 118:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2001.
