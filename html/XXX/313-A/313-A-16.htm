TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 313-A
BARBERING, COSMETOLOGY, AND ESTHETICS

Section 313-A:16

    313-A:16 Applications. – Applicants shall make written application to the secretary of the board on a form prescribed and supplied by the board which shall contain satisfactory evidence of the qualifications required of the applicant; and the applicant shall also pay the examination fee.

Source. 2000, 118:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2001.
