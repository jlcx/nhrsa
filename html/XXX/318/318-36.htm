TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 318
PHARMACISTS AND PHARMACIES

Conferences of Boards, etc.

Section 318:36

    318:36 Information. – The board may subscribe for and secure the service of an association engaged in the compilation of pharmaceutical information, knowledge and progress specially adapted to secure efficiency in the work of the board.

Source. 1921, 122:20. PL 210:37. RL 256:37. RSA 318:36. 1971, 458:5. 1979, 155:26. 1981, 484:18, eff. July 1, 1981.
