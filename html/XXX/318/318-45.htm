TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 318
PHARMACISTS AND PHARMACIES

Regulation of Pharmacies

Section 318:45

    318:45 Sales Permitted. – This chapter shall not prevent the sale of the following: alum, blue vitriol, borax, camphor gum, copperas, epsom salts, glauber salts, castor oil, oil of turpentine, sulphur, cottonseed oil, saltpetre, household ammonia, flavoring extracts, and unofficial chlorinated solutions.

Source. 1921, 122:28. PL 210:46. 1931, 123:7. RL 256:46.
