TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 318
PHARMACISTS AND PHARMACIES

Pharmacy Board

Section 318:6

    318:6 Secretary. – It shall be the duty of the secretary to keep a record of the meetings of the board and to conduct its correspondence. The secretary shall file a record of the licensure of pharmacists which shall be open to the inspection of any person in the office of the secretary of state.

Source. 1921, 122:3. PL 210:6. RL 256:6. 1949, 280:2. RSA 318:6. 1979, 155:13. 1981, 484:18, 20, eff. July 1, 1981.
