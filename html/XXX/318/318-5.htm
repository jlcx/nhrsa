TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 318
PHARMACISTS AND PHARMACIES

Pharmacy Board

Section 318:5

    318:5 Officers and Duties. –
    I. The board shall have a president, vice-president, secretary, and a treasurer who shall be elected from among their number annually in the month of September.
    II. The president shall serve as chief executive officer of the board and shall preside at all meetings and hearings of the board and shall appoint any subcommittees from among the members as deemed necessary. In the absence of the president, the vice-president shall perform the duties of the president.

Source. 1921, 122:2. PL 210:5. RL 256:5. RSA 318:5. 1979, 155:12. 1981, 484:18. 1985, 324:2, eff. Jan. 1, 1986.
