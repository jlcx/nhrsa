TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 318
PHARMACISTS AND PHARMACIES

Examinations and Licenses

Section 318:29-c

    318:29-c Immunity From Civil Action. – No civil action shall be maintained against the board or any member thereof or its agents or employees with regard to any action or activity taken in the performance of any duty or authority established by this chapter. No civil action shall be maintained against any organization or its members or against any other person for or by reason of any good faith statement, report, communication, or testimony to the board or determination by the board in relation to proceedings under this chapter.

Source. 1992, 97:9, eff. July 1, 1992.
