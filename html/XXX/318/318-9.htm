TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 318
PHARMACISTS AND PHARMACIES

Pharmacy Board

Section 318:9

    318:9 Administrative, Clerical, and Inspectional Services. –
    I. The board shall employ one person to serve as a full-time employee of the board in the position of executive secretary. The executive secretary shall be responsible for the performance of the regular administrative functions of the board and other duties as the board may direct. The executive secretary may not perform any discretionary or decision-making functions for which the board is solely responsible.
    II. The board may employ such clerical assistance and obtain such inspectional services as may in their judgment be deemed necessary, subject to the approval of the governor and council.

Source. 1921, 122:5. PL 210:9. RL 256:9. RSA 318:9. 1963, 83:3. 1981, 484:18. 1988, 210:1. 2003, 174:8, eff. July 1, 2003.
