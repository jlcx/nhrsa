TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 318
PHARMACISTS AND PHARMACIES

Pharmacy Board

Section 318:4

    318:4 Compensation. – The members of the board shall be paid $100 a day and their necessary expenses while actually engaged in the performance of their duties.

Source. 1921, 122:2. PL 210:4. RL 256:4. RSA 318:4. 1963, 83:1. 1973, 453:2. 1979, 155:11. 1981, 484:18, eff. July 1, 1981. 2007, 202:4, eff. Jan. 1, 2008.
