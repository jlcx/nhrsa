TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 318
PHARMACISTS AND PHARMACIES

Examinations and Licenses

Section 318:20

    318:20 Impersonating Applicant. – No one shall impersonate an applicant before the board of pharmacy applying for licensure under the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 1921, 122:14. PL 210:21. RL 256:21. RSA 318:20. 1981, 484:18, 20, eff. July 1, 1981.
