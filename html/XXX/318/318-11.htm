TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 318
PHARMACISTS AND PHARMACIES

Pharmacy Board

Section 318:11

    318:11 Reports. – The board shall file with the governor and council, on or before December 1 biennially, a report upon the condition of pharmacy in the state and containing a record of their acts and proceedings.

Source. 1921, 122:31. PL 210:11. RL 256:11. RSA 318:11. 1973, 140:16. 1981, 484:18, eff. July 1, 1981.
