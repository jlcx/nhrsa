TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 329-B
PSYCHOLOGISTS

Section 329-B:16

    329-B:16 Electronic Practice of Psychology, Tele-health, Telemedicine. – Persons licensed by the board who practice electronically shall be subject to standards of care for the practice of telemedicine and tele-health for psychology established by the board pursuant to rules adopted under RSA 541-A.

Source. 2012, 233:1, eff. July 1, 2013.
