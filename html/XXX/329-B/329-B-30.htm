TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 329-B
PSYCHOLOGISTS

Section 329-B:30


[RSA 329-B:30 effective July 1, 2013.]
    329-B:30 Sexual Misconduct Subject to Disciplinary Action. –
    Sexual relations with a client or patient or a former client or patient shall be considered sexual misconduct and shall be subject to disciplinary action under RSA 329-B:21 and the duty to inform established in RSA 329-B:31.

Source. 2012, 233:1, eff. July 1, 2013.
