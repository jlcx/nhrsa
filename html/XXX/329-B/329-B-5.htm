TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 329-B
PSYCHOLOGISTS

Section 329-B:5


[RSA 329-B:5 effective July 1, 2013.]
    329-B:5 Administrative Attachment. –
    The board shall be administratively attached under RSA 21-G:10, to the department of health and human services. In addition, the board shall be subject to the provisions of RSA 126-A:10-a.

Source. 2012, 233:1, eff. July 1, 2013.
