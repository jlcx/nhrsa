TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 329-B
PSYCHOLOGISTS

Section 329-B:19

    329-B:19 Civil Claims. – Any person injured by the actions of a person engaged in the practice of unlicensed psychology in violation of any of the provisions of this chapter may bring a civil action to recover damages suffered by reason of the violations.

Source. 2012, 233:1, eff. July 1, 2013.
