TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 329-B
PSYCHOLOGISTS

Section 329-B:18

    329-B:18 Injunction. – The board may request the attorney general to commence an action to enjoin the operation of any person engaged in practicing unlicensed psychology in violation of this chapter. Said action shall be filed in the superior court.

Source. 2012, 233:1, eff. July 1, 2013.
