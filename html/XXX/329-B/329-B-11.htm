TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 329-B
PSYCHOLOGISTS

Section 329-B:11

    329-B:11 Receipts and Disbursements. – The board of mental health practice, under its continuing responsibility to establish fees for psychologists, shall receive and account for all fees under the provisions of this chapter, and shall pay such moneys to the state treasurer to be deposited in the general fund.

Source. 2012, 233:1, eff. July 1, 2013.
