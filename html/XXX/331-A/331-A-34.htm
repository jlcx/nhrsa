TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 331-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE REAL ESTATE PRACTICE ACT

Section 331-A:34

    331-A:34 Penalty. – Any person acting as a real estate broker or real estate salesperson, without a license, shall be guilty of a class A misdemeanor if a natural person, or guilty of a felony if any other person.

Source. 1993, 348:1. 2005, 206:2. 2006, 98:2, eff. July 8, 2006.
