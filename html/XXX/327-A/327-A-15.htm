TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 327-A
OPHTHALMIC DISPENSING

Section 327-A:15

    327-A:15 Injunction. – The executive director may request the attorney general to commence an action to enjoin the operation of any person engaged in the selling or fitting of ophthalmic devices or contact lenses in violation of this chapter. Said action shall be filed in the superior court in Merrimack county.

Source. 1989, 389:1. 1995, 310:183, eff. Nov. 1, 1995. 2015, 276:93, eff. July 1, 2015.
