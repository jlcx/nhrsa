TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 327-A
OPHTHALMIC DISPENSING

Section 327-A:16

    327-A:16 Civil Claims. – Any person injured by the actions of a person engaged in the dispensing of ophthalmic devices or contact lenses in violation of any of the provisions of this chapter may bring a civil action to recover damages suffered by reason of the violation.

Source. 1989, 389:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
