TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 327-A
OPHTHALMIC DISPENSING

Section 327-A:8

    327-A:8 Display of Certificate. – Any person who has been issued a certificate of registration under this chapter shall conspicuously display such certificate in his principal place of business.

Source. 1989, 389:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
