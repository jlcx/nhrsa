TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 310-B
STATE LICENSED OR CERTIFIED REAL ESTATE APPRAISERS

Section 310-B:10

    310-B:10 Term of Licensure or Certification. – All initial licenses or certificates issued by the board shall expire on the last day of the month of the holder's birth in the year 2 years following the year of issuance. The renewal term of a license or certificate issued under the authority of this chapter shall be 2 years from the date of issuance, and shall expire on the last day of the month of the license or certificate holder's birth. The expiration date of the license or certificate shall appear on the license or certificate and notice of its expiration shall be given to its holder.

Source. 1990, 167:3. 1991, 308:13, eff. July 1, 1991. 2006, 318:8, eff. Aug. 18, 2006. 2012, 164:6, eff. Aug. 6, 2012.
