TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 310-B
STATE LICENSED OR CERTIFIED REAL ESTATE APPRAISERS

Section 310-B:12

    310-B:12 Nonresident Licensure or Certification by Reciprocity. – An applicant who is licensed or certified under the laws of another state and whose license or certificate is in good standing may obtain a license or certificate as a licensed or certified real estate appraiser in this state.

Source. 1990, 167:3. 1991, 308:15. 2013, 98:2, eff. June 20, 2013.
