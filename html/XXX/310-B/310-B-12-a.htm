TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 310-B
STATE LICENSED OR CERTIFIED REAL ESTATE APPRAISERS

Section 310-B:12-a

    310-B:12-a Temporary Practice. – After application and payment of fee, the board shall issue a temporary license or certificate to an individual who is either licensed or certified in another jurisdiction if the application complies with rules adopted by the board pursuant to RSA 541-A.

Source. 1994, 215:3, eff. Jan. 1, 1995.
