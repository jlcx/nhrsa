TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 317-A
DENTISTS AND DENTISTRY

Dental Hygienist

Section 317-A:21-b

    317-A:21-b Display of License. – Licensees who provide dental hygiene services in this state shall cause their licenses to be at all times displayed in a conspicuous place in the setting wherein they shall treat patients, and shall whenever requested, exhibit such license to any of the members of the board or its authorized agent.

Source. 1994, 240:3, eff. Jan. 1, 1995.
