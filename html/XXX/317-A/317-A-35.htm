TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 317-A
DENTISTS AND DENTISTRY

Regulations and Penalties

Section 317-A:35

    317-A:35 Disposal of Fines. – All fines imposed and collected under this chapter shall be paid by the court or justice to the executive secretary of the board.

Source. 1971, 364:1. 2003, 149:11, eff. July 1, 2003.
