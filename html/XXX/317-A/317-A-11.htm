TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 317-A
DENTISTS AND DENTISTRY

Examinations and Licenses

Section 317-A:11

    317-A:11 Display of License. – Any person issued a license under this chapter shall display the license at all times in a conspicuous place in the office where the licensee practices, and shall, whenever requested, exhibit such license to any of the members of said board or its authorized agent.

Source. 1971, 364:1. 2000, 5:10, eff. July 1, 2000.
