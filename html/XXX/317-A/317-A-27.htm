TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 317-A
DENTISTS AND DENTISTRY

Regulations and Penalties

Section 317-A:27

    317-A:27 Display of Names. – Any individual engaged in the practice of dentistry shall display at the entrance of his or her place of business the name of the individual practicing dentistry at that place of business.

Source. 1971, 364:1. 2000, 5:25, eff. July 1, 2000.
