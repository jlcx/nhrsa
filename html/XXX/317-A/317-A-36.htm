TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 317-A
DENTISTS AND DENTISTRY

Regulations and Penalties

Section 317-A:36

    317-A:36 Location of Office. – The office of the New Hampshire state board of dental examiners shall be located in the bureau of dental public health, department of health and human services.

Source. 1971, 364:1. 1995, 310:181, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
