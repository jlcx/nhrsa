TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 317-A
DENTISTS AND DENTISTRY

Regulations and Penalties

Section 317-A:30

    317-A:30 Death of Licensee. – No person not a registered dentist shall, directly or indirectly, practice dentistry in this state, except as is provided in this chapter; but the spouse, executor, or administrator of a registered dentist who has died, or the spouse of one who is incapacitated, may continue such business under a registered dentist.

Source. 1971, 364:1. 1994, 289:4, eff. Aug. 5, 1994.
