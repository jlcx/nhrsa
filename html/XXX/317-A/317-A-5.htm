TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 317-A
DENTISTS AND DENTISTRY

Examining Board

Section 317-A:5

    317-A:5 Report; Receipts. – The board shall make a biennial report of its proceedings to the governor and council in October, and all fees and fines received by the board shall be paid monthly by the executive director to the state treasurer.

Source. 1971, 364:1. 1973, 140:44. 2003, 149:3. 2014, 11:3, eff. May 14, 2014.
