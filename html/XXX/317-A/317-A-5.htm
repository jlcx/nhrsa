TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 317-A
DENTISTS AND DENTISTRY

Examining Board

Section 317-A:5

    317-A:5 Report; Receipts. – The office of professional licensure and certification shall make a biennial report of the board's proceedings to the governor and council in October, and all fees and fines received shall be accounted for by the office of professional licensure and certification, and shall be deposited in the state treasury.

Source. 1971, 364:1. 1973, 140:44. 2003, 149:3. 2014, 11:3, eff. May 14, 2014. 2015, 276:78, eff. July 1, 2015.
