TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 328-I
BOARD OF REGISTRATION OF MEDICAL TECHNICIANS

Section 328-I:12

    328-I:12 Civil Claims. – Any person injured by the actions of a person engaged as a medical technician in violation of any of the provisions of this chapter may bring a civil action to recover damages suffered by reason of the violation.

Source. 2014, 295:1, eff. Oct. 1, 2014.
