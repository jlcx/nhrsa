TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 328-I
BOARD OF REGISTRATION OF MEDICAL TECHNICIANS

Section 328-I:8


[RSA 328-I:8 effective October 1, 2014.]
    328-I:8 Renewal of Registration. –
    Certificates of registration issued under this chapter shall be subject to renewal every 2 years and shall expire unless renewed in the manner prescribed by the board. The fee for renewal of certificates of registration shall be $110. Certificates of registration for medical technician shall be renewed upon the payment of the renewal fee.

Source. 2014, 295:1, eff. Oct. 1, 2014.
