TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 328-I
BOARD OF REGISTRATION OF MEDICAL TECHNICIANS

Section 328-I:15


[RSA 328-I:15 effective October 1, 2014.]
    328-I:15 Annual Report. –
    The board shall make an annual report commencing on November 1, 2016 relative to the conduct of activities under this chapter to the speaker of the house of representatives, the president of the senate, and the governor and council.

Source. 2014, 295:1, eff. Oct. 1, 2014.
