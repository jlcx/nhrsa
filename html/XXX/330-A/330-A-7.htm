TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 330-A
MENTAL HEALTH PRACTICE

Section 330-A:7

    330-A:7 Compensation; Expenses. – Members of the board and members of the committees shall receive a per diem compensation of $100, for a meeting or any other board or committee activity requiring 2 or more hours in a 24-hour period, and shall be reimbursed for all actual travel and incidental expenses necessarily incurred in carrying out the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 1998, 234:1. 2006, 168:4. 2010, 244:5, eff. July 1, 2010.
