TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 330-A
MENTAL HEALTH PRACTICE

Section 330-A:18

    330-A:18 Clinical Social Workers. – The board shall issue a clinical social worker license to any person who meets all of the following requirements or their equivalent:
    I. Has received a college undergraduate degree.
    II. Has received a 2-year master's degree or doctorate degree in social work from a school approved by the Council on Social Work Education.
    III. Has completed a minimum of 2 years of post-masters experience including completion of a minimum of 3,000 hours of post-masters, supervised clinical experience.
    IV. Has passed a national proctored examination approved by the board.

Source. 1998, 234:1. 2010, 244:12, 13, eff. July 1, 2010.
