TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 330-A
MENTAL HEALTH PRACTICE

Section 330-A:26

    330-A:26 Applicants From Other States. – The board may license any applicant who is licensed in any other state, provided the other state's licensure requirements are substantially equivalent to or higher than those of this state.

Source. 1998, 234:1, eff. Oct. 31, 1998.
