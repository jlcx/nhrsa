TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 328-C
FAMILY MEDIATORS

Section 328-C:13

    328-C:13 Board of Marital Mediator Certification Administratively Attached. – The board of family mediator certification established under RSA 328-C:4 shall be administratively attached, under RSA 21-G:10, to the judicial council established in RSA 494.

Source. 1992, 207:1. 2009, 21:4, eff. Jan. 1, 2010.
