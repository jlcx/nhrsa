TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 328-C
FAMILY MEDIATORS

Section 328-C:13

    328-C:13 Board of Family Mediator Certification Administrative Functions. – All administrative, clerical, and business processing functions of the board shall be transferred to the office of professional licensure and certification established in RSA 310-A:1 through RSA 310-A:1-e.

Source. 1992, 207:1. 2009, 21:4, eff. Jan. 1, 2010. 2015, 276:58, eff. July 1, 2015.
