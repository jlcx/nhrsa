TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 328-C
FAMILY MEDIATORS

Section 328-C:12

    328-C:12 Expenses. – Members of the board shall be reimbursed for all actual travel, incidental, telephone and clerical expenses necessarily incurred in carrying out the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 1992, 207:1, eff. May 12, 1992.
