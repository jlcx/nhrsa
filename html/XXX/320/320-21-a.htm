TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 320
HAWKERS AND PEDDLERS

Sales for Religious and Benevolent Purposes

Section 320:21-a

    320:21-a Certain Negotiable Instruments Prohibited. – In a consumer credit sale or consumer lease by a hawker or peddler, other than a sale or lease primarily for an agricultural purpose, the hawker or peddler may not take a negotiable instrument other than a check as evidence of the obligation of the buyer or lessee. A holder is not in good faith if he takes a negotiable instrument with notice that it is issued in violation of this section.

Source. 1969, 481:9, eff. Sept. 1, 1969.
