TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 320
HAWKERS AND PEDDLERS

General Provisions

Section 320:10

    320:10 Record. – The secretary of state shall keep records of all licenses issued by him with the number of each, the names and residences of the persons licensed and the sums received therefor, and all such records shall be open for public inspection.

Source. 1931, 102:1. 1941, 210:1. RL 188:10. RSA 320:10. 1969, 481:5, eff. Sept. 1, 1969.
