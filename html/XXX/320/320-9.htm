TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 320
HAWKERS AND PEDDLERS

General Provisions

Section 320:9

    320:9 Term; Transfer. – All licenses issued under the provisions of this chapter shall bear the date on which they are issued and shall continue in force, subject to the provisions of RSA 320:8, III, unless revoked by the secretary of state under RSA 320:14. Any hawker or peddler who does not comply with the requirements for license renewal under RSA 320:8, III shall be subject to the same penalty as if he had no license. Said licenses may not be transferred.

Source. 1931, 102:1. RL 188:9. RSA 320:9. 1969, 481:4. 1983, 186:2, eff. Aug. 9, 1983.
