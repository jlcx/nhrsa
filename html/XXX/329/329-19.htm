TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 329
PHYSICIANS AND SURGEONS

Disciplinary Proceedings

Section 329:19

    329:19 Accounts. – The board shall keep a full and true record of all fees received and all sums actually paid for expenses, and at the end of each year shall account to the governor and council.

Source. 1915, 167:14. PL 204:16. RL 250:16.
