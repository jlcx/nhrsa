TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 329
PHYSICIANS AND SURGEONS

Renewal of Licenses

Section 329:16-d

    329:16-d Notice of Renewal. – On or before March 1 of each licensee's renewal year, the board shall mail each licensee, except those on the inactive list, an application for renewal of license.

Source. 1959, 144:3. 1977, 417:14. 1983, 377:8. 1991, 382:14. 1995, 286:15. 2001, 228:7. 2004, 108:2, eff. July 1, 2004.
