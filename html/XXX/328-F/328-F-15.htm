TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 328-F
ALLIED HEALTH PROFESSIONALS

Board of Directors

Section 328-F:15

    328-F:15 Fees. –
    I. The board of directors shall establish fees for:
       (a) The processing of applications for initial licensure.
       (b) Initial licenses.
       (c) Renewal of licenses.
       (d) Late filing of applications for license renewal.
       (e) Reinstatement of licenses after lapse.
       (f) Transcribing and transferring records.
       (g) The costs of a hearing by any governing board at which the issue is denial of, or imposition of conditions on, an initial license, including the per diem and mileage of board members attending the hearing and the cost of a shorthand court reporter if one is used to record the hearing.
       (h) Initial certification, certification renewal, and certification reinstatement of speech-language assistants, as defined in RSA 326-F:1, II-a.
    II. Such fees shall be sufficient to produce estimated revenues equal to 125 percent of the direct operating expenses for the board of directors and the governing boards for the previous fiscal year.

Source. 1997, 287:1. 2003, 310:48. 2006, 268:3, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
