TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 328-F
ALLIED HEALTH PROFESSIONALS

Board of Directors

Section 328-F:13

    328-F:13 Board of Directors; Rulemaking Authority. – The board of directors shall adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, establishing:
    I. Procedures for the voluntary surrender of licenses issued by the governing boards.
    II. Procedures for the receipt of misconduct complaints and the conduct of disciplinary proceedings.
    III. Fees authorized by this chapter.

Source. 1997, 287:1. 2003, 310:47, eff. July 1, 2003.
