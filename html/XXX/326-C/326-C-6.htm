TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 326-C
OCCUPATIONAL THERAPISTS

Section 326-C:6

    326-C:6 Renewal of License. –
    I. Persons licensed as occupational therapists or occupational therapy assistants are eligible for renewal of their licenses if they:
       (a) Have not violated this chapter or RSA 328-F or demonstrated poor moral character.
       (b) Meet any continuing competency requirements and any requirements for continuing education, clinical experience, or training established by the board in rules adopted pursuant to RSA 541-A.
    II. The board shall approve continuing education programs approved through the American Occupational Therapy Association's approved provider program.

Source. 2003, 310:1, eff. July 1, 2003.
