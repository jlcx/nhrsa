TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 319-C
ELECTRICIANS

Section 319-C:11


[RSA 319-C:11 repealed by 2012, 237:7, I, effective July 1, 2013.]
    319-C:11 Disposition of Funds. –
    The state fire marshal shall cause to be recorded all receipts and disbursements under this chapter. The state fire marshal or designee shall forward all funds received under this chapter to the state treasurer for deposit in the fire standards and training and emergency medical services fund established in RSA 21-P:12-d.

Source. 1975, 485:1. 1994, 238:8. 2005, 122:6, eff. July 1, 2005; 177:110, eff. July 1, 2005.
