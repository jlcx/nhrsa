TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 316-A
CHIROPRACTIC

Section 316-A:14


[RSA 316-A:14 repealed by 2014, 322:3, II, effective September 30, 2014.]
    316-A:14 Licenses and Certificates. –
    Each applicant who qualifies and who attains a minimum grade of 70 percent upon the examination given under RSA 316-A:13, I shall receive a license from the board as a chiropractor permitted to practice in New Hampshire. The fee for an initial license and for a license renewal shall be $300. The license shall be valid for 2 years from the date of issuance.

Source. 1988, 87:2, eff. April 18, 1988. 2014, 167:46, eff. July 1, 2014.
