TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 316-A
CHIROPRACTIC

Section 316-A:8

    316-A:8 Income. – All income received by the board shall be accounted for and paid to the state treasurer annually at such time as the state treasurer shall prescribe.

Source. 1988, 87:2. 1995, 132:2, eff. July 1, 1995.
