TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 332-J
ATHLETE AGENTS

Section 332-J:15

    332-J:15 Administrative Penalty. – Notwithstanding RSA 651:2, IV, the secretary of state may assess a civil penalty against an athlete agent not to exceed $25,000 for a violation of this chapter.

Source. 2006, 265:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
