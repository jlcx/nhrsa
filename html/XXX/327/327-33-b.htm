TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 327
OPTOMETRY

Regulations and Penalties

Section 327:33-b

    327:33-b Consumer Publication. – The board of optometry shall develop a consumer publication that contains the information necessary to educate consumers regarding optical services and products, the services available from the optometrist authorized to use therapeutics to treat eye conditions, and the services available from other optometrists. The board shall supply reasonable quantities of this publication at no charge to all licensed practitioners.

Source. 1993, 333:11, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
