TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 311-B
AUCTIONEERS

Section 311-B:14

    311-B:14 Disposition of Revenue. – All moneys collected under this chapter shall be paid into the general fund and are appropriated therefrom to the secretary of state for the purpose of carrying out the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 1969, 469:1, eff. Sept. 1, 1969.
