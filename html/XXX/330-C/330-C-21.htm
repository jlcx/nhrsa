TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 330-C
ALCOHOL AND OTHER DRUG USE PROFESSIONALS

Section 330-C:21

    330-C:21 Applicants From Other States. – The board may license or issue a certification to any applicant who is licensed or certified in any other state, provided the other state's requirements are substantially equivalent to or more stringent than those of this state.

Source. 2008, 189:1. 2010, 249:17, eff. Sept. 4, 2010.
