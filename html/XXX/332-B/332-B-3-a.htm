TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 332-B
NEW HAMPSHIRE VETERINARY PRACTICE ACT

Section 332-B:3-a

    332-B:3-a Temporary Member. – The president or acting president of the board is authorized to appoint an additional person or persons to sit on a temporary or emergency basis at any hearing at which one or more board members is absent, so long as each person so chosen has in the past served as a board member.

Source. 1979, 401:1. 1999, 314:9, eff. Jan. 1, 2000.
