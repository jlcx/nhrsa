TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 328-E
NATUROPATHIC HEALTH CARE PRACTICE

Section 328-E:13

    328-E:13 License Renewal and Continuing Education. –
    I. The license to practice naturopathic medicine shall be renewed biennially. A fee in the amount of $300 shall accompany the application for renewal.
    II. As a condition of renewal of license, the board shall require each licensee to show proof at least every 3 years that the licensee has completed an approved continuing naturopathic medical education program within the preceding 3 years. For the purposes of this section an "approved continuing naturopathic medical education program'' means a program designed to continue the education of the licensee in current developments, skills, procedures, or treatment in the licensee's field of practice, which has been certified by a national or state naturopathic medical society or college or university and approved by the board.

Source. 1994, 392:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1995. 2014, 167:29, eff. July 1, 2014.
