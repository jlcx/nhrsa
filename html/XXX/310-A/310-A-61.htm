TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 310-A
JOINT BOARD OF LICENSURE AND CERTIFICATION

Land Surveyors

Section 310-A:61

    310-A:61 Receipts and Disbursements. – The board may employ such clerical or other assistants as are necessary for the proper performance of its work, and may make expenditures for any purpose which, in the opinion of the board, are reasonably necessary for the proper performance of its duties under this subdivision.

Source. 1981, 485:1. 1995, 284:56, eff. Jan. 1, 1996. 2013, 144:113, eff. July 1, 2013.
