OFFICE OF PROFESSIONAL LICENSURE AND CERTIFICATION

Septic System Evaluators

Section 310-A:220


[RSA 310-A:220 effective January 1, 2017.]
    310-A:220 Restraint of Violations. –
    The superior court shall have jurisdiction in equity to restrain violations of RSA 310-A:219 on proceedings brought by the attorney general or the board.

Source. 2016, 266:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2017.
