TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 310-A
OFFICE OF PROFESSIONAL LICENSURE AND CERTIFICATION

Septic System Evaluators

Section 310-A:202


[RSA 310-A:202 effective January 1, 2017.]
    310-A:202 Purpose. –
    The purpose of this subdivision is to assure that a septic system evaluator performs professional services only when the septic system evaluator is qualified by education and training in the specific technical areas involved.

Source. 2016, 266:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2017.
