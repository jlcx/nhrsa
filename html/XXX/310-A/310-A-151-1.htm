TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 310-A
JOINT BOARD OF LICENSURE AND CERTIFICATION

Licensing Landscape Architects

Section 310-A:151


[RSA 310-A:151 effective September 9, 2014; see also RSA 310-A:151 set out above.]
    310-A:151 Examinations. –
    Written technical examination in landscape architecture shall be held at least annually as the board shall determine. The scope of the technical and professional examination and the methods of procedure shall be prescribed by the board. A candidate failing an examination may apply for reexamination upon payment of an additional fee determined by the board and shall be reexamined on the next regularly scheduled examination date.

Source. 2006, 246:3, eff. July 1, 2006. 2014, 174:14, eff. Sept. 9, 2014.
