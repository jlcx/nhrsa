TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 310-A
OFFICE OF PROFESSIONAL LICENSURE AND CERTIFICATION

Septic System Evaluators

Section 310-A:204


[RSA 310-A:204 effective January 1, 2017.]
    310-A:204 Standards of Practice. –
    The board shall adopt, under RSA 541-A, minimum and uniform standards of practice which shall apply to New Hampshire septic system evaluators and be enforced by the board.

Source. 2016, 266:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2017.
