TITLE XXX
OCCUPATIONS AND PROFESSIONS

CHAPTER 332-H
DISTRIBUTION OF PUBLICATIONS BY LICENSING COMMISSIONS AND BOARDS

Section 332-H:3

    332-H:3 Cost of Publications. – Every board shall recover the cost of printing and issuing publications to examinees and licensees as provided in RSA 332-H:2. The expenses for such publications shall be covered by the examination and licensing fees charged by the board.

Source. 1986, 94:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1987.
