TITLE XXIX
RELIGIOUS SOCIETIES

CHAPTER 307
SALE, REPAIRS, ETC., OF MEETINGHOUSES

Sale

Section 307:5

    307:5 Division of Proceeds. – The proceeds of the sale, after the reasonable expenses incurred in making it have been paid therefrom, shall be divided among the parties interested in the property sold according to their respective interests.

Source. 1848, 719:3. CS 153:18. GS 140:3, 4. GL 154:3, 4. PS 153:5. PL 233:5. RL 282:5.
