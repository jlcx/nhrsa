TITLE XXIX
RELIGIOUS SOCIETIES

CHAPTER 307
SALE, REPAIRS, ETC., OF MEETINGHOUSES

Sale

Section 307:6

    307:6 Suit in Equity. – In case of disagreement as to division of the proceeds of sale, the same may be determined upon equity proceedings in the superior court as in other similar cases.

Source. PS 153:6. PL 233:6. RL 282:6.
