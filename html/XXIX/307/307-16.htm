TITLE XXIX
RELIGIOUS SOCIETIES

CHAPTER 307
SALE, REPAIRS, ETC., OF MEETINGHOUSES

Repairs, Alterations, Removal

Section 307:16

    307:16 Conveyance by Agent. – In case of sale, the proprietors may, by their agent, make a valid conveyance of the interest so sold to the purchaser.

Source. 1859, 2248:2. GS 140:10. GL 154:10. PS 153:16. PL 233:16. RL 282:16.
