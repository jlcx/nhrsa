TITLE XXIX
RELIGIOUS SOCIETIES

CHAPTER 306
RELIGIOUS SOCIETIES GENERALLY

Section 306:10

    306:10 Income. – The income of any grant or donation made to or for the use of a church shall not exceed $7,500 a year, exclusive of the income of any parsonage land granted to or for the use of the ministry.

Source. RS 144:14. CS 153:14. GS 139:12. GL 153:12. PS 152:10. 1923, 12:1. PL 232:10. RL 281:10.
