TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 287-E
BINGO AND LUCKY 7

Lucky 7

Section 287-E:16

    287-E:16 Administration and Enforcement. – The racing and charitable gaming commission shall administer and enforce this subdivision relating to the sale of lucky 7 tickets.

Source. 1983, 417:1. 1986, 67:13. 2004, 97:8; 257:12. 2008, 25:1, eff. July 11, 2008.
