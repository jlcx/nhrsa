TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 287-E
BINGO AND LUCKY 7

Bingo Games

Section 287-E:13

    287-E:13 Children's Bingo at Private Campgrounds or Hotels. – Any campground or hotel may conduct special bingo games for children under the conditions specified in RSA 287-E:12, except:
    I. No games shall be conducted prior to 11:00 a.m. on a weekday, prior to noon on a Sunday or after 8:00 p.m. on any day.
    II. There shall be no charge for play.
    III. Games shall be open only to persons under the age of 18.
    IV. Nonmonetary prizes may be awarded, but the value of any prize shall not exceed $2. Prizes or awards shall not be exchanged for money.

Source. 1983, 417:1, eff. July 1, 1983. 2011, 259:15, eff. Sept. 11, 2011.
