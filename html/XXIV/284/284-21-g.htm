TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 284
HORSE AND DOG RACING

Lottery

Section 284:21-g

    284:21-g Report of the Commission. – The commission shall make an annual report to the governor in accordance with RSA 20:7, including therein an account of its actions, receipts derived under the provisions of this subdivision, the practical effects of the application thereof, and any recommendation for legislation which the commission deems advisable.

Source. 1963, 52:1. 2007, 112:3, eff. Aug. 10, 2007.
