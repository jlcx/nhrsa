TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 284
HORSE AND DOG RACING

Racing and Charitable Gaming Commission

Section 284:7

    284:7 Office. – The state racing and charitable gaming commission shall have one office in Concord and during the time in which racing is conducted in the state may maintain branch offices elsewhere.

Source. 1933, 62. 1935, 27:3. RL 171:3. RSA 284:7. 1982, 42:143. 2008, 25:1, eff. July 11, 2008.
