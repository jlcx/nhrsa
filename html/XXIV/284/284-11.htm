TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 284
HORSE AND DOG RACING

Racing and Charitable Gaming Commission

Section 284:11

    284:11 Report of the Commission. – The racing and charitable gaming commission shall make an annual report to the governor and council in accordance with RSA 20:7, including therein an account of its actions, receipts derived under the provisions hereof, the practical effects of the application of this chapter, and any recommendation for legislation which the commission deems advisable.

Source. 1933, 62. 1935, 27:7. RL 171:7. RSA 284:11. 1961, 93:1. 1973, 140:6, eff. Jan. 1, 1974. 2010, 190:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2011.
