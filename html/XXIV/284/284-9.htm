TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 284
HORSE AND DOG RACING

Racing and Charitable Gaming Commission

Section 284:9

    284:9 Expenses. – Each member of the commission shall be allowed reasonable expenses, including transportation, incurred in the performance of the member's duties, to be audited and allowed by the governor and council.

Source. 1933, 62. 1935, 27:5. RL 171:5. 1947, 243:1. 1951, 246:2. RSA 284:9. 1961, 221:9. 1996, 157:3, eff. May 31, 1996.
