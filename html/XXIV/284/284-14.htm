TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 284
HORSE AND DOG RACING

Racing and Charitable Gaming Commission

Section 284:14

    284:14 Breeding of Horses. – The commission shall encourage and promote the improvement of the breed of horses in New Hampshire. It may accept donations of thoroughbred, standardbred, or other well-bred stallions by licensees or others to the state for this purpose. It may cooperate with and aid the officials of the University of New Hampshire in furthering this program.

Source. 1933, 62. 1935, 27:23. RL 171:26.
