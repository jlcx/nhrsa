TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 287-D
GAMES OF CHANCE

Section 287-D:9


[RSA 287-D:9 effective until July 1, 2015; see also RSA 287-D:9 set out below.]
    287-D:9 Subpoenas. –
    The racing and charitable gaming commission may issue subpoenas for witnesses and for documents relative to investigations or adjudicatory hearings held by the racing and charitable gaming commission.

Source. 2006, 311:15. 2008, 25:1, eff. July 11, 2008.
