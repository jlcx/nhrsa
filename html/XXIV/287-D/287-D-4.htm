TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 287-D
GAMES OF CHANCE

Section 287-D:4


[RSA 287-D:4 effective until July 1, 2015; see also RSA 287-D:4 set out below.]
    287-D:4 Agency Not Permitted. –
    No charitable organization shall act as an agent for conducting a game of chance, where it is unlawful for the charitable organization's principal to conduct such a game.

Source. 1977, 329:1, eff. June 29, 1977.
