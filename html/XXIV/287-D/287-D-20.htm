TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 287-D
GAMES OF CHANCE

Section 287-D:20

    287-D:20 Prizes. –
    I. In games where chips have no monetary value, 3 percent of all funds collected from players, less moneys used by the commission to fund authorized personnel expenses and related costs, shall be paid to the state treasurer to be deposited into the special fund established in RSA 284:21-j. Such payments shall be made once per month not later than the 5th day of the month for the funds collected in the previous month.
    II. In games where chips have monetary value, 10 percent of the rake or house winnings and other moneys collected by the game operator that are not paid out as prizes to players, less moneys used by the commission to fund authorized personnel expenses and related costs, shall be paid to the state treasurer for deposit into the special fund established in RSA 284:21-j. Such payments shall be made once per month not later than the 5th day of the month for the funds collected in the previous month.

Source. 2014, 329:1, eff. July 1, 2015.
