TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 285
BOXING AND WRESTLING COMMISSION

Section 285:2

    285:2 Establishment of Commission. – There is hereby established a boxing and wrestling commission which shall oversee amateur and professional fighting sports in the state.

Source. 1981, 457:1. 2008, 261:1, eff. June 26, 2008.
