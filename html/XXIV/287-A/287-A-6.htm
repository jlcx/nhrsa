TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 287-A
RAFFLES

General Provisions

Section 287-A:6

    287-A:6 Effect on Other Laws. – RSA 647 shall not apply to the sale of raffle tickets in the manner provided for in this chapter.

Source. 1971, 43:1. 1973, 40:4, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
