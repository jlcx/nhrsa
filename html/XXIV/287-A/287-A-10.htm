TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 287-A
RAFFLES

Raffles Held in Conjunction With Bingo Games

Section 287-A:10

    287-A:10 Prizes. – Prizes awarded at any raffle held in conjunction with a bingo game shall not exceed $500 wholesale cost.

Source. 1985, 374:1. 1991, 276:3, eff. June 10, 1991.
