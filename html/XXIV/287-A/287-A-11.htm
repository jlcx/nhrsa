TITLE XXIV
GAMES, AMUSEMENTS, AND ATHLETIC EXHIBITIONS

CHAPTER 287-A
RAFFLES

Raffles Held in Conjunction With Bingo Games

Section 287-A:11

    287-A:11 Permit Not Required. – The provisions of RSA 287-A:7 shall not apply to raffles held in conjunction with bingo games.

Source. 1985, 374:1, eff. June 18, 1985.
