TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 460
HUSBAND AND WIFE

Property Rights and Liabilities

Section 460:2-a

    460:2-a Antenuptial Agreements. – A man and woman in contemplation of marriage may enter into a written interspousal contract and the courts of this state shall give the same effect to such contracts entered in other jurisdictions as would the courts of that other jurisdiction. However, no contract otherwise enforceable under this section may contain any term which attempts to abrogate the statutory or common law rights of minor children of the contemplated marriage.

Source. 1981, 369:1, eff. Aug. 22, 1981.
