TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 457
MARRIAGES

Uniform Marriage Recognition Law

Section 457:44

    457:44 Nonresidents. – No marriage shall be contracted in this state by a party residing and intending to continue to reside in another jurisdiction if such marriage would be void if contracted in such other jurisdiction, and every marriage contracted in this state in violation hereof shall be null and void.

Source. 1979, 45:1, eff. June 10, 1979.
