TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 457
MARRIAGES

Proof of Marriage

Section 457:41


[RSA 457:41 effective until January 1, 2015; see also RSA 457:41 set out below.]
    457:41 In Criminal Cases. –
    In actions for criminal conversation, and in indictments for adultery, bigamy, and the like, there must be proof of a marriage in fact.

Source. GS 161:18. GL 180:18. PS 174:17. PL 286:38. RL 338:41.
