TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 457
MARRIAGES

Documentation of Marriages

Section 457:22

    457:22 Completion of Marriage License Application. – All persons proposing to be joined in marriage within the state shall complete a marriage license application with all facts required by RSA 5-C:41 to be entered in any town clerk's office. The clerk shall record the application in a book to be kept for that purpose.
