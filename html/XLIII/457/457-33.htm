TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 457
MARRIAGES

Solemnization of Marriage

Section 457:33

    457:33 Fee for Solemnizing. – The persons joined in marriage by a minister or justice of the peace shall pay the minister or justice a minimum of $5.

Source. RS 147:7. CS 156:7. GS 161:10. GL 180:10. PS 174:9. PL 286:30. RL 338:33. RSA 457:33. 1969, 244:2. 1988, 121:13, eff. Oct. 1, 1988.
