TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 457
MARRIAGES

Proof of Marriage

Section 457:38

    457:38 Certified Copy of Record. – A copy of the record of a marriage, certified by a city or town clerk or by the registrar of vital records, shall be received in all courts and places as evidence of the fact of the marriage.

Source. RS 147:14. CS 156:11. GS 161:12. GL 180:12. PS 174:14. PL 286:35. RL 338:38. RSA 457:38. 1981, 160:5. 2003, 319:72, eff. July 1, 2003.
