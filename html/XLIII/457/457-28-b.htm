TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 457
MARRIAGES

Documentation of Marriages

Section 457:28-b

    457:28-b Destruction of Records. – The clerk of the superior court and the clerk of the circuit court may destroy petitions by parties under age under RSA 457:6 and related documents after 5 years from the time of filing.

Source. 1981, 518:2, eff. Aug. 28, 1981. 2006, 86:3, eff. July 4, 2006. 2011, 88:20, eff. July 1, 2011.
