TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 458-A
UNIFORM CHILD CUSTODY JURISDICTION AND ENFORCEMENT ACT

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 458-A:39

    458-A:39 Application and Construction. – In applying and construing this chapter, consideration must be given to the need to promote uniformity of the law with respect to its subject matter among states that enact it.

Source. 2009, 191:1, eff. Dec. 1, 2010.
