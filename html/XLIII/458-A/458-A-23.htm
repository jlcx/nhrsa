TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 458-A
UNIFORM CHILD CUSTODY JURISDICTION AND ENFORCEMENT ACT

Enforcement

Section 458-A:23

    458-A:23 Enforcement Under Hague Convention. – Under this subdivision a court of this state may enforce an order for the return of the child made under the Hague Convention on the Civil Aspects of International Child Abduction as if it were a child-custody determination.

Source. 2009, 191:1, eff. Dec. 1, 2010.
