TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 458-A
UNIFORM CHILD CUSTODY JURISDICTION AND ENFORCEMENT ACT

Section 458-A:2

    458-A:2 Proceedings Governed by Other Law. – This chapter does not govern an adoption proceeding or a proceeding pertaining to the authorization of emergency medical care for a child.

Source. 2009, 191:1, eff. Dec. 1, 2010.
