TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 458-A
UNIFORM CHILD CUSTODY JURISDICTION AND ENFORCEMENT ACT

Enforcement

Section 458-A:38

    458-A:38 Costs and Expenses. – If the respondent is not the prevailing party, the court may assess against the respondent all direct expenses and costs incurred by the prosecutor or other appropriate public official and law enforcement officers under RSA 458-A:36 or RSA 458-A:37.

Source. 2009, 191:1, eff. Dec. 1, 2010.
