TITLE XLIII
DOMESTIC RELATIONS

CHAPTER 458-A
UNIFORM CHILD CUSTODY JURISDICTION AND ENFORCEMENT ACT

Enforcement

Section 458-A:30

    458-A:30 Service of Petition and Order. – Except as otherwise provided in RSA 458-A:32, the petition and order must be served, by any method authorized by the law of this state, upon the respondent and any person who has physical custody of the child.

Source. 2009, 191:1, eff. Dec. 1, 2010.
