TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 483-B
SHORELAND WATER QUALITY PROTECTION ACT

Section 483-B:7

    483-B:7 Reporting; On-Site Inspections; Local Participation. – The department may devise a system whereby municipal officials may voluntarily assist with the permitting process under RSA 483-B:6 and the subsequent enforcement of permit conditions, by performing certain reporting functions relative to on-site inspections. Utilization of such reports shall be at the department's discretion, but may, when appropriate, obviate the need for further on-site review by department staff.

Source. 1991, 303:1. 1992, 235:23, eff. Jan. 1, 1993.
