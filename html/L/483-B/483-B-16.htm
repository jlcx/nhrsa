TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 483-B
SHORELAND WATER QUALITY PROTECTION ACT

Section 483-B:16

    483-B:16 Assistance to Municipalities; Office of State Planning and Energy Programs. – The office of energy and planning may assist municipalities with the implementation of local ordinances under this chapter, upon the request of an individual municipality.

Source. 1991, 303:1. 2003, 319:9, eff. July 1, 2003. 2004, 257:44, eff. July 1, 2004.
