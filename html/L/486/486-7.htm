TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 486
AID TO MUNICIPALITIES FOR WATER POLLUTION CONTROL

Section 486:7

    486:7 Application for Funding. – Application for payments under the provisions of this chapter shall be made in accordance with rules adopted by the department under RSA 541-A, and shall be based upon reports filed with the department prior to January 31 in the calendar year for which payment is being requested.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1996, 228:106, eff. July 1, 1996.
