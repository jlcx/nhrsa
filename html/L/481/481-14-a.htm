TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 481
STATE DAMS, RESERVOIRS AND OTHER WATER CONSERVATION PROJECTS

General Provisions

Section 481:14-a

    481:14-a Terms Defined. – The words "appurtenances thereto'' as used in RSA 481:14 shall be deemed to include only land actually occupied by physical structures of the department and dam and to exclude all lands whether submerged or out of water.

Source. 1957, 294:1. 1986, 202:6, I(k). 1996, 228:106, eff. July 1, 1996.
