TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Proceedings in Court

Section 482:34

    482:34 Contents of Petition. – The petition shall set out the title and description of the premises damaged, the right by reason of which the grievance arises, the location of the dam and the extent of the damages that the dam or dam addition has or may have occasioned.

Source. 1989, 339:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
