TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Dams in Disrepair; Acquisition by Municipality

Section 482:73

    482:73 Employment of Assistants. – A municipality acting through its local legislative body may employ engineers, counsel and others and defray other expenses incident to proceedings under this subdivision.

Source. 1989, 339:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
