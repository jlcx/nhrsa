TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Dams in Disrepair; Acquisition by Municipality

Section 482:68

    482:68 Notice. – Upon receipt of the petition provided for in RSA 482:67, the department shall notify the owners of property likely to be affected by such proceedings by publication as provided in RSA 482:61, and set a time and place for a hearing to determine what property shall be taken by the municipality in order for it to acquire the dam and affected property. Such notice shall be considered adequate as to all persons affected by these and all subsequent proceedings before the department to acquire the said property.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1996, 228:106, eff. July 1, 1996.
