TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Acquisition by State of Certain Dams and Water Rights

Section 482:50

    482:50 Dams Owned by Fish and Game. – Revenue to the state resulting from the leasing for hydroelectric generation of any dams owned by the department of fish and game which were funded by the Pittman-Robertson or Dingell-Johnson federal aid in fish and wildlife restoration programs shall be credited to the fund established by RSA 482:55, but shall be expended exclusively for work on any federally-funded dams owned by the department of fish and game. Such revenue shall be so expended during the same year in which it is generated.

Source. 1989, 339:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
