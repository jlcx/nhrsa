TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Dam Maintenance Fund

Section 482:56

    482:56 Bonds Authorized. – To provide working capital for initiation of the fund established in RSA 482:55, the state treasurer is hereby authorized to borrow upon the credit of the state the sum of $9,800,000 and for said purpose shall issue bonds and notes in the name of and on behalf of the state of New Hampshire in accordance with RSA 6-A.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1991, 351:7. 1993, 359:5. 1995, 309:27. 1997, 349:17, eff. July 1, 1997.
