TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Dams in Disrepair; Acquisition by Municipality

Section 482:74

    482:74 Access to Dam. – Access to a dam and adjacent property may be had at all times by representatives of the department or a municipality to implement the purposes and intent of this subdivision.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1996, 228:106, eff. July 1, 1996.
