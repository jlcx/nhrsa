TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Acquisition by State of Certain Dams and Water Rights

Section 482:52

    482:52 Investigation and Construction of a Lake Management Structure at Silver Lake in Belmont. – The department is authorized to investigate conditions affecting the use and enjoyment of Silver Lake and when funds are available therefor to construct an outlet control structure and make channel improvements.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1996, 228:106, eff. July 1, 1996.
