TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Dams in Disrepair; Acquisition by Municipality

Section 482:62

    482:62 Inspection. – Upon receipt of a petition under RSA 482:60, the department may, either before or after said hearing, cause an inspection of said dam to be made by a competent person, who may be one of its employees.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1996, 228:106, eff. July 1, 1996.
