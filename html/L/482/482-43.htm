TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

License to Flow Public Lands

Section 482:43

    482:43 Deeds. – Any such grant of right of flowage and the conditions upon which the same is made shall be evidenced by deed executed in the name of the state by the governor and attested by the secretary of state and recorded in the county where the property is situated.

Source. 1989, 339:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
