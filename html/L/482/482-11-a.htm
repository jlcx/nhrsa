TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Erection and Inspection of Dams

Section 482:11-a

    482:11-a Duty of Owner. – The owner of a dam shall maintain and repair the dam so that it shall not become a dam in disrepair. The owner shall develop an emergency action plan for any dam, the failure of which may threaten life or property.

Source. 1997, 178:3, eff. Aug. 16, 1997.
