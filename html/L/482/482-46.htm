TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Dams in Disrepair and Channel Improvement

Section 482:46

    482:46 Application of Statutes. – The provisions of RSA 481:1-19, 482:85, and 482:91 shall apply to the facilities undertaken under the authority of this subdivision insofar as the same are not inconsistent with the provisions of this subdivision.

Source. 1989, 339:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
