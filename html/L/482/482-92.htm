TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Administration, Procedure, and Appeals

Section 482:92

    482:92 Interstate Commerce. – Nothing in this chapter shall be construed as applying to interstate or foreign commerce except as may be permitted by the federal constitution and laws enacted under it.

Source. 1989, 339:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
