TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482
DAMS, MILLS, AND FLOWAGE

Proceedings in Court

Section 482:38

    482:38 Jury Trial. – Before reference of such petition to the committee, if either party shall so elect, the court shall direct an issue to the jury to the facts alleged in the petition and to assess the damages, and judgment shall be rendered on the verdict of the jury. The court may award costs to either party at its discretion.

Source. 1989, 339:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
