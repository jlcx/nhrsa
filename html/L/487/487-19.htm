TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 487
CONTROL OF MARINE POLLUTION AND AQUATIC GROWTH

New Hampshire Clean Lakes Program

Section 487:19

    487:19 Public Hearings. – No project for the implementation of a lakes restoration or preservation program shall be approved or initiated until at least 2 public hearings have been held on the project. Said hearings shall be held in one or more of the affected municipalities.

Source. 1990, 143:3, eff. June 18, 1990.
