TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 487
CONTROL OF MARINE POLLUTION AND AQUATIC GROWTH

New Hampshire Volunteer Lake Assessment Program

Section 487:32

    487:32 Volunteer Lake Assessment Program Coordinator. – There is established in the department of environmental services a state volunteer lake assessment program coordinator who shall be a classified employee qualified by reason of education and experience and who shall administer the New Hampshire volunteer lake assessment program.

Source. 2005, 114:1, eff. Aug. 14, 2005.
