TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 487
CONTROL OF MARINE POLLUTION AND AQUATIC GROWTH

New Hampshire Clean Lakes Program

Section 487:22

    487:22 Municipal Agreements. – Whenever a project requires a commitment of cooperative action or local cost sharing involving 2 or more municipalities, all participating municipalities shall execute an intermunicipal agreement relative to their respective obligations. No project which requires a local match shall be initiated with state funding until such an agreement, if applicable, has been approved by the legislative bodies of all the involved municipalities.

Source. 1990, 143:3, eff. June 18, 1990.
