TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 487
CONTROL OF MARINE POLLUTION AND AQUATIC GROWTH

Marine Toilets and Disposal of Sewage from Boats

Section 487:9

    487:9 Required Registrations. – No boat shall be operated upon waters of this state having within or on it a marine toilet without a certificate of registration from the department of safety, division of motor vehicles, as required by RSA 270 and RSA 270-E.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1990, 229:22, eff. Jan. 1, 1991.
