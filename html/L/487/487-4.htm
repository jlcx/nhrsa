TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 487
CONTROL OF MARINE POLLUTION AND AQUATIC GROWTH

Marine Toilets and Disposal of Sewage From Boats

Section 487:4

    487:4 Responsibility of Marinas and Shore Property Owners. –
    I. All owners and operators of any marina or shore property located on fresh waters of the state shall cooperate with the department by publicizing the requirements of this chapter among the boating fraternity and other interested parties.
    II. The provisions of this chapter shall not apply to new boats held in inventory by marine dealers which are placed in fresh waters of the state for demonstration purposes only; provided that toilets, sinks, or showers on said new boats shall not be placed in commission and shall be sealed and clearly posted to preclude their use.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1996, 228:106, eff. July 1, 1996.
