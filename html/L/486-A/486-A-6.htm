TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 486-A
AID TO PUBLIC WATER SYSTEMS

Section 486-A:6

    486-A:6 Application for Funding. – Application for payments under the provisions of this chapter shall be made in accordance with rules adopted by the commissioner under RSA 541-A, and, for surface water treatment projects and regional water system projects and evaluation of groundwaters contributing area of public wells, shall be based upon reports filed with the commissioner prior to January 31, in the calendar year for which payment is being requested.

Source. 1993, 341:1. 1996, 228:110. 2000, 310:1. 2003, 178:8, eff. July 1, 2003.
