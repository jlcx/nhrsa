TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 486-A
AID TO PUBLIC WATER SYSTEMS

Section 486-A:10

    486-A:10 Notice to Public Utilities Commission. – Any public water system regulated by the public utilities commission shall notify the public utilities commission when it receives funds under the provisions of this chapter, to ensure that the money received is not used in calculating rate setting.

Source. 1993, 341:1, eff. June 30, 1993.
