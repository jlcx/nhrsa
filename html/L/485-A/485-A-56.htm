TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 485-A
WATER POLLUTION AND WASTE DISPOSAL

Certain Household Cleansing Products Prohibited

Section 485-A:56

    485-A:56 Products Prohibited. – No household cleansing products except those used for lead exposure hazard control purposes shall be distributed, sold or offered for sale in this state, which contain a phosphorus compound in concentrations in excess of a trace quantity.

Source. 1994, 303:1. 1995, 306:7. 2009, 282:1, eff. July 1, 2010.
