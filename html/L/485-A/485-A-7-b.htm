TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 485-A
WATER POLLUTION AND WASTE DISPOSAL

Wastewater Operator Certification

Section 485-A:7-b

    485-A:7-b Examinations. – The department shall prepare written examinations to determine the knowledge, ability, and judgment of operators. Such examinations shall be administered in accordance with rules adopted by the department pursuant to RSA 485-A:6.

Source. 1990, 197:6. 1996, 228:106, eff. July 1, 1996.
