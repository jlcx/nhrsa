TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 485-A
WATER POLLUTION AND WASTE DISPOSAL

Certain Household Cleansing Products Prohibited

Section 485-A:57

    485-A:57 Penalty. – Any person who violates the provisions of this subdivision shall be subject to a civil penalty not to exceed $50.

Source. 1994, 303:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1995.
