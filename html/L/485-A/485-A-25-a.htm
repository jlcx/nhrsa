TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 485-A
WATER POLLUTION AND WASTE DISPOSAL

Safety Regulations for Camps, Pools, and Bathing Places

Section 485-A:25-a

    485-A:25-a Statement of Health for Recreational Camps. – Notwithstanding any law or rule to the contrary, any physical examination which is required before a child may enter a recreational camp may be conducted by a physician, a licensed advanced nurse practitioner or a physician assistant.

Source. 1990, 102:1, eff. April 13, 1990.
