TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 485-A
WATER POLLUTION AND WASTE DISPOSAL

Classification of Waters

Section 485-A:11

    485-A:11 Public Waters Classified. – All lakes and ponds defined as public waters of the state by RSA 271:20 shall be classified by the passage of this section as not less than Class B, as set forth in RSA 485-A:8 relating to standards for classification of surface waters of the state.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1999, 232:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2000.
