TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 485-A
WATER POLLUTION AND WASTE DISPOSAL

Safety Regulations for Camps, Pools, and Bathing Places

Section 485-A:27

    485-A:27 Injunction. – Any person operating or maintaining a recreation camp, youth skill camp, public swimming pool, or bathing place without the same having been approved by the department may be enjoined by the superior court or any justice of the court upon petition brought by the attorney general.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1996, 228:106. 1997, 267:1, eff. July 1, 1997. 2013, 250:5, eff. Jan. 1, 2014.
