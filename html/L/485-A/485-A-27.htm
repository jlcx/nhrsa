TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 485-A
WATER POLLUTION AND WASTE DISPOSAL

Safety Regulations

Section 485-A:27

    485-A:27 Injunction. – Any person operating or maintaining a youth camp or public swimming pool or bathing place without the same having been approved by the department may be enjoined by the superior court or any justice of the court upon petition brought by the attorney general.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1996, 228:106. 1997, 267:1, eff. July 1, 1997.
