TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482-A
FILL AND DREDGE IN WETLANDS

Section 482-A:9

    482-A:9 Notice to Abutters. – Like notice shall be mailed to all known abutting landowners, supplemented by reasonable notice by newspaper publications to those unknown, as may be ordered by the department.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1996, 296:44, eff. Aug. 9, 1996.
