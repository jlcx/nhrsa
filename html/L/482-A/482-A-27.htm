TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482-A
FILL AND DREDGE IN WETLANDS

Restrictions on Use of Structures Built Over the Waters of the State

Section 482-A:27

    482-A:27 Penalty. – Any person who violates any provision of RSA 482-A:26 shall be required to remove the structure or portion of the structure constructed, reconstructed, repaired, converted, or modified in violation of said section and shall be subject to the civil, criminal, and other penalties set forth in RSA 482-A:13, 14, and 14-b. Any criminal fine collected for a violation of RSA 482-A:26 shall accrue to the use of the municipality in which the structure is located.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1998, 23:3, eff. Jan. 1, 1999.
