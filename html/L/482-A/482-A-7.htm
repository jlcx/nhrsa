TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482-A
FILL AND DREDGE IN WETLANDS

Section 482-A:7

    482-A:7 Gifts, Grants or Donations. – The department is authorized to solicit and receive any gifts, grants or donations made for the efforts of the department under the provisions of this chapter and to disburse and administer the same through the department.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1996, 296:44, eff. Aug. 9, 1996.
