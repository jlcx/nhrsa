TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482-A
FILL AND DREDGE IN WETLANDS

Aquatic Resource Compensatory Mitigation

Section 482-A:28

    482-A:28 Aquatic Resource Compensatory Mitigation. – In lieu of other forms of compensatory mitigation, the department may accept payment for an unavoidable loss of aquatic resource functions and values from impacts to resources protected under this chapter.

Source. 2006, 313:1. 2009, 303:1, eff. Sept. 29, 2009.
