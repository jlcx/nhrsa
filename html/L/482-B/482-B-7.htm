TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 482-B
NEW HAMPSHIRE WATER WELL BOARD

Section 482-B:7

    482-B:7 Plumbers Exempted. – This chapter shall not restrict a plumber from engaging in the trade for which the plumber has been licensed if such plumber holds a pump installer's license. No examination shall be required for a person who is a plumber licensed under RSA 153:27-a and holds a valid pump installer's license on the effective date of this section.

Source. 1989, 339:1. 1998, 89:12, eff. July 18, 1998. 2013, 275:13, eff. July 1, 2013.
