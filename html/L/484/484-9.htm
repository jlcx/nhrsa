TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 484
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION COMPACTS

Merrimack River Flood Control Compact

Section 484:9

    484:9 Appointment of Commission. – Within 30 days after the aforesaid agreement or compact shall have become effective as provided in RSA 484:8, the governor shall, with the advice and consent of the council, appoint 3 members of the Merrimack River Valley Flood Control Commission, one of whom shall serve until March 1, 1958, one of whom shall serve until March 1, 1959 and one of whom shall serve until March 1, 1960. In the month of February 1958, and each year thereafter, the governor, by and with the advice and consent of the council, shall appoint one member of the said commission, whose term of office shall be 3 years from and after March 1 of the year in which he is appointed.

Source. 1989, 339:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1990.
