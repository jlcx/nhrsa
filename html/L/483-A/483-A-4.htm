TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 483-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE LAKES MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION PROGRAM

Section 483-A:4

    483-A:4 Lakes Coordinator. – There is established in the office of the commissioner, department of environmental services, a state lakes coordinator, who shall be a classified employee qualified by reason of education and experience, and who shall administer the New Hampshire lakes management and protection program.

Source. 1990, 118:2, eff. June 18, 1990.
