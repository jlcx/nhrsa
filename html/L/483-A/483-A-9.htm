TITLE L
WATER MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION

CHAPTER 483-A
NEW HAMPSHIRE LAKES MANAGEMENT AND PROTECTION PROGRAM

Section 483-A:9

    483-A:9 State Agency Cooperation. – Affected state agencies shall cooperate with and assist the lakes coordinator and the advisory committee in the development and implementation of lakes management plans established under RSA 483-A:7.

Source. 1990, 118:2, eff. June 18, 1990.
