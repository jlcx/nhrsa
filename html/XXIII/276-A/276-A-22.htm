TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 276-A
YOUTH EMPLOYMENT LAW

Hours of Labor

Section 276-A:22

    276-A:22 Evidence of Violation. – The employment of any youth in any place or establishment defined in RSA 276-A:11 at any time other than the posted hours of labor shall be prima facie evidence of a violation of this subdivision.

Source. 1989, 53:1, eff. June 18, 1989.
