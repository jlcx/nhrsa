TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 276-A
YOUTH EMPLOYMENT LAW

Hours of Labor

Section 276-A:20

    276-A:20 Notice of Hours. – Every employer shall post in a conspicuous place in every room where youths are employed a printed notice stating the hours of work, the time allowed for dinner or other meals, and the maximum number of hours any youth is permitted to work in any one day.

Source. 1989, 53:1, eff. June 18, 1989.
