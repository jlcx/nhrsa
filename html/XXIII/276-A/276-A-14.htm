TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 276-A
YOUTH EMPLOYMENT LAW

Hours of Labor

Section 276-A:14

    276-A:14 Special Agreement. – By mutual agreement between employer and employees, the provisions of RSA 276-A:13 relative to restrictions on night work for youths may be relaxed, if the agreement is approved by the commissioner.

Source. 1989, 53:1, eff. June 18, 1989.
