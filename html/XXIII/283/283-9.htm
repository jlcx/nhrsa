TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 283
PREFERENCE OF RESIDENT LABORERS AND VETERANS

Clerks, Mechanics, Etc.

Section 283:9

    283:9 Penalty. – Any person who violates any provision of this subdivision shall be guilty of a violation if a natural person, or guilty of a misdemeanor if any other person.

Source. 1943, 190:5 par. 8. RSA 283:9. 1973, 530:45, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
