TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 283
PREFERENCE OF RESIDENT LABORERS AND VETERANS

Clerks, Mechanics, Etc.

Section 283:6

    283:6 State and Political Subdivisions. – The hiring authority of the state or any political subdivision thereof shall take any necessary action to secure the employment of said veterans in said service of the state or political subdivisions thereof respectively.

Source. 1935, 144:2. RL 219:5. RSA 283:6. 1971, 399:1, eff. Aug. 27, 1971.
