TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 283
PREFERENCE OF RESIDENT LABORERS AND VETERANS

Clerks, Mechanics, Etc.

Section 283:8

    283:8 Terms Defined. – For the purposes of this chapter:
    I. The term "armed forces'' shall include those forces listed in RSA 72:28, IV.
    II. The terms "in time of war'' and "during any war'' shall include all the terms used in RSA 72:28, V.

Source. 1943, 190:5, par. 7. RSA 283:8. 1969, 370:1. 1971, 399:4. 2003, 299:22, eff. April 1, 2003.
