TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 275-A
CITIZENS JOB PROTECTION

Section 275-A:4-a

    275-A:4-a Employment of Illegal Aliens Prohibited. – No employer may employ an alien whom the employer knows is not a citizen of the United States and not in possession of Form I-151, Alien Registration Receipt Card or any other document issued by the United States Immigration and Naturalization Service or the Attorney General of the United States which authorizes him to work.

Source. 1976, 31:1, eff. July 25, 1976.
