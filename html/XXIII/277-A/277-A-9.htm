TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 277-A
TOXIC SUBSTANCES IN THE WORKPLACE

Section 277-A:9

    277-A:9 Penalty. – Any person who violates any provisions of this chapter shall be liable for a penalty of not more than $2,500 for each such violation, to be collected in a civil action by the commissioner of labor. If the violation is of a continuing nature, each day during which it continues shall constitute an additional and separate offense.

Source. 1983, 466:1, eff. Oct. 26, 1983.
