TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 279
MINIMUM WAGE LAW

Hourly Rate

Section 279:22-a

    279:22-a Special Authorization for Sheltered Workshops. – Nonprofit organizations or institutions wishing to engage in a sheltered workshop program must make application to the labor commissioner for special authorization for sub-minimum wage rates as provided by regulations issued by the commissioner of labor.

Source. 1967, 440:12, eff. Feb. 1, 1968.
