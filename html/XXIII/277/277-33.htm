TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 277
SAFETY AND HEALTH OF EMPLOYEES

Inspectors and Other Assistants

Section 277:33

    277:33 Inspectors. – For the purpose of inspecting establishments subject to this chapter, the commissioner may employ competent persons who shall be known as inspectors, one of whom shall be a woman, and shall fix their compensation in accordance with the state personnel regulations and within the limits of available appropriations and funds.

Source. 1917, 183:12. 1921, 130:2. PL 177:29. RL 215:29. 1950, 5, part 18:13. 1951, 168:1. RSA 277:33. 1957, 172:1. 1963, 220:7, eff. July 1, 1963.
