TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 277
SAFETY AND HEALTH OF EMPLOYEES

Review of Orders

Section 277:26

    277:26 Hearing. – Upon receipt of the petition the commissioner shall, if necessary to determine the issue raised, order a hearing. Notice of the time and place of hearing, which shall be open to the public, shall be given to the petitioner and to such other persons as the commissioner may find to be directly interested in the issues raised.

Source. 1917, 183:7. PL 177:22. RL 215:22.
