TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 277
SAFETY AND HEALTH OF EMPLOYEES

Building Construction and Repair Work

Section 277:4

    277:4 Weight Requirement. – All scaffolding shall be so constructed as to bear 4 times the maximum weight required to be dependent therefrom or placed thereon when in use.

Source. 1953, 201:1 par. 39-c, eff. June 3, 1953.
