TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 277
SAFETY AND HEALTH OF EMPLOYEES

Generally

Section 277:1

    277:1 Application. – This chapter shall apply to all places of employment in which one or more persons are regularly employed.

Source. 1917, 183:1. 1919, 66:1. 1921, 130:1. PL 177:1. RL 215:1. RSA 277:1. 1955, 291:1. 1963, 220:1. 1985, 243:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1986.
