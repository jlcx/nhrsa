TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 277
SAFETY AND HEALTH OF EMPLOYEES

Inspections and Orders by Commissioner

Section 277:13

    277:13 Inspection. – The labor commissioner shall cause all places of employment subject to this law to be inspected at intervals to be determined by him, for the purpose of determining whether the provisions of this chapter have been complied with and the conditions therein with respect to the safety and health of the employees.

Source. 1917, 183:4. PL 177:9. RL 215:9. RSA 277:13. 1957, 187:15. 1963, 220:2, eff. July 1, 1963.
