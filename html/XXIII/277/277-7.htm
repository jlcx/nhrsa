TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 277
SAFETY AND HEALTH OF EMPLOYEES

Maintenance of First Aid Equipment, Appliances, Etc.

Section 277:7

    277:7 Penalty. – Whoever violates RSA 277:6 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor if a natural person, or guilty of a felony if any other person. Each week during which such violation continues shall constitute a separate offense.

Source. 1911, 30:2. PL 177:3. RL 215:3. RSA 277:7. 1973, 529:55, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
