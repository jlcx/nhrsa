TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 277
SAFETY AND HEALTH OF EMPLOYEES

Inspections and Orders by Commissioner

Section 277:21-a

    277:21-a Consultation. – The commissioner shall provide occupational health and safety services to public and private employers, and shall consult with the commissioner of the department of health and human services relative to the implementation of occupational health unit programs authorized by RSA 125:16-16-d.

Source. 1983, 291:1, I; 410:2. 1995, 310:182, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
