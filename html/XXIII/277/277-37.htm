TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 277
SAFETY AND HEALTH OF EMPLOYEES

Prosecutions, Penalties, Etc.

Section 277:37

    277:37 Statements. – Every employer subject to the provisions of RSA 277 shall immediately send to the commissioner a statement setting forth his name, address, business and approximate number of employees. Any employer wilfully neglecting to comply with the provisions of this section shall be guilty of a violation.

Source. 1917, 183:14. PL 177:33. RL 215:33. RSA 277:37. 1973, 531:95, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
