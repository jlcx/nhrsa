TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 277
SAFETY AND HEALTH OF EMPLOYEES

Inspectors and Other Assistants

Section 277:34

    277:34 Assistants. – The commissioner may employ such other assistants and clerical employees as may be necessary to the proper discharge of his duties.

Source. 1917, 183:12. 1921, 130:2. PL 177:30. RL 215:30. RSA 277:34. 1963, 220:8, eff. July 1, 1963.
