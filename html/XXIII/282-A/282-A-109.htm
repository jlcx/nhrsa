TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Administrative Organization and Administration

Section 282-A:109

    282-A:109 Deputy Commissioner. – There shall be a deputy commissioner of the department of employment security who shall act as commissioner whenever the commissioner of the department of employment security is incapacitated, absent or unable to act for any cause. The deputy commissioner shall also act as commissioner of the department of employment security until a new commissioner is duly appointed whenever there is no commissioner. During such period as the deputy commissioner acts as commissioner, his status as a classified state employee shall continue and shall in no way be altered, affected or changed.
