TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Administrative Organization and Administration

Section 282-A:108

    282-A:108 Commissioner; Term; Removal; Salary. –
    I. There shall be a commissioner of the department of employment security who shall be appointed by the governor with the advice and consent of the council.
    II. The commissioner shall hold office for 5 years from the date of appointment. Otherwise the commissioner may be removed only as provided in RSA 4:1.
    III. The annual salary of the commissioner of the department of employment security shall be that prescribed by RSA 94:1-4.
