TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Contributions

Section 282-A:76

    282-A:76 Overpayments Chargeable to Fund. – The account of the most recent employer shall immediately be relieved of charges where benefits are determined to be overpayments as a result of the application of any provision of this chapter, and such benefits shall be charged against the fund, unless the overpayment was caused by error of the employer; in such case, benefits shall be charged to the fund in an amount equal to the benefits that have been repaid from any source to the unemployment compensation fund.
