TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Administrative Organization and Administration

Section 282-A:114

    282-A:114 Publication of Rules. – The commissioner of the department of employment security shall cause to be printed in proper form for distribution to the public the text of this chapter, his rules, his annual reports to the governor and any other material he deems relevant and suitable and shall furnish the same to any person upon request.
