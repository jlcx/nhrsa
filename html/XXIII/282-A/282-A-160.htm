TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Protection of Rights and Benefits

Section 282-A:160

    282-A:160 Prohibition Against Discrimination. – No person shall discriminate in any way against another person because of his appearance or intended appearance as a witness or party, or for giving or furnishing information in connection with any proceeding under this chapter or an appeal therefrom. Any person who violates any provision of this section shall be subject to the penalties provided in RSA 282-A:161-168.

Source. 1937, 178:1. RL 218:12. 1953, 261:4. RSA 282:13(D). 1957, 118:15, 16. 1973, 528:175, 176. 1981, 408:3, eff. Oct. 1, 1981.
