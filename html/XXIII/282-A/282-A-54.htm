TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Appeals

Section 282-A:54

    282-A:54 Tenure. – The appointed members shall serve at the pleasure of the commissioner and shall be paid $40 for each day or any part thereof during which they perform services at the request of the commissioner. They shall be reimbursed for all expenses determined by the commissioner to be necessary to the performance of their duties.
