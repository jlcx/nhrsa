TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Administrative Organization and Administration

Section 282-A:116

    282-A:116 Segregation of Special Risks. – The commissioner of the department of employment security shall investigate and report upon the degree of unemployment hazard in various industries and occupations and their cost to the unemployment fund. He shall recommend to employers in industries or occupations showing an excessive cost to the fund means for stabilizing employment. He shall also, if necessary, recommend to the legislature a higher rate of contribution for any classification of industries or occupations in which unemployment is excessive or chronic.
