TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Claims for Benefits

Section 282-A:51

    282-A:51 Opportunity to Appear. – It shall be a condition precedent to the cessation of payment of benefits to any individual that he be given an opportunity to appear in person at a designated office in accordance with the commissioner's rules to be heard relative to his continued entitlement to benefits. His failure to appear within the designated period shall be deemed a waiver of such right, and he shall be bound by the determination unless he appeals as provided in RSA 282-A:48.
