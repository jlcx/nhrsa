TITLE XXIII

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Contributions

Section 282-A:97

    282-A:97 Appeal to Appellate Board. – The commissioner's decision on said appeal shall be final and conclusive as to the liability of the employing unit or employer or both unless an appeal therefrom is filed in accordance with the provisions of RSA 282-A:64.
