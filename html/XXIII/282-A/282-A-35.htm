TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Disqualification for Benefits

Section 282-A:35

    282-A:35 Gross Misconduct. – An unemployed individual who has been discharged for arson, sabotage, felony, assault which causes bodily injury, criminal threatening, or a single theft or multiple thefts in the aggregate of an amount equal to or greater than $250, where such conduct is connected with his or her work, shall suffer the loss of all wage credits earned prior to the date of such dismissal.
