TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Period for Election and Termination of Employer's Coverage

Section 282-A:100

    282-A:100 Termination; Death, Etc. – The commissioner may terminate an employer and permanently remove his separate account as of the time an employer has died, ceased business or removed from the state.

Source. 1937, 178:1. RL 218:7. 1945, 138:17, 18. 1953, 261:1. RSA 282:7(B)(1). 1957, 118:8. 1971, 156:30. 1981, 408:3, eff. Oct. 1, 1981.
