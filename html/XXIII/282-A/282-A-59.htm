TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Appeals

Section 282-A:59

    282-A:59 Appearance and Postponement. – In order to protect the rights of the interested parties, if the commissioner, in his sole judgment, finds sufficient grounds to justify or excuse an interested party from appearing at a hearing or from requesting a postponement of a hearing, he shall direct the appeal tribunal to hold a further hearing. Such further hearing shall be de novo notwithstanding that a decision may have become final.
