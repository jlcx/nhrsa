TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Claims for Benefits

Section 282-A:47

    282-A:47 Referral to Appeal Tribunal. – Any claim or question involved in a determination may be referred by the certifying officer to an appeal tribunal, and such appeal tribunal shall make its decision with respect thereto in accordance with the procedure described in RSA 282-A:53-58.
