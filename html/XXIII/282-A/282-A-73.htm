TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Contributions

Section 282-A:73

    282-A:73 Credit for Other Funds Received. –
    I. The state and all other employers who have reimbursed the unemployment compensation fund for benefits paid shall be given a credit against future billing, or, in whole or in part, shall be given a refund at the discretion of the commissioner in an amount equal to the amount of such benefits which are repaid to the unemployment compensation fund from any other source.
    II. The provisions of RSA 282-A:74, RSA 282-A:75 and RSA 282-A:76 shall not apply to any employer electing reimbursement under RSA 282-A:69, II, RSA 282-A:70, RSA 282-A:71, or RSA 282-A:72.
