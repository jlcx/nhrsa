TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Administrative Organization and Administration

Section 282-A:110

    282-A:110 Expenses. – Subject to compliance with laws and regulations approved by the United States department of labor, the actual expenses incurred by the commissioner of the department of employment security in the work of his office shall be paid.
