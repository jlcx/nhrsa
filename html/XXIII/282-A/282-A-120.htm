TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 282-A
UNEMPLOYMENT COMPENSATION

Administrative Organization and Administration

Section 282-A:120

    282-A:120 Destruction of Records. – The commissioner may by rules order the destruction, after reasonable periods, of any and all records, reports, transcripts or reproductions thereof, or other papers kept pursuant to the administration of the unemployment compensation law, which are not considered by him as necessary to the administration of this chapter.
