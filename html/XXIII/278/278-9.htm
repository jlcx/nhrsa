TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 278
APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAMS IN TRADE AND INDUSTRY

Section 278:9

    278:9 Apprenticeship Agreements Defined. – For the purposes of this chapter an apprenticeship agreement is an individual written agreement between an employer and an apprentice, or a written agreement between an apprentice and an association of employers, or an organization of employees, or where an approved joint committee exists, a written agreement between an apprentice and such committee.

Source. 1947, 166:1 par. 8, eff. May 29, 1947.
