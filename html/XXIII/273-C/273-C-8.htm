TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 273-C
DOG AND HORSE RACING EMPLOYEES

Section 273-C:8

    273-C:8 Injunctions. – The board shall petition the superior court for the county in which the party sought to be enjoined is principally located for such order of the court as may be necessary to compel obedience to an order of the board and the superior court shall issue its order upon satisfying itself that:
    I. The order of the board was within its jurisdiction to issue; and
    II. There is substantial evidence on the record considered as a whole to support the finding of the board.

Source. 1990, 214:1, eff. July 31, 1990.
