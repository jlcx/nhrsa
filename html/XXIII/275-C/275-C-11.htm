TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 275-C
GOVERNOR'S COMMISSION ON DISABILITY

Committee on Architectural Barrier-Free Design

Section 275-C:11

    275-C:11 Permanent Committee for Barrier-Free Design Established. – There is hereby established a permanent committee of the commission, to be known as the committee on architectural barrier-free design. Such committee shall be appointed by the chairman, with the approval of the commission and shall consist of at least 11 members, a majority of whom shall be persons with a disability who have demonstrated an understanding of and commitment to architectural barrier-free design. One member shall be a representative of the interests of the building trades, one member shall be a registered engineer or architect, and the remaining members shall be ex officio members of the governor's commission, or their designees.

Source. 1977, 269:1; 545:5. 1985, 207:8. 1990, 140:2, I. 1992, 50:1, eff. June 12, 1992.
