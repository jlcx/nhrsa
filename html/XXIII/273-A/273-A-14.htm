TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 273-A
PUBLIC EMPLOYEE LABOR RELATIONS

Section 273-A:14

    273-A:14 Appeals. – Any person aggrieved by a final order of the board granting or denying in whole or in part the relief sought may obtain review of such order in the manner prescribed in RSA 541.

Source. 1975, 490:2, eff. Aug. 23, 1975.
