TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 273-A
PUBLIC EMPLOYEE LABOR RELATIONS

Section 273-A:13

    273-A:13 Strikes Prohibited. – Strikes and other forms of job action by public employees are hereby declared to be unlawful. A public employer shall be entitled to petition the superior court for a temporary restraining order, pending a final order of the board under RSA 273-A:6 for a strike or other form of job action in violation of the provisions of this chapter, and may be awarded costs and reasonable legal fees at the discretion of the court.

Source. 1975, 490:2, eff. Aug. 23, 1975.
