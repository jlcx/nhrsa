TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 273-A
PUBLIC EMPLOYEE LABOR RELATIONS

Section 273-A:15

    273-A:15 Actions by or Against Public Employee Organizations. – Actions by or against the exclusive representative of a bargaining unit may be brought, without respect to the amount of damages, in the superior court of the county in which it is principally located, or where the plaintiff resides or has its principal place of business, if the plaintiff is a resident of this state or is incorporated in this state.

Source. 1975, 490:2, eff. Aug. 23, 1975.
