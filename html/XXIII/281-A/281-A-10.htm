TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 281-A
WORKERS' COMPENSATION

Section 281-A:10

    281-A:10 Notice of Revocation. – Revocation of coverage under RSA 281-A:3 by termination of workers' compensation insurance shall be effective 30 days after the filing of a termination notice with the commissioner.

Source. 1988, 194:2, eff. July 1, 1989.
