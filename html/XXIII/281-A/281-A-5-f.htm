TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 281-A
WORKERS' COMPENSATION

Section 281-A:5-f

    281-A:5-f Application of Chapter to Nonresident Employees and Employers. – Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the provisions of this chapter shall apply to nonresident employees and employers doing business in New Hampshire.

Source. 2006, 122:1, eff. July 14, 2006.
