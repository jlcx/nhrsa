TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 281-A
WORKERS' COMPENSATION

Section 281-A:27

    281-A:27 Payment for Death After Payment for Disability. – The total amount of benefit in case of a death shall not exceed the balance remaining between the amounts paid for disability and the total compensation payable under this chapter.

Source. 1988, 194:2, eff. July 1, 1989.
