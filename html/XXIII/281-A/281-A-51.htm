TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 281-A
WORKERS' COMPENSATION

Section 281-A:51

    281-A:51 Preferences of Claims for Compensation. – All rights of compensation granted by the provisions of this chapter shall have the same preference or priority against the assets of the employer as is allowed by law for a claim by an employee against an employer for unpaid wages.

Source. 1988, 194:2, eff. July 1, 1989.
