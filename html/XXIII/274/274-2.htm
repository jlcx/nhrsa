TITLE XXIII
LABOR

CHAPTER 274
EMPLOYMENT OFFICES

Section 274:2

    274:2 Power to License. – The mayor and aldermen of a city or the selectmen of a town may, for the purposes mentioned in the preceding section, grant licenses to suitable persons, subject to the provisions hereof, and may revoke the same at pleasure.

Source. 1901, 60:2. PL 179:2. RL 217:2.
