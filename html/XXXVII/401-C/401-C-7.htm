TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 401-C
OWN RISK AND SOLVENCY ASSESSMENT

Section 401-C:7

    401-C:7 Contents of ORSA Summary Report. –
    I. The ORSA summary report shall be prepared consistent with the ORSA guidance manual subject to the requirements of paragraph II. Documentation and supporting information shall be maintained and made available upon examination or upon request of the commissioner.
    II. The review of the ORSA summary report, and any additional requests for information, shall be made using similar procedures currently used in the analysis and examination of multi-state or global insurers and insurance groups.

Source. 2013, 152:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
