TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408-A
CREDIT LIFE AND ACCIDENT AND HEALTH INSURANCE

Section 408-A:9

    408-A:9 Issuance of Policies. – All policies of credit life insurance and credit accident and health insurance shall be delivered or issued for delivery in this state only by an insurer authorized to do an insurance business therein, and shall be issued only through holders of licenses or authorizations issued by the commissioner.

Source. 1959, 66:1, eff. June 22, 1959.
