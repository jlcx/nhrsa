TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 400-A
INSURANCE DEPARTMENT

Section 400-A:29-a

    400-A:29-a Fees for Continuing Education Course Submissions; Special Fund Established. –
    I. The commissioner may impose and collect reasonable application fees in amounts to be determined by the commissioner from sponsoring organizations for submissions of continuing education courses to meet educational requirements established by rule for producers, consultants, and adjusters.
    II. The fees collected by the commissioner under paragraph I shall be forwarded to the state treasurer for deposit in the continuing education advisory council reimbursement fund. Moneys in this fund shall lapse to the general fund at the close of each fiscal year. Moneys from this fund shall be used by the commissioner to pay the expenses of compensating the continuing education advisory councils, within the limits of appropriations made in the operating budget for this purpose.
    III. Members of the respective continuing education advisory councils shall be reimbursed for all actual travel.

Source. 1988, 105:1, eff. April 18, 1988. 2010, 13:2, eff. July 1, 2010.
