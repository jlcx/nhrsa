TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 402-C
INSURERS REHABILITATION AND LIQUIDATION

Interstate Relations

Section 402-C:59

    402-C:59 Attachment, Garnishment and Levy of Execution. – During the pendency in this or any other state of a liquidation proceeding, whether called by that name or not, no action or proceeding in the nature of an attachment, garnishment or levy of execution shall be commenced or maintained in this state or elsewhere against the delinquent insurer or its assets.

Source. 1969, 272:1, eff. June 23, 1969.
