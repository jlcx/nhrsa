TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 402-C
INSURERS REHABILITATION AND LIQUIDATION

Interstate Relations

Section 402-C:56

    402-C:56 Ancillary Summary Proceedings. – The commissioner in his sole discretion may institute proceedings under RSA 402-C:11-13 at the request of the commissioner or other appropriate official of the domiciliary state of any foreign or alien insurer having property located in this state.

Source. 1969, 272:1, eff. June 23, 1969.
