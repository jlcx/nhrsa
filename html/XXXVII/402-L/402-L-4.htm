TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 402-L
SALE OF LIMITED LINES TRAVEL INSURANCE

Section 402-L:4

    402-L:4 Responsibility. – As the insurer designee, the limited lines travel insurance producer shall be responsible for the acts of the travel retailer and shall use reasonable means to ensure compliance by the travel retailer with the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 2015, 62:1, eff. Aug. 31, 2015.
