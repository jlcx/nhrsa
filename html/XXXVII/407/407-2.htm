TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 407
THE FIRE INSURANCE CONTRACT AND SUITS THEREON

Section 407:2

    407:2 Use Required. – No policy or contract of fire insurance, except policies used to effect reinsurance between insurers, shall be made, issued or delivered by any insurer or by any agent or representative thereof, on any property in this state unless it shall conform to all the provisions of the policy form prescribed herein. The insurance commissioner may, after hearing, revoke or suspend the license of any insurer using any other form of such policy.

Source. 1959, 163:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1960.
