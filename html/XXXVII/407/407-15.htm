TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 407
THE FIRE INSURANCE CONTRACT AND SUITS THEREON

Section 407:15

    407:15 Notice to Insured. – Unless the company shall notify the insured that any action will be forever barred by law if his writ is not served on the company within 12 months next after such notification, he may bring his action at any time.

Source. 1959, 163:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1960.
