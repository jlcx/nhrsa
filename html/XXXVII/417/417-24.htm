TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 417
UNFAIR INSURANCE TRADE PRACTICES

Insurance Fraud Investigation Unit

Section 417:24

    417:24 Staff. – The commissioner shall appoint full-time, classified persons who shall be qualified by training and experience to perform the duties of their positions.

Source. 1993, 357:6. 2003, 144:9, eff. Jan. 1, 2004.
