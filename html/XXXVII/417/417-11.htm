TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 417
UNFAIR INSURANCE TRADE PRACTICES

General Provisions

Section 417:11

    417:11 Appeal. – Any party to the proceedings aggrieved by any order or decision of the insurance commissioner pursuant to RSA 417:10 shall be entitled to an appeal to the supreme court in accordance with the provisions of RSA 541, except that in the case of a fine imposed by any order or decision, the party aggrieved shall be entitled to an appeal to the superior court in the county in which the party resides, if a resident, or, if a nonresident, in the Merrimack county superior court and a trial de novo in said court.

Source. 1947, 189:1, par. 11. RSA 417:11. 1971, 519:6; 561:12. 1996, 238:13, eff. Jan. 1, 1997.
