TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 417
UNFAIR INSURANCE TRADE PRACTICES

General Provisions

Section 417:5-a

    417:5-a Cumulative Authority. – The enumeration in this chapter of powers vested in the commissioner and of specific unfair methods of competition and unfair or deceptive acts and practices in the business of insurance is not exclusive or restrictive or intended to limit the powers of the commissioner or any court of review but the provisions of such chapter are in all respects cumulative of and supplemental to the insurance code and all other applicable New Hampshire statutes and common law.

Source. 1971, 519:2, eff. Sept. 5, 1971.
