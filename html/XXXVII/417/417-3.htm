TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 417
UNFAIR INSURANCE TRADE PRACTICES

General Provisions

Section 417:3

    417:3 Unfair Methods Prohibited. – No person shall engage in this state in any trade practice which is defined in this chapter or determined pursuant to this chapter as an unfair method of competition or an unfair or deceptive act or practice in the business of insurance.

Source. 1947, 189:1, par. 3, eff. June 12, 1947.
