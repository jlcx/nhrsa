TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 420-O
SELF-FUNDED STUDENT HEALTH BENEFIT PLANS

Section 420-O:8


[RSA 420-O:8 effective January 1, 2017.]
    420-O:8 Investigations; Subpoenas. –
    The commissioner may make such investigations as he or she deems necessary both within and outside the state in accordance with the provisions of RSA 400-A:16 and RSA 400-A:20-22.

Source. 2016, 257:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2017.
