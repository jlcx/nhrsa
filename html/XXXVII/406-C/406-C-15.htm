TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 406-C
SALES OF INSURANCE BY FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS

Section 406-C:15

    406-C:15 Examination Authority. – The insurance activities of financial institutions pursuant to RSA 384:16-b, III and this chapter shall be subject to examination by the commissioner and the commissioner is authorized to impose reasonable and customary assessments on the financial institution for such examination.

Source. 1997, 223:3, eff. Aug. 17, 1997.
