TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 406-C
SALES OF INSURANCE BY FINANCIAL INSTITUTIONS

Section 406-C:13


[RSA 406-C:13 effective until October 1, 2015; see also RSA 406-C:13 set out below.]
    406-C:13 Application of State and Federal Consumer Protection Provisions. –
    Any financial institution which solicits or sells insurance products pursuant to RSA 384:16-b, III and this chapter shall be subject to all applicable federal and state consumer protection laws, including the federal anti-tying provisions of 12 U.S.C. section 1972.

[RSA 406-C:13 effective October 1, 2015; see also RSA 406-C:13 set out above.]


    406-C:13 Application of State and Federal Consumer Protection Provisions. --
    Any financial institution which solicits or sells insurance products pursuant to RSA 383-B:3-301(d) and this chapter shall be subject to all applicable federal and state consumer protection laws, including the federal anti-tying provisions of 12 U.S.C. section 1972.

Source. 1997, 223:3, eff. Aug. 17, 1997. 2015, 272:33, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
