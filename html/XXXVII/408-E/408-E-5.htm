TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408-E
FUNDING AGREEMENTS

Section 408-E:5

    408-E:5 Amounts Guaranteed or Credited Under Funding Agreements. – An amount shall not be guaranteed or credited under a funding agreement except upon:
    I. Reasonable assumptions as to investment income and expenses; and
    II. A basis equitable to all holders of funding agreements of a given class.

Source. 2013, 108:1, eff. Aug. 23, 2013.
