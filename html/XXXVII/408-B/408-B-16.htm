TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408-B
LIFE AND HEALTH INSURANCE GUARANTY ASSOCIATION OF 1996

Section 408-B:16

    408-B:16 Tax Exemptions. – The association shall be exempt from payment of all fees and all taxes levied by this state or any of its subdivisions, except taxes levied on real property.

Source. 1995, 305:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
