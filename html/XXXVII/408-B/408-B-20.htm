TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408-B
LIFE AND HEALTH INSURANCE GUARANTY ASSOCIATION OF 1996

Section 408-B:20

    408-B:20 Prospective Application. – This chapter shall not apply to any insurer which is insolvent or unable to fulfill its contractual obligations on January 1, 1996.

Source. 1995, 305:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
