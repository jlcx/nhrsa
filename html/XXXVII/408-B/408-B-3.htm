TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408-B
LIFE AND HEALTH INSURANCE GUARANTY ASSOCIATION OF 1996

Section 408-B:3

    408-B:3 Construction. – This chapter shall be liberally construed to effect the purpose under RSA 408-B:2 which shall constitute an aid and guide to interpretation.

Source. 1995, 305:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
