TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Reinsurance

Section 405:51

    405:51 Reinsurance Contract. – No person shall have any rights against the reinsurer which are not specifically set forth in the contract of reinsurance or in a specific agreement between the reinsurer and the person.

Source. 2004, 186:1, eff. July 31, 2004.
