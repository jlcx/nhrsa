TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Insurance Consultants

Section 405:44-g

    405:44-g Penalty for Acting Without a License. – Any person, corporation, partnership, or association who acts as an insurance consultant without such license or during a suspension of his or its license shall be subject to an administrative fine not to exceed $2,500 for each such act.

Source. 1986, 58:1, eff. July 1, 1986.
