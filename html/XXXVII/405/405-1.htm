TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Licenses for Companies

Section 405:1

    405:1 Requirement. – No insurance company not organized under the laws of this state shall do insurance business within the state unless it has obtained a license from the insurance commissioner authorizing it to do so.

Source. GL 174:22. 1889, 86:1. PS 169:1. PL 275:1. RL 325:1.
