TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Licenses for Agents of Foreign Companies

Section 405:15

    405:15 Definition; Agent. – An insurance producer license to act as an agent shall be issued to any eligible person or business entity pursuant to the provisions of RSA 402-J.

Source. 1915, 32:1. 1919, 18:1. PL 275:17. RL 325:18. 2000, 315:10, eff. Jan. 1, 2001.
