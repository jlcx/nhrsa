TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Brokers' Licenses

Section 405:34

    405:34 Brokers; Producer License. – An insurance producer license to act as a broker shall be issued to any eligible person or business entity pursuant to the provisions of RSA 402-J.

Source. 1915, 63:4. 1917, 23:1. PL 275:29. RL 325:35. 2000, 315:13, eff. Jan. 1, 2001.
