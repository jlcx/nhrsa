TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Licenses for Agents of Unlicensed Companies

Section 405:26

    405:26 Companies Excluded. – Such licensee shall not offer any portion of such insurance to any company which has not been approved by the insurance commissioner and does not possess and maintain a surplus to policyholders of at least $500,000 or to any company which has within the preceding 24 months been in an impaired condition.

Source. 1911, 168:2. PL 275:22. RL 325:27. RSA 405:26. 1969, 283:8, eff. June 25, 1969.
