TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Licenses for Agents of Unlicensed Companies

Section 405:29-a

    405:29-a Annual Statement; Tax; Insurance Transactions on Multi-State Risks. – For each licensed producer of unlicensed surplus lines companies reporting multi-state transactions, the provisions of RSA 405-B shall apply.

Source. 2011, 191:1, eff. Aug. 13, 2011.
