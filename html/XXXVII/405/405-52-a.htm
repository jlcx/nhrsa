TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Reinsurance

Section 405:52-a

    405:52-a Rulemaking. – The commissioner may adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, relative to forms of trusts and acceptable security, required information in filings, enforcement and penalties provided in this subdivision.

Source. 2004, 186:1, eff. July 31, 2004.
