TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Brokers' Licenses

Section 405:43

    405:43 Effect of Broker's Knowledge. – Companies issuing policies through their agents on applications from brokers shall be charged with the broker's knowledge of facts to the same extent as if he were their agent.

Source. 1915, 63:3. PL 275:38. RL 325:43.
