TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Licenses for Agents of Unlicensed Companies

Section 405:30

    405:30 Investigation. – The commissioner shall have authority at all times to investigate any alleged violations of this subdivision, and should he find any to exist he shall report the same to the attorney-general, who shall take proceedings to collect all fees and taxes which may be due from said licensee.

Source. 1911, 168:5. PL 275:26. RL 325:31.
