TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Reinsurance

Section 405:52

    405:52 Reinsurance Agreements Affected. – Credit for reinsurance ceded to a certified reinsurer is allowed only for reinsurance contracts entered into or renewed on or after the effective date of the certification of the assuming insurer by the insurance commissioner.

Source. 2004, 186:1, eff. July 31, 2004. 2013, 235:6, eff. Sept. 13, 2013.
