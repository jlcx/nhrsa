TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Reinsurance

Section 405:52

    405:52 Penalty. – Any domestic ceding insurer failing to comply with any of the provisions of this subdivision shall be fined not more than $2,500 for each ceded policy, or the commissioner may revoke the license of such company for one year, or both.

Source. 2004, 186:1, eff. July 31, 2004.
