TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Redomestication

Section 405:65


[RSA 405:65 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA 405:65 set out below.]
    405:65 Filing With Secretary of State. –
    An insurer transferring its domicile to this state shall file the following documents with the secretary of state, together with applicable filing fees:
    I. Restated articles of incorporation or equivalent, as amended pursuant to RSA 405:62;
    II. Executed order of the commissioner approving the redomestication; and
    III. The statement set forth in RSA 421-B:11, II.

[RSA 405:65 effective January 1, 2016; see also RSA 405:65 set out above.]


    405:65 Filing with Secretary of State. --

    I. Restated articles of incorporation or equivalent, as amended pursuant to RSA 405:62; and
