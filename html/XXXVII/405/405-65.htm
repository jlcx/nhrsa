TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Redomestication

Section 405:65

    405:65 Filing with Secretary of State. – An insurer transferring its domicile to this state shall file the following documents with the secretary of state, together with applicable filing fees:
    I. Restated articles of incorporation or equivalent, as amended pursuant to RSA 405:62; and
    II. Executed order of the commissioner approving the redomestication.

Source. 2008, 255:5, eff. Aug. 25, 2008. 2015, 273:16, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
