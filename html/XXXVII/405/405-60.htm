TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Denial of Privileges, etc., by Other States

Section 405:60

    405:60 Revocation of Licenses. – If any such company shall refuse or neglect to pay any tax, fine, penalty, license, or fee imposed by the laws of this state, or fail to comply with any other requirement thereof, the commissioner may revoke its license.

Source. 1895, 100:1. 1921, 27:1. PL 275:54. RL 325:59.
