TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 405
FOREIGN INSURANCE COMPANIES AND THEIR AGENTS

Licenses for Agents of Unlicensed Companies

Section 405:31

    405:31 Penalty. – Any person who violates or fails to comply with any of the provisions of this subdivision shall be subject to a fine not more than $2,500 or have any license suspended or revoked or shall both be fined and have any license suspended or revoked.

Source. 1911, 168:5. PL 275:27. RL 325:32. RSA 405:31. 1973, 530:55. 1983, 473:14, eff. Sept. 3, 1983.
