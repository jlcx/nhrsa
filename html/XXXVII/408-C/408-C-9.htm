TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408-C
INTERSTATE INSURANCE PRODUCT COMPACT

Section 408-C:9

    408-C:9 Dispute Resolution. – The commission shall attempt, upon the request of a member, to resolve any dispute or other issues that are subject to this compact and which may arise between 2 or more compacting states, or between compacting states and non-compacting states, and the commission shall promulgate an operating procedure providing for resolution of such disputes.

Source. 2004, 196:2, eff. Aug. 6, 2004.
