TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408-C
INTERSTATE INSURANCE PRODUCT COMPACT

Section 408-C:15

    408-C:15 Severability and Construction. – The provisions of this compact shall be severable; and if any phrase, clause, sentence or provision of this compact is declared to be unconstitutional or the applicability thereof, to any state, agency, person, or circumstances held invalid, the constitutionality of the remainder of this compact and the applicability thereof, to any other state, agency, person or circumstance shall not be affected thereby. It is the legislative intent that the provisions of this compact be reasonably and liberally construed to effectuate its purpose.

Source. 2004, 196:2, eff. Aug. 6, 2004.
