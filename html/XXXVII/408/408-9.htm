TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408
LIFE INSURANCE

Section 408:9

    408:9 Policy Contract. – Every policy of insurance issued or delivered within this state by any life insurance corporation doing business within the state, together with the application therefor, a copy of which application shall be endorsed upon or attached to the policy and made a part thereof, shall constitute the entire contract between the parties.

Source. 1907, 110:1. 1925, 122:1. PL 277:8. RL 327:9. RSA 408:9. 1969, 322:1, eff. Aug. 29, 1969.
