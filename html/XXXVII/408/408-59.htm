TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408
LIFE INSURANCE

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 408:59

    408:59 Possession of Certificate. – Any insurance corporation authorized to issue variable contracts and any person named in a registration certificate as above provided may, in behalf of any such corporation, sell, offer for sale or invite offers for or inquiries about variable contracts in this state, but shall at all times when so engaged carry with him the registration certificate, or a copy thereof certified by the commissioner, which shall at any time be shown to any prospective customer upon request.

Source. 1967, 272:2. 1977, 264:16, eff. Aug. 21, 1977.
