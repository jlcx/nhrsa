TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408
LIFE INSURANCE

Registration of Agents and Salesmen

Section 408:43

    408:43 Appointment. – Pursuant to the provisions of RSA 402-J, a registered insurance corporation may make a lifetime appointment of a producer to issue variable contracts.

Source. 1967, 272:2. 1969, 336:11. 1971, 244:5. 2001, 95:6, eff. Aug. 20, 2001.
