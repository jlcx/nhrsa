TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408
LIFE INSURANCE

Examinations, and Revocation and Suspension of Registrations

Section 408:54

    408:54 Effect. – The revocation or suspension of the insurance corporation's registration shall constitute a revocation or suspension of the registration of any of its agents or salesmen.

Source. 1967, 272:2, eff. Jan. 1, 1968.
