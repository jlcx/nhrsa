TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408
LIFE INSURANCE

Licenses for Foreign Casualty Companies

Section 408:19

    408:19 Provisions Applicable. – All companies, after they are licensed under the provisions of this subdivision, shall in all respects be subject to the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 1895, 81:1. 1897, 38:1. 1913, 80:2. PL 277:37. RL 327:17.
