TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408
LIFE INSURANCE

Separate Accounts

Section 408:26

    408:26 Company Not a Trustee. – Amounts allocated by an insurance company to separate accounts in the exercise of the power granted by this chapter shall be owned by the company, and the company shall not be, or hold itself out to be, a trustee in respect to such amounts.

Source. 1967, 272:2, eff. Jan. 1, 1968.
