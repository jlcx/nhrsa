TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408
LIFE INSURANCE

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 408:60

    408:60 Advertising Registration. – No insurance corporation authorized to issue variable contracts, agent or salesman shall advertise publicly the fact of his registration, or use such fact or the registration certificate in connection with any sale or effort to sell variable contracts, except by statement of the fact or by exhibiting the certificate or a certified copy thereof.

Source. 1967, 272:2. 1977, 264:17, eff. Aug. 21, 1977.
