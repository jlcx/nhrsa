TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408
LIFE INSURANCE

Registration of Agents and Salesmen

Section 408:42

    408:42 Registration Required. – No salesman or agent shall in this state, in behalf of any insurance corporation authorized to issue variable contracts, sell, offer for sale, or invite offers for or inquiries about variable contracts unless registered as a salesman or agent of such insurance corporation under the provisions of this chapter. As a condition to such registration the commissioner can require that a salesman or agent pass a written examination.

Source. 1967, 272:2. 1977, 264:6, eff. Aug. 21, 1977.
