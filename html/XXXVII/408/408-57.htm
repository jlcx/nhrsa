TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408
LIFE INSURANCE

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 408:57

    408:57 Information to be Furnished. – The commissioner shall at any time on request, by mail or otherwise, inform any inquirer as to any individual, partnership, corporation, or association which is registered either as insurance corporation authorized to issue variable contracts, agent or salesman.

Source. 1967, 272:2. 1977, 264:14, eff. Aug. 21, 1977.
