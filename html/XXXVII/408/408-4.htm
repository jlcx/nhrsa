TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 408
LIFE INSURANCE

Section 408:4

    408:4 Exception. – The provisions of RSA 408:1-3 do not apply to claims of creditors of any person effecting any life or endowment insurance which arise out of, or are based upon, any obligation created before May 9, 1931.

Source. 1931, 175:2. RL 327:4.
