TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 406-A
UNAUTHORIZED INSURERS FALSE ADVERTISING PROCESS ACT

Section 406-A:6

    406-A:6 Constitutionality. – If any provision of this chapter or the application thereof to any person or circumstances is held invalid, such invalidity shall not affect other provisions or application of the chapter which can be given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to this end the provisions of this chapter are declared to be severable.

Source. 1961, 48:1, eff. May 29, 1961.
