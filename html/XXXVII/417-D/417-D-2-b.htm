TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 417-D
WOMEN'S HEALTH CARE

Section 417-D:2-b

    417-D:2-b Reconstructive Surgery. – Every insurer subject to this chapter that provides individual or group coverage for mastectomy surgery shall provide coverage for reconstruction of the breast on which surgery has been performed and surgery and reconstruction of the other breast to produce a symmetrical appearance if the patient elects reconstruction and in the manner chosen by the patient and the physician.

Source. 1997, 311:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
