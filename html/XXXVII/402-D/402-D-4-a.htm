TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 402-D
PUBLIC ADJUSTERS

Section 402-D:4-a

    402-D:4-a Continuing Education. – Every 2 years, at least 60 days prior to the renewal date of their license, persons holding a public adjusters license shall be certified by the insurance department as having completed 15 hours of continuing education instruction. Such continuing education instruction shall be approved by the insurance department.

Source. 1993, 317:2, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
