TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 420-B
HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS

Section 420-B:16

    420-B:16 Fees. – Every organization subject to this chapter shall pay to the commissioner those fees required for payment by hospital and medical service corporations under RSA 400-A:29.

Source. 1977, 282:1, eff. Aug. 21, 1977.
