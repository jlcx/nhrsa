TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 420-B
HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS

Section 420-B:8-h

    420-B:8-h Notification of Allowable Benefit. – All health maintenance organizations shall provide to each subscriber, on request, a written statement of the dollar amount of allowable benefit for any procedure which is requested by the appropriate procedure code.

Source. 1993, 102:6, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
