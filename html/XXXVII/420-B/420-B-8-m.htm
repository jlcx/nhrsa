TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 420-B
HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS

Section 420-B:8-m

    420-B:8-m Patients' Bill of Rights. – All health maintenance organizations shall provide to each new subscriber who is a resident of this state a copy of the patients' bill of rights law under RSA 151:21.

Source. 1997, 331:17, eff. Aug. 22, 1997.
