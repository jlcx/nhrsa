TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 420-B
HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS

Section 420-B:3

    420-B:3 Application for Certificate of Authority. – Each application for a certificate of authority shall be verified by an officer or authorized representative of the applicant and shall be in a form prescribed by the commissioner, containing such information as the commissioner may require.

Source. 1977, 282:1, eff. Aug. 21, 1977.
