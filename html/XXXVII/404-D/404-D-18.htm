TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 404-D
NEW HAMPSHIRE LIFE AND HEALTH INSURANCE GUARANTY ASSOCIATION

Section 404-D:18

    404-D:18 Stay of Proceedings; Reopening Default Judgments. – All proceedings in which the impaired insurer is a party in any court in this state shall be stayed 60 days from the date an order of liquidation, rehabilitation, or conservation is final to permit proper legal action by the association on any matters germane to its powers or duties. As to a judgment under any decision, order, verdict, or finding based on default the association may apply to have such judgment set aside by the same court that made such judgment and shall be permitted to defend against such suit on the merits.

Source. 1971, 509:1, eff. Sept. 4, 1971.
