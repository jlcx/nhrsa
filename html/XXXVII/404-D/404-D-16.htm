TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 404-D
NEW HAMPSHIRE LIFE AND HEALTH INSURANCE GUARANTY ASSOCIATION

Section 404-D:16

    404-D:16 Tax Exemptions. – The association shall be exempt from payment of all fees and all taxes levied by this state or any of its subdivisions, except taxes levied on real property.

Source. 1971, 509:1, eff. Sept. 4, 1971.
