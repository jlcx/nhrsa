TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 409
STANDARD NONFORFEITURE LAW FOR LIFE INSURANCE

Section 409:1-a

    409:1-a Definition. – In this chapter, "operative date of the valuation manual'' means the January 1 of the first calendar year that the valuation manual as defined in RSA 410:2, XII is effective.

Source. 2013, 221:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2014.
