TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 401-B
INSURANCE HOLDING COMPANIES

Section 401-B:8

    401-B:8 Rules and Orders. – Upon notice and opportunity for all interested persons to be heard, the commissioner may issue such rules and orders as shall be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 1971, 176:1, eff. Aug. 7, 1971.
