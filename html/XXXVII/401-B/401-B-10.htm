TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 401-B
INSURANCE HOLDING COMPANIES

Section 401-B:10

    401-B:10 Sanctions. – Any insurer who knowingly fails to file any registration statement as required in this chapter shall be subject to an administrative fine not to exceed $2,500.

Source. 1971, 176:1. 1973, 529:98. 1991, 99:9, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
