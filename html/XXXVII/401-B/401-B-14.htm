TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 401-B
INSURANCE HOLDING COMPANIES

Section 401-B:14

    401-B:14 Appeal. – Any person aggrieved by any act, determination, rule, regulation, or order of any other action of the commissioner pursuant to this chapter may appeal in accordance with RSA 541.

Source. 1971, 176:1. 2000, 166:3, eff. July 22, 2000.
