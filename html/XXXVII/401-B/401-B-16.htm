TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 401-B
INSURANCE HOLDING COMPANIES

Section 401-B:16

    401-B:16 Common Management. – Nothing herein shall preclude an authorized insurer from having or sharing a common management or cooperative or joint use of personnel, property or services with one or more other persons under arrangements meeting the standards of RSA 401-B:5, I and II.

Source. 1971, 176:1, eff. Aug. 7, 1971.
