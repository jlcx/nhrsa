TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 420-C
PREFERRED PROVIDER AGREEMENTS

Section 420-C:8

    420-C:8 Other Applicable Statutes. – Preferred provider agreements and health benefit plans subject to this chapter shall also be governed by the provisions of RSA 420-G and RSA 420-J.

Source. 1997, 344:9, eff. July 1, 1997; 344:17, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
