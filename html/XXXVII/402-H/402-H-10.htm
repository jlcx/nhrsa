TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 402-H
THIRD PARTY ADMINISTRATORS

Section 402-H:10

    402-H:10 Delivery of Materials to Covered Individuals. – Any policies, certificates, booklets, termination notices or other written communications delivered by the insurer to the administrator for delivery to insured parties or covered individuals shall be delivered by the administrator promptly after receipt of instructions from the insurer to deliver them.

Source. 1993, 253:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
