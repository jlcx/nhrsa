TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 402-H
THIRD PARTY ADMINISTRATORS

Section 402-H:5

    402-H:5 Approval of Advertising. – An administrator may use only advertising pertaining to the business underwritten by an insurer that has been approved in writing by the insurer in advance of its use.

Source. 1993, 253:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1994. 2006, 271:4, eff. Aug. 8, 2006.
