TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 402-H
THIRD PARTY ADMINISTRATORS

Section 402-H:16

    402-H:16 Penalties. – An administrator who violates or fails to comply with the provisions of this chapter or rules adopted pursuant to this chapter shall be subject to suspension or revocation of its certificate of authority or an administrative penalty not to exceed $2,500 for each violation.

Source. 1993, 253:1. 1997, 284:14, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
