TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 402-M
ADMINISTRATIVE SUPERVISION

Section 402-M:10


[RSA 402-M:10 effective January 1, 2017.]
    402-M:10 Other Laws; Conflicts; Meetings Between the Commissioner and the Supervisor. –
    The commissioner may meet with a supervisor appointed under this chapter and with the attorney or other representative of the supervisor, without the presence of any other person, at the time of any proceeding or during the pendency of any proceeding held under authority of this chapter to carry out the commissioner's duties under this chapter or for the supervisor to carry out his or her duties under this chapter.

Source. 2016, 17:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2017.
