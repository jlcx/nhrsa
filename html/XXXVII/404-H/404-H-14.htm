TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 404-H
NEW HAMPSHIRE INSURANCE GUARANTY ASSOCIATION ACT OF 2004

Section 404-H:14

    404-H:14 Examination of the Association. – The association shall be subject to examination and regulation by the commissioner. The board of directors shall submit, not later than March 30 of each year, a financial report for the preceding calendar year in a form approved by the commissioner.

Source. 2004, 197:1, eff. Aug. 6, 2004.
