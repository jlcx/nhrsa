TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 404-H
NEW HAMPSHIRE INSURANCE GUARANTY ASSOCIATION ACT OF 2004

Section 404-H:4

    404-H:4 Construction. – This chapter shall be liberally construed to effect the purpose under RSA 404-H:2 which shall constitute an aid and guide to interpretation.

Source. 2004, 197:1, eff. Aug. 6, 2004.
