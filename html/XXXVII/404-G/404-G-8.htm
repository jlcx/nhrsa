TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 404-G
INDIVIDUAL HEALTH INSURANCE MARKET

Section 404-G:8

    404-G:8 Tax Exemption. – The association shall be exempt from payment of all fees and all taxes levied by this state or any of its subdivisions, except taxes levied on real property.

Source. 1998, 340:6, eff. Aug. 25, 1998.
