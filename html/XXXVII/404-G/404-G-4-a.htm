TITLE XXXVII
INSURANCE

CHAPTER 404-G
INDIVIDUAL HEALTH INSURANCE MARKET

Section 404-G:4-a

    404-G:4-a Report Required. – The association shall submit an annual report to the commissioner, in a manner and form determined by the commissioner, listing the association membership base, providing a count of covered lives by member, identifying changes in association membership and covered lives, describing the collection of assessments, listing payment delinquencies, and containing such other related information as the commissioner may require.

Source. 2008, 375:5, eff. July 1, 2008.
