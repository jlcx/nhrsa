TITLE LXII
CRIMINAL CODE

CHAPTER 651-A
PAROLE OF PRISONERS

Section 651-A:16-a

    651-A:16-a Intermediate Sanction. –
    I. The commissioner shall establish a 7-day residential sanction located in a halfway house facility.
    II. Probation/parole officers may place a parolee in an intermediate sanction in lieu of a parole revocation hearing only if the offender agrees to participate.

Source. 2010, 247:8, eff. July 1, 2010. 2013, 156:4, eff. July 1, 2013.
