TITLE LXII
CRIMINAL CODE

CHAPTER 651-A
PAROLE OF PRISONERS

Section 651-A:11-a

    651-A:11-a Victims Permitted to Speak at Parole Hearings. – The victim of any person seeking parole, or the victim's next of kin if the victim has died, shall have the right to appear at the parole hearing of such person, personally or by counsel, and to reasonably express his views concerning the offense and the person responsible.

Source. 1983, 319:3, eff. Aug. 17, 1983.
