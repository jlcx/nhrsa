TITLE LXII
CRIMINAL CODE

CHAPTER 651-A
PAROLE OF PRISONERS

Section 651-A:5

    651-A:5 Executive Assistant. – The board may appoint an executive assistant who shall be an unclassified employee and shall serve at its pleasure. The salary of the executive assistant shall be that established in RSA 94:1-a.

Source. 1983, 461:16, eff. July 1, 1983.
