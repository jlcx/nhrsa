TITLE LXII
CRIMINAL CODE

CHAPTER 651-A
PAROLE OF PRISONERS

Section 651-A:14

    651-A:14 Early Discharge for Good Conduct. – Whenever the board finds that the parolee is no longer in need of supervision because of his good conduct it may issue him a certificate of discharge.

Source. 1983, 461:16, eff. July 1, 1983.
