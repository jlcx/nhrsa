TITLE LXII
CRIMINAL CODE

CHAPTER 649
SABOTAGE PREVENTION

Section 649:11

    649:11 Relation to Other Statutes. – All acts and parts of acts inconsistent with this chapter are hereby suspended in their application to any proceedings hereunder. If conduct prohibited by this chapter is also made unlawful by another or other laws, the offender may be convicted for the violation of this chapter or of such other law or laws.

Source. 1971, 518:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
