TITLE LXII
CRIMINAL CODE

CHAPTER 635
UNAUTHORIZED ENTRIES

Section 635:3

    635:3 Trespassing Stock. – If any person having the charge or custody of any sheep, goats, cattle, horses, or swine shall knowingly, recklessly, or negligently suffer or permit the same to enter upon, pass over, or remain upon any improved or enclosed land of another without written permission of the owner, occupant, or his agent, and thereby injures his crops, or property, he shall be guilty of a violation.

Source. 1971, 518:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
