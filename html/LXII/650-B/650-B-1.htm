TITLE LXII
CRIMINAL CODE

CHAPTER 650-B
FELONIOUS USE OF BODY ARMOR

Section 650-B:1

    650-B:1 Definition. – In this chapter, "body armor'' means any device designed to be worn on the body which is bullet resistant and is designed and intended to provide ballistic and trauma protection.

Source. 1983, 193:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1983.
