TITLE LXII
CRIMINAL CODE

CHAPTER 632-A
SEXUAL ASSAULT AND RELATED OFFENSES

Section 632-A:5

    632-A:5 Spouse as Victim; Evidence of Husband and Wife. – An actor commits a crime under this chapter even though the victim is the actor's legal spouse. Laws attaching a privilege against the disclosure of communications between husband and wife are inapplicable to proceedings under this chapter.

Source. 1975, 302:1. 1981, 415:1, eff. Aug. 22, 1981.
