TITLE LXII
CRIMINAL CODE

CHAPTER 649-A
CHILD PORNOGRAPHY

Section 649-A:6

    649-A:6 Proving Age of Child. – Whether a child depicted in a visual representation is a minor for the purposes of this chapter is a question of fact for the jury and may be found by expert or lay testimony, or by viewing the images.

Source. 2008, 323:3, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
