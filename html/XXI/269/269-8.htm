TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 269
ELECTRIC PERSONAL ASSISTIVE MOBILITY DEVICES

Section 269:8

    269:8 Additional Regulations. – A city or town shall have the authority to regulate the operation of EPAMDs within its limits. The provisions of RSA 269:3, 269:4, and 269:5 shall not supersede the provisions of any local ordinance.

Source. 2002, 4:3, eff. Feb. 15, 2002.
