TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Overtaking and Passing, Highway Markings, Right of Way

Section 265:32

    265:32 Vehicle Entering Way From Private Road or Driveway. – The driver of a vehicle about to enter or cross a way from a private road or driveway shall yield the right of way to all vehicles approaching on said way.

Source. RSA 262-A:30. 1963, 330:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
