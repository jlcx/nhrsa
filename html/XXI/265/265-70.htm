TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Stopping, Standing or Parking

Section 265:70

    265:70 Local Ordinances Not Superseded. – The provisions of RSA 265:69 shall not supersede the provisions of any local ordinance which has been adopted to regulate parking in restricted areas in the compact part of any city or town.

Source. RSA 262-A:71-a. 1971, 127:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
