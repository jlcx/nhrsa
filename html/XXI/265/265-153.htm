TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Special Rules for Bicycles and Mopeds

Section 265:153

    265:153 Penalty. –
    I. Any person violating the provisions of this subdivision or of any ordinance, bylaw or rule made under the provisions of this subdivision shall be guilty of a violation.
    II. The maximum fine for a violation of RSA 265:144, X shall be $35 for each occurrence.
    III. The fine for a violation of RSA 265:143-a shall be $100.

Source. 1981, 422:14. 2005, 100:3. 2008, 209:5, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
