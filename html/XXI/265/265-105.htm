TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Special Rules

Section 265:105

    265:105 Use of Amateur or Citizen Band Radio. – Notwithstanding any other provision of law, it shall be lawful for any person to use an amateur or citizen band radio, radio telephone, radio telegraph equipment or any other electronic receiving device while driving a vehicle upon a way.

Source. RSA 262-A:85. 1977, 375:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
