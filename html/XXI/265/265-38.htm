TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Pedestrians' Rights and Duties

Section 265:38

    265:38 Pedestrians to Use Right Half of Crosswalks. – Pedestrians shall move, whenever practicable upon the right half of crosswalks.

Source. RSA 262-A:36. 1963, 330:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
