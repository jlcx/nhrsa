TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Special Rules

Section 265:97

    265:97 Riding in Trailers. – No person or persons shall occupy any type of house trailer or automobile utility trailer as defined by RSA 259:5, while it is being moved upon a way.

Source. RSA 262-A:78. 1963, 330:1. 1973, 180:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
