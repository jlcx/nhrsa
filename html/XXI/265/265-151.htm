TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Special Rules for Bicycles and Mopeds

Section 265:151

    265:151 Limitation of Prosecution. – Prosecutions under this subdivision shall be instituted within 60 days from the time the offense was committed.

Source. 1897, 93:4. PL 90:17. RL 106:21. 1945, 188:1, part 20:21. RSA 250:21. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
