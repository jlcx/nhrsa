TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Overtaking and Passing, Highway Markings, Right of Way

Section 265:26-a

    265:26-a Driving Upon Sidewalk. – No person shall drive any vehicle upon a sidewalk or sidewalk area except when a permanent or authorized temporary driveway crosses the sidewalk area.

Source. 1981, 479:32, eff. Jan. 1, 1982 at 12:01 a.m.
