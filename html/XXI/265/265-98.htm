TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Special Rules

Section 265:98

    265:98 Driving on Mountain Ways. – The driver of a vehicle traveling through defiles or canyons or on mountain ways shall hold such vehicle under control and as near the right-hand edge of the way as reasonably possible and, upon approaching any curve where the view is obstructed within a distance of 200 feet along the way, shall give audible warning with the horn of such vehicle.

Source. RSA 262-A:79. 1963, 330:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
