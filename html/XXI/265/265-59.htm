TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Buses

Section 265:59

    265:59 Vehicle Formerly Used as School Bus to be Repainted. – Any person who drives a vehicle formerly used as a school bus, as defined in RSA 259:96, on the ways of the state shall cause it to be painted a color readily distinguishable from national school bus chrome yellow.

Source. RSA 262-A:53-a. 1969, 3:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
