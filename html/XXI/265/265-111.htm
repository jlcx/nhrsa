TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Use of Lights

Section 265:111

    265:111 Enforcement. – The director, his agents and examiners, sheriffs and their deputies, peace officers, constables and selectmen shall have authority to enforce the provisions of this subdivision.

Source. 1913, 105:7. 1921, 126:2. 1925, 105:2. PL 90:11. RL 106:11. 1945, 188:1, part 20:11. RSA 250:11. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
