TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 265
RULES OF THE ROAD

Overtaking and Passing, Highway Markings, Right of Way

Section 265:27

    265:27 Restricted Access. – No person shall drive a vehicle onto or from any controlled access roadway except at such entrances and exits as are established by public authority.

Source. RSA 262-A:26. 1963, 330:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
