TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 264
ACCIDENTS AND FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

Applicability of Chapter

Section 264:34

    264:34 Other Remedies Unaffected. – This chapter shall not be construed to prevent the plaintiff in any action at law from relying for security upon the other processes provided by law.

Source. 1937, 161:27. RL 122:26. RSA 268:27. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
