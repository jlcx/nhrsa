TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 261
CERTIFICATES OF TITLE AND REGISTRATION OF VEHICLES

Certificate of Title

Section 261:6

    261:6 Department to Check Vehicle Identification Number. – The department, upon receiving application for a first certificate of title, shall check the vehicle identification number shown in the application against the records of vehicles required to be maintained by RSA 261:7 and against the record of stolen and converted vehicles required to be maintained by RSA 262:2.

Source. RSA 269-A:8. 1967, 357:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
