TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 261
CERTIFICATES OF TITLE AND REGISTRATION OF VEHICLES

Number Plates

Section 261:85

    261:85 Ski Area Plates. – A motor vehicle shall qualify for ski area vehicle registration rates and number plates and shall be driven with such registration only if such motor vehicle is used exclusively for ski area purposes and is driven within a 3 mile radius of the main entrance of the ski area. The ski area vehicle shall be prohibited from hauling any material within the 3 mile radius area outside of the ski area property, except for such material that may be used for ballast on said vehicle.
