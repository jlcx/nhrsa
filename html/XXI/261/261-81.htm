TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 261
CERTIFICATES OF TITLE AND REGISTRATION OF VEHICLES

Number Plates

Section 261:81

    261:81 Motorized Locomotives. – The department is hereby authorized to issue registration number plates to the New Hampshire State Voitures of La Soci%21et%21e des 40 Hommes et 8 Chevaux for use on their motorized locomotives. For each set of plates so issued the department shall charge a fee of $5.

Source. 1941, 200:1, 2. RL 116:75. RSA 260:74. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
