TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 261
CERTIFICATES OF TITLE AND REGISTRATION OF VEHICLES

Number Plates

Section 261:91-a

    261:91-a Commemorative License Plates. – The director is authorized to adopt rules pursuant to RSA 541-A for the design, sale, and distribution by towns and cities celebrating the centennial, bicentennial, tricentennial, or sesquicentennial of the founding of the town or city or similar special occasion in the history of the town or city, and the display of said plates in place of the front license plate on vehicles for a period not to exceed one year, during which the celebration is taking place.

Source. 2004, 209:3, eff. June 11, 2004.
