TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 261
CERTIFICATES OF TITLE AND REGISTRATION OF VEHICLES

Municipal Permits for Registration

Section 261:159

    261:159 Exemption for Blind Veterans. – No fee shall be charged for a permit to register a vehicle owned by a veteran who has been determined by the Department of Veterans Affairs to be suffering from total blindness as a result of a service-connected disability.

Source. 1951, 144:3. RSA 260:29. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
