TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Powers and Duties

Section 260:6

    260:6 Hearing. – A person aggrieved by an act or omission to act of the department under this chapter is entitled, upon request, to rehearing and appeal in accordance with RSA 541.

Source. RSA 269-A:30. 1967, 357:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
