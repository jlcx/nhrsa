TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Powers and Duties

Section 260:8

    260:8 Seal. – The director shall have a seal for use in preparing certified copies of papers and records pertaining to his office, the form of which shall be approved by the governor and council.

Source. 1915, 154:2. 1919, 82:1. PL 99:3. RL 115:3. RSA 259:5. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
