TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Powers and Duties

Section 260:6-a

    260:6-a Administrative Review of Sanctions Against Inspection Stations. – In all cases in which a sanction is imposed by a hearings officer against an official inspection station for violation of the rules relative to inspection stations and the sanction involves a loss of inspection privileges for a period of greater than 20 days, any party aggrieved by the decision may, prior to seeking the remedies provided in RSA 541, request administrative review of the decision by the commissioner or his designee. The commissioner or his designee shall affirm or modify such sanction as justice may require.

Source. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
