TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Actions Against Operators

Section 260:69

    260:69 Record of Processes. – The director shall keep a record of all such processes, which shall show the day and hour of service.

Source. 1925, 106:1. PL 100:34. RL 116:44. RSA 264:3. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
