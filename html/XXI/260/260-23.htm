TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Powers and Duties

Section 260:23

    260:23 Disposition of Receipts. – All fees, fines and forfeitures received by any person under the provisions of any laws of the state relative to the use and driving of vehicles shall be paid to the department within 14 days after the receipt thereof; and all moneys received by the department shall be paid monthly to the state treasurer.

Source. 1911, 133:27. 1919, 161:7. 1921, 119:26. PL 102:28. RL 118:30. 1945, 65:1. 1949, 94:1. 1951, 163:15; 245:9. RSA 262:35. 1957, 110:1. 1967, 182:3. 1981, 146:1. 1983, 383:16, eff. Jan. 1, 1984.
