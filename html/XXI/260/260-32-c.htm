TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Road Tolls

Section 260:32-c

    260:32-c Levy of Road Toll on Alternative Fuels. – There is hereby imposed a road toll on alternative fuels, as defined in RSA 259:3-d and sold by licensed alternative fuel dealers, on the motor fuel equivalent gallon, as defined in RSA 259:58-b, for such alternative fuel at the rate per gallon established in RSA 260:32. The alternative fuel road toll shall be subject to the exemptions provided for by RSA 260:32, I-V and the refund provisions as provided in RSA 260:47, RSA 260:52-b, and RSA 260:52-e.

Source. 2014, 268:3, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
