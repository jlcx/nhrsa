TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Road Tolls

Section 260:35

    260:35 Use of Tolls. – The road tolls shall be used initially to pay the interest and principal due on any bonds and notes issued pertaining to highway purposes, and the remainder shall be paid into the highway fund.

Source. 1951, 83:9. RSA 265:6. 1955, 280:4. 1957, 273:2. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
