TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Road Tolls

Section 260:46

    260:46 When Toll Payment is in Default; Procedure. – If any distributor shall be in default for more than 10 days in the payment of any road tolls, interest or penalties, the department may proceed to collect the same by civil suit.

Source. 1923, 75:6. PL 104:5. RL 120:8. 1943, 65:1. RSA 265:18. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
