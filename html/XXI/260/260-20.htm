TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Powers and Duties

Section 260:20

    260:20 Motor Vehicle Law to be Printed. – The department shall cause this title and the rules hereunder to be printed in pamphlet form for distribution.

Source. 1921, 119:27. PL 99:9. RL 115:9. RSA 259:16. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
