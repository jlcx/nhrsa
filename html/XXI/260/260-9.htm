TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Powers and Duties

Section 260:9

    260:9 Facsimile Signature. – The director shall have the authority to use an engraved or printed facsimile of his signature for the purpose of affixing the same to any official document for which his signature is required, in accordance with the provisions of RSA 93-A.

Source. RSA 259:5-a. 1973, 204:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
