TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Powers and Duties

Section 260:4

    260:4 Responsibility for Registration of Boats and Motor Carriers. – Those powers, duties, functions and personnel, relating to the registration of boats and motors, motor carriers of property and motor carriers of passengers, shall be within the division as provided in RSA 106-A:12.

Source. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
