TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Road Tolls

Section 260:36-a

    260:36-a Denial of License. – If an application is filed by any person whose license was previously revoked or suspended for cause or, in the opinion of the department, the application was not filed in good faith, the commissioner shall grant the applicant a hearing, after which the commissioner may issue or refuse to issue the license. The applicant shall have 10 days' notice, by certified mail, of the hearing and shall have the right to appear.

Source. 1996, 292:12, eff. Jan. 1, 1997.
