TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Bureau of Certificate of Title

Section 260:24

    260:24 Bureau Established. – There is hereby established in the division a bureau of certificate of title which under the direction of the director shall carry out the work and purposes of the certificate of title and anti-theft provisions of this title.

Source. RSA 269-A:45. 1967, 357:1. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
