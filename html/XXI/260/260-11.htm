TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 260
ADMINISTRATION OF MOTOR VEHICLE LAWS

Powers and Duties

Section 260:11

    260:11 Bonds. – The director and the administrative assistants shall give bonds to the state conditioned upon the faithful discharge of the duties of their offices.

Source. 1915, 154:2. 1919, 82:1. 1921, 118:2. PL 99:4. 1929, 34:1. RL 115:4. 1950, 5:1, part 24:25. RSA 259:6. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
