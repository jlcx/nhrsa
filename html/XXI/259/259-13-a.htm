TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 259
WORDS AND PHRASES DEFINED

Section 259:13-a

    259:13-a Consigned. – "Consigned,'' as used in RSA 261:104, shall mean entrusted by a notarized written statement to a dealer as consignee, whereby the dealer has agreed to hold a vehicle for the consignor for the purpose of selling it.

Source. 1981, 290:4, eff. Jan. 1, 1982 at 12:01 a.m.
