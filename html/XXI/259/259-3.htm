TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 259
WORDS AND PHRASES DEFINED

Section 259:3

    259:3 Agriculture and Farming. – The words "agriculture'' and "farming'' mean all operations of a farm, as defined in RSA 21:34-a.

Source. RSA 259:1, II-a. 1957, 283:1. 1979, 60:2. 1981, 146:1; 1999, 191:3, eff. Sept. 4, 1999. 2007, 103:1, eff. Aug. 10, 2007.
