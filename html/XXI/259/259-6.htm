TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 259
WORDS AND PHRASES DEFINED

Section 259:6

    259:6 Bicycle. – "Bicycle'' shall mean every pedalled vehicle propelled solely by human power upon which any person may ride, except child's tricycles and similar devices.

Source. 1897, 93:2. PL 90:15. RL 106:15. 1945, 188:1, part 20:15. RSA 250:15. 1976, 4:2. 1979, 486:3. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
