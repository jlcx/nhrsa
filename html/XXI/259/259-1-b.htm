TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 259
WORDS AND PHRASES DEFINED

Section 259:1-b


[RSA 259:1-b effective until January 1, 2017; see also RSA 259:1-b set out below.]
    259:1-b Access Aisle. –
    "Access aisle'' shall mean a designated space for maneuvering a wheelchair or other mobility device when entering or exiting a vehicle, and that is immediately adjacent to a properly designated parking space for persons with a walking disability, whether on public or private property. Access aisles shall be marked so as to discourage parking in them.

[RSA 259:1-b effective January 1, 2017; see also RSA 259:1-b set out above.]


259:1-b Access Aisle. --
    "Access aisle'' shall mean a designated space for maneuvering a wheelchair or other mobility device when entering or exiting a vehicle, and that is immediately adjacent to a properly designated parking space for persons with a walking disability, whether on public or private property. Access aisles shall be designated as "no parking'' areas or otherwise marked so as to discourage parking in them.

Source. 2003, 71:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2004. 2016, 304:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2017.
