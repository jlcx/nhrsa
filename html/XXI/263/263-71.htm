TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 263
DRIVERS' LICENSES

Effect of Reckless Driving

Section 263:71

    263:71 License Restored Upon Expiration of Revocation Period. – Whenever any court revokes or suspends a license pursuant to RSA 265:79 for a stated period of time, the department shall restore said license immediately upon the expiration of the period of revocation or suspension ordered by the court provided that:
    I. Any required application and fee have been submitted; and
    II. No other suspension or revocation of such license, pursuant to RSA 264:2, I(e) or any other provision of this title, is still in effect.

Source. RSA 262-A:62-c. 1977, 316:2. 1981, 146:1. 1983, 373:7; 431:11, eff. Aug. 23, 1983.
