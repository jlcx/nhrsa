TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 263
DRIVERS' LICENSES

Conditions and Requirements for a License

Section 263:33

    263:33 Driving Mopeds. – No person may drive a moped upon any way in this state unless he holds a current valid driver's license issued by any state, a special motorcycle license issued pursuant to RSA 263:31 or a special moped license. A person who holds a special moped license may drive any moped without holding any other class of driver's license.

Source. RSA 261:11-b. 1976, 4:13. 1979, 11:1. 1981, 146:1; 301:1, 4, eff. Jan. 1, 1982 at 12:01 a.m.
