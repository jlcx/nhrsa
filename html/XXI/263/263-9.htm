TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 263
DRIVERS' LICENSES

Change of Name or Address

Section 263:9

    263:9 Notice of Change of Licensee's Name or Address. – Whenever any person who has been duly licensed to drive a motor vehicle within this state shall change his legal name or his permanent residence he shall notify the director in writing of such change within 10 days.

Source. 1945, 44:4. RSA 261:2. 1981, 146:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
