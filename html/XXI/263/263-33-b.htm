TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 263
DRIVERS' LICENSES

Conditions and Requirements for a License

Section 263:33-b

    263:33-b Applicability to Mopeds. – Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, the holder of a special moped license shall be subject to all of the provisions of this title relative to licenses to which motorcycle licensees are subject including, but not limited to, revocation and suspension provisions and offenses related to the possession or failure to possess a license or permit.

Source. 1981, 301:5, eff. Jan. 1, 1982 at 12:01 a.m.
