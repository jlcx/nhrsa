TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 261-C
MULTI-USE VETERANS DECAL PLATES

Section 261-C:6


[RSA 261-C:6 effective July 1, 2017.]
    261-C:6 Misuse of Decals. –
    Only decals authorized under this chapter may be displayed on multi-use veterans decal number plates. Use of an unauthorized decal shall be a violation punishable by a fine of $50 plus penalty assessment.

Source. 2016, 279:1, eff. July 1, 2017.
