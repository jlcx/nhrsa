TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 261-C
MULTI-USE VETERANS DECAL PLATES

Section 261-C:1


[RSA 261-C:1 effective July 1, 2017.]
    261-C:1 Multi-Use Veterans Decal Plates Authorized. –
    The department of safety shall issue multi-use veterans decal plates. The design shall provide an area 3 inches by 3 inches for the placement of decals representing branches of service and medals authorized under RSA 261-C:4. Except as otherwise provided in this chapter, multi-use veterans decal plates shall have the same effect and be issued in the same manner as other number plates. Multi-use veterans decal plates shall retain the "live free or die'' logo.

Source. 2016, 279:1, eff. July 1, 2017.
