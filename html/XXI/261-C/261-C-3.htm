TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 261-C
MULTI-USE VETERANS DECAL PLATES

Section 261-C:3


[RSA 261-C:3 effective July 1, 2017.]
    261-C:3 Eligibility. –
    Multi-use veterans decal plates shall be issued only to those veterans defined in RSA 21:50, I(a). Additionally, an individual shall demonstrate to the satisfaction of the director of the state office of veterans services that he or she was a member of a specific branch of service or was awarded the medal represented on the requested decal.

Source. 2016, 279:1, eff. July 1, 2017.
