TITLE XXI
MOTOR VEHICLES

CHAPTER 261-C
MULTI-USE VETERANS DECAL PLATES

Section 261-C:4


[RSA 261-C:4 effective July 1, 2017.]
    261-C:4 Authorized Decals. –
    I. The following decals shall be issued in accordance with this chapter:
       (a) Branch of service:
          (1) United States Army.
          (2) United States Navy.
          (3) United States Air Force.
          (4) United States Marine Corps.
          (5) United States Coast Guard.
       (b) Medal for Valor:
          (1) Medal of Honor.
          (2) Distinguished Service Cross.
          (3) Navy Cross.
          (4) Air Force Cross.
          (5) Silver Star.
          (6) Bronze Star.
    II. The state office of veterans services shall provide a report annually to the director of the division of motor vehicles. Such report shall contain the names of veterans to whom decal or decals were issued, indicating which decal was issued and its issue number.
    III. Eligible veterans shall obtain new decals no less frequently than 36 months from the date of issue.

Source. 2016, 279:1, eff. July 1, 2017.
