TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 38-D
ENERGY COMMISSIONS

Section 38-D:6

    38-D:6 Energy Commission Support. – The office of energy and planning and New Hampshire regional planning commissions may establish programs to assist, at their request, the cities and towns which have established an energy commission.

Source. 2009, 275:1, eff. Sept. 27, 2009.
