TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 38-C
BOARDS OF PUBLIC WORKS COMMISSIONERS

Section 38-C:3

    38-C:3 Appointment. – The commissioners may be appointed by the mayor and board of aldermen or city council, by the selectmen of the town, by the town council, or by the commissioners of the district if the municipality fails to elect or votes to provide for appointment.

Source. 1996, 197:1, eff. Aug. 2, 1996.
