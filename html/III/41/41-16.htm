TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 41
CHOICE AND DUTIES OF TOWN OFFICERS

Town Clerk

Section 41:16

    41:16 Election and Bond. – Every town at the annual meeting shall choose, by ballot, a town clerk, who shall record all votes passed by the town while he remains in office, and discharge all the duties of the office according to law.

Source. RS 34:1. CS 36:1. GS 37:1. GL 40:1. PS 43:1. 1921, 18:1. PL 47:1. RL 59:1. RSA 41:16. 1979, 376:9, eff. Aug. 22, 1979.
