TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 52-A
SPECIAL ASSESSMENT DISTRICTS

Section 52-A:8


[RSA 52-A:8 effective September 11, 2015.]
    52-A:8 Use of Proceeds. –
    Any special assessments collected pursuant to this chapter shall be used, in whole or in part, only for public facilities and services authorized by this chapter or for the payment of the principal and interest of bonds and other obligations of indebtedness for such public facilities and services.

Source. 2015, 240:2, eff. Sept. 11, 2015.
