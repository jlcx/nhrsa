TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Curfew Regulations

Section 31:43-d

    31:43-d Curfew Signal. – Whistles which can be heard in every part of the city, town, or village shall be blown, or bells shall be rung at the appointed time, and shall be called the curfew signal, after which all children under 16 years of age shall be required to be off the street, except they are in company of a parent, guardian, or other suitable adult person.

Source. 1913, 172:3. PL 379:10. RL 441:10. RSA 571:10. 1973, 532:7, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
