TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Liability for Damages Limited, Indemnification, Insurance

Section 31:108

    31:108 Attachment, Trustee Process Prohibited. – No attachment or trustee process shall be available or allowed where immunity has been granted pursuant to RSA 31:104 or where indemnification has been voted pursuant to RSA 31:105 or where indemnification is required pursuant to RSA 31:106.

Source. 1973, 595:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1974.
