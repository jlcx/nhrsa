TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Curfew Regulations

Section 31:43-a

    31:43-a Adoption of Curfew Law. – Any city by vote of its city council, and any town at any meeting, may adopt the provisions of this subdivision.

Source. 1913, 172:1. PL 379:8. RL 441:8. RSA 571:8. 1973, 532:7, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
