TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Emergency Borrowing

Section 31:14

    31:14 Recovered Funds. – Whenever such municipality recovers any portion of the funds, the net sum so recovered shall be applied to the payment of any balance remaining unpaid on such notes or bonds.

Source. 1925, 1:1. PL 42:14. RL 51:14.
