TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Powers

Section 31:6

    31:6 For Holidays. – City councils may, at any legal meeting, grant and vote money, not exceeding $2,000, for providing municipal Christmas trees or for public patriotic exercises for Memorial Day, Independence Day, Veterans Day or other holidays.

Source. 1909, 77:1. 1921, 42:1. PL 42:6. RL 51:6. RSA 31:6. 1969, 445:3, eff. July 3, 1969.
