TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Miscellaneous

Section 31:90

    31:90 Stock Voting. – The selectmen of any town holding stock in any railroad as trustee or otherwise are authorized to vote thereon at all meetings of such corporation, and may appoint, in writing, an agent for that purpose.

Source. 1864, 2890:5. GS 34:20. GL 37:17. PS 40:18. Const., Art. 5. PL 42:69. RL 51:88.
