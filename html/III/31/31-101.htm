TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Miscellaneous

Section 31:101

    31:101 Effect of License. – A person so licensed, who shall first cause the license to be recorded at length by the town clerk, and who shall comply with the terms expressed therein, shall be exempt from the penalties imposed by this chapter for any act authorized by the license.

Source. 1844, 144:2. CS 119:9. GS 252:9. GL 269:9. PS 264:10. PL 378:10. RL 440:9. RSA 570:9. 1973, 532:1, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
