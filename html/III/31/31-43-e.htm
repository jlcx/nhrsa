TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Curfew Regulations

Section 31:43-e

    31:43-e Penalty, Custodian. – Any parent, guardian, or person having the control of any minor under the age of 16 years who shall permit any such minor to be upon any public street or in any public place in any such city or town, in violation of this subdivision, shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1913, 172:2. PL 379:11. RL 441:11. RSA 571:11. 1973, 532:7, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
