TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Central Business Service Districts

Section 31:125

    31:125 Limit on Liability. – The provisions of RSA 507-B relative to bodily injury actions against governmental units shall apply to all municipal activities performed in connection with a central business service district.

Source. 1983, 109:1, eff. July 23, 1983.
