TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Curfew Regulations

Section 31:43-c

    31:43-c Minors under 16. – In all such cities and towns it shall be unlawful for any minor under the age of 16 years to be upon any public street, or in any public place, after the hour of 9 o'clock in the evening, unless accompanied by a parent, guardian, or other suitable person.

Source. 1913, 172:1. PL 379:9. RL 441:9. RSA 571:9. 1973, 532:7, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
