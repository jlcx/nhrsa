TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Curfew Regulations

Section 31:43-f

    31:43-f Penalty, Minors. – For the first violation of this subdivision by any child it shall be taken to its home by the officers, and the parents or guardian shall be notified of the penalty for any subsequent violation.

Source. 1913, 172:4. PL 379:12. RL 441:12. RSA 571:12. 1973, 532:7, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
