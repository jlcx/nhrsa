TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Corporate Entities

Section 31:2

    31:2 Parishes. – All places incorporated by the name of parishes with town privileges are towns, and are entitled to the privileges, vested with all the powers, and subject to all the liabilities, of towns.

Source. RS 31:2. CS 32:2. GS 34:2. GL 37:2. PS 40:2. PL 42:2. RL 51:2.
