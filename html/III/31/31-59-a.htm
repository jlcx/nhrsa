TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Town Central Purchasing Department

Section 31:59-a

    31:59-a Authority Granted. – Any town at an annual meeting, under an article in the warrant for said meeting, may vote to establish a central purchasing department for said town.

Source. 1959, 111:1, eff. July 19, 1959.
