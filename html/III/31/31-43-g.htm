TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Curfew Regulations

Section 31:43-g

    31:43-g Second Offense. – Upon any subsequent violation by any child said parents or guardians shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1913, 172:4. PL 379:13. RL 441:13. RSA 571:13. 1973, 532:7, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
