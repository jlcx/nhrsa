TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Miscellaneous

Section 31:95-f

    31:95-f Geographic Information Systems. – Any town may establish computer-based geographic information systems and control the distribution of that information, subject to RSA 91-A. The town may finance the completion and perpetuation of the system through establishing a special revenue fund under RSA 31:95-c and 31:95-d or through nonprofit corporations. The town may charge fees for the use of the system.

Source. 1994, 76:2, eff. July 5, 1994.
