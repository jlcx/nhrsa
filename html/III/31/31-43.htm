TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Power to Make Bylaws

Section 31:43

    31:43 When in Force. – Bylaws adopted by any town without limitation shall continue in force until altered or annulled by vote of the town or bylaw.

Source. RS 31:8. CS 32:9. GS 34:8. GL 37:8. PS 40:12. PL 42:31. RL 51:33.
