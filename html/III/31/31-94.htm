TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Miscellaneous

Section 31:94

    31:94 Fiscal Year. – The fiscal year of towns, village precincts and departments thereof excepting school districts shall end on December 31.

Source. 1869, 26:4. GL 40:10. PS 43:49. 1893, 24:1. 1915, 102:1. 1917, 129:9, 10. PL 42:73. RL 51:92. 1943, 142:1, eff. Dec. 31, 1944.
