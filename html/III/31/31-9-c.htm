TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Powers

Section 31:9-c

    31:9-c Lockups. – The selectmen of any town, upon petition of 30 or more legal voters of the town, shall provide a suitable lockup for the temporary detention of offenders.

Source. 1874, 53:1. GL 269:25. 1883, 60:1. PS 264:23. PL 378:27. RL 440:26. RSA 570:29. 1973, 532:4, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
