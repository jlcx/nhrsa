TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Miscellaneous

Section 31:91

    31:91 Soliciting Funds. – The right to grant permits for soliciting funds for charitable purposes and for the sale of tags, flowers or other objects for charitable purposes shall be vested in the mayor and aldermen of a city or the selectmen of towns.

Source. 1923, 121:1. PL 42:70. RL 51:89. RSA 31:91. 1955, 270:1, eff. July 22, 1955.
