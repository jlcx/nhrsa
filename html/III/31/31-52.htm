TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Powers as to Shade and Ornamental Trees

Section 31:52

    31:52 Rights of Owners. – Nothing in this subdivision shall be construed to deprive the owner of real estate of the right to plant, rear and protect any tree between the carriage path and sidewalk in any public street or highway on which his estate is situate, if it does not interfere with the public travel.

Source. 1861, 2502:3. GS 34:11. GL 37:11. PS 40:11. PL 42:40. RL 51:42.
