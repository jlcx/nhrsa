TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31
POWERS AND DUTIES OF TOWNS

Manufactured Housing Foundations

Section 31:116

    31:116 Manufactured Housing Foundations. – No town shall impose requirements that manufactured housing, as defined by RSA 674:31, which is located in parks be placed on other than the structural carriers designed for that purpose.

Source. 1977, 481:1. 1983, 230:3, eff. Aug. 17, 1983.
