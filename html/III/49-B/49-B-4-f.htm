TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 49-B
HOME RULE--MUNICIPAL CHARTERS

Amendment of Municipal Charter

Section 49-B:4-f


[RSA 49-B:4-f effective September 30, 2014.]
    49-B:4-f Definition of Amendment. –
    For purposes of this chapter, an "amendment'' to a municipal charter shall mean any change to an existing charter that does not constitute a revision under RSA 49-B:4-d.

Source. 2014, 292:2, eff. Sept. 30, 2014.
