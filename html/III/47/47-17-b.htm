TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 47
POWERS OF CITY COUNCILS

Bylaws and Ordinances

Section 47:17-b


[There is no RSA 47:17-a.]
    47:17-b Enforcement of Bylaws and Ordinances. –
    In addition to any other enforcement procedure authorized by law, any city code, ordinance, bylaw, or regulation may be enforced pursuant to the procedures established in RSA 31:39-c, RSA 31:39-d, or both, subject to the provisions and limitations thereof.

Source. 2009, 270:4, eff. Jan. 1, 2010.
