TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 34
CAPITAL RESERVE FUNDS FOR CITIES

Section 34:4

    34:4 Limitations on Appropriations. – No city shall raise and appropriate or transfer from any of its unencumbered surplus funds in any one year for such reserves a total amount in excess of 1/4 of one percent of the last assessed valuation of the city.

Source. 1949, 181:4, eff. May 3, 1949.
