TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 34
CAPITAL RESERVE FUNDS FOR CITIES

Section 34:15

    34:15 Application of Chapter. – The provisions of any city charter inconsistent with the provisions hereof are hereby repealed to the extent of such inconsistency.

Source. 1949, 181:15, eff. May 3, 1949.
