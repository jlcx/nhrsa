TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 40
GOVERNMENT OF TOWN MEETING

Proceedings

Section 40:11

    40:11 Consideration of Warrant Articles. – In towns with 10,000 or more inhabitants, every article included in a warrant for a town meeting shall be acted upon before final adjournment of the town meeting.

Source. 1994, 197:2, eff. July 23, 1994.
