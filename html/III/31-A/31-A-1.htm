TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 31-A
RETURN OF REVENUE TO CITIES AND TOWNS

Section 31-A:1


[RSA 31-A:1 suspended for the biennium ending June 30, 2013 as provided by 2011, 224:2.]
    31-A:1 Return of Revenue. –
    Commencing in 1970 and in each year thereafter, a portion of the general revenue of the state shall be returned to each city and town of the state.

Source. 1970, 5:16, eff. April 22, 1970.
