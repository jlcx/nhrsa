TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 39
TIME FOR HOLDING TOWN MEETINGS AND WARNING THEREOF

Section 39:1-c

    39:1-c Updated Checklist. – An updated checklist shall be used at all town meetings and elections as provided in RSA 669:5.

Source. 1981, 571:2, eff. Sept. 4, 1981.
