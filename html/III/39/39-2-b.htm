TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 39
TIME FOR HOLDING TOWN MEETINGS AND WARNING THEREOF

Section 39:2-b

    39:2-b Time Polls Open. – The warrant for any town meeting shall provide that polls at the additional polling place, if any, shall open at the same time as the polls at the central polling place.

Source. 1969, 117:1, eff. May 9, 1969.
