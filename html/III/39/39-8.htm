TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 39
TIME FOR HOLDING TOWN MEETINGS AND WARNING THEREOF

Section 39:8

    39:8 Selectmen's Power. – In case of the death or removal of any of the selectmen of a town the major part of those who remain in office shall have power to warn meetings.

Source. RS 32:8. CS 34:8. GS 35:8. GL 38:8. PS 41:8. PL 45:7. RL 57:7.
