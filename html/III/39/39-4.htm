TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 39
TIME FOR HOLDING TOWN MEETINGS AND WARNING THEREOF

Section 39:4

    39:4 Special Meetings. – The selectmen when calling a special town meeting shall, within one week after posting the warrant therefor, cause copy of said warrant to be published once in a newspaper of general circulation in said town.

Source. 1945, 39:1, eff. March 6, 1945.
