TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 39
TIME FOR HOLDING TOWN MEETINGS AND WARNING THEREOF

Section 39:12

    39:12 Other Warning. – Any town may, by vote, prescribe a different method of warning meetings; and the meetings warned in pursuance of such vote shall be legal and valid.

Source. RS 32:6. CS 34:6. GS 35:6. GL 38:6. PS 41:6. PL 45:11. RL 57:11.
