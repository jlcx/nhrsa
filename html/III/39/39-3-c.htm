TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 39
TIME FOR HOLDING TOWN MEETINGS AND WARNING THEREOF

Section 39:3-c

    39:3-c Limitation. – Any petitioned article, which if adopted, would abolish a planning board, or zoning commission shall not be included in the warrant, unless such petition meets the requirements established by RSA 673:18, II.

Source. 1971, 396:1. 1985, 103:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1986.
