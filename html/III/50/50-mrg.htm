TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 50
PARKS

Section 50:1

    50:1 Establishment. – The selectmen or mayor and aldermen shall not lay out, establish, or enlarge a park or common unless the town or city councils of the city shall have voted in favor thereof.

Source. 1866, 4271:1. GS 45:4. 1873, 54:1. 1874, 29:1. 1877, 17:1. GL 49:4. PS 51:6. PL 55:14. RL 68:16.

Section 50:2

    50:2 Discontinuance. – If any person suffers special damage by the discontinuance of a common or park he may have the damage assessed in the same manner as special damages are assessed in case of the discontinuance of a highway.

Source. PS 51:7. PL 55:15. RL 68:17.
