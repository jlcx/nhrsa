TITLE III
TOWNS, CITIES, VILLAGE DISTRICTS, AND UNINCORPORATED PLACES

CHAPTER 50
PARKS

Section 50:2

    50:2 Discontinuance. – If any person suffers special damage by the discontinuance of a common or park he may have the damage assessed in the same manner as special damages are assessed in case of the discontinuance of a highway.

Source. PS 51:7. PL 55:15. RL 68:17.
