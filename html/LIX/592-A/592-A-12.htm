TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 592-A
JURISDICTION AND PROCEDURE GENERALLY

Section 592-A:12


[RSA 592-A:12 effective until January 1, 2015; see also RSA 592-A:12 set out below.]
    592-A:12 Payment of Witnesses in Criminal Cases. –
    Any person who attends any court for the state in criminal cases pursuant to subpoena shall be paid the witness fees provided by RSA 516:16. It shall be the duty of the clerk of court to maintain a register of all persons who have been required to attend court for the state. The attorney general shall pay all witness fees to all persons who are entitled to such fees. The attorney general shall adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, relative to the payment of witness fees under this section.

Source. 1951, 163, par. 12-a. RSA 592:13. 1957, 244:8. 1959, 20:1. 1979, 316:1. 1983, 383:59. 1993, 190:17. 1994, 273:3, eff. June 6, 1994.
