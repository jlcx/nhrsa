TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 592-A
JURISDICTION AND PROCEDURE GENERALLY

Section 592-A:15

    592-A:15 Default. – If a party, being summoned by delivering to him an attested copy of the summons as provided in RSA 592-A:14, shall not appear, the justice may then issue a warrant as provided in RSA 592-A:8. Such party shall be in contempt of court for failing to attend after being so summoned and may be fined not more than $100.

Source. RS 113:17, 18. CS 119:19, 20. GS 234:15. GL 252:14. PS 248:14. PL 362:14. 1933, 35:2. RL 421:14. RSA 592:16. 1957, 244:8. 1977, 588:46. 1983, 225:1, eff. Aug. 15, 1983.
