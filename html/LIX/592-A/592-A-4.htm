TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 592-A
JURISDICTION AND PROCEDURE GENERALLY

Section 592-A:4

    592-A:4 Binding Over by Justice. – A justice of a district or municipal court may cause to be apprehended and committed to jail or bound over with sufficient sureties to the superior court all persons charged with offenses committed or triable in the county exceeding his jurisdiction to try.

Source. RS 222:3. CS 237:3. GS 234:5. GL 252:5. PS 248:5. PL 362:5. RL 421:5. RSA 592:5. 1957, 244:8, eff. Sept. 23, 1957.
