TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 592-A
JURISDICTION AND PROCEDURE GENERALLY

Section 592-A:11

    592-A:11 Summons for Witnesses. – In any warrant the officer may be required to summon the persons therein named as witnesses to attend the trial.

Source. GS 234:13. GL 252:13. PS 248:13. PL 362:12. RL 421:12. RSA 592:12. 1957, 244:8, eff. Sept. 23, 1957.
