TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 599
APPEALS FROM CONVICTIONS IN MUNICIPAL OR DISTRICT COURT

Section 599:3

    599:3 Failure to Prosecute Appeal. – If the appellant fails to enter his appeal within the time limited and prosecute his appeal, a record thereof shall be made, his recognizance shall be declared forfeited, and, within 10 days, the clerk of court shall transmit to the district or municipal court appealed from a certificate of such forfeiture.

Source. 1862, 2606. GS 240:4. GL 258:4. PS 252:4. PL 366:4. 1927, 10:2. 1937, 49:2. RL 425:4. RSA 599:3. 1957, 244:39, eff. Sept. 23, 1957.
