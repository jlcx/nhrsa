TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 596-A
PRELIMINARY EXAMINATIONS

Section 596-A:6

    596-A:6 Excluding Witnesses. – Any of the witnesses for or against the accused may be excluded from the place of examination, and be kept separate and apart from each other during the examination of other witnesses.

Source. 1965, 116:1, eff. July 27, 1965.
