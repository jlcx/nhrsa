TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 598-A
PROFESSIONAL BONDSMEN

Section 598-A:4

    598-A:4 Rules of Secretary of State or His Designee. – All professional bondsmen shall be governed by rules which shall be adopted under RSA 541-A by the secretary of state or his designee.

Source. 1967, 156:1. 1977, 386:5. 1989, 8:5. 1992, 288:55, eff. July 1, 1992.
