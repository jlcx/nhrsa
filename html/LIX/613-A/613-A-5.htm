TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 613-A
UNIFORM RENDITION OF PRISONERS AS WITNESSES IN CRIMINAL PROCEEDINGS ACT

Section 613-A:5

    613-A:5 Exceptions. – This chapter does not apply to any person in this state confined as insane or mentally ill or as a defective delinquent or under sentence of death.

Source. 1959, 83:1, eff. July 5, 1959.
