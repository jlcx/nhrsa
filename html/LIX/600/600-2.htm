TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 600
GRAND JURIES

Section 600:2

    600:2 Issuance of Venires in Emergencies. – In case of emergency, venires may be issued for grand jurors to be drawn and notified and to attend forthwith as in the case of petit jurors.

Source. RS 176:15. CS 186:16. GS 242:3. GL 260:3. PS 253:3. PL 367:3 RL 427:3.
