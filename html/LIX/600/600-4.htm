TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 600
GRAND JURIES

Section 600:4

    600:4 Oath to Witnesses. – The foreman of the grand jury, the prosecuting officer or any justice upon the jury may administer oaths to witnesses to be examined before them.

Source. GS 242:6. GL 260:6. PS 253:6. PL 367:5. RL 427:5.
