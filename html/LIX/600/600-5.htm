TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 600
GRAND JURIES

Section 600:5

    600:5 Clerk; Minutes. – The grand jury may appoint one of their number to be clerk to preserve minutes of the proceedings before them, which shall be delivered to the attorney general or county attorney.

Source. GS 242:7. GL 260:7. PS 253:7. PL 367:7 RL 427:6.
