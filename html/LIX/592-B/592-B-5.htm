TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 592-B
CRIMINAL PROCEDURE IN SUPERIOR COURT

Section 592-B:5


[RSA 592-B:5 effective January 1, 2016 and as provided by 2015, 249:17.]
    592-B:5 When Warrants Returnable to Superior Court. –
    If such warrant is issued, it shall be made returnable before the superior court having jurisdiction over the case and not elsewhere.

Source. 2015, 249:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2016 and as provided by 2015, 249:17.
