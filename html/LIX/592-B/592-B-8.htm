TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 592-B
CRIMINAL PROCEDURE IN SUPERIOR COURT

Section 592-B:8


[RSA 592-B:8 effective January 1, 2016 and as provided by 2015, 249:17.]
    592-B:8 Dispositional Conference. –
    Upon entry of a plea of not guilty, the case shall be scheduled for a dispositional conference in accordance with superior court rules.

Source. 2015, 249:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2016 and as provided by 2015, 249:17.
