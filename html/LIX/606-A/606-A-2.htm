TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 606-A
AGREEMENT ON DETAINERS

Section 606-A:2

    606-A:2 Definition. – The phrase "appropriate court'' as used in the agreement on detainers shall, with reference to the courts of this state, mean the municipal court, the district court or the superior court.

Source. 1959, 107:2. 1977, 588:20, eff. Sept. 16, 1977.
