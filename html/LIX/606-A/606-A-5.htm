TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 606-A
AGREEMENT ON DETAINERS

Section 606-A:5

    606-A:5 Duty of Warden. – It shall be lawful and mandatory upon the warden or other official in charge of a penal or correctional institution in this state to give over the person of any inmate thereof whenever so required by the operation of the agreement on detainers.

Source. 1959, 107:5, eff. July 17, 1959.
