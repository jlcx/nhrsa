TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 606-A
AGREEMENT ON DETAINERS

Section 606-A:4

    606-A:4 Application. – Nothing in this chapter or in the agreement on detainers shall be construed to require the application of the habitual offenders law to any person on account of any conviction had in a proceeding brought to final disposition by reason of the use of said agreement.

Source. 1959, 107:4, eff. July 17, 1959.
