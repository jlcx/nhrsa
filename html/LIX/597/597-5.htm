TITLE LIX
PROCEEDINGS IN CRIMINAL CASES

CHAPTER 597
BAIL AND RECOGNIZANCES

General Provisions

Section 597:5

    597:5 When Requirable. – Every court and justice may, when a person is accused of an offense in which said court or justice is authorized to receive bail, release said person on personal recognizance or require him to recognize, with sureties, to appear at a future time before himself or any other competent tribunal.

Source. RS 222:3. CS 237:3. GS 241:1. GL 259:1. PS 252:14. PL 366:17. RL 425:17. RSA 597:5. 1969, 78:4, eff. June 3, 1969.
