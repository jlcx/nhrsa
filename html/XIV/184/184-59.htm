TITLE XIV
MILK AND MILK PRODUCTS

CHAPTER 184
INSPECTION AND SALE OF DAIRY PRODUCTS

Receiving Stations for Milk, Etc.

Section 184:59

    184:59 Definition. – The term "receiving station'' as used in this subdivision shall include creameries, cheese factories, condensaries, receiving stations, milk depots, bulk tank collector trucks and processing plants for milk and cream.

Source. 1925, 148:1. PL 163:54. RL 194:54. RSA 184:59. 1955, 86:1, eff. April 16, 1955.
