TITLE XIV
MILK AND MILK PRODUCTS

CHAPTER 184
INSPECTION AND SALE OF DAIRY PRODUCTS

Prosecutions

Section 184:58

    184:58 Connivance. – Any board of health, or any agent thereof, or selectman or inspector appointed under the provisions of RSA 184, who willfully connives at or assists in a violation of the provisions of RSA 184, shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1883, 42:8. PS 127:10. 1901, 107:1. PL 163:53. RL 194:53. RSA 184:58. 1973, 528:102, eff. at 11:59 P.M., Oct. 31, 1973.
