TITLE XIV
MILK AND MILK PRODUCTS

CHAPTER 184
INSPECTION AND SALE OF DAIRY PRODUCTS

Renovated or Process Butter, Oleomargarine, etc.

Section 184:47

    184:47 Marking. – Whoever shall sell any renovated butter, or process butter, in quantity less than the original unbroken package, as duly labeled in accordance with the regulations of the United States Internal Revenue Department, shall stamp upon the outside of such broken or retail package as delivered to the purchaser the words "Renovated Butter'' in letters not less than 3/8 inch square, of plain, uncondensed Gothic caps.

Source. 1833, 42:9. PS 127:18. 1901, 107:2. 1909, 148:9. 1911, 71:1. PL 163:40. RL 194:42.
