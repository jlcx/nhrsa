TITLE XIV
MILK AND MILK PRODUCTS

CHAPTER 184
INSPECTION AND SALE OF DAIRY PRODUCTS

Receiving Stations for Milk, Etc.

Section 184:60-c

    184:60-c Penalty. – Any person who purchases milk from producers in this state using the bulk tank collector system of collecting milk and who fails to have a valid permit from the commissioner to operate each individual route shall be guilty of a violation.

Source. 1961, 129:1. 1973, 531:41, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
