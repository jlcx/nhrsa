TITLE XIV
MILK AND MILK PRODUCTS

CHAPTER 184
INSPECTION AND SALE OF DAIRY PRODUCTS

Renovated or Process Butter, Oleomargarine, etc.

Section 184:53

    184:53 Advertisements. – No person, with intent to deceive, shall use in any way, in connection or association with the sale or exposure for sale or advertisement of any substance designed to be used as a substitute for butter, the word "butter'', "cream'', "dairy'', "cow'' or the name or representation of any breed of dairy cattle, or any combination of such word or words and representation, or any other words or symbols or combination thereof commonly used in the sale of butter.

Source. 1923, 36:1. PL 163:46. RL 194:48.
