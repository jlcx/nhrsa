TITLE XIV
MILK AND MILK PRODUCTS

CHAPTER 184
INSPECTION AND SALE OF DAIRY PRODUCTS

Milk Producers Emergency Relief Fund

Section 184:111

    184:111 Mislabeling of Milk; Prohibition and Penalties. –
    I. The labeling of milk that does not meet the requirements of RSA 184:79, XXII-XXII-c as "fresh,'' "New Hampshire fresh,'' or "northeastern fresh'' is prohibited.
    II. Any person who knowingly violates any provision of this section shall be subject to an administrative fine levied by the commissioner not to exceed $1,000 for each violation.

Source. 2008, 160:2, eff. Aug. 5, 2008.
