TITLE XIV
MILK AND MILK PRODUCTS

CHAPTER 184
INSPECTION AND SALE OF DAIRY PRODUCTS

Receiving Stations for Milk, Etc.

Section 184:60-a

    184:60-a Permit Required. – Any person who purchases milk from producers in this state using the bulk tank collector system of collecting milk shall procure from the commissioner a permit to operate each individual route.

Source. 1955, 86:2, eff. April 16, 1955.
