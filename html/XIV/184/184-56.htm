TITLE XIV
MILK AND MILK PRODUCTS

CHAPTER 184
INSPECTION AND SALE OF DAIRY PRODUCTS

Prosecutions

Section 184:56

    184:56 Definition. – The terms "butter'' and "cheese'' shall be understood to mean the products usually known by those names, and which are manufactured exclusively from milk, or cream, or both, with salt and with or without coloring matter, and, if cheese, with rennet.

Source. 1881, 57:5. 1885, 68:3. PS 127:22. PL 163:49. RL 194:51.
