TITLE XIV
MILK AND MILK PRODUCTS

CHAPTER 184
INSPECTION AND SALE OF DAIRY PRODUCTS

Milk Producers Emergency Relief Fund

Section 184:108

    184:108 Target Price. – The target price for eligible participating milk producers shall be $16.94 per hundredweight and shall be adjusted as provided in RSA 184:110, II.

Source. 2007, 381:1, eff. July 1, 2007. 2008, 74:3, eff. July 1, 2008.
