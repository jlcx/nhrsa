TITLE XIV
MILK AND MILK PRODUCTS

CHAPTER 184
INSPECTION AND SALE OF DAIRY PRODUCTS

Milk for Drinking

Section 184:30-e

    184:30-e Enforcement. – The department of health and human services and local boards of health shall be charged with the enforcement of the provisions of this subdivision.

Source. 1957, 183:1. RSA 184:27-a. 1961, 138:1. RSA 184:30-e. 1983, 291:1, I. 1995, 310:181, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
