TITLE XIV
MILK AND MILK PRODUCTS

CHAPTER 184
INSPECTION AND SALE OF DAIRY PRODUCTS

Renovated or Process Butter, Oleomargarine, Etc.

Section 184:54

    184:54 Penalty. – Any person, firm, corporation or agent violating any of the provisions of the preceding sections of this subdivision shall be guilty of a violation for the first offense, and for each subsequent offense, shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1895, 115:4. PL 163:47. 1931, 176:3. RL 194:49. RSA 184:54. 1973, 528:154, 530:16, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
