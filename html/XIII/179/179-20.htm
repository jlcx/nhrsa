TITLE XIII
ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES

CHAPTER 179
ENFORCEMENT, REQUIREMENTS AND PENALTIES

Section 179:20

    179:20 Employee Restrictions. –
    I. No person, except a citizen of the United States or legal resident alien, shall be employed to sell or deliver any liquor or beverage.
    II. No on-premises or off-premises licensee shall consume beverage or liquor while working. No employee, as defined in RSA 275:4, II, shall consume beverage or liquor while working.

Source. 1990, 255:1. 2003, 231:22. 2008, 327:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
