TITLE XIII
ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES

CHAPTER 176
THE LIQUOR COMMISSION

Section 176:14

    176:14 Rulemaking. – The commission shall adopt rules, under RSA 541-A, necessary to carry out its powers and duties under this title. The commission shall not adopt any rule in conflict with any provision of RSA 541-A.

Source. 1990, 255:1. 1991, 204:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
