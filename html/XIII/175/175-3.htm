TITLE XIII
ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES

CHAPTER 175
DEFINITIONS AND GENERAL PROVISIONS

Section 175:3

    175:3 Purchases of Sacramental Wines. – Any duly authorized officer of a regularly organized religious body may order from any source for shipment to such religious body wines to be used for sacramental purposes only in the services of such religious body.

Source. 1990, 255:1, eff. July 1, 1990.
