TITLE XIII
ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES

CHAPTER 178
LIQUOR LICENSES AND FEES

Section 178:19-a

    178:19-a Retail Tobacco License. –
    I. The commission may issue a retail tobacco license to a person engaged in the business of retail sales and distribution of tobacco products in this state. Each retail outlet shall have a separate license regardless of the fact that one or more outlets may be owned or controlled by a single person.
    II. A retail tobacco license shall be prominently displayed on the premises described in it.
    III. The commission, when issuing or renewing a retail tobacco license, shall furnish a sign which shall read or be substantially similar to the following: "State Law prohibits the sale of tobacco products to persons under age 18. Warning: violators of these provisions may be subject to a fine.''

Source. 2008, 341:15, eff. Jan. 1, 2009.
