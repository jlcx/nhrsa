TITLE XIX-A
FORESTRY

CHAPTER 227-L
WOODLAND FIRE CONTROL

Northeastern Interstate Forest Fire Protection Compact

Section 227-L:34

    227-L:34 Reimbursement Procedure. – When the state has made any payment under RSA 227-L:32, the director of the forests and lands shall, in writing, notify all towns involved, of the amount of their liability as provided in RSA 227-L:33, and within 60 days of the receipt of such notice, the respective towns shall remit the amount due to the state treasurer.

Source. 1995, 299:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
