TITLE XIX-A
FORESTRY

CHAPTER 227-L
WOODLAND FIRE CONTROL

Forest Fire Personnel

Section 227-L:4

    227-L:4 Forest Fire Personnel. – The commissioner, upon the recommendation of the director, shall appoint such field and office personnel and such other assistants as the state woodland fire control program may warrant.

Source. 1995, 299:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
