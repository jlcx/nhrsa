TITLE XIX-A
FORESTRY

CHAPTER 227-L
WOODLAND FIRE CONTROL

Northeastern Interstate Forest Fire Protection Compact

Section 227-L:32

    227-L:32 Payment by State. – Upon recommendation of the director of the division of forests and lands, department of resources and economic development, and upon warrant of the governor with the approval of the council, the state treasurer shall pay out of any money in the treasury not otherwise appropriated to any state giving aid within the state under this chapter, such sums as shall be due under the terms of this chapter.

Source. 1995, 299:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
