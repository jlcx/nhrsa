TITLE XIX-A
FORESTRY

CHAPTER 227-L
WOODLAND FIRE CONTROL

Northeastern Interstate Forest Fire Protection Compact

Section 227-L:29

    227-L:29 Compensation. – The commissioners shall serve without compensation but shall be reimbursed for their actual expenses incurred in the performance of their duties.

Source. 1995, 299:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
