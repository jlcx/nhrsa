TITLE LX
CORRECTION AND PUNISHMENT

CHAPTER 616
PECUNIARY PENALTIES AND FORFEITURES

Section 616:8

    616:8 General Issue. – In any such action the defendant may plead the general issue, and under it give any special matter in evidence.
