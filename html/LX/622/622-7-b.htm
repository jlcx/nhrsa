TITLE LX
CORRECTION AND PUNISHMENT

CHAPTER 622
THE STATE PRISONS

General Provisions

Section 622:7-b

    622:7-b Victims' Fund. – Every commissary in a state prison operated for the sale of commodities shall collect a surcharge of 5 percent of the sales price of every item sold. All funds collected pursuant to this section shall be deposited in and continually appropriated to the victims' assistance fund.

Source. 1999, 261:5, eff. July 1, 1999.
