TITLE LX
CORRECTION AND PUNISHMENT

CHAPTER 622-A
NEW ENGLAND INTERSTATE CORRECTIONS COMPACT

Section 622-A:3

    622-A:3 Authority of Commissioner of Corrections. – The commissioner of corrections is hereby authorized and directed to do all things necessary or incidental to the carrying out of the compact in every particular.

Source. 1961, 101:1, eff. May 4, 1961.
