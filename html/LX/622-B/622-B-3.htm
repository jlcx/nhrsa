TITLE LX
CORRECTION AND PUNISHMENT

CHAPTER 622-B
INTERSTATE CORRECTIONS COMPACT

Section 622-B:3

    622-B:3 Duty of the Commissioner of Corrections. – The commissioner of corrections is authorized and directed to do all things necessary or incidental to implement this compact and he may, in his discretion, delegate this authority to other persons.

Source. 1979, 111:1, eff. July 20, 1979.
