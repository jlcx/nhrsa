TITLE LVIII
PUBLIC JUSTICE

CHAPTER 571-C
RESPONSIBILITY TO MINORS

Section 571-C:2

    571-C:2 Intoxicating Beverages at Interscholastic Athletic Contests. – No person shall drink or have in his possession any intoxicating beverage while in attendance, as a spectator or otherwise, at any place where a school interscholastic athletic contest is being conducted. Whoever violates the provisions of this section shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1971, 141:1. RSA 570:24-a. 1973, 532:25, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
