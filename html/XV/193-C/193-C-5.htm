TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 193-C
STATEWIDE EDUCATION IMPROVEMENT AND ASSESSMENT PROGRAM

Section 193-C:5


[RSA 193-C:5 effective until September 30, 2014; see also RSA 193-C:5 set out below.]
    193-C:5 Areas of Assessment. –
    The academic areas to be assessed shall include, but not be limited to: reading and language arts, mathematics, science, history, geography, civics, and economics.

Source. 1993, 290:2. 2007, 3:1, eff. June 19, 2007.
