TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 189
SCHOOL BOARDS, SUPERINTENDENTS, TEACHERS, AND TRUANT OFFICERS; SCHOOL CENSUS

School Boards, Transportation and Instruction of Pupils

Section 189:14-c

    189:14-c Revocation of Certification. – Any teacher certified in this state who has been convicted of any felony involving child pornography or of a felonious physical assault on a minor or of any sexual assault, shall have such teacher certification revoked by the New Hampshire state board of education.

Source. 1988, 257:3. 1995, 174:11, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
