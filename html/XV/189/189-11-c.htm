TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 189
SCHOOL BOARDS, SUPERINTENDENTS, TEACHERS, AND TRUANT OFFICERS; SCHOOL CENSUS

School Boards, Transportation and Instruction of Pupils

Section 189:11-c

    189:11-c Cursive Handwriting and Memorization of Multiplication Tables. – The school board of each school district is encouraged to provide instruction in cursive handwriting and memorization of multiplication tables.

Source. 2015, 41:1, eff. July 8, 2015.
