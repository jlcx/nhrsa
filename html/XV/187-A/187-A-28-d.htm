TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 187-A
STATE COLLEGE AND UNIVERSITY SYSTEM

Public Higher Education Study Committee

Section 187-A:28-d

    187-A:28-d Report and Recommendations. – The committee shall submit a report to the general court by January 15 of each year. Copies of the report shall be submitted to the governor and council, the senate finance and education committees, the house of representatives finance and education committees, the board of trustees of the university system, the chancellor of the community college system of New Hampshire, and to any other individual or organization as the committee deems advisable.

Source. 1995, 292:2. 1999, 99:1. 2007, 361:14, eff. July 17, 2007.
