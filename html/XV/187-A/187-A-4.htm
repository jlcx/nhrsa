TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 187-A
STATE COLLEGE AND UNIVERSITY SYSTEM

University of New Hampshire

Section 187-A:4

    187-A:4 Colleges and Schools. – The university shall include a college of engineering and physical sciences, a college of liberal arts, a college of life sciences and agriculture, a school of business and economics, a school of health studies, a college at Manchester, a graduate school, and may include a school of social work and such other colleges, schools, departments and divisions as are consistent with such organization.

Source. 1981, 331:1. 1985, 140:2, eff. July 1, 1985.
