TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 200-G
COMPACT FOR EDUCATION

Section 200-G:2

    200-G:2 Members of the Educational Commission of the States. – Of the members of the educational commission from this state, the speaker of the house of representatives shall appoint one such member from the membership of the house and the president of the senate shall appoint one such member from the membership of the senate. Said legislative members of the commission shall serve for a term of 2 years each.

Source. 1970, 25:1, eff. July 1, 1970.
