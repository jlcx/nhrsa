TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 193
PUPILS

Scholarships for Orphans of Veterans

Section 193:21

    193:21 Payment. – The amounts payable to recipients shall be determined by the department of education, division of higher education. The department shall determine the eligibility in accordance with rules adopted under RSA 541-A of the children who make application for the benefits provided for in this subdivision.

Source. 1943, 35:3. RSA 193:21. 1981, 409:4. 1987, 171:4. 2005, 97:3, eff. Aug. 7, 2005. 2011, 224:132, eff. July 1, 2011.
