TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 193
PUPILS

Parents as Teachers Program

Section 193:36

    193:36 Rulemaking. – The commissioner of the department of education shall adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, necessary to carry out the provisions of this subdivision.

Source. 2000, 140:1. 2003, 317:3, eff. July 1, 2003.
