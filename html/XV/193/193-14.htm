TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 193
PUPILS

School Attendance

Section 193:14

    193:14 Assignment of Pupils to Schools. – No pupil who shall have been assigned to a particular school by the school board shall attend any other school until assigned thereto.

Source. GS 83:4. GL 91:4. PS 93:4. 1921, 85, III:11. PL 118:13. RL 137:13. RSA 193:14. 1969, 356:6, eff. July 1, 1969.
