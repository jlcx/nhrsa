TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 193
PUPILS

School Attendance

Section 193:11

    193:11 Disturbance. – Any person not a pupil who shall wilfully interrupt or disturb any school shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. GL 91:17. 1887, 15:1. PS 93:17. 1921, 85, III:8. PL 118:10. RL 137:10. RSA 193:11. 1973, 528:106, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
