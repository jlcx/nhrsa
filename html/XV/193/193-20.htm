TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 193
PUPILS

Scholarships for Orphans of Veterans

Section 193:20

    193:20 Tuition. – Children, as described in RSA 193:19, enrolled at a New Hampshire public institution of higher education shall receive free tuition.

Source. 1943, 35:2. RSA 193:20. 1981, 409:3. 1987, 171:3. 2005, 97:2, eff. Aug. 7, 2005.
