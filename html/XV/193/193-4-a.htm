TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 193
PUPILS

School Attendance

Section 193:4-a

    193:4-a Tuition Liability; Dual Enrollment. – Any district shall be liable for the tuition of any child who as a resident of the district has been assigned to attend public school in another district in accordance with an approved dual enrollment plan; such payment shall be made as provided in RSA 193:4.

Source. 1971, 273:1. 1973, 501:4, eff. June 30, 1973.
