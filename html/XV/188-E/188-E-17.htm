TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 188-E
REGIONAL VOCATIONAL EDUCATION

Pre-Engineering Technology Curriculum and Pre-Engineering Technology Advisory Council

Section 188-E:17

    188-E:17 Duties. – The advisory council shall advise the department of education in the following areas relative to the implementation of the pre-engineering technology curriculum:
    I. Curriculum expansion and revision.
    II. Curriculum eligibility requirements.
    III. Curriculum quality.
    IV. Fund raising from private and other sources.
    V. Allocation of funds necessary for the curriculum.
    VI. Evaluation of performance of pre-engineering technology program sites.

Source. 2002, 271:1, eff. July 1, 2002. 2008, 244:5, eff. Aug. 23, 2008.
