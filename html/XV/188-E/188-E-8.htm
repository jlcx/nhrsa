TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 188-E
REGIONAL CAREER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION (CTE)

Section 188-E:8

    188-E:8 Transportation. – The department of education is authorized to reimburse from its regular budget the cost of transportation for (a) regional career and technical education students who attend regional career and technical education centers and for (b) at-risk students who attend alternative education programs located at a regional career and technical education center or other comprehensive high school. Transportation costs shall not exceed the rate adopted pursuant to RSA 541-A by the state board. The sending district shall be responsible for paying the transportation costs and shall be reimbursed from state funds.

Source. 1973, 567:1. 1981, 94:2. 1990, 28:7. 2007, 232:5, eff. Aug. 24, 2007. 2015, 252:9, eff. July 1, 2015.
