TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 188-B
ALLIED HEALTH PROFESSIONS

Section 188-B:8

    188-B:8 Specialized Advisory Boards. – The state board of education may appoint special advisory boards relative to any of the allied health professions to assist and advise it in all matters pertaining thereto.

Source. 1967, 410:1, eff. July 1, 1967.
