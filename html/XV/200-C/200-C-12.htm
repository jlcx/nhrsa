TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 200-C
VOCATIONAL REHABILITATION PROGRAMS

Workers' Personal Care Assistance Program

Section 200-C:12

    200-C:12 Appeals. – The contested case provisions of RSA 541-A:31-36 shall apply when a person is aggrieved by a decision of the administrator under this subdivision. Appeals shall be governed by the provisions of RSA 541.

Source. 1986, 138:1. 1994, 379:22, eff. June 9, 1994; 412:25, eff. Aug. 9, 1994.
