TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 188-F
COMMUNITY COLLEGE SYSTEM OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

New Hampshire Regional Community-Technical Institute

Section 188-F:33

    188-F:33 Institute Security Force. – The president of the New Hampshire regional community-technical institute is authorized to organize a security force for the purpose of patrolling the institute's buildings, roads, and grounds and providing for general security at the institute. The campus security force shall be under the immediate control of the president of the institute or designee.

Source. 1987, 53:1. 1995, 133:12; 182:28, eff. Aug. 4, 1995.
