TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 188-F
COMMUNITY COLLEGE SYSTEM OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

Police Standards and Training Council

Section 188-F:32

    188-F:32 Attendance by Persons Other Than Police Officers. – Persons who are not police officers as defined by RSA 188-F:23 may attend courses given by the police standards and training council under such conditions and for such tuition as may be established by the council. Certain courses may be closed to persons who are not police officers on recommendation of the director and approval by the council.

Source. 1985, 152:1. 1989, 303:1. 1995, 182:29. 1998, 272:19. 2007, 361:25, eff. July 17, 2007.
