TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 188-F
COMMUNITY COLLEGE SYSTEM OF NEW HAMPSHIRE

Police Standards and Training Council

Section 188-F:30

    188-F:30 Police Standards and Training Council Training Fund. – There is established in the state treasury a separate fund to be known as the police standards and training council training fund from which the state treasurer shall pay expenses incurred in the administration of this subdivision.

Source. 1985, 152:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
