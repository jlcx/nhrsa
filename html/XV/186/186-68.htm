TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 186
THE STATE SCHOOL ORGANIZATION

Military Recruiters Access

Section 186:68

    186:68 Duty to Provide Access. – Notwithstanding any other provision of law to the contrary, all public high schools and all institutions within the state university system, and all private high schools, colleges and universities which receive state funds shall offer the same on-campus recruiting opportunities to representatives of state or United States armed services as they offer to nonmilitary recruiters.

Source. 1983, 464:1, eff. Aug. 26, 1983.
