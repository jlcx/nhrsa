TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 186
THE STATE SCHOOL ORGANIZATION

Approval of Branches of Out-Of-State Institutions

Section 186:13-c

    186:13-c Penalty. – Any such out-of-state institution establishing a branch, branches, or extension courses in this state without such branch, branches or extension courses being accredited by said commission as provided in RSA 186:13-b, shall be listed by the state board of education as non-approved branch, branches, or extension courses and this action shall be published through the press and through correspondence with colleges and secondary schools in this region.

Source. 1965, 35:1. 1973, 533:3, eff. July 1, 1973.
