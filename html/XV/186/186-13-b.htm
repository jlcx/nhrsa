TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 186
THE STATE SCHOOL ORGANIZATION

Approval of Branches of Out-Of-State Institutions

Section 186:13-b

    186:13-b Branches or Extension Courses in This State. – Any out-of-state institution of higher learning planning to establish a branch, branches, or extension courses, in this state, shall apply to the the higher education commission for an evaluation of its plans. Plans for each such branch, branches, or extension courses shall thereupon be evaluated, and, if approved, the branch, branches, or extension courses shall thereupon be accredited for such period and under such regulations as the commission may determine. If a branch, or branches, or program of extension courses are disapproved at any time by the commission all operations and publicity of it shall cease without delay.

Source. 1965, 35:1. 1973, 533:2, eff. July 1, 1973. 2011, 224:129, eff. July 1, 2011.
