TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 200-E
INTERSTATE AGREEMENT ON QUALIFICATION OF EDUCATIONAL PERSONNEL

Section 200-E:2

    200-E:2 Designated State Official. – The "designated State official'' for this State shall be the commissioner of education. The commissioner shall enter into contracts pursuant to Article III of the Agreement only with the approval of the specific text thereof by the state board of education.

Source. 1969, 103:1, eff. June 24, 1969.
