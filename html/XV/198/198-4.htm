TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 198
SCHOOL MONEY

District Taxes

Section 198:4

    198:4 Estimates. – The school board of each district in its annual report shall state in detail the additional sums of money, if any, which will be required during the ensuing fiscal year for the support of the public schools, for the purchase of textbooks, scholars' supplies, flags and appurtenances, for the payment of the tuition of the pupils in the district in high schools and academies in accordance with law, and for the payment of all other statutory obligations of the district.

Source. RS 72:2. CS 76:2. GS 77:2. GL 85:2. PS 88:2. 1909, 52:1. 1915, 68:1. 1919, 106:21. 1921, 85, VI:4. PL 121:4. RL 140:4.
