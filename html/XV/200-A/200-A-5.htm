TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 200-A
THE NEW ENGLAND HIGHER EDUCATION COMPACT

Payment to Outside Schools

Section 200-A:5

    200-A:5 Certification to Department of Education. – The New England Board of Higher Education shall certify to the department of education, division of higher education on or before October 1 of the year preceding each legislative session the amounts needed to carry out the purposes of RSA 200-A:4 for the coming biennium. Upon such certification, the division shall include such amounts in the budget request for its division. The sums appropriated by the legislature in accordance with the provisions of this subdivision shall be a continuing appropriation and shall not lapse.

Source. 1959, 214:1. 1977, 600:22, I, eff. July 1, 1977. 2011, 224:135, eff. July 1, 2011.
