TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 190
COUNCIL FOR TEACHER EDUCATION

Section 190:3

    190:3 Duties. – The council for teacher education shall coordinate teacher education in the state in an advisory capacity through a continuing study and discussion of its problems and shall issue advisory reports to agencies and institutions, public and private, concerned with teacher education or its financing in this state.

Source. 1951, 143:3, eff. June 6, 1951.
