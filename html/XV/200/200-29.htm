TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 200
HEALTH AND SANITATION

School Health Services

Section 200:29

    200:29 School Nurse. – Each school board may appoint a school nurse to function in the school health program, and provide said nurse with proper facilities and equipment. A school nurse shall be a registered professional nurse currently licensed in New Hampshire.

Source. 1971, 499:1, eff. Sept. 4, 1971.
