TITLE XV
EDUCATION

CHAPTER 200-L
INTERSTATE AGREEMENT ON QUALIFICATION OF EDUCATIONAL PERSONNEL (NORTHEASTERN STATES)

Section 200-L:3

    200-L:3 Designated State Official. – The "designated state official'' as provided in Article 2 for this state shall be the commissioner of education.

Source. 1990, 42:1, eff. May 22, 1990.
