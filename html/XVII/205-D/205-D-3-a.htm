TITLE XVII
HOUSING AND REDEVELOPMENT

CHAPTER 205-D
MANUFACTURED HOUSING INSTALLATION STANDARDS

Section 205-D:3-a

    205-D:3-a Administrative Functions. – All administrative, clerical, and business processing functions of the board shall be transferred to the joint board of licensure and certification, established in RSA 310-A:1, on July 1, 2011.

Source. 2011, 224:101, eff. July 1, 2011.
