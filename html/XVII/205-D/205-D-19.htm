TITLE XVII
HOUSING AND REDEVELOPMENT

CHAPTER 205-D
MANUFACTURED HOUSING INSTALLATION STANDARDS

Section 205-D:19

    205-D:19 Federal Funds and Other Funding Sources. – The board may seek and receive funds from the federal government and other public or private sources to further activities under this chapter.

Source. 2004, 191:1, eff. July 1, 2004. 2011, 224:102, eff. July 1, 2011.
