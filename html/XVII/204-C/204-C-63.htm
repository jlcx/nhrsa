TITLE XVII
HOUSING AND REDEVELOPMENT

CHAPTER 204-C
HOUSING FINANCE AUTHORITY

Low and Moderate Income Housing Loan Program

Section 204-C:63


[RSA 204-C:63 effective until September 30, 2014; see also RSA 204-C:63 set out below.]
    204-C:63 Purpose. –
    The purpose of this subdivision is to assist low and moderate income persons and families to purchase housing.

Source. 1988, 240:10, eff. July 1, 1988.
