TITLE XVII
HOUSING AND REDEVELOPMENT

CHAPTER 205-A
REGULATION OF MANUFACTURED HOUSING PARKS

Board of Manufactured Housing

Section 205-A:29

    205-A:29 Meetings and Records. – The board shall hold meetings in Concord, New Hampshire or in any other location deemed appropriate by the board. The records of the board shall be maintained at the office of the board of manufactured housing.

Source. 1994, 368:1. 2002, 92:6, eff. May 3, 2002.
