TITLE XVII
HOUSING AND REDEVELOPMENT

CHAPTER 205-A
REGULATION OF MANUFACTURED HOUSING PARKS

Board of Manufactured Housing

Section 205-A:29-a

    205-A:29-a Administrative and Business Processing Functions. – All administrative, clerical, and business processing functions of the board shall be transferred to the joint board of licensure and certification, established in RSA 310-A:1, on July 1, 2011.

Source. 2011, 224:104, eff. July 1, 2011.
