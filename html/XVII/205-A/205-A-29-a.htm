TITLE XVII
HOUSING AND REDEVELOPMENT

CHAPTER 205-A
REGULATION OF MANUFACTURED HOUSING PARKS

Board of Manufactured Housing

Section 205-A:29-a

    205-A:29-a Administrative and Business Processing Functions. – All administrative, clerical, and business processing functions of the board shall be transferred to the office of professional licensure and certification established in RSA 310-A:1 through RSA 310-A:1-e.

Source. 2011, 224:104, eff. July 1, 2011. 2015, 276:48, eff. July 1, 2015.
