TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 547
JUDGES OF PROBATE AND THEIR JURISDICTION

Disqualifications and Vacancies

Section 547:18-a

    547:18-a Absence or Inability to Attend. – Whenever a judge of probate in any county shall be absent or unable to attend a session of the probate court, the administrative judge of the probate court shall call upon the judge of some other county, who shall act during such absence or inability. Such judge who is called upon shall receive per diem compensation determined by the supreme court upon recommendation by the judicial branch administrative council, plus reasonable expenses, which shall be paid by the state.

Source. 1967, 401:2. 1979, 125:1. 1983, 383:50. 1987, 384:8. 1992, 284:54, eff. Jan. 1, 1993.
