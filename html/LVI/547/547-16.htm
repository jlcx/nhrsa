TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 547
JUDGES OF PROBATE AND THEIR JURISDICTION

Disqualifications and Vacancies

Section 547:16

    547:16 Compensation of Judge Acting for Another. – Such judge shall be allowed the same per diem compensation as is generally allowed to masters by the superior court plus his reasonable expenses, which shall be paid by the state.

Source. RS 152:13. CS 161:13. 1864, 2882:2. GS 170:11. GL 189:11. PS 182:15. PL 293:16. RL 346:16. 1953, 106:1. RSA 547:16. 1963, 127:1. 1979, 125:2. 1983, 383:49. 1997, 100:8, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
