TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 547
JUDGES OF PROBATE AND THEIR JURISDICTION

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 547:31

    547:31 Stenographer. – The judge of probate may employ a stenographer who shall make a stenographic record of any contested proceeding.

Source. 1949, 76:1. RL 346:30.
