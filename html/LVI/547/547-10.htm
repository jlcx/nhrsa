TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 547
JUDGES OF PROBATE AND THEIR JURISDICTION

Jurisdiction

Section 547:10

    547:10 Guardianship Proceedings. – All proceedings in relation to the property or estate of a person under guardianship shall be had in the court of probate of the county in which the guardian was appointed.

Source. RS 152:9. CS 161:9. GS 170:8. GL 189:8. PS 182:10. PL 293:10. RL 346:10.
