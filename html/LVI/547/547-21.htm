TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 547
JUDGES OF PROBATE AND THEIR JURISDICTION

Place of Holding Court

Section 547:21

    547:21 Adjournment. – He may adjourn his court for the transaction of any business to any convenient time and place.

Source. RS 151:16. CS 161:16. GS 170:15. GL 189:15. PS 182:20. PL 293:21. RL 346:21.
