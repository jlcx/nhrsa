TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 547
JUDGES OF PROBATE AND THEIR JURISDICTION

Place of Holding Court

Section 547:20

    547:20 At Dwelling-house, etc. – The judge may attend at the dwelling-house or in the neighborhood of the residence of an administrator, guardian, trustee or other person who shall be unable, by reason of sickness or other sufficient cause, to attend the court of probate at the time and place appointed by law, whenever the personal attendance of such person is required.

Source. RS 152:14. CS 161:14. GS 170:13. GL 189:13. PS 182:19. PL 293:20. RL 346:20.
