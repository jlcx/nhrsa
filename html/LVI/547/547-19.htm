TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 547
JUDGES OF PROBATE AND THEIR JURISDICTION

Disqualifications and Vacancies

Section 547:19

    547:19 Powers of Judge Acting for Another. – The judge so holding court is authorized to do any business that the judge for the county could lawfully do.

Source. 1883, 71:2. PS 182:18. 1917, 107:2. PL 293:19. RL 346:19. RSA 547:19. 1997, 100:9, eff. Jan. 1, 1998.
