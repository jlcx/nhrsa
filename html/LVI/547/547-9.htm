TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 547
JUDGES OF PROBATE AND THEIR JURISDICTION

Jurisdiction

Section 547:9

    547:9 Settlement of Estates. – All proceedings in relation to the settlement of the estate of a person deceased shall be had in the probate court of the county in which his will was proved or administration on his estate was granted.

Source. RS 152:8. CS 161:8. GS 170:7. GL 189:7. PS 182:9. PL 293:9. RL 346:9.
