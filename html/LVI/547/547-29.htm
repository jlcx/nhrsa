TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 547
JUDGES OF PROBATE AND THEIR JURISDICTION

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 547:29

    547:29 Service of Process. – Sheriffs, deputy sheriffs and constables are required to serve any legal process to them directed by a judge of probate.

Source. RS 152:18. CS 161:18. GS 170:17. GL 189:18. PS 182:26. PL 293:28. RL 346:28.
