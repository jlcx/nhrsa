TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 563-B
UNIFORM DISCLAIMER OF PROPERTY INTERESTS ACT

Section 563-B:7

    563-B:7 Application. – An interest in property that exists on January 1, 1997, as to which, if a present interest, the time for delivering a disclaimer under this chapter, has not expired or, if a future interest, the interest has not become indefeasibly vested or the taker finally ascertained, may be disclaimed within 9 months after January 1, 1997.

Source. 1996, 265:12, eff. Jan. 1, 1997.
