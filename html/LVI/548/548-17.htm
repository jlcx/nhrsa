TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 548
REGISTERS OF PROBATE

Compensation

Section 548:17

    548:17 Salaries. – The annual salary of the registers of probate shall be $100 per year.
