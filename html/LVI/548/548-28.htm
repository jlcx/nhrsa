TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 548
REGISTERS OF PROBATE

Prohibited Acts, etc.

Section 548:28

    548:28 Penalty. – Any register who violates any of the provisions of this chapter shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. GS 171:13. GL 190:13. PS 183:18. PL 294:17. RL 347:18. RSA 548:28. 1973, 529:126, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
