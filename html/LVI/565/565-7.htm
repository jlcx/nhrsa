TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 565
BONDS TO THE JUDGE AND SUITS THEREON

Section 565:7

    565:7 Indorsement of Writ. – The name and residence of every person at whose request the order is made and suit instituted shall be indorsed on the writ before the service thereof, with a brief statement of his claim.

Source. RS 169:5. CS 179:5. GS 187:5. GL 206:5. PS 199:5. PL 310:7. RL 364:7.
