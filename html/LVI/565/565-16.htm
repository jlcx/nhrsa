TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 565
BONDS TO THE JUDGE AND SUITS THEREON

Section 565:16

    565:16 Obligors Not Sued. – No suit or judgment on such bond shall operate as an abatement or bar to any suit thereon against any obligor, against whom no suit has previously been commenced or judgment rendered.

Source. RS 169:14. CS 179:14. GS 187:14. GL 206:14. PS 199:14. PL 310:16. RL 364:16.
