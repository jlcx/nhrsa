TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 565
BONDS TO THE JUDGE AND SUITS THEREON

Section 565:2

    565:2 Corporate Surety. – Any corporation established by the laws of this state, or authorized to transact business therein, and authorized thereto by its charter, may be accepted by the judge as sufficient surety if he is satisfied with the ability of the corporation as such surety.

Source. RS 169:1. CS 179:1. GS 187:1. 1874, 32:1. GL 206:1. PS 199:1. 1901, 109:1. PL 310:2. RL 364:2.
