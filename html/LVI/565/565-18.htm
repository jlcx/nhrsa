TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 565
BONDS TO THE JUDGE AND SUITS THEREON

Section 565:18

    565:18 Costs. – Execution may be awarded, for costs adjudged to the defendants in any such suit, against the parties for whose benefit the suit was brought or maintained; or the defendants may have their remedy on the bonds taken in the probate office.

Source. RS 169:16. CS 179:16. GS 187:16. GL 206:16. PS 199:16. PL 310:18. RL 364:18.
