TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 565
BONDS TO THE JUDGE AND SUITS THEREON

Section 565:9

    565:9 Forfeiture. – When it shall appear, upon confession, verdict, demurrer or in any other way, that the penalty of the bond is forfeited judgment shall be rendered against the defendant for such penalty; and such judgment shall be security for all parties interested.

Source. RS 169:7. CS 179:7. GS 187:7. GL 206:7. PS 199:7. PL 310:9. RL 364:9.
