TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 551
WILLS

Section 551:11

    551:11 Share of Unnamed Child. – If the property not devised nor bequeathed shall be insufficient to satisfy the just share of such child, after allowing advancements received by him, the same shall be made up in just proportion from the property devised or bequeathed to others.

Source. RS 156:10. CS 165:10. GS 174:11. GL 193:11. PS 186:11. PL 297:11. RL 350:11.
