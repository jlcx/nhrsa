TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 554
INVENTORY, ACCOUNTS, AND DEALING WITH ASSETS

Section 554:3

    554:3 Appraiser. – The judge may appoint one or more appraisers, if in the opinion of the judge the nature of the property or the size of the estate makes it advisable to do so.

Source. 1911, 48:1. PL 300:3. RL 353:3. 2005, 252:5, eff. Jan. 1, 2006.
