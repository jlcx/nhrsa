TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 554
INVENTORY, ACCOUNTS, AND DEALING WITH ASSETS

Section 554:11

    554:11 Debts Due Estate. – All debts due to the estate, which by due diligence might have been collected, shall be accounted for in money.

Source. RS 159:7. CS 168:7. GS 177:8. GL 196:8. PS 189:9. PL 300:11. RL 353:11.
