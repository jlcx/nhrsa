TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 554
INVENTORY, ACCOUNTS, AND DEALING WITH ASSETS

Section 554:22

    554:22 Care of Lots. – Executors, administrators and trustees may pay, upon the order of the judge, to a cemetery corporation or to the city or town in which the decedent has a burial place, a reasonable sum of money for the perpetual care of the lot, in which their decedent is buried, and the monuments thereon.

Source. 1911, 32:1. 1917, 65:1. PL 300:22. RL 353:22.
