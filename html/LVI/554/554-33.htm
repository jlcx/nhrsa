TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 554
INVENTORY, ACCOUNTS, AND DEALING WITH ASSETS

Section 554:33

    554:33 Issuance of License. – No such license shall issue later than two years after the date of the appointment of the administrator or executor, and no license shall be available to sustain a mortgage made by the administrator or executor, under said license unless made within ninety days from the granting thereof.

Source. 1957, 167:1, eff. July 1, 1957.
