TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 554
INVENTORY, ACCOUNTS, AND DEALING WITH ASSETS

Section 554:21

    554:21 Monuments. – Administrators of estates actually solvent may erect suitable monuments at the graves of the testators or intestates, and the reasonable expense thereof shall be allowed them on settlement of their accounts.

Source. RS 159:16. CS 168:16. GS 177:17. GL 196:17. PS 189:19. PL 300:21. RL 353:21.
