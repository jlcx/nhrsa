TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 554
INVENTORY, ACCOUNTS, AND DEALING WITH ASSETS

Section 554:6

    554:6 Accountability for Assets. – All assets, though not inventoried, shall be accounted for, and the administrator charged therewith in the account of administration.

Source. RS 159:4. CS 168:4. GS 177:4. GL 196:4. PS 189:4. PL 300:6. RL 353:6.
