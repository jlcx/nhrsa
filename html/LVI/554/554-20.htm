TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 554
INVENTORY, ACCOUNTS, AND DEALING WITH ASSETS

Section 554:20

    554:20 Expenses of Assignment, etc. – The expenses of assigning the homestead right, of the division and assignment of the real estate, and of appointing guardians of minors and others incapacitated to take care of their interest, whether heirs or legatees, shall be chargeable as expenses of administration.

Source. RS 159:15. CS 168:15. GS 177:16. GL 196:16. PS 189:18. PL 300:20. RL 353:20. RSA 554:20. 1971, 179:15, eff. date Aug. 10, 1971.
