TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 554
INVENTORY, ACCOUNTS, AND DEALING WITH ASSETS

Section 554:18

    554:18 Real Estate Purchased, etc. – Real estate purchased by, or set off to, an administrator to secure or satisfy a debt due to the estate shall vest in the heirs in the same manner as if the deceased had died seized thereof, and may be sold for the payment of debts, or divided as part of the estate.

Source. RS 159:13. CS 168:13. GS 177:14. GL 196:14. PS 189:16. PL 300:18. RL 353:18.
