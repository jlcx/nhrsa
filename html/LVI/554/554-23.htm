TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 554
INVENTORY, ACCOUNTS, AND DEALING WITH ASSETS

Section 554:23

    554:23 Amount Allowed. – The judge shall determine, after notice to all parties in interest, to whom the same shall be paid and the amount thereof, if any, and such sum shall be allowed in the accounts of such executor, administrator or trustee.

Source. 1911, 32:1. 1917, 65:1. PL 300:23. RL 353:23.
