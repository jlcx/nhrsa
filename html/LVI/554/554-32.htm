TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 554
INVENTORY, ACCOUNTS, AND DEALING WITH ASSETS

Section 554:32

    554:32 License. – The license shall include the homestead rights and other rights therein, upon consent in writing by the widow or widower of the decedent.

Source. 1957, 167:1. 1971, 179:16, eff. date Aug. 10, 1971.
