TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 552
PROBATE OF WILLS

Section 552:10

    552:10 Failure to Present. – If a will filed in the probate office shall not be presented for probate by the executor, or by a person interested therein, the judge, on application of the widow or an heir of the deceased, and due notice, may approve and allow or disallow it; and the decree so made shall be of the same force as if made on application of the executor, or of a person interested in the will.

Source. RS 157:10. CS 166:10. GS 175:10. GL 194:10. PS 187:10. PL 298:10. RL 351:10.
