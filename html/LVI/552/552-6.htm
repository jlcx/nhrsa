TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 552
PROBATE OF WILLS

Section 552:6

    552:6 Proof, Common Form. – If the probate of a will is not contested the judge may allow and approve it in common form, upon the testimony of one of the subscribing witnesses, though the others are living and within process of the court.

Source. RS 157:6. CS 166:6. GS 175:6. GL 194:6. PS 187:6. PL 298:6. RL 351:6. RS 552:6. 1983, 255:4. 1985, 200:4, eff. Jan. 1, 1986.
