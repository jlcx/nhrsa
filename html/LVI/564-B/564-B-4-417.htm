TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 564-B
UNIFORM TRUST CODE

ARTICLE 4
CREATION, VALIDITY, MODIFICATION, AND TERMINATION OF TRUST

Section 564-B:4-417

    564-B:4-417 Combination and Division of Trusts. – After notice to the qualified beneficiaries, a trustee may combine 2 or more trusts into a single trust or divide a trust into 2 or more separate trusts, if the result does not impair rights of any beneficiary or adversely affect achievement of the purposes of the trust.

Source. 2004, 130:1, eff. Oct. 1, 2004.
