TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 564-B
UNIFORM TRUST CODE

ARTICLE 9
UNIFORM PRUDENT INVESTOR ACT

Section 564-B:9-905

    564-B:9-905 Reviewing Compliance. – Compliance with the prudent investor rule is determined in light of the facts and circumstances existing at the time of a trustee's decision or action and not by hindsight.

Source. 2004, 130:1, eff. Oct. 1, 2004.
