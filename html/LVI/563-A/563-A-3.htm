TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 563-A
UNIFORM TESTAMENTARY ADDITIONS TO TRUSTS ACT

Section 563-A:3

    563-A:3 Uniformity of Application and Construction. – This chapter shall be applied and construed to effectuate its general purpose to make uniform the law with respect to the subject of this chapter among states enacting it.

Source. 1996, 200:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1997.
