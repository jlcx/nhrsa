TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 564
TRUSTEES OF ESTATES

Section 564:4

    564:4 Banks, etc. – Any trust company or national bank, being authorized under RSA 390, may be appointed trustee. Immediately upon the qualification of such a trustee it shall be the duty of the clerk to notify the bank commissioner of such appointment.

Source. 1917, 193:1. PL 309:4. 1933, 67:9. RL 363:4. 2011, 88:20, eff. July 1, 2011.
