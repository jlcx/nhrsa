TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 564
TRUSTEES OF ESTATES

Section 564:4


[RSA 564:4 effective until October 1, 2015; see also RSA 564:4 set out below.]
    564:4 Banks, etc. –
    Any trust company or national bank, being authorized under RSA 390, may be appointed trustee. Immediately upon the qualification of such a trustee it shall be the duty of the clerk to notify the bank commissioner of such appointment.

[RSA 564:4 effective October 1, 2015; see also RSA 564:4 set out above.]


    564:4 Banks, etc. --
    Any bank as defined in RSA 383-A:2-201(a)(3) that holds trust powers, may be appointed trustee. Immediately upon the qualification of such a trustee it shall be the duty of the clerk to notify the bank commissioner of such appointment.

Source. 1917, 193:1. PL 309:4. 1933, 67:9. RL 363:4. 2011, 88:20, eff. July 1, 2011. 2015, 272:49, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
