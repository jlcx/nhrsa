TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 564
TRUSTEES OF ESTATES

Section 564:25


[RSA 564:25 effective until October 1, 2015; see also RSA 564:25 set out below.]
    564:25 Application of the Uniform Trust Code. –
    The provisions of RSA 564-B, the Uniform Trust Code, shall apply to all trusts under a will governed by this chapter, except to the extent that any provisions of the Uniform Trust Code conflict with the express provisions of this chapter, in which case the provisions of this chapter shall control.

[RSA 564:25 effective October 1, 2015; see also RSA 564:25 set out above.]


    564:25 Application of the New Hampshire Trust Code. --
    The provisions of RSA 564-B, the New Hampshire Trust Code, shall apply to all trusts under a will governed by this chapter, except to the extent that any provisions of the New Hampshire Trust Code conflict with the express provisions of this chapter, in which case the provisions of this chapter shall control.

Source. 2011, 243:6, eff. Sept. 11, 2011. 2015, 272:70, eff. Oct. 1, 2015.
