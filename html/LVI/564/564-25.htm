TITLE LVI
PROBATE COURTS AND DECEDENTS' ESTATES

CHAPTER 564
TRUSTEES OF ESTATES

Section 564:25

    564:25 Application of the Uniform Trust Code. – The provisions of RSA 564-B, the Uniform Trust Code, shall apply to all trusts under a will governed by this chapter, except to the extent that any provisions of the Uniform Trust Code conflict with the express provisions of this chapter, in which case the provisions of this chapter shall control.

Source. 2011, 243:6, eff. Sept. 11, 2011.
