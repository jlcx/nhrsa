TITLE XLVIII
CONVEYANCES AND MORTGAGES OF REALTY

CHAPTER 479
MORTGAGES OF REALTY

Performance of Conditions and Redemption

Section 479:8


[RSA 479:8 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA 479:8 set out below.]
    479:8 Penalty. –
    Any mortgagee violating the provisions of RSA 479:7, or any mortgagee who after the satisfaction of his mortgage refuses to give a sufficient discharge of such mortgage, shall be guilty of a violation.

[RSA 479:8 effective January 1, 2016; see also RSA 479:8 set out above.]


    479:8 Penalty. --
    Any mortgagee violating the provisions of RSA 479:7, or any mortgagee who after the satisfaction of the mortgage refuses to give a sufficient discharge of such mortgage, shall be liable for damages to the mortgagor at the rate of $200 for each week after the expiration of the 60 days up to a maximum of $2,500 or in an amount equal to the loss sustained as a result of the failure of the mortgagee to execute and deliver a release, whichever is greater, plus costs and reasonable attorney's fees.

Source. 1905, 37:2. PL 215:7. RL 261:7. RSA 479:8. 1973, 531:134. 1989, 171:2, eff. Jan. 1, 1990. 2015, 224:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
