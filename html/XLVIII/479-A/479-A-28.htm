TITLE XLVIII
CONVEYANCES AND MORTGAGES OF REALTY

CHAPTER 479-A
UNIT OWNERSHIP OF REAL PROPERTY

Section 479-A:28

    479-A:28 Severability. – If any provision of this chapter or any section, sentence, clause, phrase or word, or the application thereof in any circumstance, is held invalid, the validity of the remainder of the chapter and of the application of any such provision, section, sentence, clause, phrase or word in any other circumstances shall not be affected thereby.

Source. 1965, 155:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1965.
