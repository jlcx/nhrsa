TITLE XLVIII
CONVEYANCES AND MORTGAGES OF REALTY

CHAPTER 479-A
UNIT OWNERSHIP OF REAL PROPERTY

Section 479-A:19

    479-A:19 Books of Receipts and Expenditures; Availability for Examination. – The manager or board of directors, as the case may be, shall keep detailed, accurate records in chronological order of the receipts and expenditures affecting the common areas and facilities, specifying and itemizing the maintenance and repair expenses of the common areas and facilities and any other expenses incurred. Such records and the vouchers authorizing the payments shall be available for examination by the unit owners at convenient hours of weekdays.

Source. 1965, 155:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1965.
