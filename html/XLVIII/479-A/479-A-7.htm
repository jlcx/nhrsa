TITLE XLVIII
CONVEYANCES AND MORTGAGES OF REALTY

CHAPTER 479-A
UNIT OWNERSHIP OF REAL PROPERTY

Section 479-A:7

    479-A:7 Certain Work Prohibited. – No unit owner shall do any work which would jeopardize the soundness or safety of the property, reduce the value thereof or impair any easement or hereditament without in every such case the unanimous consent of all the other unit owners being first obtained.

Source. 1965, 155:1, eff. Aug. 14, 1965.
