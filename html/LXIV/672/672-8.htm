TITLE LXIV
PLANNING AND ZONING

CHAPTER 672
GENERAL PROVISIONS

Words and Phrases Defined

Section 672:8

    672:8 Local Legislative Body. – "Local legislative body'' means one of the following basic forms of government utilized by a municipality:
    I. Council, whether city or town;
    II. Mayor--council;
    III. Mayor--board of aldermen;
    IV. Village district or precinct;
    V. Town meeting; or
    VI. County convention.

Source. 1983, 447:1. 1985, 103:18. 1989, 266:8, eff. July 1, 1989.
