TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 79-A
CURRENT USE TAXATION

Miscellaneous

Section 79-A:23

    79-A:23 Enforcement. – All taxes levied pursuant to RSA 79-A:7 which shall not be paid when due shall be collected in the same manner as provided in RSA 80:1-42-a.

Source. 1973, 372:1. 1996, 176:5, eff. Aug. 2, 1996.
