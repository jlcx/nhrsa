TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 83-E
ELECTRICITY CONSUMPTION TAX

Section 83-E:3

    83-E:3 Collection of Tax. – Every provider shall collect and monthly remit the tax to the state as provided in RSA 83-E:5. Providers whose average monthly tax collections do not exceed $100 may apply to the commissioner to remit taxes annually. Approval of such requests shall be at the discretion of the commissioner.

Source. 1997, 347:4.
