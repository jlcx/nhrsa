TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 72
PERSONS AND PROPERTY LIABLE TO TAXATION

Wind-Powered Energy Systems Exemption

Section 72:65

    72:65 Definition of Wind-Powered Energy Systems. – In this subdivision "wind-powered energy system'' means any wind-powered devices which supplement or replace electrical power supplied to households or businesses at the immediate site.

Source. 1977, 185:1, eff. Aug. 13, 1977.
