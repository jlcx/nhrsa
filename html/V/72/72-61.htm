TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 72
PERSONS AND PROPERTY LIABLE TO TAXATION

Solar Energy Systems Exemption

Section 72:61

    72:61 Definition of Solar Energy Systems. – In this subdivision "solar energy system'' means a system which utilizes solar energy to heat or cool the interior of a building or to heat water for use in a building and which includes one or more collectors and a storage container. "Solar energy system'' also means a system which provides electricity for a building by the use of photovoltaic panels.

Source. 1975, 391:1. 1993, 93:1, eff. April 1, 1993.
