TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 73
PERSONS AND PROPERTY, WHERE AND TO WHOM TAXED

Section 73:10

    73:10 Real Estate. – Real and personal property shall be taxed to the person claiming the same, or to the person who is in the possession and actual occupancy thereof, if such person will consent to be taxed for the same; but such real estate shall be taxed in the town in which it is situate. Any assessments report issued by the commissioner pursuant to RSA 21-J:11-a shall not affect the obligation of the taxpayer to pay property taxes otherwise lawfully assessed.

Source. RS 40:7. CS 42:9. GS 50:11. GL 54:11. PS 56:14. PL 61:10. RL 74:10. 2001, 158:58. 2003, 307:10, eff. July 1, 2003.
