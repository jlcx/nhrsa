TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 79-H
TAXATION OF CERTAIN CHARTERED PUBLIC SCHOOL FACILITIES

Section 79-H:7

    79-H:7 Enforcement. – All taxes levied pursuant to assessments under this chapter which are not paid when due shall be collected in the same manner as provided in RSA 80.

Source. 2015, 266:1, eff. July 20, 2015.
