TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 79-H
TAXATION OF CERTAIN CHARTERED PUBLIC SCHOOL FACILITIES

Section 79-H:1

    79-H:1 Declaration of Public Interest. – The general court hereby finds it to be in the public interest to authorize municipalities to allow a chartered public school to be able to rent or lease its building or facilities from a property owner which is not exempt from property taxes, and not have the property taxes attributable to the chartered public school facilities be taxed to the owner at the full market value of the facilities.

Source. 2015, 266:1, eff. July 20, 2015.
