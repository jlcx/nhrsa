TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 71-B
BOARD OF TAX AND LAND APPEALS

Section 71-B:14

    71-B:14 Staff. – The board shall have upon its staff 2 review appraisers who shall be classified state employees and who shall be competent to review the value of property for tax and eminent domain purposes. In addition, the board shall have such clerical and technical staff as may be necessary within the limits of appropriation made therefor.

Source. 1973, 544:2. 2003, 63:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2004.
