TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 79-C
DISCRETIONARY EASEMENTS

Section 79-C:14

    79-C:14 Rulemaking. – The commissioner of the department of revenue administration shall adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, relative to:
    I. The application procedures under RSA 79-C:4.
    II. The payment and collection procedures under RSA 79-C:9.

Source. 1996, 176:1, eff. Aug. 2, 1996.
