TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 85
EXTENTS

Issuance, Levy, Sale, Etc.

Section 85:12

    85:12 Fees. – Every extent may include the legal fees and charges incurred upon any former extent issued for the collection of the same tax.

Source. RS 48:12. CS 51:12. GS 59:12. GL 66:12. PS 66:12. PL 71:12. RL 86:12.
