TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 85
EXTENTS

Issuance, Levy, Sale, Etc.

Section 85:8

    85:8 Sale of Personal Property. – Personal property seized upon any extent shall be sold in the same manner as similar property is required to be sold on execution.

Source. RS 48:8. CS 51:8. GS 59:8. GL 66:8. PS 66:8. PL 71:8. RL 86:8.
