TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 78
TOBACCO TAX

Section 78:7

    78:7 Tax Imposed. – A tax upon the retail consumer is hereby imposed at the rate of $1.78 for each package containing 20 cigarettes or at a rate proportional to such rate for packages containing more or less than 20 cigarettes, on all cigarettes sold at retail in this state. The payment of the tax shall be evidenced by affixing stamps to the smallest packages containing the cigarettes in which such products usually are sold at retail. The word "package'' as used in this section shall not include individual cigarettes. No tax is imposed on any transactions, the taxation of which by this state is prohibited by the Constitution of the United States.
