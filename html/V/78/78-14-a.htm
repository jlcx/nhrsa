TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 78
TOBACCO TAX

Section 78:14-a

    78:14-a Possession of Tobacco Products of Foreign States. – Licensed wholesalers, sub-jobbers and vending machine operators may possess tobacco products bearing a foreign state's tax stamp or indicia with an intent to sell such products only if the licensee is currently and legitimately doing business in that state.

Source. 1981, 210:19, eff. July 1, 1981.
