TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 72-B
EXCAVATION TAX

Section 72-B:16

    72-B:16 Administration and Enforcement Fee. – There shall be an administration and enforcement fee of $100 for each original notice of intent to excavate filed with the department. Such fee shall accompany the original intent to excavate and shall be deposited into the general fund.

Source. 1997, 219:2, eff. Jan. 1, 1998. 2015, 276:40, eff. July 1, 2015.
