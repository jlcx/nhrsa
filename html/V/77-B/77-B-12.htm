TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 77-B
COMMUTERS INCOME TAX

Withholding of Tax

Section 77-B:12

    77-B:12 Employer's Liability. – Every employer required to deduct and withhold a tax under RSA 77-B:10 is hereby made liable for such tax and is hereby indemnified against the claims and demands of any individual, corporation or partnership for the amount of any payments made in accordance with the provisions of this chapter.

Source. 1970, 20:1, eff. July 1, 1970.
