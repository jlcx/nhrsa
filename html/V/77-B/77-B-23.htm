TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 77-B
COMMUTERS INCOME TAX

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 77-B:23

    77-B:23 Penalty. – Whoever violates any of the provisions of RSA 77-B shall be guilty of a misdemeanor if a natural person, or guilty of a felony if any other person.

Source. 1970, 20:1. 1973, 528:23, eff. Oct. 31, 1973 at 11:59 p.m.
