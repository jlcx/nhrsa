TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 77-B
COMMUTERS INCOME TAX

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 77-B:27

    77-B:27 Preference. – The taxes and interest imposed by this chapter have preference in any distribution of the assets of the taxpayer, whether in insolvency or otherwise.

Source. 1970, 20:1, eff. July 1, 1970.
