TITLE V
TAXATION

CHAPTER 76
APPORTIONMENT, ASSESSMENT AND ABATEMENT OF TAXES

Assessment

Section 76:2


[RSA 76:2 effective until April 1, 2013; see also RSA 76:2 set out below.]
    76:2 Property Tax Year. –
    The property tax year shall be April 1 to March 31 and all property taxes shall be assessed on the inventory taken in April of that year.

Source. RS 43:1. 1851, 1115. CS 45:1. 1871, 16:1. GS 53:1. GL 57:1. PS 59:1. 1913, 82:7. PL 64:1. RL 77:1. RSA 76:2. 1969, 23:9. 1981, 178:1, eff. June 2, 1981.
