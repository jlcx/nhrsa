TITLE XLI
LIENS

CHAPTER 454-B
UNIFORM FEDERAL LIEN REGISTRATION ACT

Section 454-B:5-a

    454-B:5-a Disposition of Funds. – The expenses of administering, staffing, maintaining, and improving services provided by the secretary of state under this chapter shall be paid out of a fund to be known as the federal lien registration fund herein established in the office of the state treasurer.

Source. 2005, 219:6, eff. Jan. 1, 2006.
