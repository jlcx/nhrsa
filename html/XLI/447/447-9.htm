TITLE XLI
LIENS

CHAPTER 447
LIENS FOR LABOR AND MATERIALS

Section 447:9

    447:9 Duration. – The lien created by RSA 447:2-7, inclusive, shall continue for 120 days after the services are performed, or the materials, supplies or other things are furnished, unless payment therefor is previously made, and shall take precedence of all prior claims except liens on account of taxes.

Source. 1861, 2500. 1866, 4245:1. GS 125:12, 14. 1871, 1:1, 2, 3. 1875, 20:1. 1878, 36:1. GL 139:12. 1879, 57:25. PS 141:16. PL 217:19. RL 264:19. 1949, 184:2. RSA 447:9. 1991, 270:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1992.
