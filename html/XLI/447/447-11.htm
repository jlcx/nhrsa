TITLE XLI
LIENS

CHAPTER 447
LIENS FOR LABOR AND MATERIALS

Section 447:11

    447:11 Precedence. – Such attachment shall have precedence over all lien claims for labor, professional design services, materials, or other things done or furnished after the attachment was made, except the same were done or furnished in the performance of a contract existing when the attachment was made, or were necessary for the preservation of the property attached.

Source. PL 217:21. RL 264:21. 2012, 158:3, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
