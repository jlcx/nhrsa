TITLE XLI
LIENS

CHAPTER 447
LIENS FOR LABOR AND MATERIALS

Section 447:1

    447:1 Vessels. – If a person shall perform labor, provide professional design services as defined in RSA 447:2, or furnish materials toward building, repairing, fitting or furnishing a vessel within this state, payment for which is due, he or she shall have a lien therefor on the vessel for the space of 4 days after it is completed.

Source. 1851, 1116. CS 139:1. GS 125:9. GL 139:9. PS 141:9. PL 217:11. RL 264:11. 2012, 158:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2013.
