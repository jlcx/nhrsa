TITLE XLI
LIENS

CHAPTER 447
LIENS FOR LABOR AND MATERIALS

Section 447:10

    447:10 How Secured. – Any such lien may be secured by attachment of the property upon which it exists at any time while the lien continues, the writ and return thereon distinctly expressing that purpose.

Source. 1861, 2500. 1866, 4245:1. GS 125:10, 12, 13, 14, 15. GL 139:10, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16, 17, 19. PS 141:17. PL 217:20. RL 264:20.
