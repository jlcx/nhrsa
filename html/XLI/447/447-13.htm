TITLE XLI
LIENS

CHAPTER 447
LIENS FOR LABOR AND MATERIALS

Section 447:13

    447:13 Boomage. – If logs are attached, the officer making the attachment may pay the boomage thereon, if any, and return the amount so paid on the writ, and it shall be included in the costs recovered.

Source. 1861, 2500. 1866, 4245:1. GS 125:15. GL 139:19. PS 141:17. PL 217:23. RL 264:23.
