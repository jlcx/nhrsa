TITLE XLI
LIENS

CHAPTER 448
LIENS FOR BOARD, LODGING; AGISTMENT, SERVICE OF STALLION

Section 448:3

    448:3 Service Fee. – Colts foaled in this state shall be subject to a lien to secure the payment of the service fee for the stallion, which shall continue in force until the colt is 8 months old, and may be enforced by an attachment of such colt at any time after it is 4 months old; said lien shall take precedence of any other claim.

Source. 1905, 33:1. PL 217:3. RL 264:3.
