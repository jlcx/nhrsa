TITLE XLV
ANIMALS

CHAPTER 466
DOGS AND CATS

Guard Dogs in Commercial Establishments

Section 466:48

    466:48 Registration. – In addition to the dog license required by RSA 466:4, no guard dog shall be used in any city or town without prior approval of the local law enforcement authority and appropriate registration with that authority.

Source. 1985, 29:2, eff. June 8, 1985.
