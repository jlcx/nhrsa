TITLE XLV
ANIMALS

CHAPTER 466
DOGS AND CATS

Guard Dogs in Commercial Establishments

Section 466:54

    466:54 Penalty. – Any person, firm, partnership or corporation who violates any provision of this subdivision shall be guilty of a violation for the first offense and guilty of a misdemeanor for subsequent offenses.

Source. 1985, 29:2, eff. June 8, 1985.
