TITLE XLV
ANIMALS

CHAPTER 466
DOGS AND CATS

Penalties, etc.

Section 466:38

    466:38 How Recoverable. – All fines and penalties provided in this chapter relating to dogs may be recovered on complaint before a district court or municipal court in the town or county where the offense is committed.

Source. 1891, 60:24. PL 150:40. RL 180:40. RSA 466:38. 1957, 244:19, eff. Sept. 23, 1958.
