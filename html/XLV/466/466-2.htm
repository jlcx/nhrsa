TITLE XLV
ANIMALS

CHAPTER 466
DOGS AND CATS

Licensing of Dogs

Section 466:2

    466:2 Part of Year. – An owner of a dog may at any time have it licensed until the ensuing May 1; and a person becoming the owner or keeper of a dog not duly licensed after May 1 shall cause it to be registered, numbered, described and licensed as provided in RSA 466:1.

Source. 1891, 60:2. PL 150:7. RL 180:7.
