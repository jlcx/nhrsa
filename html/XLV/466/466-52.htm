TITLE XLV
ANIMALS

CHAPTER 466
DOGS AND CATS

Guard Dogs in Commercial Establishments

Section 466:52

    466:52 Hearing. – If the custodian of a registered guard dog has had the dog's registration voided pursuant to RSA 466:51, the custodian may request in writing a hearing before the city council or board of selectmen, as appropriate, and said hearing shall be conducted within 30 days of receipt of the request for the hearing. The decision of either the city council or board of selectmen shall be final; provided, however, the custodian may appeal to the appropriate superior court within 30 days for a trial de novo.

Source. 1985, 29:2, eff. June 8, 1985.
