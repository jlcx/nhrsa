TITLE XLV
ANIMALS

CHAPTER 466
DOGS AND CATS

Guard Dogs in Commercial Establishments

Section 466:49

    466:49 Registration Fee. – The annual registration fee for a guard dog shall be $10, paid to the local law enforcement authority for deposit in the general fund of the city or town. Each registration shall expire one year from the date of issue.

Source. 1985, 29:2, eff. June 8, 1985.
