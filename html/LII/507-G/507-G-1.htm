TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 507-G
FRIVOLOUS ACTIONS AGAINST TEACHERS AND OTHER EDUCATION EMPLOYEES

Section 507-G:1

    507-G:1 Title. – This chapter may be cited as the Penalties for Frivolous Actions Against Teachers and Other Education Employees Act.

Source. 2002, 149:1, eff. July 14, 2002.
