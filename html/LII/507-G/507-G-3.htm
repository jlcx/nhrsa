TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 507-G
FRIVOLOUS ACTIONS AGAINST TEACHERS AND OTHER EDUCATION EMPLOYEES

Section 507-G:3

    507-G:3 Definitions. – In this chapter, "employee'' means any individual elected or appointed to an educational entity, any individual who is an employee of such an entity, and any employee of a company under contract to a school or school district who is directly engaged in student-related services.

Source. 2002, 149:1, eff. July 14, 2002.
