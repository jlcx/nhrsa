TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 509
WRITS AND THEIR INDORSEMENT

Section 509:5

    509:5 New Forms. – In cases where no form of process is prescribed, the process shall be made conformable to the forms prescribed, so far as the nature of the case will admit.

Source. RS 182:7. CS 193:7. GS 203:5. GL 222:5. PS 218:5. PL 330:5. RL 386:5.
