TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 509
WRITS AND THEIR INDORSEMENT

Section 509:8

    509:8 Indorsement. – Original writs, before service thereof, shall be indorsed by the plaintiff or by his agent or attorney if the plaintiff is an inhabitant of the state, otherwise by some responsible person who is an inhabitant.

Source. RS 182:17. CS 193:17. GS 203:8. GL 222:8. PS 218:8. PL 330:8. RL 386:8.
