TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 509
WRITS AND THEIR INDORSEMENT

Section 509:10

    509:10 Time for Enforcement. – A scire facias pursuant to RSA 509:9 shall be served upon the indorser within one year after judgment, and not afterward.

Source. RS 182:19. CS 193:19. GS 203:10. GL 222:10. PS 218:10. PL 330:10. RL 386:10.
