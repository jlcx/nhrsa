TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 509
WRITS AND THEIR INDORSEMENT

Section 509:3

    509:3 Direction. – Writs returnable to the supreme or superior court shall be directed to the sheriff of any county or his deputy; provided, however, that writs of attachment shall be directed to the sheriff, the plaintiff, his attorney or any other person.

Source. RS 179:3. CS 193:2. GS 203:3. GL 216:4; 222:3. PS 218:3. PL 330:3. RL 386:3. RSA 509:3. 1977, 519:4, eff. Sept. 13, 1977.
