TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 509
WRITS AND THEIR INDORSEMENT

Section 509:4

    509:4 Original Process. – Original process in the courts shall be summons and attachment and shall be in the form prescribed by law.

Source. RS 182:3. CS 193:3. GS 203:4. GL 222:4. PS 218:4. PL 330:4. RL 386:4. RSA 509:4. 1971, 227:6, eff. Aug. 17, 1971.
