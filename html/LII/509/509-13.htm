TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 509
WRITS AND THEIR INDORSEMENT

Section 509:13

    509:13 Municipal Courts; Attachment; Execution. – If the defendant in an action before a municipal court has personal property liable to attachment in a county of which he is not an inhabitant, the original writ and execution may be directed to the sheriff of any county, his deputy or to any constable of any town in which the defendant resides or has such property.

Source. RS 182:6. CS 193:6. GS 203:13. GL 222:13. PS 218:13. PL 330:13. RL 386:13. RSA 509:13. 1957, 244:36, eff. Sept. 23, 1957.
