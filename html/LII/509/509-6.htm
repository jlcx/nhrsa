TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 509
WRITS AND THEIR INDORSEMENT

Section 509:6

    509:6 Real Actions. – In real actions and actions of ejectment, the original writ shall be summons or attachment only.

Source. RS 182:8. CS 193:8. GS 208:6. GL 222:6. PS 218:6. PL 330:6. RL 386:6.
