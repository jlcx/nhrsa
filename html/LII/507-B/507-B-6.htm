TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 507-B
BODILY INJURY ACTIONS AGAINST GOVERNMENTAL UNITS

Section 507-B:6

    507-B:6 Property Exempt From Attachment. – No property, either real or personal, of the governmental unit shall be subject to attachment or execution to secure payment of or to satisfy any judgment entered against the governmental unit under this chapter.

Source. 1975, 483:1, eff. July 1, 1975.
