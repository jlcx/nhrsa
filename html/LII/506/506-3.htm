TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 506
PREVENTION OF FRAUDS AND PERJURIES

Section 506:3

    506:3 Assignment of Wages. – No assignment of, or order for, wages to be earned in the future shall be valid against a creditor of the person making it until it has been accepted in writing and a copy of it and of the acceptance has been filed with the clerk of the town or city where the party making it resides.

Source. 1873, 9:1. 1878, 2:1. GL 249:48. PS 215:4. PL 327:3. RL 383:3.
