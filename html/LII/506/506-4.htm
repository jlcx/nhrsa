TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 506
PREVENTION OF FRAUDS AND PERJURIES

Section 506:4

    506:4 Record of Assignments. – The clerks of towns and cities shall keep for public inspection an alphabetical list of all such orders and assignments filed with them.

Source. 1873, 9:1. 1878, 2:1. GL 249:48. PS 215:4. PL 327:4. RL 383:4.
