TITLE LII
ACTIONS, PROCESS, AND SERVICE OF PROCESS

CHAPTER 511-A
PRE-JUDGMENT ATTACHMENT PROCEDURE

Section 511-A:1


[RSA 511-A:1 effective until July 1, 2015; see also RSA 511-A:1 set out below.]
    511-A:1 Pre-Judgment Attachments; Limitation. –
    In all civil actions, except as otherwise provided in replevin actions, a defendant shall be given notice and an opportunity for a preliminary hearing before any pre-judgment attachment, including attachments of property held by a trustee, shall be made. This chapter shall apply only to pre-judgment attachments, except as provided in RSA 676:17.

Source. 1973, 537:1. 1975, 428:4. 1985, 210:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1986 at 12:01 a.m.
