TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 3
NEGOTIABLE INSTRUMENTS

Part 3
Enforcement of Instruments

Section 382-A:3-301

    382-A:3-301 Person Entitled to Enforce Instrument. – "Person entitled to enforce'' an instrument means (i) the holder of the instrument, (ii) a nonholder in possession of the instrument who has the rights of a holder, or (iii) a person not in possession of the instrument who is entitled to enforce the instrument pursuant to Section 3-309 or 3-418(d). A person may be a person entitled to enforce the instrument even though the person is not the owner of the instrument or is in wrongful possession of the instrument.

Source. 1993, 346:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
