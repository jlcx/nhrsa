TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 5
LETTERS OF CREDIT

Section 382-A:5-105

    382-A:5-105 Consideration. – Consideration is not required to issue, amend, transfer, or cancel a letter of credit, advice, or confirmation.

Source. 1998, 200:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1999.
