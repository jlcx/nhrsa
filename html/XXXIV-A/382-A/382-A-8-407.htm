TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 8
INVESTMENT SECURITIES

Part 4
Registration

Section 382-A:8-407

    382-A:8-407 Authenticating Trustee, Transfer Agent, and Registrar. – A person acting as authenticating trustee, transfer agent, registrar, or other agent for an issuer in the registration of a transfer of its securities, in the issue of new security certificates or uncertificated securities, or in the cancellation of surrendered security certificates has the same obligation to the holder or owner of a certificated or uncertificated security with regard to the particular functions performed as the issuer has in regard to those functions.

Source. 1998, 326:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1999.
