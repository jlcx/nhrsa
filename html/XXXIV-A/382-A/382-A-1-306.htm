TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 1
GENERAL PROVISIONS

Part 3
Territorial Applicability and General Rules

Section 382-A:1-306

    382-A:1-306 Waiver or Renunciation of Claim or Right After Breach. – A claim or right arising out of an alleged breach may be discharged in whole or in part without consideration by agreement of the aggrieved party in an authenticated record.

Source. 2006, 169:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
