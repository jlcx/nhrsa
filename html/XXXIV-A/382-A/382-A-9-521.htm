       (c) Form of subsequent pages and correction statement. Any pages subsequent to the first page of a written initial financing statement or a written amendment to a financing statement that do not contain any new or changed information with regard to any debtor or secured party or any information specified in blocks numbered 17 or 18 of the UCC Financing Statement Addendum, and all pages of any written correction statement, need not be in the form and format set forth in subsection (a) or (b), but in such event must conform to any other formatting requirements established by statute or filing-office rule.

Source. 2001, 102:25, eff. July 1, 2001.
