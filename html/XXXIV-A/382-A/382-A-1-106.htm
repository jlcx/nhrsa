ARTICLE 1
GENERAL PROVISIONS

Part 1
General Provisions

Section 382-A:1-106

    382-A:1-106 Use of Singular and Plural; Gender. – In this chapter, unless the statutory context otherwise requires:
    (1) words in the singular number include the plural, and those in the plural include the singular; and
    (2) words of any gender also refer to any other gender.

Source. 2006, 169:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
