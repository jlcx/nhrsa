TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 2A
LEASES

Part 5
Default

C. Default by Lessee

Section 382-A:2A-532

    382-A:2A-532 Lessor's Rights to Residual Interest. – In addition to any other recovery permitted by this Article or other law, the lessor may recover from the lessee an amount that will fully compensate the lessor for any loss of or damage to the lessor's residual interest in the goods caused by the default of the lessee.

Source. 1993, 345:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
