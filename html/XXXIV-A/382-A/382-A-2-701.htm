TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 2
SALES

Part 7
Remedies

Section 382-A:2-701

    382-A:2-701 Remedies for Breach of Collateral Contracts Not Impaired. – Remedies for breach of any obligation or promise collateral or ancillary to a contract for sale are not impaired by the provisions of this Article.

Source. 1959, 247:1, eff. July 1, 1961.
