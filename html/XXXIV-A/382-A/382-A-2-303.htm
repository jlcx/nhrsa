TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 2
SALES

Part 3
General Obligation and Construction of Contract

Section 382-A:2-303

    382-A:2-303 Allocation or Division of Risks. – Where this Article allocates a risk or a burden as between the parties "unless otherwise agreed'', the agreement may not only shift the allocation but may also divide the risk or burden.

Source. 1959, 247:1, eff. July 1, 1961.
