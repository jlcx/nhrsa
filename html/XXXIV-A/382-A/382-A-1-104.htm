TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 1
GENERAL PROVISIONS

Part 1
General Provisions

Section 382-A:1-104

    382-A:1-104 Construction Against Implied Repeal. – Since this chapter is a general act intended to provide unified coverage of its subject matter, no part of it shall be deemed to be impliedly repealed by subsequent legislation if such construction can reasonably be avoided.

Source. 2006, 169:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
