TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 2A
LEASES

Part 3
Effect of Lease Contract

Section 382-A:2A-311

    382-A:2A-311 Priority Subject to Subordination. – Nothing in this Article prevents subordination by agreement by any person entitled to priority.

Source. 1993, 345:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
