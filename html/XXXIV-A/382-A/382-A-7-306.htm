TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 7
DOCUMENTS OF TITLE

Part 3
Bills of Lading: Special Provisions

Section 382-A:7-306

    382-A:7-306 Altered Bills of Lading. – An unauthorized alteration or filling in of a blank in a bill of lading leaves the bill enforceable according to its original tenor.

Source. 2006, 169:17, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
