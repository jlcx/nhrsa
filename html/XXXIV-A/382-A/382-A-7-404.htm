TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 7
DOCUMENTS OF TITLE

Part 4
Warehouse Receipts and Bills of Lading: General Obligations

Section 382-A:7-404

    382-A:7-404 No Liability for Good-Faith Delivery Pursuant to Document of Title. – A bailee that in good faith has received goods and delivered or otherwise disposed of the goods according to the terms of a document of title or pursuant to this article is not liable for the goods even if:
       (1) the person from which the bailee received the goods did not have authority to procure the document or to dispose of the goods; or
       (2) the person to which the bailee delivered the goods did not have authority to receive the goods.

Source. 2006, 169:17, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
