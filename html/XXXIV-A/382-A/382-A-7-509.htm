TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 7
DOCUMENTS OF TITLE

Part 5
Warehouse Receipts and Bills of Lading: Negotiation and Transfer

Section 382-A:7-509

    382-A:7-509 Adequate Compliance With Commercial Contract. – Whether a document of title is adequate to fulfill the obligations of a contract for sale, a contract for lease, or the conditions of a letter of credit is determined by Article 2, 2A, or 5.

Source. 2006, 169:17, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
