TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 2A
LEASES

Part 3
Effect of Lease Contract

Section 382-A:2A-302

    382-A:2A-302 Title to and Possession of Goods. – Except as otherwise provided in this Article, each provision of this Article applies whether the lessor or a third party has title to the goods, and whether the lessor, the lessee, or a third party has possession of the goods, notwithstanding any statute or rule of law that possession or the absence of possession is fraudulent.

Source. 1993, 345:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
