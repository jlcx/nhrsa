TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 2A
LEASES

Part 3
Effect of Lease Contract

Section 382-A:2A-301

    382-A:2A-301 Enforceability of Lease Contract. – Except as otherwise provided in this Article, a lease contract is effective and enforceable according to its terms between the parties, against purchasers of the goods and against creditors of the parties.

Source. 1993, 345:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
