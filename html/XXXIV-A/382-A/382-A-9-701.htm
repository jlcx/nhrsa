Section 382-A:9-701

    382-A:9-701 Effective Date. – This Act takes effect on July 1, 2001. References in this part to "this Act'' refer to the legislative enactment by which this part is added to RSA 382-A:9. References in this part to "former Article 9'' refer to RSA 382-A:9 as in effect immediately before this Act takes effect.

Source. 2001, 102:25, eff. July 1, 2001.
