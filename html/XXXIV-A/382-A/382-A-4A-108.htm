TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 4A
FUNDS TRANSFERS

Part 1
Subject Matter and Definitions

Section 382-A:4A-108

    382-A:4A-108 Exclusion of Consumer Transactions Governed by Federal Law. – This Article does not apply to a funds transfer any part of which is governed by the Electronic Fund Transfer Act of 1978 (Title XX, Public Law 95-630, 92 Stat. 3728, 15 U.S.C. section 1693 et seq.) as amended from time to time.

Source. 1993, 346:7, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
