TITLE XXXIV-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 4
BANK DEPOSITS AND COLLECTIONS

Part 2
Collection of Items: Depositary and Collecting Banks

Section 382-A:4-206

    382-A:4-206 Transfer Between Banks. – Any agreed method that identifies the transferor bank is sufficient for the item's further transfer to another bank.

Source. 1993, 346:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1994.
