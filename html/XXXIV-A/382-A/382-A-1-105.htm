CHAPTER 382-A
UNIFORM COMMERCIAL CODE

ARTICLE 1
GENERAL PROVISIONS

Part 1
General Provisions

Section 382-A:1-105

    382-A:1-105 Severability. – If any provision or clause of this chapter or its application to any person or circumstance is held invalid, the invalidity does not affect other provisions or applications of this chapter which can be given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to this end the provisions of this chapter are severable.

Source. 2006, 169:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2007.
