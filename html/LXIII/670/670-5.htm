TITLE LXIII
ELECTIONS

CHAPTER 670
VILLAGE DISTRICT ELECTIONS

Election Procedure

Section 670:5

    670:5 Warrant. – Village district elections shall be warned by the commissioners in the same manner as town elections are warned by the selectmen as provided in RSA 669:2 and RSA 39.

Source. 1979, 410:1, eff. July 1, 1979.
