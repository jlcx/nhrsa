TITLE LXIII
ELECTIONS

CHAPTER 654
VOTERS AND CHECKLISTS

General Voter Registration

Section 654:7-b

    654:7-b Effect of Registration on Election Day. – Any person who registers to vote on election day according to the provisions of RSA 654:7-a shall be registered to vote at all subsequent town, state, and federal elections.

Source. 1994, 154:2, eff. May 23, 1994.
