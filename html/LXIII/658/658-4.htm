TITLE LXIII
ELECTIONS

CHAPTER 658
PRE-ELECTION PROCEDURE

Inspectors of Election

Section 658:4

    658:4 Oath; Term. – Each inspector of election shall be sworn to the faithful performance of his duties and shall hold office for 2 years from November 1 in the year in which he is appointed or until a successor is appointed and qualified.

Source. 1979, 436:1, eff. July 1, 1979.
