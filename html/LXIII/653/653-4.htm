TITLE LXIII
ELECTIONS

CHAPTER 653
ELECTION OF OFFICERS AND DELEGATES

Federal Officers to be Elected

Section 653:4


[RSA 653:4 effective until implementation of 1995, 108:3 amendment; see also RSA 653:4 set out below.]
    653:4 United States Representatives. –
    At every state general election, the voters of each congressional district shall elect one United States representative.

Source. 1979, 436:1, eff. July 1, 1979.
