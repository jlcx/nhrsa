TITLE LXIII
ELECTIONS

CHAPTER 653
ELECTION OF OFFICERS AND DELEGATES

Election of Delegates to Party Conventions

Section 653:6


[RSA 653:6 effective January 1, 2015; see also RSA 653:6 set out above.]
    653:6 Delegates to State Party Conventions. –
    At every state primary election, the voters in each state representative district shall elect delegates to each state party convention. In order to be elected as a delegate to a state party convention, a person shall be a registered voter of the same party in the district from which the person is to be elected, and shall receive either a minimum of 10 votes or 10 percent of the votes cast in the district from which the person is to be elected, whichever is less. A person who is entitled to serve as a delegate because he or she has been nominated for office as provided in RSA 667:21 shall not be entitled to serve as an elected delegate under this section, and the next highest eligible vote-getter shall be elected.

Source. 1979, 436:1. 1992, 68:1. 1995, 47:3. 2005, 5:1, eff. July 2, 2005. 2012, 63:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2015.
