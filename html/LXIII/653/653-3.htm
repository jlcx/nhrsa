TITLE LXIII
ELECTIONS

CHAPTER 653
ELECTION OF OFFICERS AND DELEGATES

Federal Officers to be Elected

Section 653:3


[The amendment to RSA 653:3 by 1995, 108:2 is omitted in light of the United States supreme court decision invalidating state-imposed congressional term limits. See U.S. Term Limits, Inc. v. Thornton (1995) 514 U.S. 779.]
    653:3 United States Senators. –
    At the state general election in 1980 and at the state general election every sixth year thereafter and at the state general election in 1984 and at the state general election every sixth year thereafter, the voters of the state shall elect one United States senator.

Source. 1979, 436:1, eff. July 1, 1979.
