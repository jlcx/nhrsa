TITLE LXIII
ELECTIONS

CHAPTER 671
SCHOOL DISTRICT ELECTIONS

Conduct of Election: Coordination With Town Election

Section 671:24

    671:24 Checklist. – If it has not already done so, the school district shall, simultaneously with its vote to elect its district officers at the town election, be deemed to have adopted the checklist of the town in accordance with RSA 671:15.

Source. 1979, 321:1. 1987, 276:10, eff. July 24, 1987.
