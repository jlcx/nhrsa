TITLE LXIII
ELECTIONS

CHAPTER 671
SCHOOL DISTRICT ELECTIONS

Cooperative School Districts

Section 671:16

    671:16 Checklists in Other Districts. – In any school district which has not adopted the town checklist, the school board, acting as supervisors of the checklist for the school district, shall assume all the duties the supervisors of the checklist have in RSA 671:15.

Source. 1979, 321:1, eff. Aug. 21, 1979.
