TITLE LXIII
ELECTIONS

CHAPTER 656
PREPARATION OF VOTING MATERIALS

State General Election

Section 656:4-a


[RSA 656:4-a temporarily suspended; see suspension note set out below.]
    656:4-a Candidates for the Office of United States Senator and United States Representative. –
    The state general election ballot shall not contain the name of a candidate for the office of United States senator or United States representative who is described under RSA 655:15-a, I(a)-(c).

Source. 1995, 108:5, eff. July 15, 1995.
