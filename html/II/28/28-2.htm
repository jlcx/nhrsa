TITLE II
COUNTIES

CHAPTER 28
COUNTY COMMISSIONERS

Section 28:2

    28:2 Clerk. – The clerk shall keep a record of the proceedings of the commissioners, the claims allowed by them, the annual report of the county treasurer and the proceedings of the county convention, and shall keep on file, in a county safe, all original papers, claims and accounts acted upon by the board.

Source. 1855, 1659:37. GS 24:1. GL 25:1. PS 27:1. PL 38:2. RL 47:2.
