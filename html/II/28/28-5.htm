TITLE II
COUNTIES

CHAPTER 28
COUNTY COMMISSIONERS

Section 28:5

    28:5 County Buildings. – The county commissioners may repair, enlarge, or erect county buildings at an expense not exceeding $5,000.

Source. RS 19:2. CS 20:2. 1855, 1659:37. GS 24:2. GL 25:2. PS 27:3. PL 38:5. RL 47:5. RSA 28:5. 1986, 8:1, eff. May 26, 1986.
