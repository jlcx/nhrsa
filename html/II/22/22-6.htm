TITLE II
COUNTIES

CHAPTER 22
TERRITORIAL EXTENT

Section 22:6

    22:6 Merrimack. – The county of Merrimack is bounded thus: Beginning at the northeast corner of Franklin; thence southerly and easterly by the easterly line of Franklin and the southerly line of the county of Belknap to the county of Rockingham; thence southwesterly by the county of Rockingham to the north line of Manchester; thence westerly and northerly by the lines of Manchester, Goffstown, Weare, Deering and Hillsborough to the east line of Washington; thence northerly by the easterly lines of Washington, Goshen, Sunapee and Springfield to the westerly line of Danbury; thence northerly by the westerly line of Danbury to Alexandria; thence southerly by the easterly line of Danbury to Hill; thence easterly and southerly by the northerly and easterly lines of Hill to the bound first mentioned.

Source. RS 16:8. CS 17:8. GS 19:6. 1868, 14:1. 1874, 96:1. GL 20:6. PS 21:6. PL 20:6. RL 28:6.
