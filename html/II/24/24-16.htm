TITLE II
COUNTIES

CHAPTER 24
COUNTY CONVENTIONS

Meetings

Section 24:16

    24:16 Penalty. – Any violation of the provisions of the previous section or of provisions of RSA 29:8 shall subject the person or persons so violating to the provisions of RSA 661:9, providing for removal from office. A petition of 5 resident taxpayers of the county may be made to the superior court for such removal or for removal for official misconduct.

Source. 1951, 73:1, par. 7-b. RSA 24:16. 1995, 277:1, eff. Aug. 19, 1995.
