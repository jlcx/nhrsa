TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 433
SEEDS, PLANTS AND NURSERY STOCK

Nurseries and Nursery Stock

Section 433:29-a

    433:29-a License Required. – A person, firm, or corporation may not engage in or continue in the business of selling nursery stock without first obtaining a license to conduct such business in the state. Licenses are non-transferable and each business shall possess a valid license for each business location.

Source. 2002, 7:7, eff. Mar. 12, 2002.
