TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 433
SEEDS, PLANTS AND NURSERY STOCK

Nurseries and Nursery Stock

Section 433:24

    433:24 Cooperative Agreements; Grants-in-Aid. – The department is authorized to cooperate, receive grants-in-aid, and enter into agreements with any individual, organization, or county, state or federal agency for the purpose of implementing the provisions of this subdivision. The department may also enforce any federal quarantine or regulation promulgated under federal quarantine within the state of New Hampshire.

Source. 1985, 33:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
