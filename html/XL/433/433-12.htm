TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 433
SEEDS, PLANTS AND NURSERY STOCK

Certification

Section 433:12

    433:12 Acceptance for Certification. – Any variety of plant or seed entered for certification must first be approved by a plant and seed certification board, appointed by the commissioner of agriculture, markets, and food with the approval of the advisory board of the department.

Source. 1985, 72:1. 1995, 130:5, eff. July 23, 1995.
