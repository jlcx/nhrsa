TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 433
SEEDS, PLANTS AND NURSERY STOCK

Nurseries and Nursery Stock

Section 433:33

    433:33 Right of Entry. – The commissioner, director, and nursery inspectors shall at all times have the right to enter any public or private grounds in the performance of any duty under this subdivision.

Source. 1985, 33:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
