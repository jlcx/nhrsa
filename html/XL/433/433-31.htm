TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 433
SEEDS, PLANTS AND NURSERY STOCK

Nurseries and Nursery Stock

Section 433:31

    433:31 Lack of Certificate. – If nursery stock is brought into the state without a certificate of inspection, or notification of licensure, the consignee shall return it to the consignor at the expense of the consignor, or shall call the director to inspect the nursery stock.

Source. 1985, 33:1, eff. July 1, 1985. 2002, 7:9, eff. Mar. 12, 2002.
