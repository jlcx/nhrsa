TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 433
SEEDS, PLANTS AND NURSERY STOCK

New Hampshire Seed Law

Section 433:9

    433:9 Injunction. – When, in the performance of his duties, the commissioner applies to any court for a temporary or permanent injunction restraining any person from violating or continuing to violate any of the provisions of this subdivision or any rules adopted under this subdivision, the injunction shall be issued without bond.

Source. 1990, 126:2, eff. June 18, 1990.
