TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 433
SEEDS, PLANTS AND NURSERY STOCK

Nurseries and Nursery Stock

Section 433:27

    433:27 Regulation of Horticultural Services. – Any person engaged in horticultural services shall be subject to regulation by the commissioner under this subdivision.

Source. 1985, 33:1, eff. July 1, 1985. 2002, 7:4, eff. Mar. 12, 2002.
