TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 433
SEEDS, PLANTS AND NURSERY STOCK

Nurseries and Nursery Stock

Section 433:35

    433:35 Suspension of License. – The director may at any time suspend a license issued under the provisions of RSA 433:29-a if dangerous insect or plant disease pests are found upon the premises, either on the plants or in the soil, and shall not reinstate the license until such time as such pests have been suppressed or eradicated.

Source. 1985, 33:1, eff. July 1, 1985. 2002, 7:12, eff. Mar. 12, 2002.
