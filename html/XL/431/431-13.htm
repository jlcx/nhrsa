TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 431
SOIL CONDITIONERS

Fertilizers

Section 431:13

    431:13 Rulemaking. – For the enforcement of this subdivision, the commissioner shall adopt under RSA 541-A, after public hearing, such rules relating to investigational allowances, definitions, records, distribution, storage, use, and application of fertilizers as may be necessary to carry into effect the full intent and meaning of this subdivision.

Source. 1985, 72:1. 1995, 233:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1996.
