TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Rabies Control

Section 436:108

    436:108 Enforcement. – The commissioner shall enforce the provisions of this subdivision for the control of rabies in dogs, cats, or ferrets, and shall adopt such rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, as necessary to carry out the intent of this subdivision.

Source. 1985, 72:1. 1992, 250:11. 1995, 202:12, eff. June 12, 1995.
