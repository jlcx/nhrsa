TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Sale of Vaccines

Section 436:82

    436:82 Exception. – The provisions of this subdivision shall not apply to biological products and diagnostic reagents manufactured or issued by the United States Department of Agriculture.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
