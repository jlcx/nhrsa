TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Retention for Breeding Purposes

Section 436:67

    436:67 Premises. – The premises on which such animals are kept shall be at all times subject to inspection by the commissioner or his agent or by the department of health and human services.

Source. 1985, 72:1. 1995, 310:181, eff. Nov. 1, 1995.
