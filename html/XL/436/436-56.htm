TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Appraisal and Indemnity

Section 436:56

    436:56 Valuation. – In making such appraisals, the fact that the animals have been condemned for disease shall not be considered, and the animals shall be appraised for their true market value.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
