TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Report of Animals Killed

Section 436:79

    436:79 Penalty. – Any person who shall fail or neglect to send notifications as provided by RSA 436:77 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
