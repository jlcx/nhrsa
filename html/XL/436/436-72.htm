TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Marks and Records for Tested Animals

Section 436:72

    436:72 Records. – The commissioner shall keep a complete record of all tags so issued. In the case of reacting bovines, he shall also keep a complete description of said animals.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
