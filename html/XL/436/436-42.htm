TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Tests on Application of Owner; Vaccination

Section 436:42

    436:42 By Commissioner. – The owner of cattle, horses, asses, mules, sheep or swine may apply to the commissioner for an examination of his animals for tuberculosis, brucellosis or glanders, subject to the provisions of this subdivision.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
