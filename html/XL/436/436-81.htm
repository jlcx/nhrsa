TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Sale of Vaccines

Section 436:81

    436:81 Virulent Hog Cholera Virus Prohibited. – No person may manufacture, transport, possess or use virulent hog cholera virus.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
