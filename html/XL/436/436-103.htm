TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Rabies Control

Section 436:103

    436:103 Cost. – The cost of rabies vaccination shall be paid by the owner of the dog, cat, or ferret.

Source. 1985, 72:1. 1992, 250:6. 1995, 202:7, eff. June 12, 1995.
