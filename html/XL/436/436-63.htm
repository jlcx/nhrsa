TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Retention for Breeding Purposes

Section 436:63

    436:63 Killing. – Such animals may be condemned and killed at any time by the commissioner when, in his judgment, conditions require such action.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
