TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Retention for Breeding Purposes

Section 436:65

    436:65 Milk of Retained Animals. – The milk of such animals may, with the approval of the commissioner, be used after boiling or heating to a temperature of 212 degrees Fahrenheit or after proper pasteurization.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
