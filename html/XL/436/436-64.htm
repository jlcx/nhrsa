TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Retention for Breeding Purposes

Section 436:64

    436:64 No Compensation. – In case such animals quarantined under the provisions of this subdivision are at any time condemned or killed after having been used for breeding purposes, the owner shall not receive compensation therefor from the state.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
