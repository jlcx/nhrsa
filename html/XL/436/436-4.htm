TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 436
DISEASES OF DOMESTIC ANIMALS

Division of Animal Industry

Section 436:4

    436:4 Report. – The commissioner shall, between September 1 and October 31 of each year, make a written report to the governor and council, stating in detail the work done during the preceding year.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
