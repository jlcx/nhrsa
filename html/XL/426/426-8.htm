TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 426
STANDARDS FOR FARM PRODUCTS

Section 426:8

    426:8 Rulemaking; Fees. – The commissioner may adopt rules, pursuant to RSA 541-A, for carrying out the provisions of this chapter, including the fixing of fees for inspections and certifications authorized by RSA 426:6-b and RSA 426:7. Such fees shall be credited to the general fund of the state.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985. 2002, 38:4, eff. April 19, 2002. 2003, 177:3, eff. Aug. 22, 2003; 177:6, eff. July 1, 2007.
