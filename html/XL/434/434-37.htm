TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 434
MARKETING AND GRADING OF CERTAIN COMMODITIES

Apple Marketing

Section 434:37

    434:37 Gifts and Donations. – The commissioner may accept grants, gifts and donations to further the purpose of any statewide marketing order. Such sums shall be deposited in the apple marketing account established by RSA 434:36.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
