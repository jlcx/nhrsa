TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 427
LIVESTOCK AND MEAT INSPECTION

Shipment of Livestock

Section 427:52

    427:52 Penalty. –
    I. Whoever knowingly violates any provision of RSA 427:48-51 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor if a natural person, or guilty of a felony if any other person.
    II. In addition to the penalty under paragraph I, whoever violates any of the provisions of RSA 427:48-51 or rule adopted under such provisions may be subject to an administrative fine levied by the commissioner, not to exceed $1,000 for each violation.

Source. 1985, 72:1. 1989, 11:2, eff. April 4, 1989.
