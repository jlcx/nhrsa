TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 427
LIVESTOCK AND MEAT INSPECTION

Shipment of Livestock

Section 427:54

    427:54 License Required. – No person, except a common carrier, shall transport livestock in a motor vehicle upon any public highway in this state without being licensed so to do as provided in this subdivision.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
