TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 427
LIVESTOCK AND MEAT INSPECTION

Shipment of Livestock

Section 427:56

    427:56 Fees. – The fee for each license shall be $50. All fees received under this section shall be paid to the commissioner of agriculture, markets, and food for the payment of expenses incurred under this subdivision.

Source. 1985, 72:1. 1995, 130:5, eff. July 23, 1995.
