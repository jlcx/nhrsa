TITLE XL
AGRICULTURE, HORTICULTURE AND ANIMAL HUSBANDRY

CHAPTER 427
LIVESTOCK AND MEAT INSPECTION

Shipment of Livestock

Section 427:58

    427:58 Exception. – A farmer resident of the state shall be excepted from the provisions of RSA 427:54-57 when engaged in the transportation in daylight of only his own livestock.

Source. 1985, 72:1, eff. July 1, 1985.
