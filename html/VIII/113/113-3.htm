TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 113
FIDUCIARIES ENTERING MILITARY SERVICE

Section 113:3

    113:3 Extent. – Any decree entered pursuant to the provisions of RSA 113:2 shall remain in force until revoked by the court upon cause shown and no act done by any substituted fiduciary pro tem or co-fiduciary or co-fiduciaries while such decree is in force shall be impeached on the ground that a fiduciary was not in or had ceased to be in military service.

Source. 1943, 196:5, eff. May 18, 1943.
