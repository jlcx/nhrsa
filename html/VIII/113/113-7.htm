TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 113
FIDUCIARIES ENTERING MILITARY SERVICE

Section 113:7

    113:7 Power of Removal. – The court appointing a substituted fiduciary pro tem under the provisions of RSA 113:2 shall have the same powers of control, removal, discharge and settlement of the accounts of such substituted fiduciary as are conferred upon it by existing law with respect to other fiduciaries.

Source. 1943, 196:7, eff. May 18, 1943.
