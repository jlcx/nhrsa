TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 110-B
THE MILITIA

National Guard Scholarship Fund

Section 110-B:62

    110-B:62 Scholarship Fund Committee. – The fund established pursuant to RSA 110-B:60 shall be administered by a scholarship committee consisting of the adjutant general or his designee, the commissioner of education or his designee, one person appointed by the governor, one member of the house of representatives committee on public protection and veterans' affairs appointed by the speaker of the house and one member of the senate appointed by the president of the senate. The appointed members shall serve a 2-year term and may be reappointed for an additional term. The committee shall make the final determination of the recipients of the scholarships offered in any fiscal year. The scholarship awards shall be made annually between the close of the fiscal year and the following September 1.

Source. 1981, 434:1, eff. Aug. 22, 1981.
