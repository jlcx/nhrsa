TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 115-B
VETERANS PRIORITY FOR TRAINING PROGRAMS

Section 115-B:2

    115-B:2 Program Eligibility. – For a veteran to be given priority of service for a specific training program, the veteran shall first meet the eligibility criteria and qualification requirements of the specific training program.

Source. 1997, 80:2, eff. Aug. 2, 1997.
