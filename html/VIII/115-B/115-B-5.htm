TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 115-B
VETERANS PRIORITY FOR TRAINING PROGRAMS

Section 115-B:5

    115-B:5 Discrimination Complaints. – Complaints of discrimination arising under this chapter shall be investigated and remedied to the extent allowed by law. Complaints shall be made to the state office of veterans services by the veteran or such veteran's authorized representative.

Source. 1997, 80:2. 2010, 119:7, eff. July 31, 2010.
