TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 115-B
VETERANS PRIORITY FOR TRAINING PROGRAMS

Section 115-B:6

    115-B:6 Federal Law. – Provisions of this chapter shall be superseded by federal laws and regulations which are more stringent or provide specific veterans preference or priority of service relative to administering training programs or training program grants to the states.

Source. 1997, 80:2, eff. Aug. 2, 1997.
