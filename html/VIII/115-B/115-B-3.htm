TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 115-B
VETERANS PRIORITY FOR TRAINING PROGRAMS

Section 115-B:3

    115-B:3 Application for Priority of Service. – A veteran shall apply for veterans priority of service with the training provider. The veteran is responsible for furnishing proof of service in the form of a copy of such veteran's DD214 certificate of release or discharge from active duty prior to the beginning of training.

Source. 1997, 80:2, eff. Aug. 2, 1997.
