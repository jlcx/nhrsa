TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 115-B
VETERANS PRIORITY FOR TRAINING PROGRAMS

Section 115-B:4

    115-B:4 Priority Placement. – Any eligible veteran who has applied for priority of service in a training program, has provided proof of veterans status, and has met all basic criteria for the program shall be given priority placement into the program.

Source. 1997, 80:2, eff. Aug. 2, 1997.
