TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 119
NEW HAMPSHIRE VETERANS' HOME

Section 119:14

    119:14 Compensation of Board Members. – No member of the board, excepting the secretary, shall receive compensation for any services rendered; but the necessary expenses actually incurred by each member in the performance of his duties shall be paid by the state. The board may fix the compensation of the secretary, to be paid in like manner.

Source. 1889, 76:8, 15. PS 13:10. PL 12:12. RL 18:12.
