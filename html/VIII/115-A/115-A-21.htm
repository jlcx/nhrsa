TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 115-A
AID FOR VETERANS' PROGRAMS

Global War on Terrorism Operations Service Bonus Payments

Section 115-A:21

    115-A:21 Time Limit; Late Applications. – No bonus payment shall be made under this subdivision unless application for the bonus payment is submitted within 5 years after the effective date of this subdivision. Any claim for bonus payment after such 5-year period shall be made to the director of the office of veterans services, and if approved by the director shall be submitted to the state treasurer and shall be a charge upon the general fund.

Source. 2004, 207:1, eff. June 11, 2004. 2010, 119:7, eff. July 31, 2010.
