TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 111
THE STATE GUARD

Section 111:17

    111:17 Oath of Officers. – The oath to be taken by officers commissioned in such forces shall be substantially in the form now prescribed for officers of the national guard, substituting the words "New Hampshire State Guard'' where necessary.

Source. 1941, 46:17. RL 144:17.
