TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 111
THE STATE GUARD

Section 111:23

    111:23 Acceptance of Gifts Prohibited. – The acceptance of gifts, donations, gratuities or anything of value by the state guard or by any member of such forces from any individual, firm, association, or corporation, by reason of such membership is prohibited. Violation of this section may be cause for dishonorable discharge from further service as a member of the state guard.

Source. 1941, 46:23. RL 144:23.
