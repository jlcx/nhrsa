TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 111
THE STATE GUARD

Section 111:14

    111:14 Enlistment of Civil Groups. – No civil organization, society, club, post, order, fraternity, association, brotherhood, body, union, league, or other combination of persons or civil group shall be enlisted in the state guard as an organization or unit.

Source. 1941, 46:14. RL 144:14.
