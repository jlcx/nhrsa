TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 111
THE STATE GUARD

Section 111:20

    111:20 New Hampshire Code of Military Justice. – Whenever the state guard or any part thereof shall be ordered out for active service, the New Hampshire code of military justice applicable to members of the national guard of this state in relation to courts martial, their jurisdiction and the limits of punishment and the rules and regulations prescribed thereunder shall be in full force and effect with respect to the state guard.

Source. 1941, 46:20. RL 144:20. RSA 111:20. 1981, 327:8, eff. Aug. 16, 1981.
