TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 111
THE STATE GUARD

Section 111:19

    111:19 Oath of Enlisted Men. – The oath to be taken upon enlistment in the state guard shall be substantially in the form now prescribed for enlisted men of the national guard, substituting the words "New Hampshire State Guard'' where necessary.

Source. 1941, 46:19. RL 144:19.
