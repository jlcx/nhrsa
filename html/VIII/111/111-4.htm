TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 111
THE STATE GUARD

Section 111:4

    111:4 Uniform. – The uniforms, the insignia of rank and grade, the devices, buttons, emblems, and decorations of the state guard shall be distinctive, specifications as to design and manner of wearing the same to be as prescribed in regulations to be issued by the adjutant general.

Source. 1941, 46:4. RL 144:4.
