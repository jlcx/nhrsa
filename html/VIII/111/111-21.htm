TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 111
THE STATE GUARD

Section 111:21

    111:21 Freedom from Arrest. – No officer or enlisted man of the state guard shall be arrested on any warrant, except for treason or felony, while going to, remaining at, or returning from a place where he is ordered to attend for military duty. For any suit or claim brought against a member of the state guard, indemnification shall be accorded as provided by the provisions of RSA 99-D.

Source. 1941, 46:21. RL 144:21. RSA 111:21. 1985, 144:8, eff. July 19, 1985.
