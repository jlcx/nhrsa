TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 111
THE STATE GUARD

Section 111:9

    111:9 Use of Armories. – The adjutant general is authorized to make available to the state guard the facilities of the state armories and military reservations and their equipment and such other state premises and property as may be available.

Source. 1941, 46:9. RL 144:9.
