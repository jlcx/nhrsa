TITLE VIII
PUBLIC DEFENSE AND VETERANS' AFFAIRS

CHAPTER 110-D
INTERSTATE COMPACT ON EDUCATIONAL OPPORTUNITY FOR MILITARY CHILDREN

ARTICLE XVII

Section 110-D:18


[RSA 110-D:18 effective September 30, 2014.]
    110-D:18 Severability and Construction. –
    I. The provisions of this compact shall be severable, and if any phrase, clause, sentence, or provision is deemed unenforceable, the remaining provisions of the compact shall be enforceable.
    II. The provisions of this compact shall be liberally construed to effectuate its purposes.
    III. Nothing in this compact shall be construed to prohibit the applicability of other interstate compacts to which the states are members.

Source. 2014, 308:1, eff. Sept. 30, 2014.
