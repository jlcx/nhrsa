TITLE VII
SHERIFFS, CONSTABLES, AND POLICE OFFICERS

CHAPTER 105
POLICE OFFICERS AND WATCHMEN

Domestic Animals

Section 105:16

    105:16 Expenses. – All expenses incurred under RSA 105:14 and 15 shall be paid by the owner of such animal, and may be collected by an action on the case, or by enforcing a lien upon the animal.

Source. 1881, 10:2. PS 267:3. 1897, 22:1. PL 382:4. RL 445:4. RSA 575:4. 1973, 532:15, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
