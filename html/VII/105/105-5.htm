TITLE VII
SHERIFFS, CONSTABLES, AND POLICE OFFICERS

CHAPTER 105
POLICE OFFICERS AND WATCHMEN

Section 105:5

    105:5 Police Matrons. – Women may be appointed as police matrons, and thereupon shall have, within the limits of the city or town, all the powers conferred by law upon constables except in relation to the service of civil process.

Source. 1915, 91:1. PL 363:5. RL 422:5.
