TITLE VII
SHERIFFS, CONSTABLES, AND POLICE OFFICERS

CHAPTER 105
POLICE OFFICERS AND WATCHMEN

Domestic Animals

Section 105:17

    105:17 Arrest. – If any person shall be found violating the laws in relation to cruelty to animals he may be arrested and held without warrant, in the same manner as in case of persons found breaking the peace.

Source. 1870, 4:4. GL 281:29. PS 267:8. PL 382:12. RL 445:12. RSA 575:12. 1973, 532:15, eff. Nov. 1, 1973.
