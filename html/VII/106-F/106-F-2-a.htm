TITLE VII
SHERIFFS, CONSTABLES, AND POLICE OFFICERS

CHAPTER 106-F
PRIVATE INVESTIGATIVE AGENCIES, BAIL ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES, AND SECURITY SERVICES

Section 106-F:2-a

    106-F:2-a Prohibition. – No person who is a sworn law enforcement officer employed by the state of New Hampshire, any of its political subdivisions, any other state or its political subdivisions, or the federal government shall be issued or hold a private investigator license or agency license, under this chapter.

Source. 1996, 37:2. 2010, 292:4, eff. Jan. 1, 2011.
