TITLE VII
SHERIFFS, CONSTABLES, AND POLICE OFFICERS

CHAPTER 106-F
PRIVATE INVESTIGATIVE AGENCIES, BAIL ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES, AND SECURITY SERVICES

Section 106-F:10

    106-F:10 Disposition of Fees. – All fees collected under this chapter shall be paid into the general fund of the state.

Source. 1977, 582:1, eff. Sept. 16, 1977.
