TITLE VII
SHERIFFS, CONSTABLES, AND POLICE OFFICERS

CHAPTER 106-F
PRIVATE INVESTIGATIVE AGENCIES, BAIL ENFORCEMENT AGENCIES, AND SECURITY SERVICES

Section 106-F:16

    106-F:16 Penalty. – Any person who engages in business as a private investigative agency, bail enforcement agency, or security guard service without first having obtained a license therefor or who violates any other provisions of this chapter shall be guilty of a misdemeanor for a natural person and a felony for any other person.

Source. 1977, 582:1. 1991, 308:35. 2010, 292:19, eff. Jan. 1, 2011.
