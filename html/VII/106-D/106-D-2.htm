TITLE VII
SHERIFFS, CONSTABLES, AND POLICE OFFICERS

CHAPTER 106-D
NEW ENGLAND STATE POLICE COMPACT

Section 106-D:2

    106-D:2 Director, Division of State Police. – For the purposes of this chapter, the director of the division of state police shall be this state's representative to the conference.

Source. 1969, 224:1, eff. June 11, 1969.
