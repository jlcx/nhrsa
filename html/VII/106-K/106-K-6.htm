TITLE VII
SHERIFFS, CONSTABLES, AND POLICE OFFICERS

CHAPTER 106-K
CRIMINAL JUSTICE INFORMATION SYSTEM

Section 106-K:6

    106-K:6 Confidentiality. – All data stored in J-One shall be confidential and shall be exempt from disclosure under RSA 91-A; provided, however, that nothing in this chapter shall affect the continued application of RSA 91-A to such information, to the extent that it is collected and maintained separately by a member agency.

Source. 2005, 244:2, eff. Jan. 1, 2006.
