TITLE VII
SHERIFFS, CONSTABLES, AND POLICE OFFICERS

CHAPTER 106-G
ADVISORY COMMITTEE ON BREATH ANALYZER MACHINES

Section 106-G:2

    106-G:2 Terms. – The terms of the advisory committee members designated in RSA 106-G:1, I, II and IV-IX shall be coterminous with their office. The public member shall be appointed to a term of 3 years.

Source. 1988, 268:4, eff. July 1, 1988.
