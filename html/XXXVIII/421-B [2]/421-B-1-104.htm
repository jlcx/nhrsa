TITLE XXXVIII
SECURITIES

CHAPTER 421-B [2]
UNIFORM SECURITIES ACT

ARTICLE 1
General Provisions

Section 421-B:1-104


[RSA 421-B:1-104 effective January 1, 2016.]
    421-B:1-104 References to Federal Agencies. –
    A reference in this chapter to an agency or department of the United States is also a reference to a successor agency or department.

Source. 2015, 273:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
