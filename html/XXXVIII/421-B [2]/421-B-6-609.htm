TITLE XXXVIII
SECURITIES

CHAPTER 421-B [2]
UNIFORM SECURITIES ACT

ARTICLE 6
Administration and Judicial Review

Section 421-B:6-609


[RSA 421-B:6-609 effective January 1, 2016.]
    421-B:6-609 Judicial Review. –
    Final orders issued by the secretary of state under this chapter are subject to judicial review in accordance with RSA 541.

Source. 2015, 273:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
