TITLE XXXVIII
SECURITIES

CHAPTER 421-B [1]
SECURITIES

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 421-B:32


[RSA 421-B:32 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA Chapter 421-B set out below.]
    421-B:32 Statutory Policy. –
    This chapter shall be so construed as to effectuate its general purpose to make uniform the law of those states which enact it and to coordinate the interpretation of this chapter with the related federal regulation.

Source. 1981, 214:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
