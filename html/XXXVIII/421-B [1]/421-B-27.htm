TITLE XXXVIII
SECURITIES

CHAPTER 421-B [1]
SECURITIES

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 421-B:27


[RSA 421-B:27 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA Chapter 421-B set out below.]
    421-B:27 Judicial Review of Orders. –
    Requests for rehearings and appeals from orders of the secretary of state shall be governed by RSA 541 and by the rules adopted by the secretary of state pursuant to title XXXVIII and RSA 541-A.

Source. 1981, 214:1. 1987, 411:2. 1991, 355:85. 1992, 288:53, eff. July 1, 1992.
