TITLE XXXVIII
SECURITIES

CHAPTER 421-B [1]
SECURITIES

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 421-B:28-a


[RSA 421-B:28-a effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA Chapter 421-B set out below.]
    421-B:28-a Advisory Councils and Committees. –
    The secretary of state may appoint such advisory councils and committees to aid in the enforcement of this chapter as are deemed necessary.

Source. 1994, 388:24, eff. June 9, 1994.
