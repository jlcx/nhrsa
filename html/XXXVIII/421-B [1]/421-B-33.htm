TITLE XXXVIII
SECURITIES

CHAPTER 421-B [1]
SECURITIES

Miscellaneous Provisions

Section 421-B:33


[RSA 421-B:33 effective until January 1, 2016; see also RSA Chapter 421-B set out below.]
    421-B:33 Severability of Provisions. –
    If any provision of this chapter or the application of this chapter to any person or circumstance is held invalid, the invalidity shall not affect other provisions or applications of the chapter which can be given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to this end the provisions of this chapter are severable.

Source. 1981, 214:1, eff. Jan. 1, 1982.
