TITLE XXXVIII
SECURITIES

CHAPTER 421-B
UNIFORM SECURITIES ACT

Article 6
Administration and Judicial Review

Section 421-B:6-612

    421-B:6-612 Severability Clause. – If any provision of this chapter or its application to any person or circumstances is held invalid, the invalidity does not affect other provisions or applications of this chapter that can be given effect without the invalid provision or application, and to this end the provisions of this chapter are severable.

Source. 2015, 273:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
