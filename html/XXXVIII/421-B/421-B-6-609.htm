TITLE XXXVIII
SECURITIES

CHAPTER 421-B
UNIFORM SECURITIES ACT

Article 6
Administration and Judicial Review

Section 421-B:6-609

    421-B:6-609 Judicial Review. – Final orders issued by the secretary of state under this chapter are subject to judicial review in accordance with RSA 541.

Source. 2015, 273:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
