TITLE XXXVIII
SECURITIES

CHAPTER 421-B
UNIFORM SECURITIES ACT

Article 1
General Provisions

Section 421-B:1-104

    421-B:1-104 References to Federal Agencies. – A reference in this chapter to an agency or department of the United States is also a reference to a successor agency or department.

Source. 2015, 273:1, eff. Jan. 1, 2016.
