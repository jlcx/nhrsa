TITLE XLII
NOTARIES, COMMISSIONERS, JUSTICES OF THE PEACE, AND ACKNOWLEDGMENTS

CHAPTER 455-A
JUSTICES OF THE PEACE

Section 455-A:1

    455-A:1 Appointment. – Justices of the peace shall be appointed by the governor, with the advice and consent of the executive council, and shall be commissioned for 5 years, as provided in the New Hampshire constitution.

Source. 1988, 121:10, eff. Oct. 1, 1988.
